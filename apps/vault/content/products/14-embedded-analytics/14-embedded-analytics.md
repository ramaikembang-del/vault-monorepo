# 14 - Embedded Analytics

**Product Code:** B1 (Business Pillar - White-label/Partnership)
**Sprint:** Sprint 4+ (Post-Scale)
**Status:** Planning
**Owner:** Product / Partnerships

---

## Overview

Embedded Analytics is a white-label analytics platform that allows partners and resellers to integrate PWBI F&B Growth analytics capabilities into their own products and services. This B2B2C product enables third-party platforms to offer analytics to their F&B customers.

**Target Market:**
- POS system providers (Pawoon, Moka, Qasir)
- Accounting software vendors
- F&B management platforms
- Franchise management systems
