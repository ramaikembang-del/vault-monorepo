## Pricing Strategy

### Core Pricing

**Annual Plan (Recommended):**
- Price: **Rp 500,000/year**
- Equivalent: Rp 41,666/month
- Savings: 1 month free vs. monthly
- Payment: Full prepayment

**Monthly Plan:**
- Price: **Rp 50,000/month**
- No commitment
- Cancel anytime
- Total if paid monthly: Rp 600K/year

**Free Trial:**
- Duration: 14 days
- Full features unlocked
- No credit card required
- Converts to paid automatically (opt-in)

### Add-Ons

**Additional Location:**
- Price: +Rp 300,000/year per location
- Example: Main café + 2 branches = Rp 500K + Rp 600K = Rp 1.1M/year

**Premium Support:**
- Price: +Rp 200,000/year
- Benefits:
 - Priority email/chat (< 4 hour response)
 - Phone support
 - Dedicated success manager
 - Quarterly business review

**WhatsApp Notifications:**
- First 100 messages/month: Free
- Additional: Rp 50/message (WhatsApp Business API fees)

### Pricing Rationale

**Value-Based Pricing:**
- Customer saves: Rp 5-10M/year (waste reduction)
- Our price: Rp 500K/year
- Customer ROI: **10-20x**
- Value capture: ~5% of value delivered

**Competitive Positioning:**
| Solution | Price | Our Advantage |
|----------|-------|---------------|
| Manual (Excel) | Free | We're automated, predictive |
| POS built-in | Included | We're smarter, mobile-first |
| Enterprise systems | Rp 5-10M/year | **We're 10x cheaper** |
| A4a | Rp 500K/year | **Sweet spot** |

**Affordability:**
- Rp 500K = ~1 day of revenue for most SMB F&B
- Rp 50K/month = cost of 5 coffees
- Lower than hiring someone to manage inventory

## Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Beta (January 2025)

**Objective:** Validate product-market fit, gather feedback

**Timeline:** 4 weeks (Jan 1 - Jan 31)

**Participants:**
- 50 beta customers
- Mix: 30 cafés, 15 restaurants, 5 bakeries
- Geography: Jakarta (40), Bandung (5), Surabaya (5)
- Selection criteria: Existing PWBI customers + referrals

**Beta Pricing:**
- Free for first 3 months
- Then 50% discount (Rp 250K/year) if they stay

**Success Criteria:**
- 80%+ completion of onboarding
- 70%+ weekly active usage
- NPS > 40
- 5+ strong testimonials
- < 5 critical bugs

**Activities:**
- Week 1: Onboard all 50
- Week 2-3: Daily check-ins, rapid iteration
- Week 4: Feedback synthesis, prep for launch

## Risks & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Low trial-to-paid conversion**
- Mitigation: Onboarding optimization, visible quick wins
- Trigger: If < 15%, investigate friction points

**Risk 2: High churn**
- Mitigation: Proactive customer success, feature education
- Trigger: If > 25% annual, pause growth, fix retention

**Risk 3: CAC too high**
- Mitigation: Optimize ad spend, focus on organic channels
- Trigger: If > Rp 300K, reduce paid spend

**Risk 4: Competition**
- Mitigation: Speed to market, superior UX, strong brand
- Monitor: Competitive intel monthly
