## Executive Summary

AI-powered inventory management tool that reduces waste by 50% and prevents stock-outs for small F&B businesses. Simple, affordable alternative to complex enterprise systems.

**Target Market:** 30,000+ small cafÃ©s, restaurants, bakeries in Indonesia struggling with manual inventory management.

---

## Problem Statement

### Current Pain Points

**1. Excessive Waste (Rp 5-15M annual loss)**
- 20-30% of inventory expires before use
- Over-ordering due to fear of stock-outs
- No visibility into usage patterns

**2. Frequent Stock-Outs**
- Lose sales when popular items run out
- Damage customer experience
- Happen 2-3 times per month on average

**3. Time-Consuming Manual Management**
- 5-10 hours/week on inventory counting
- Excel spreadsheets error-prone
- No systematic reorder process

**4. Poor Supplier Relationships**
- Last-minute rush orders (higher prices)
- Inconsistent order quantities
- No leverage for better terms

### Market Validation

**Research Findings (100 SMB F&B interviews):**
- 85% manage inventory manually (Excel/paper)
- 70% experience weekly stock-outs
- Average waste: 25% of inventory value
- Willing to pay: Rp 500K-1M/year for solution

**Competitive Landscape:**
- Full inventory systems: Rp 5-10M/year (too expensive)
- POS built-in: Basic, not predictive
- **Market Gap:** Affordable, AI-powered solution

---

## Solution

### Core Value Proposition

**"Stop guessing. Start saving. Reduce inventory waste 50% with AI-powered recommendations."**

**How It Works:**
1. **Connect Data** - Integrate POS or upload sales history
2. **AI Learns** - Analyzes 3+ months of patterns
3. **Smart Alerts** - Get reorder notifications 2-3 days before stock-out
4. **Order Optimal Qty** - AI calculates perfect order size
5. **Track & Improve** - Monitor waste reduction over time

### Key Features

#### 1. Smart Reorder Alerts
**Functionality:**
- Predictive algorithm forecasts stock-out date
- Considers supplier lead time (auto-adjusts timing)
- Accounts for seasonal patterns (holidays, weekends)
- SMS/WhatsApp/Push notifications

**Business Impact:**
- 90% reduction in stock-outs
- Never over-order again

#### 2. Optimal Order Quantity Calculator
**Algorithm:**
- Historical sales velocity
- Shelf life constraints
- Minimum order quantities
- Storage capacity limits
- Price break points (order more for better unit price)

**Business Impact:**
- Reduce waste 40-60%
- Lower procurement costs 5-10%

#### 3. Waste Prediction & Prevention
**Features:**
- "Use it or lose it" alerts (items expiring soon)
- FIFO tracking (first-in, first-out)
- Slow-moving item identification
- Promotional recommendations (discount expiring items)

**Business Impact:**
- Cut waste in half
- Save Rp 5-10M/year

#### 4. Supplier Performance Tracking
**Metrics:**
- On-time delivery rate
- Price consistency
- Quality scores (user-rated)
- Lead time accuracy

**Business Impact:**
- Identify best suppliers
- Negotiate better terms

#### 5. Inventory Health Dashboard
**Visualizations:**
- Current stock levels (color-coded: red/yellow/green)
- Days of inventory remaining
- Waste trend (month-over-month)
- Savings calculator (before/after comparison)

**Business Impact:**
- Actionable insights at a glance
- Data-driven decisions

## Risk & Mitigation

**Risk 1: Low adoption (SMB slow to adopt tech)**
- Mitigation: Free trial, simple onboarding, visible ROI

**Risk 2: Data accuracy issues (GIGO - garbage in, garbage out)**
- Mitigation: Data validation, user education, error alerts

**Risk 3: Churn due to business closures (70% of F&B fail)**
- Mitigation: Target established businesses (> 1 year old)

**Risk 4: Competition (larger players copy us)**
- Mitigation: Speed to market, brand, superior UX
