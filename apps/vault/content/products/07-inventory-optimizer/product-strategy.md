# Loyalty Builder Product Strategy
**Micro-SaaS for Customer Retention**

**Product Code:** A4c (Pillar 1 - Acquisition)
**Target Segment:** Phase 1-2, restaurants wanting repeat customers
**Pricing:** Rp 75K/month

---

## Purpose

Simple loyalty program builder for restaurants without complex CRM. Stamp cards, point systems, and VIP tiers  -  all digital.

## Key Features

- **Digital stamp card:** 10 stamps = free item
- **Point system:** Rp 10K spent = 1 point, redeem for rewards
- **VIP tiers:** Bronze  ->  Silver  ->  Gold based on visit frequency
- **Customer database:** Name, phone, visit history
- **WhatsApp marketing:** Blast promos to loyalty members

## Market Sizing

- **TAM:** 100,000 restaurants
- **SAM:** 25,000 restaurants (want loyalty program but no budget for complex CRM)
- **SOM:** 500-1,000 subscribers by Year 2

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | Rp 75K/month | 500 members, stamp cards, basic reports |
| Pro | Rp 150K/month | 2,000 members, points + tiers, WhatsApp marketing |

## Pain Points Solved

- **"How do I get customers to come back?"**  ->  Gamified loyalty
- **"I don't know my regulars"**  ->  Customer database
- **"Paper stamp cards get lost"**  ->  Digital, tied to phone number

## Upgrade Path

 ->  Loyalty data + sales data  ->  Powerful customer analytics
 ->  Want CLV, churn prediction  ->  Full SaaS Advanced

## Data Synergy

Loyalty data feeds into:
- Customer analytics (repeat rate, CLV)
- Marketing ROI (which promos work)
- Cohort retention analysis
