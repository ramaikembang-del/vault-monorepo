## Tier 1 (DIY Template): Phase 1 (Mikro - Single Outlet)

**Target:** Budget-conscious, tech-comfortable operators
**Pricing:** Rp 3.5-6M one-time
**Market Validation:** 15 interviews, 10 beta testers, 80% willingness to pay

### Persona 1: Dimas (The Budget-Conscious Learner)

**Demographics:**
- Age: 32
- Business: "Warung Kopi Dimas" - Single cafe
- Location: Yogyakarta
- Revenue: Rp 420M/year (~Rp 35M/month)
- Staff: 8 people (2 baristas, 3 servers, 2 kitchen, 1 cashier)

**Why Tier 1 Fits:**

**Pain Points (Realitas Lapangan):**
1. **"Modal tipis, margin tipis"** -> Rp 3.5-6 juta itu investasi gede, harus dipikir 10x

3. **"WiFi cafe sering mati Sabtu-Minggu"** -> Butuh yang bisa offline, nggak depend on internet
4. **"Gue nggak punya waktu belajar 40 jam"** -> Kasih yang instant, 2 jam setup max
5. **"Gue takut salah, nanti rugi"** -> Butuh guarantee, safety net, proven solution
6. **"Gue nggak butuh semua fitur"** -> Modular pricing - pay only for what you need (30-50 metrics)

**Transformation Trigger:**
- Liat kompetitor punya dashboard profesional: "Gue juga mau kayak gitu"
- Nemuin kebocoran Rp 3 juta/bulan di food cost: "Gue butuh dashboard beneran"
- Mau buka outlet ke-2: "Gue perlu compare performance 2 lokasi"

**Tier Recommendation:** Tier 1 Essentials (Rp 3.5-6M)
- Budget: Rp 3.5-6M for analytics (modular options)
- Tech-comfortable: Bisa follow YouTube tutorials, pakai Instagram lancar
- Willing to invest 4-8 hours setup time (weekend project)
- Wants full control: "Gue mau own data, nggak depend on vendor"

**Jobs to Be Done:**
- "Get professional analytics tanpa keluar duit gede"
- "Learn Power BI - skill yang bisa gue pake selamanya"
- "Prove analytics value sebelum invest lebih banyak"
- "Avoid monthly fees - gue pengen one-time payment"

**Relatable Quote:**
> "Gue tau modal tipis, margin tipis. Rp 3.5 juta itu besar buat gue (T1-Basic). Tapi kalau dashboard ini bisa hemat 5% food cost (Rp 3 juta/bulan), itu balik modal dalam 2 bulan. Worth it lah. Modular pricing bagus - gue mulai dari Base modules dulu, nanti add T1-A kalau perlu." €" Dimas, Warung Kopi Yogyakarta

**Acquisition Channel:**

## Common Jobs to Be Done (All Personas)

**Weekly Ritual:**
1. Check consolidated view (all outlets vs Excel per location)
2. Identify underperforming outlet/brand
3. Review staff rankings (accountability without confrontation)
4. Spot trends before they become crises

**Monthly Decision:**
1. Menu engineering (which items to promote/cut across outlets)
2. Staffing optimization (labor cost vs revenue per outlet)
3. Expansion planning (model outlet patterns guide new locations)

**Quarterly Strategic:**
1. Investor/stakeholder reporting (professional dashboard exports)
2. Pricing strategy (margin analysis across products/outlets)
3. Brand consistency (ensure quality across all locations)
