## Value Proposition

**"Naik Kelas Analytics - Look Like a Professional Operation, Think Like a Data-Driven Business"**

**For:** SME restaurant owners/managers
**Who:** Want professional-grade analytics without hiring BI consultants
**Our Product:** Power BI dashboard template (3 tiers: Tier 1 Rp 3.5-6M | Tier 2 Rp 7-12M | Tier 3 Rp 10-15M)

## Competitive Positioning

### Vs. BI Consultants

**Consultant Approach:**
- Rp 50-100M project (custom build)
- 3-6 months development time
- Ongoing Rp 5-10M/month maintenance
- **Target:** Large chains only (Rp 500M+ revenue)

**PWBI Approach:**
- **Tier 1:** Rp 3.5-6M (30-50 metrics, DIY) | **Tier 2:** Rp 7-12M (50-100 metrics, assisted) | **Tier 3:** Rp 10-15M (100-150 metrics, managed)
- 1-7 days setup time
- Optional Rp 75K/month (update subscription) or optional support (see Product 02) (T3 managed) - 80%+ cost savings
- **Target:** SME Rp 8-50M revenue (underserved market)

**Win:** 95% cost savings, 10x faster deployment, SME-accessible

---

### Vs. POS Built-In Reports

**POS Reports:**
- Basic sales summary (revenue, top items)
- No staff performance tracking
- No customer loyalty insights
- **Limitation:** Designed for transactions, not strategic planning

**PWBI Dashboard:**
- 30-150 operational metrics (modular framework vs. 5-10 in POS)
- Staff Performance Leaderboard (gamification) - in Professional/Enterprise modules
- Customer Patronage Ladder (First-timer -> VIP) - in Professional/Enterprise modules
- **Advantage:** Strategic insights, not just transactional data - choose modules you need

**Win:** POS = rearview mirror, PWBI = GPS navigation

---

### Vs. Excel/Manual Tracking

**Excel:**
- Manual data entry (error-prone, time-consuming)
- Static reports (no real-time updates)
- No visualization (tables only)
- **User:** Spends 5-10 hours/week updating spreadsheets

**PWBI:**
- Automated data import (CSV from POS)
- Dynamic dashboards (click to filter)
- Visual insights (charts, heatmaps, rankings)
- **User:** 1-minute daily refresh, 30 seconds to check

**Win:** 95% time savings, eliminate human error, actionable visuals

## Objection Handling Library

**"I don't have time to learn Power BI"**
- Tier 1: 30-day email support + video tutorials (self-paced)
- Tier 2: We set it up together, you just refresh daily (1 click)
- Tier 3: We maintain it, you just view dashboard (zero learning curve)

**"My staff won't understand the dashboard"**
- Simplified views per role (Owner sees all, Staff sees only their performance card)
- Bahasa Indonesia labels (not English metrics)
- Visual-first (charts, not tables)

**"What if my POS format changes?"**
- Tier 1: We provide updated import script (email support)
- Tier 2: 1 free update included (we adapt to new format)
- Tier 3: Maintenance plan covers all POS changes (see [[products/02-template-support/02-template-support|Product 02]] for support)

**"Is this a subscription? I hate subscriptions"**
- Tier 1-2: One-time payment, own it forever (no ongoing fees)
- Optional subscription (Rp 75K/month) for updates + support, but NOT required
- Tier 3: Yes subscription, but transparent pricing (no hidden fees)

**"I'll just hire a junior analyst for Rp 5M/month"**
- Junior analyst = Rp 5M/month x 12 = Rp 60M/year + training time
- Template Tier 2 = Rp 7-12M one-time (payback in 2-3 months of analyst salary)
- Plus: Dashboard doesn't take sick leave, doesn't resign, always accurate
- Modular: Pay only for metrics you need (30-100 metrics depending on tier)
