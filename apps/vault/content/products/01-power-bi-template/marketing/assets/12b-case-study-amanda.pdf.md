## Story Outline

### The Challenge
- Amanda's first café was successful, wanted to open 2nd location
- Needed to prove profitability to potential investors/partners
- Existing POS reports weren't investor-ready
- Spent hours each week manually creating Excel reports

### The Solution
- Purchased Power BI Template Tier 2 (Rp 7.5M one-time)
- Professional dashboards ready to show investors
- Product mix analysis revealed coffee margins vs. pastry
- Labor cost tracking showed optimal staffing levels

### The Results
- Secured partnership investment with dashboard-backed pitch
- Opened 2nd location with 30% better margins from Day 1
- Reduced reporting time from 10 hours/week to 30 min/week
- Quote: *"Dashboard ini jadi senjata utama saya waktu pitch ke investor."*
