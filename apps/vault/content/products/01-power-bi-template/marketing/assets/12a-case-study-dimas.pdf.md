## Story Outline

### The Challenge
- Dimas ran his warung for 3 years without understanding true profitability
- Thought he was making good money, but couldn't explain where it went
- No visibility into food costs, wastage, or best-selling items
- Making business decisions based on gut feel, not data

### The Solution
- Purchased Power BI Template Tier 1 (Rp 2.5M one-time)
- Spent 2 hours setting up with POS data export
- Immediately saw food cost % was 55% (way too high)
- Discovered 3 menu items were losing money

### The Results
- Reduced food cost from 55% to 38% in 2 months
- Increased profit margin by 15% (Rp 2M -> Rp 2.3M monthly net)
- ROI: Template paid for itself in first month
- Quote: *"Saya baru sadar ternyata nasi goreng ayam rugi terus. Sekarang saya tahu menu mana yang untung."*
