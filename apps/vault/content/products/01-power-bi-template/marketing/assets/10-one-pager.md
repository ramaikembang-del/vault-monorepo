## The Problem We Solve

**Pain Point 1: Time Waste**
- F&B owners spend 4-8 hours/week on manual Excel reporting
- That's 16-32 hours/month = Rp 8-16M in lost time (owner rate)

**Pain Point 2: No Insights**
- "I have data, but don't know what it MEANS"
- Food cost creeping up but can't pinpoint why
- Don't know which menu items are actually profitable

**Pain Point 3: Failed BI Attempts**
- 60% of people who buy generic Power BI templates fail to set up
- Tableau/Qlik too expensive (Rp 240K-1.1M/month per user)
- POS built-in analytics too basic (5-10 metrics only)
