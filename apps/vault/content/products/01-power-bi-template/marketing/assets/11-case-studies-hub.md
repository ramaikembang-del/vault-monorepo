## Case Study 1: "Excel to Dashboard in 30 Minutes"

### Customer Profile
**Name:** Dimas Pratama
**Business:** Coffee Shop ABC (Single outlet, Jakarta Selatan)
**Revenue:** Rp 500M/year
**Tier:** Tier 1 Essentials (Rp 4.5M)

---

### The Problem ("8-Hour Excel Hell")

**Before A1 Ecosystem:**
- Spent 4 hours every Monday updating Excel pivot tables
- Bought a Power BI template 6 months ago -> gave up after 2 weeks
- **Pain Point:** "I couldn't figure out how to format my CSV exports for Power BI. Every POS export has different columns. I tried 3 times, wasted 8 hours total, then went back to Excel."
- Lost Rp 3M/month to food cost creep (didn't notice ingredient prices increasing)

**Quote:**
> "Gue udah bosan Excel berantakan, tapi gue juga gak ngerti Power BI. YouTube tutorial semua bahasa Inggris, nggak ada yang spesifik buat restoran Indonesia."

---

### The Solution (Ecosystem Bundle, Not Just Template)

**What Changed:**
1. **Received complete bundle** (not just .pbix file):
 - Power BI Dashboard
 - 4 Excel templates (pre-formatted for F&B)
 - Google Sheets links
 - One-click refresh script
 - 5 video tutorials (Bahasa Indonesia)

2. **30-Minute Setup (vs 8 hours previous template):**
 - **Minute 0-10:** Watched Excel template walkthrough video
 - **Minute 10-25:** Filled `Daily-Sales.xlsx` with his data (dropdowns made it easy)
 - **Minute 25-28:** Opened Power BI, clicked Refresh
 - **Minute 28-30:** Dashboard populated with his actual data -> **WOW moment**

**Key Insight:**
> "Excel template = game changer. Staff gue nggak perlu ditraining Power BI. Mereka tetap isi Excel kayak biasa. Gue tinggal klik Refresh. Simpel."

---

### The Results (Month 1-3)

**Month 1:**
- Discovered food cost 37% (should be 30%) -> Saved Rp 2.1M
- Identified 1 staff stealing (sales Rp 1.8M vs others Rp 4.2M) -> Saved Rp 1.5M
- Time saved: 4 hours/week x 4 weeks = 16 hours (worth Rp 2M at owner time rate)

**Month 2:**
- VIP customer alert: 12 regulars hadn't visited 30+ days -> Called them, 8 returned (Rp 1.2M recovered revenue)
- Optimized staffing for Friday 7-9pm rush (added 2 servers) -> Rp 1M extra revenue/week

**Month 3:**
- Dashboard became daily habit (streak: 78 days)
- Food cost down to 32% (target 30% almost hit)
- Total savings/gains: **Rp 8.5M/month** (vs Rp 4.5M one-time investment)

**ROI:** Paid back in 16 days

---

### Customer Quote

## Case Study 2: "Multi-Outlet Consolidation in 2 Hours"

### Customer Profile
**Name:** Amanda Wong
**Business:** Cafe Nusantara (3 outlets: Menteng, Kemang, BSD)
**Revenue:** Rp 2.4B/year (Rp 800M/outlet avg)
**Tier:** Tier 2 Professional (Rp 8.25M - Base + Multi-Outlet module)

---

### The Problem ("3-File Excel Nightmare")

**Before A1 Ecosystem:**
- Each outlet manager sent separate Excel reports (different formats!)
- Amanda spent 5 hours every Sunday manually consolidating 3 files
- **Pain Point:** "Format di setiap outlet beda. Manager A pakai Moka export, Manager B manual Excel, Manager C pakai Google Sheets. Gue harus copy-paste satu-satu, terus rumus sering error."

**Monthly Cost:**
- 20 hours/month x Rp 500K/hour = **Rp 10M/month in owner time**
- Insights always 1 week late (by the time data ready, problem already escalated)

**Quote:**
> "Gue nggak bisa compare apple-to-apple. Revenue outlet A Rp 800M, outlet B Rp 600M. Tapi itu karena size beda atau performance beda? Gue nggak tau."

---

### The Solution (Custom Excel Template + Multi-Outlet Summary)

**What Tier 2 Included:**
1. **Custom Excel template** formatted to match Moka CSV export (POS-specific)
2. **Multi-Outlet Summary** (auto-consolidates 3 files)
3. **2-hour setup call** with our team (standardized format across all outlets)

**Setup Process:**
- **Week 1:** 2-hour call with Amanda + 3 outlet managers
 - Standardized data entry format for all 3 outlets
 - Trained managers on filling `Daily-Sales.xlsx` (15 minutes training each)
 - Set up folder structure: `C:\Cafe-Nusantara\Outlet-A\`, `Outlet-B\`, `Outlet-C\`

- **Week 2:** Managers started filling Excel templates daily
 - Each manager: 5 minutes/day (vs 30 minutes before)
 - WhatsApp reminder: "Jangan lupa isi Excel hari ini!"

- **Week 3:** Amanda's new workflow:
 - Managers save Excel to Google Drive (auto-sync)
 - Amanda downloads all 3 files (1 minute)
 - Opens Power BI -> Click Refresh -> **Consolidated dashboard in 10 seconds**

**Time Savings:** 5 hours -> 10 minutes (97% time reduction)

---

### The Results (Month 1-3)

**Month 1 - Immediate Insights:**
- **Outlet Comparison:** Menteng (Rp 850M) vs Kemang (Rp 650M) vs BSD (Rp 550M)
 - BSD underperforming -> Investigated -> Found: Wrong pricing (menu 20% cheaper than others)
 - Fixed pricing -> BSD revenue jumped Rp 100M/month

**Month 2 - Staff Optimization:**
- **Cross-Outlet Staff Leaderboard:** Best server (Rina from Menteng: Rp 4.5M/shift)
 - Moved Rina to train Kemang team -> Kemang revenue +15%
 - Fired bottom 2 performers (Rp 1.5M/shift vs avg Rp 3M) -> Hired 2 new, revenue +Rp 80M/month

**Month 3 - Strategic Decisions:**
- **Menu Engineering Across Outlets:**
 - Nasi Goreng: 40% food cost in Menteng, 28% in Kemang (why?)
 - Investigation -> Different suppliers -> Centralized purchasing -> Food cost unified at 30%
 - Savings: Rp 3M/month

**Total Impact:** Rp 13M/month (vs Rp 8.25M one-time investment)

**ROI:** Paid back in 14 days

---

### Customer Quote

> "Custom Excel template yang POS-specific = absolute game changer. Gue nggak perlu ngajarin manager format baru. Moka export langsung compatible. Dan Multi-Outlet Summary? Magic. Literally magic. Gue klik satu tombol, ketiga outlet muncul side-by-side. Mind blown."

**Recommendation:**
"Kalau lu punya 2+ outlets, Tier 2 worth it. Custom template saves you SO much headache."

## Case Study 3: "From Spreadsheet Chaos to Executive Dashboards"

### Customer Profile
**Name:** Pak Andi
**Business:** Andi's Bakery (5 outlets across Jabodetabek)
**Revenue:** Rp 8B/year (Rp 1.6B/outlet avg)
**Tier:** Tier 3 Enterprise (Rp 11.25M setup (support optional - see Product 02) - Base + Executive Intelligence)

---

### The Problem (Corporate Scale, Excel Won't Work)

**Before A1 Ecosystem:**
- Hired junior analyst (Rp 8M/month) to make weekly reports
- Analyst spent 12 hours/week manually consolidating 5 Excel files
- **Pain Point:** "Report datang tiap Senin sore. Data udah 3 hari telat. Gue nggak bisa make decision real-time. Plus, kalau analyst sakit/resign, gue stuck."

**Hidden Cost:**
- Analyst salary: Rp 96M/year
- Late insights: Missed Rp 15M/month opportunities (slow response to trends)
- Knowledge risk: Analyst quit -> 2 weeks to train replacement

**Quote:**
> "Board meeting tiap bulan. Investor minta professional report. Excel print-out keliatan amatir. Power BI template gue pernah coba, tapi terlalu basic. Gue butuh white-label, branded dashboard."

---

### The Solution (White-Label + Monthly Service)

**What Tier 3 Included:**
1. **White-label Excel templates** (Andi's Bakery logo, brand colors)
2. **Monthly data refresh service** (our team handles it)
3. **Quarterly business reviews** (strategic consulting)
4. **Priority WhatsApp support** (< 4-hour response)

**Implementation:**
- **Week 1:** Full white-glove setup
 - Our team visited all 5 outlets
 - Trained 5 managers on Excel template (30 min each)
 - Set up automated data pipeline (managers upload to Dropbox -> we import monthly)

- **Ongoing (Monthly):**
 - Managers fill Excel templates weekly (5 min/manager)
 - Our team refreshes dashboard monthly (client doesn't touch Power BI)
 - Email report sent to Pak Andi + Board (PDF export)

**Benefit:**
- Pak Andi fired junior analyst (saved Rp 8M/month)
- Redirected analyst budget -> optional support (see Product 02) service + Rp 7.6M/month savings

### The Results (Month 1-6)

**Month 1 - Analyst Replacement:**

**Month 2-3 - Strategic Insights:**

 - Decision: Replaced manager -> Revenue +25% in 2 months

**Month 4-6 - Expansion Planning:**

 - Quarterly review: "Replicate Outlet #1's playbook to Outlet #4 & #5"
 - Result: Outlet #4 revenue +18%, Outlet #5 revenue +22%

**Total Savings:**

## Key Patterns Across All Case Studies

### Why Ecosystem Bundle Works (vs Template-Only)

**Problem with Template-Only Products:**
1. 60% customers fail to set up (8-hour CSV format hell)
2. Staff can't use Power BI (only owner understands)
3. No sustainability (if owner leaves, dashboard dies)

**How Ecosystem Solves It:**
1. **Excel templates** -> Staff familiar, 2 min/day workflow
2. **Pre-formatted** -> Zero CSV confusion, 30-min setup
3. **Sustainable** -> Staff owns data entry, owner owns insights
