## Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) vs Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)

**Definitions:**
- **MRR:** Monthly recurring revenue (current month's subscription revenue)
- **ARR:** Annual recurring revenue (MRR × 12, forward-looking)

**End of Year 3:**
- **MRR:** Rp 45M (100 customers × Rp 450K avg)
- **ARR:** Rp 540M (Rp 45M × 12)

**This means:**
- By end of Year 3, Product 01 has Rp 540M ARR run-rate (if all customers renew)
- This is 67.5% of total Year 3 revenue (Rp 800M includes one-time sales too)
- Company is transitioning from "one-time sales" to "recurring revenue" business model
