## Methodology & Frameworks

### Seven-Framework Analysis Approach

This analysis synthesizes insights from seven complementary pricing frameworks, ensuring 360° validation:

#### 1. Value-Based Pricing
**Formula:** Customer Value = (Cost of Problem × Frequency) - (Cost of Solution × Effort)

**Applied to each tier:**
- Quantify time savings (hours × hourly rate)
- Calculate business improvements (food cost reduction, revenue recovery, efficiency gains)
- Compare value delivered vs price charged
- Result: Value-to-Price ratios of 16-80×

#### 2. Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)
**Framework:** Functional + Emotional + Social jobs customer hires product to do

**Key jobs identified:**
- **Functional:** "Reduce Excel time from 4 hrs/week ? 30 min/week"
- **Emotional:** "Feel like professional operator, not amateur with messy Excel"
- **Social:** "Impress investors with professional dashboards (not Excel)"

**Competing alternatives analyzed** for each tier (Do Nothing, DIY, Freelancer, Consultant)

#### 3. Customer Segmentation (Multi-Dimensional)
**Dimensions:**
- **Firmographic:** Revenue, outlets, staff, industry
- **Behavioral:** Tech adoption curve, decision style, delegation willingness
- **Needs-Based:** Primary pain point to solve
- **Psychographic:** Motivation, fears, identity
- **Value-Based:** Willingness and ability to pay

**Result:** Detailed Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs) for each tier

#### 4. Competitive Alternatives Matrix
**Forces analysis:** For each tier, what competing solutions does customer consider?

**Evaluated on:**
- Cost (one-time + ongoing)
- Time/effort required
- Success rate
- When customer chooses that alternative

**Result:** Clear competitive positioning for each tier

#### 5-7. Advanced Frameworks (Risk, Profit, Experiments)
- **Profit:** Validated 77-81% margins (Appendix G)
- **Risk:** Stress-tested against 30% shocks (Appendix I)
- **Experiments:** Roadmap for A/B testing (Appendix K)

#### 5. Profit & Margin Analysis (Development-Centric)
**Focus:** Ensuring unit economics work at scale.
- **Analysis:** Delivery cost modeling per tier.
- **Result:** Validated 77-81% margins. (See Appendix G)

#### 6. Risk & Sensitivity Analysis
**Focus:** Stress-testing assumptions.
- **Scenarios:** Price wars, success rate drops, adoption failure.
- **Result:** Pricing resilient to 30% shocks. (See Appendix I)

#### 7. Pricing Experimentation
**Focus:** Growth and optimization.
- **Plan:** A/B testing price points and bundling.
- **Result:** Roadmap for Phase 1-2 optimization. (See Appendix K)
