## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

- Company name, business type

- Staff roster (names, IDs, roles—for dashboard configuration)

**During Support:**
- Communications via email/WhatsApp
- Screenshots or files you send for troubleshooting
- Feedback and survey responses

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

**Product Usage Data (Tier 3 Only):**
- Dashboard access logs (login frequency, features used)
- Performance metrics (refresh times, errors)
- Power BI Service analytics (if using cloud deployment)

**Website & Communication:**
- IP address, browser type, device information (via website analytics)
- Email open/click rates (if you receive marketing emails)

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

**We do NOT collect:**
- Your customer's personally identifiable information (PII) from your POS system
- Employee salary or banking details
- Credit card numbers (Midtrans handles payments)
- Social media account data (unless you voluntarily share)

### 2.4 Ecosystem Bundle Data Handling (NEW)

**Excel Templates & Google Sheets:**
- Your data entered in Excel/Sheets stays on YOUR systems (we don't collect it)
- Templates are static files - no data transmitted to us
- Google Sheets subject to Google's privacy policy (if you use Sheets version)

**Data You Share with Us (Tier 2/3 only):**
- POS export samples (for Excel customization) - deleted within 30 days after delivery
- Usage logs (if you opt-in for support troubleshooting)
- Anonymous benchmarking data (aggregated, non-identifiable)

**Video Tutorials:**
- Hosted on YouTube (unlisted links)
- YouTube analytics (view counts) - cannot identify individual viewers
- No tracking cookies in videos themselves
