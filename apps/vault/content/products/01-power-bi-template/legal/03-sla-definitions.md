## SLA Measurement & Reporting

### Response Time Calculation

**Start:** When customer submits ticket via email/WhatsApp (timestamp)
**End:** When support team sends first substantive response (not auto-reply)

**Excludes:**
- Non-business hours (after 6 PM, before 9 AM)
- Weekends (Saturday after 1 PM, Sunday all day)
- Indonesian public holidays

**Example:**
- Ticket submitted: Friday 5 PM
- First response: Monday 10 AM
- **Calculated Response Time:** 1 hour (Friday 5-6 PM) + 1 hour (Monday 9-10 AM) = 2 hours (within 24h SLA for Tier 3)

---

### Resolution Time Calculation

**Start:** When customer submits ticket
**End:** When issue is fully resolved and customer confirms

**Resolution Definitions:**
- **Resolved:** Issue fixed, customer confirms dashboard working
- **Workaround Provided:** Temporary solution given while root cause investigated
- **Closed - Cannot Reproduce:** Unable to replicate issue, customer confirms not happening anymore

---

## Escalation Process

### Level 1: Support Team
- **Who:** Customer Success Specialist
- **Handles:** P3-P4 issues, standard support questions
- **SLA:** As defined above per tier

### Level 2: Technical Lead
- **When:** P1-P2 issues OR P3-P4 unresolved after 7 days
- **Who:** Senior Technical Specialist
- **SLA:** 50% faster resolution than L1 (e.g., P2 from 48h -> 24h)

### Level 3: Product Owner
- **When:** P1 issue unresolved after 48h OR customer escalation request
- **Who:** Product Lead / CTO
- **SLA:** Immediate response (<4 hours), resolution commitment provided within 24h

**How to Escalate (Tier 2/3 Customers):**
- Reply to support ticket with "ESCALATE REQUEST" in subject
- WhatsApp (Tier 3): Message "URGENT ESCALATION" to priority line
- Auto-escalation: All P1 issues auto-escalate to L2 after 24h if unresolved

## Appendix: Severity Level Guidelines

**How to Classify Your Issue:**

### P1 - Critical (Business-Impacting)
**Definition:** Dashboard completely unusable, preventing daily business decisions

**Examples:**
- Power BI file won't open (corrupted)
- All metrics showing Rp 0 (total data loss)
- Dashboard needed for board meeting in 24 hours and it's broken

**NOT P1:**
- One chart showing blank (other metrics work) -> P2
- Cosmetic issue (colors wrong) -> P3

---

### P2 - High (Major Feature Broken)
**Definition:** Significant functionality impaired, but workaround exists

**Examples:**
- Data won't refresh (can't get today's numbers)
- Staff leaderboard showing all blanks
- Date slicer not working (can't filter by month)

---

### P3 - Medium (Minor Issue)
**Definition:** Small bug or inconvenience, doesn't block daily use

**Examples:**
- Chart labels overlapping (data still visible)
- Export to PDF not working (can screenshot instead)
- Visual formatting off (not aligned properly)

---

### P4 - Low (Question or Enhancement)
**Definition:** How-to question or feature request (not broken)

**Examples:**
- "How do I add a new metric?"
- "Can you change the dashboard theme color?"
- "What does this metric mean?"
