## Comprehensive Product Analysis (January 4, 2026)

### 1. PRODUCT DEFINITION & CORE OFFERING

**What R1 Is:**
- Monthly subscription service (Rp 75K/month or Rp 750K/year)
- Provides monthly Power BI template updates, priority email/WhatsApp support (<24hr), early access to Lite Dashboard beta, learning resources & industry benchmarks
- **Pure upsell to A1 Power BI Template buyers** (not standalone product)
- Strategic role: Pillar 2 of 5-Pillar Ecosystem (Retention → Recurring Revenue)

**Pricing Model:**
- Monthly: Rp 75K/month (cancel anytime with 7-day notice)
- Annual: Rp 750K/year (17% discount, 2 months free, pro-rated refund <6 months)
- Tax: +11% PPN
- Future Premium: R2 (Rp 175K/month), R3 (Rp 500K/month) with live calls + custom work

**What's Included:**
- Monthly template updates (2-3 new metrics, bug fixes, compatibility updates)
- Priority support (email <24hr weekday, WhatsApp <4hr urgent)
- Early access benefits (beta features, feature voting)
- Learning resources (monthly tips, tutorials, benchmarks)

**What's NOT Included:**
- Custom template development, live 1-on-1 training, data entry services, Power BI Pro license

- Active subscribers: 15+ target

- Churn: <20% sustained

### 10. STRATEGIC INSIGHTS

1. **Zero cold acquisition cost** - Warm upsell to A1 base (no ads, no outreach)
2. **13.5:1 incremental LTV/CAC** - Much better than A1's 8.5:1
3. **Recurring revenue** - Sticky, predictable, compounds over time
4. **Low support load** - <5 hours/week at 30 subscribers (sustainable solo)
5. **Customer lock-in** - Monthly updates create dependency, switching cost
6. **Upgrade bridge** - Path to E1 Lite → C1/C2 SaaS (long-term LTV expansion)

**Critical Success Factors:**
1. A1 must hit 150+ cumulative buyers (TAM requirement) - Depends on A1 success
2. Maintain 30%+ conversion rate (SAM requirement) - Messaging, pricing, timing
3. Keep churn <20% monthly (SOM requirement) - Update quality, support responsiveness
4. Enforce gate criteria rigorously (no false passes) - Data-driven decisions
5. Focus on retention > acquisition (highest ROI lever) - Prevent churn, not just add subs

**Biggest Risk:**
- **A1 dependency** - If A1 fails (doesn't hit 150 buyers), R1's entire TAM collapses
- Mitigation: Ensure A1 product-market fit FIRST before investing in R1 scale
