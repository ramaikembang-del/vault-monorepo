## Conservative Scenario (Baseline - Main Documentation)

**Use Case:** Official financial planning, risk-mitigated projections

### Assumptions

**Subscriber Base:**
- Year 1: 45 subscribers (30% of 150 A1 buyers)
- Year 2: 120 subscribers (30% of 400 A1 buyers)
- Year 3: 195 subscribers (30% of 650 A1 buyers)

**Pricing:**
- Rp 75K/month flat rate
- No tiering in Year 1-3

**Conversion Funnel:**
- Month 0: A1 purchase
- Month 1-2: Welcome email sequence
- Month 3-4: Support nurture emails
- Month 5-6: R1 upsell offer (first month free)
- **30% convert by Month 6**

**Churn:**
- 20% monthly churn (18-month avg lifetime)
- LTV: Rp 75K × 18 = Rp 1.35M

**Key Constraints:**
- Opt-in model (requires customer to request trial)
- Conservative 30% conversion (below industry norm 40-60%)

### Revenue Breakdown

| Year | Subscribers | MRR | ARR | Growth |
|------|------------|-----|-----|--------|
| Year 1 | 45 | Rp 3.375M | Rp 40.5M | - |
| Year 2 | 120 | Rp 9M | Rp 108M | +167% |
| Year 3 | 195 | Rp 14.625M | Rp 175.5M | +63% |

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC (Upsell): Rp 100K
- LTV: Rp 1.35M
- **LTV/CAC: 13.5:1** ← Highest of all products

## Realistic Scenario (Team of 3 Executing Well)

**Use Case:** Aggressive but achievable targets, internal team OKRs

### What Changes from Conservative

**1. Auto-Enrollment Strategy**
- All A1 buyers auto-enrolled in 1-month free trial (opt-out vs opt-in)
- 100% try R1 vs 50% opt-in rate
- **Conversion: 50% of trial users convert** (vs 30% opt-in)

**2. Larger A1 Base**
- Year 1: 350 A1 buyers (vs 150 conservative)
- 50% conversion = 175 R1 subscribers

**3. Better Onboarding**
- Reduces early churn: 18 → 24 month lifetime
- LTV: Rp 75K × 24 = Rp 1.8M (vs Rp 1.35M)

**4. Same Pricing**
- Still Rp 75K/month (not testing higher pricing yet)
- Focus on conversion optimization first

### Revenue Breakdown

| Year | Subscribers | MRR | ARR | vs Conservative |
|------|------------|-----|-----|-----------------|
| Year 1 | 175 | Rp 13.125M | Rp 157.5M | +Rp 117M (+289%) |
| Year 2 | 450 | Rp 33.75M | Rp 405M | +Rp 297M (+275%) |
| Year 3 | 650 | Rp 48.75M | Rp 585M | +Rp 409.5M (+233%) |

### Why 50% Conversion is Realistic

**Industry Benchmarks:**
- SaaS upsells to existing customers: 40-60% typical
- Every A1 buyer already paid Rp 2M (proven willingness)
- R1 value: Rp 500K-1M per consultant question, we answer unlimited @ Rp 75K/mo
- **Clear ROI makes 50% achievable**

### Team Requirements

**Can a team of 3 support 175 subscribers?** **YES**

**Support Load:**
- 175 subscribers × 5 questions/month = 875 questions/month
- 875 / 22 work days = 40 questions/day
- 15 minutes per question = 10 hours/day

**Solution:** 1 full-time CS person + chatbot for common questions (50% deflection)

## Aggressive Scenario (Everything Goes Right)

**Use Case:** Investor TAM potential, extreme upside case

### What Changes from Realistic

**1. Premium Pricing**
- Test Rp 150K/month (vs Rp 75K)
- **Still 5-17× cheaper than consultant questions** (Rp 500K-1M each)
- Value prop: Customer gets Rp 2.5M-10M value/month, pays Rp 1.8M/year = 81-94% discount

**2. Higher Conversion**
- 60% of A1 buyers convert (vs 50%)
- Auto-enrollment + bundle Tier 2/3 A1 with 3 months R1 free
- **Locks in before trial ends**

**3. Larger A1 Base**
- Year 1: 500 A1 buyers (aggressive A1 scenario)
- 60% conversion = 300 R1 subscribers

**4. Longer Lifetime**
- 30-month avg lifetime (better retention via premium tier features)
- LTV: Rp 150K × 30 = Rp 4.5M (vs Rp 1.35M conservative)

### Revenue Breakdown

| Year | Subscribers | MRR | ARR | vs Conservative |
|------|------------|-----|-----|-----------------|
| Year 1 | 300 | Rp 45M | Rp 540M | +Rp 499.5M (+1,233%) |
| Year 2 | 900 | Rp 135M | Rp 1,620M | +Rp 1,512M (+1,400%) |
| Year 3 | 1,800 | Rp 270M | Rp 3,240M | +Rp 3,064.5M (+1,746%) |

### Unit Economics (Aggressive)

- CAC: Rp 100K (same, warm upsell)
- LTV: Rp 4.5M (vs Rp 1.35M conservative)
- **LTV/CAC: 45:1** ← Exceptional

### Team Requirements

**Can a team of 3 support 300 subscribers?** **NO**

**Support Load:**
- 300 subscribers × 5 questions/month = 1,500 questions/month
- Even with 50% chatbot deflection = 750 manual/month = 34/day
- **Need 2-3 CS people by Year 1, 5-8 by Year 3**

## Execution Levers Summary

### To Move from Conservative → Realistic (Month 1-3)

**Quick Wins:**
1. Auto-enroll all A1 buyers in 1-month free trial (vs opt-in)
2. WhatsApp bot for common questions (50% support deflection)
3. Improved onboarding: First week email + video tutorials

**Expected Impact:** +Rp 117M Year 1 (+289%), **13.5:1 incremental LTV/CAC**

**Why This is THE Priority:**
- Highest incremental LTV CAC of all products (13.5:1)
- No additional customer acquisition needed (warm A1 base)
- **Low-hanging fruit with massive ROI**

---

### To Move from Realistic → Aggressive (Month 6-12)

**Medium-Term Levers:**
1. Test Rp 150K/month pricing (still 5-17× cheaper than consultants)
2. Bundle Tier 2/3 A1 sales with 3 months R1 free (lock-in)
3. Premium tier: Rp 300K/month with <4hr SLA (vs 24hr)
4. Scale CS team: 2-3 people Year 1, 5-8 people Year 3

**Expected Impact:** +Rp 382.5M Year 1 additional (vs Realistic)

## Value Proposition Validation

### Why Customers Will Pay Rp 75K-150K/month

**Alternative 1: Free Community Support (Forums, Reddit)**
- Response time: 48-72 hours (vs our 24 hours)
- Quality: Generic Power BI advice (vs F&B-specific)
- **Our value: Guaranteed expert support**

**Alternative 2: Per-Incident Consultants**
- Cost: Rp 500K-1M per question
- R1 customers ask 5-10 questions/month = Rp 2.5M-10M value
- **At Rp 75K/month, customers save Rp 2.4M-9.9M/month** (97% discount)

**Alternative 3: In-House Power BI Expert**
- Salary: Rp 8-15M/month
- **R1 at Rp 75K/month = 99% cheaper** (100-200× cost savings)

**Conclusion:** Even at Rp 150K/month, clear ROI exists

---

## Strategic Insight: R1 is the Most Profitable Upsell

**Why R1 Matters More Than Revenue Numbers Suggest:**

1. **Highest Incremental LTV/CAC:** 13.5:1 (vs 8.5:1 A1, 6.5:1 E1)
2. **Zero cold acquisition cost:** All customers are warm A1 buyers
3. **Sticky recurring revenue:** 18-month lifetime, potential for 30+ months
4. **Customer lock-in:** Monthly updates keep template working = dependency

**Strategic Recommendation:**
- **Prioritize R1 conversion optimization over new A1 acquisition**
- Getting 30% → 50% conversion = +Rp 117M with near-zero cost
- Getting 50% → 60% conversion = +Rp 94M additional

**ROI Comparison:**
- Spending Rp 10M on R1 upsell automation: Returns Rp 135M+ (13.5× ROI)
- Spending Rp 10M on A1 ads: Returns Rp 85M (8.5× ROI)

**Winner: R1 gets priority budget allocation**
