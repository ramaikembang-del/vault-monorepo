# R1 Template Support - How to Get Support

**Your Subscription:** R1 Monthly Template Support

---

## What's Covered

 Template updates (monthly)
 Bug fixes
 Email support (<24 hour response)
 Monthly office hours
 Feature requests (prioritized)

---

## Support Channels

### 1. Email Support (Primary)

**Email:** support@pwbi.id
**Subject Line:** "R1 Support - [Brief Issue]"

**Response Time:** <24 hours (business days)

**Include in Your Email:**
- Template version (Help → About)
- Screenshot of issue
- What you've tried already
- Your data source type (CSV, POS, etc.)

**Example:**
```
Subject: R1 Support - Chart showing wrong dates

Hi, I'm using A1 template v2.3 and the sales chart
is showing dates from 2022 instead of current data.

I've tried refreshing (Home → Refresh) but the issue persists.

[Screenshot attached]

Thank you!
```

### 2. Monthly Office Hours

**When:** First Tuesday of every month, 2 PM WIB
**Duration:** 1 hour
**Format:** Group Zoom call
**Register:** Customer portal or calendar invite

**What to Bring:**
- Questions about template features
- Custom report requests
- Best practice questions
- Troubleshooting (we'll screenshare!)

**Topics Covered:**
- New features in latest update
- Q&A
- Tips & tricks
- Customer showcase

---

## Response Priority

**P0 - Critical:**
- Template completely broken
- Data loss issue
- Response: 4 hours

**P1 - High:**
- Core feature not working
- Blocking your work
- Response: 24 hours

**P2 - Medium:**
- Minor issue, workaround available
- Feature request
- Response: 48 hours

---

## Common Requests & How We Handle

**"Can you customize [X] for my business?"**

**Minor customization:** Yes! (free, part of R1)
- Color scheme changes
- Logo/branding
- Minor layout tweaks

**Major customization:** May require additional cost
- Entirely new report pages
- Complex custom calculations
- Integration with proprietary systems

We'll discuss scope and quote if needed.

**"I need this feature urgently!"**

1. Email us the feature request
2. We assess:
 - Benefits other customers? → Added to roadmap
 - Unique to you? → Custom work (may have cost)
3. You get timeline estimate

**"My template broke after the update"**

1. Revert to previous version (we keep backups)
2. Email us immediately
3. We investigate and fix
4. You get patched version <24 hours
