## Introduction

### Purpose of Video Tutorials

Video tutorials are a core component of R1 Template Support, providing **ongoing education and value** to subscribers beyond monthly template updates.

**Goals:**
- **Educate:** Help subscribers understand and use Power BI template features
- **Retain:** Demonstrate continuous value (reduce churn, justify Rp 75K/month)
- **Engage:** Build community, encourage feature adoption
- **Scale:** Reduce support load via self-service learning

### Tutorial Philosophy

**Subscriber-Centric:**
- Assume zero Power BI knowledge (explain everything in simple terms)
- Use Indonesian language (primary audience: Indonesian F&B owners)
- Focus on business outcomes (not technical features): "How to increase profit" not "How to use DAX formulas"

**Action-Oriented:**
- Every tutorial solves a real business problem
- Provide step-by-step instructions (viewers can follow along)
- Include timestamps for easy navigation

**Bite-Sized:**
- Target length: 5-8 minutes (attention span-friendly)
- One topic per video (not overwhelming)
- Playlist format (subscribers can watch series or pick topics)

## Production Guidelines

### Video Format

**Structure:**
1. **Intro (30 seconds):**
 - Hook: "Apakah Anda tahu kapan waktu tersibuk restoran Anda?" (grab attention)
 - Problem: "Tanpa data, Anda hanya menebak." (establish pain point)
 - Solution: "Di video ini, saya tunjukkan cara analisis peak hours untuk optimasi staf." (promise value)

2. **Main Content (4-6 minutes):**
 - Screen recording with voiceover
 - Step-by-step walkthrough
 - Highlight key insights ("Lihat, pukul 7-9 malam adalah peak hours!")

3. **Conclusion (30 seconds):**
 - Recap: "Jadi, dengan Peak Hours Analysis, Anda bisa..."
 - Call-to-action: "Download template terbaru di Google Drive. Link di deskripsi."
 - Teaser: "Bulan depan: Cara analisis tren musiman!"

**Technical Specs:**
- **Resolution:** 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Frame Rate:** 30 fps
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec)
- **Screen Recording Tool:** Loom, OBS Studio, or Camtasia
- **Audio:** Clear voiceover (use USB microphone, not laptop mic)
- **Subtitles:** Indonesian (auto-generated, then manually corrected)

### Voiceover Guidelines

**Tone:**
- Conversational, friendly (not robotic or overly formal)
- Enthusiastic (show passion for helping F&B owners succeed)
- Patient (speak slowly, repeat key points)

**Language:**
- Bahasa Indonesia (primary)
- Use English for Power BI terms ("Slicer", "Filter", "Refresh")
- Explain jargon: "Slicer (alat filter) di sebelah kiri..."

**Pacing:**
- Speak at 120-140 words per minute (moderate pace)
- Pause after key points (give viewers time to absorb)
- Use "signpost" phrases: "Pertama...", "Kedua...", "Terakhir..."

### Branding

**Intro Slide:**
- Logo: PWBI F&B Growth (top left)
- Title: "R1 Tutorial: [Topic]"
- Subtitle: "Bulan [Month] 2025"

**Outro Slide:**
- CTA: "Download template: [Google Drive link]"
- Support: "WhatsApp: +62 XXX-XXXX-XXXX"
- Social: "Follow @pwbi_fnb (Instagram/YouTube)"

**SCRIPT:**

**Voiceover:**
"Hai! Sudah berhasil import data POS ke Power BI? Bagus!

Tapi sekarang muncul pertanyaan: **'Angka-angka ini artinya apa, sih?'**

Di video ini, saya akan jelaskan **3 metrik terpenting** di dashboard:
1. Total Omzet (Revenue)
2. Nilai Transaksi Rata-rata (Average Transaction Value)
3. Margin Profit

Setelah nonton video ini, Anda akan bisa baca dashboard dan ambil keputusan bisnis yang lebih baik. Yuk!"

---

**[Main Content - 0:30 to 6:30]**

**Section 1: Total Revenue (Omzet Total)**

[Screen: Dashboard, highlight "Total Revenue" card]

**Voiceover:**
"Metrik pertama: **Total Omzet (Revenue)**.

Ini adalah total penjualan Anda dalam periode tertentu. Misalnya, Januari 2025: Rp 45,000,000.

**Cara baca:**
- Bandingkan dengan bulan lalu: Naik atau turun?
- Bandingkan dengan target: Misalnya, target Rp 50 juta, berarti kurang Rp 5 juta (10% di bawah target)

**Insight:**
- Jika omzet naik 20% bulan ini vs bulan lalu, artinya bisnis growing!
- Jika omzet turun, cek: Apakah karena jumlah pelanggan turun, atau nilai transaksi turun?

Mari kita cek metrik kedua."

---

**Section 2: Average Transaction Value**

[Screen: Dashboard, highlight "Avg Transaction Value" card]

**Voiceover:**
"Metrik kedua: **Nilai Transaksi Rata-rata (Average Transaction Value)**.

Ini adalah berapa rupiah rata-rata customer belanja per kunjungan.

Rumus: Total Omzet ÷ Jumlah Transaksi

Contoh:
- Total omzet: Rp 45 juta
- Jumlah transaksi: 3,000
- Avg transaction value: Rp 45,000,000 ÷ 3,000 = Rp 15,000 per transaksi

**Kenapa penting?**
- Kalau Anda mau naikkan omzet, ada 2 cara:
 1. Tambah jumlah pelanggan (traffic)
 2. Naikkan avg transaction value (upsell/cross-sell)

**Cara naikkan avg transaction value:**
- Promo paket combo (nasi goreng + es teh = Rp 25K, hemat Rp 5K)
- Training staf untuk suggestive selling ('Mau tambah gorengan, Pak?')
- Menu bundling (appetizer + main course + dessert)

Sekarang cek dashboard Anda: Berapa avg transaction value Anda? Bisa dinaikkan 10-20%?"

---

**Section 3: Profit Margin & Food Cost %**

[Screen: Dashboard, highlight "Profit Margin" and "Food Cost %" cards]

**Voiceover:**
"Metrik ketiga: **Profit Margin** dan **Food Cost %**.

**Profit Margin:**
- Rumus: (Omzet - Total Biaya) ÷ Omzet × 100%
- Contoh: Omzet Rp 45 juta, biaya Rp 30 juta → Profit margin = 33%

**Food Cost %:**
- Rumus: Biaya Bahan Baku ÷ Omzet × 100%
- Target industri F&B: 30-35%
- Jika food cost Anda 40%, artinya Anda overspending (review supplier atau portion control)

**Insight:**
- Jika profit margin < 20%, bisnis Anda unprofitable (harus cut costs atau naikkan harga)
- Jika food cost > 35%, cek:
 1. Apakah harga bahan naik? (beli dari supplier lain atau nego harga)
 2. Apakah porsi terlalu besar? (kurangi sedikit tanpa customer sadar)
 3. Apakah ada waste? (bahan kadaluarsa, salah masak)"

---

---

**[Main Content - 0:40 to 7:20]**

[Screen: Dashboard, "Food Cost Analysis" tab]

**Section 1: Food Cost Breakdown by Product**

**Voiceover:**
"Buka tab 'Food Cost Analysis' di dashboard.

Anda akan lihat tabel seperti ini:

| Produk | Omzet | Food Cost | Food Cost % |
|--------|-------|-----------|-------------|
| Nasi Goreng Spesial | Rp 5,000,000 | Rp 2,000,000 | 40% |
| Ayam Geprek | Rp 3,500,000 | Rp 1,200,000 | 34% |
| Es Teh Manis | Rp 1,000,000 | Rp 200,000 | 20% |

**Insight:**
- Nasi Goreng Spesial food cost 40% → **Terlalu tinggi!** (target 30-35%)
- Es Teh Manis food cost 20% → Sangat bagus! (margin tinggi)

**Action:**
- Fokus pada produk dengan food cost >35%
- Cek: Apakah harga bahan bisa diturunkan? Atau porsi bisa dikurangi sedikit?"

---

**Section 2: 5 Strategies to Reduce Food Cost**

**Voiceover:**
"**Strategi 1: Nego Harga Supplier**

Contoh:
- Beras: Beli dari supplier A (Rp 12,000/kg) → Cari supplier B (Rp 10,000/kg)
- Potensi saving: 2,000/kg × 100 kg/bulan = Rp 200,000/bulan

**Strategi 2: Portion Control**

- Kurangi porsi nasi 10% (dari 200g → 180g)
- Customer tidak akan complain (selisih 20g hampir tidak terasa)
- Saving: 10% food cost × Rp 2,000,000 = Rp 200,000/bulan

**Strategi 3: Reduce Waste**

- Cek: Berapa banyak bahan yang dibuang setiap hari? (sayuran layu, ayam kadaluarsa)
- Solusi: Beli sesuai kebutuhan (daily purchase vs weekly bulk)
- Implement FIFO (First In, First Out) untuk stock rotation

**Strategi 4: Menu Engineering**

- Promosikan produk dengan margin tinggi (Es Teh Manis 20% food cost → gross margin 80%!)
- De-emphasize produk dengan margin rendah (Nasi Goreng Spesial 40% → margin 60%)

**Strategi 5: Recipe Standardization**

- Standarisasi resep (1 porsi ayam geprek = 150g ayam, 50g sambal, 100g nasi)
- Training staf untuk ikuti resep (tidak asal tuang sambal atau nasi)
- Konsistensi = predictable cost"

---

**[Conclusion - 7:20 to 8:00]**

**Voiceover:**
"Jadi, 5 strategi turunkan food cost:
1. Nego supplier
2. Portion control
3. Reduce waste
4. Menu engineering
5. Recipe standardization

Coba 1-2 strategi bulan ini, dan lihat food cost Anda turun 5-10%!

Bulan depan: **Cara kenali pelanggan setia vs pelanggan sekali datang**. Sampai jumpa!"
