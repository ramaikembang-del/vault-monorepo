##How to Get Support

### Email Support
**Email:** support@pwbi.id
**Subject:** "R1 Support - [Your Issue]"
**Response time:** <24 hours (business days)

**Include:**
- Your template version
- Screenshot of issue
- What you've tried

### Monthly Office Hours
**When:** First Tuesday of month, 2 PM WIB
**Duration:** 1 hour
**Format:** Zoom (link in customer portal)

**Bring questions about:**
- Template customization
- Data issues
- Advanced features
- Best practices

---

## Getting Updates

**How Updates Work:**
1. We release new template version monthly
2. You receive email notification
3. Download from customer portal
4. Apply to your file (see below)

**Applying Updates:**
1. Save your current file as backup
2. Download new template
3. Reconnect your data source
4. Visuals/calculations auto-update
5. Verify data looks correct

**Note:** Your data isn't affected, only template structure updates

---

## Common Requests

**"Can you customize the template for my business?"**
- Minor tweaks: Yes (included in R1)
- Major custom development: Contact sales for quote

**"I need a specific report"**
- Email request with details
- If benefits multiple customers, we'll add it
- If custom to you, may require additional fee

**"My template broke after update"**
- Restore from backup
- Email us immediately
- We'll help troubleshoot and fix

---

## Cancellation

**Month-to-month:** Cancel anytime, 30 days notice
**After cancellation:** Keep current template, no more updates/support
