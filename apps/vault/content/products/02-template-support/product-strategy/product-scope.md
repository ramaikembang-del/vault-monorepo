# Product Scope - Template Support Subscription

**Product:** R1 - Template Support & Subscription
**Scope Type:** Recurring Revenue Attachment Product
**Phase:** Pillar 2 (Retention)

---

## What's Included in Subscription (Rp 75K/month)

### 1. Monthly Template Updates
- New metrics added (2-3 per month based on user feedback)
- Bug fixes & performance improvements
- POS compatibility updates (when formats change)
- Visual enhancements (better charts, modern themes)
- Delivered: First Monday of every month via Google Drive

### 2. Priority Email/WhatsApp Support
- **Q&A:** How to use template features
- **Troubleshooting:** Data not loading, refresh errors, calculation issues
- **Best practices:** How to analyze metrics, interpret dashboards
- **Response SLA:** <24 hours weekdays, <48 hours weekends
- **Channel:** Email primary, WhatsApp for urgent issues

### 3. Early Access Benefits
- Beta access to E1 Lite Dashboard (when ready, Week 6)
- New template features before public release
- Feature voting rights ("What should we build next?")
- Exclusive webinars (monthly Q&A sessions)

### 4. Learning Resources
- Monthly tips email ("How to use X metric to improve Y")
- Video tutorials (5-10 minutes each, 1-2 per month)
- Industry benchmarks ("Your 28% food cost vs. 32% average")
- Case studies from other subscribers (anonymized)

---

## What's NOT Included

**[x] Custom Template Development:**
- We don't build custom dashboards (not consulting)
- Template modifications must be reasonable additions
- Major customization = use Tier 3 Fully Managed

**[x] Live Training Sessions:**
- No 1-on-1 coaching or workshops
- Self-serve learning (videos, docs, email Q&A)

**[x] Data Entry Services:**
- Users import own CSV from POS
- We don't clean/prepare your data

**[x] Power BI Pro License:**
- Power BI Desktop is free
- We don't provide licensing

**[x] Accounting/Tax Advice:**
- We provide analytics, not financial consulting
- Refer to accountant for tax questions

## Subscription Limits

**Users per Subscription:**
- 1 business = 1 subscription
- Multiple outlets under same business OK
- Reselling support not allowed

**Support Request Limits:**
- Reasonable use = ~2-3 tickets/month
- If >10 tickets/month  ->  Suggest Tier 3 Fully Managed

**Update Frequency:**
- Major updates: Monthly
- Critical bug fixes: Immediate (as needed)
- Feature requests: Quarterly priority review

---

## Cancellation Policy

**Anytime Cancellation:**
- No contracts, no commitment
- Cancel anytime before next billing cycle
- Pro-rated refunds: NO (monthly billing)

**What Happens on Cancel:**
- Keep current template version (yours forever)
- Lose access to future updates
- Support ends immediately
- Can re-subscribe anytime (no penalty)

**Re-Subscription:**
- Same price (no "win-back" discount)
- Access to all updates released since cancel

## Upgrade Path

### R1  ->  Full SaaS Platform

**When Subscriber Outgrows:**
1. **Opens 2nd-3rd outlet**  ->  Need multi-location, upgrade to Full SaaS Pro (C2)
2. **Wants real-time data**  ->  Template is daily refresh, upgrade to Full SaaS Basic (C1)
3. **Team collaboration needs**  ->  Upgrade to Full SaaS Pro (C2)

**Migration Support:**
- We help export historical data from template
- Onboarding to Full SaaS included
- Can cancel R1 when migrated (no overlap needed)

## Pricing Strategy

### Why Rp 75K/month?

**Cost Breakdown:**
- Template development time: Rp 10K/month (amortized)
- Support time (2 hours/month avg): Rp 40K (at Rp 20K/hour)
- Updates (2 hours/month dev): Rp 20K
- Infrastructure (Google Drive, email): Rp 5K
- **Total Cost:** Rp 75K/month
- **Margin:** 0% (break-even at 1 subscriber)

**Profitability:**
- 10+ subscribers = Rp 750K MRR (profitable at scale)
- 17 active subscribers (Year 1 realistic after churn) = Rp 1.28M MRR
- 30 gross subscribers (before churn) = Rp 2.25M theoretical MRR

**Competitive Positioning:**
- vs. Hiring analyst: Rp 5M+/month (R1 is 98% cheaper)
- vs. Consultant per-issue: Rp 500K-1M (R1 is 93% cheaper for 2+ issues)
