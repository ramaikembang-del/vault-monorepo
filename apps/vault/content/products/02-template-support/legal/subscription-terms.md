To cancel your subscription:

**Notice Requirement:** Cancellation requests must be submitted at least 7 days before next billing date to avoid being charged for the next cycle.

**Confirmation:** You will receive a cancellation confirmation email within 24 hours.

### 5.3 Refund Policy

**Monthly Plan:**
- No refunds for partial months (subscription is non-refundable)
- You retain access until end of paid period after cancellation

**Annual Plan:**
- Pro-rated refund available if cancelled within first 6 months
- Pro-rated amount = (Remaining months / 12) × Rp 750,000
- No refunds if cancelled after 6 months
- Refund processed within 14 business days to original payment method

**Example (Annual Plan Refund):**
- Purchased January 1, 2025 for Rp 750,000
- Cancelled April 1, 2025 (3 months used, 9 months remaining)
- Refund: (9/12) × Rp 750,000 = Rp 562,500

**Exceptions (Full Refund):**
- Service failure: If we fail to deliver Service for >30 consecutive days due to our fault
- Misrepresentation: If Service materially differs from description at time of purchase
- Must request refund within 60 days of issue occurrence

### 5.4 Pausing Subscription

We do not currently offer subscription pausing. You must cancel and re-subscribe if you need to pause Service.

**Re-subscription:** You may re-subscribe at any time at current pricing (grandfathered pricing from first subscription may not apply).
