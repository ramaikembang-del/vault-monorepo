# Option 3: Smart Template with AI Insights Layer
## Automated Business Advisor Built Into Your Dashboard

**Product Name:** R1 Smart Template
**Tagline:** "Your template doesn't just show data - it tells you what to do"
**Core Innovation:** AI-powered insights layer that generates personalized recommendations automatically
**Pricing:** Rp 150K-500K/month (depending on features)

---

## Overview

**What is it?**
An enhanced Power BI template with an AI-powered "Insights" tab that automatically analyzes your data and generates actionable recommendations. Think: **Shopify Compass** or **HubSpot Marketing Grader** but for restaurant analytics.

**Why it works:**
- **Fully automated** (no human labor = infinite scale)
- **Always-on advisor** (24/7 insights, not waiting for analyst)
- **Personalized to YOUR data** (not generic advice)
- **Improves over time** (learns from outcomes)

**Inspiration:**
- **Shopify Compass** - AI business advisor for merchants
- **Gong.io** - AI insights on sales calls
- **Grammarly** - AI writing coach (real-time feedback)
- **Tesla Autopilot** - Proactive alerts + recommendations

---

## Core Components

### 1. AI Insights Tab (in Power BI Template)
- Auto-generated weekly insights
- "Top 3 opportunities this week"
- Anomaly alerts with root cause analysis
- Predictive warnings ("cash flow risk in 30 days")

### 2. Smart Benchmarking
- Real-time comparison to similar restaurants
- "You're in top 25% for labor cost, bottom 40% for revenue per seat"
- Drill down: "What do top performers do differently?"

### 3. Action Tracking System
- Checkbox: "Mark as implemented"
- Before/after tracking: "This recommendation improved profit 8%"
- ROI dashboard: "Recommendations followed = Rp 2.4M gained"

### 4. Predictive Analytics
- Cash flow projections (60-day forecast)
- Seasonal trend predictions
- Inventory optimization alerts
- Menu engineering (which dishes to kill/promote)

### 5. Natural Language Query
- Ask questions in plain English: "Why did sales drop last week?"
- Get AI-generated answer with data

## Value Proposition

**For FnB owners who want to make data-driven decisions but don't have time to analyze, R1 Smart Template automatically identifies opportunities and tells you exactly what to do - no analyst needed.**

**Key Benefits:**
- **Instant insights** (no waiting for human analyst)
- **Always learning** (AI improves as you use it)
- **Scalable** (works for 1 or 100 locations)
- **Transparent** (explains WHY each recommendation)
- **Affordable** (Rp 150-300K vs. Rp 10M analyst)

## Business Model

**Revenue:**
- Rp 150K/month (AI Insights only)
- Rp 300K/month (AI + Predictive Analytics)

**Unit Economics:**
- Fixed cost: GPT-4 API (Rp 20K per customer per month)
- Fixed cost: Infrastructure (Rp 10K per customer per month)

- **Breakeven:** 167 customers (at Rp 300K ARPU)
- **Gross Margin at scale:** 85-90% (software economics)
