## Product-Specific Questions

**Q: What exactly is Template Support?**
A: Monthly subscription for A1 template buyers that includes:
- Monthly template updates (new features, bug fixes)
- Priority support (24-hour response vs 48-hour)
- 1-on-1 onboarding help
- Custom modification assistance (1 hour/month)

**Q: Do I need to buy A1 first?**
A: Yes. R1 is an add-on to A1 templates.

**Q: What are "monthly updates"?**
A: New dashboard pages, bug fixes, Power BI version compatibility updates, new POS connectors.

**Q: How do I get updates?**
A: Email sent monthly with download link for latest template version. Replace old .pbix file with new one.

**Q: Will updates erase my customizations?**
A: No, but you'll need to reapply customizations to new version. We provide migration guide.

**Q: What's "custom modification assistance"?**
A: 1 hour/month of help customizing template (adding specific calculations, changing layouts, etc.). Requested via email.

**Q: Can I cancel R1 and keep A1?**
A: Yes. You keep the A1 template forever. No more updates, back to standard support tier.

**Q: Is there a discount for annual R1?**
A: Yes, 15% off (Rp 1.275M/year instead of Rp 1.5M).
