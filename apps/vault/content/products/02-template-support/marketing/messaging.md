# Product Messaging - Template Support Subscription

**Product:** R1 - Template Support & Subscription
**Positioning:** "Never Outdated Analytics"
**Target:** A1 Template Buyers (all tiers)

---

## Value Proposition

### Primary Message
**"Your Dashboard, Always Current - Rp 75K/Month"**

**For:** Power BI template buyers
**Who:** Want their dashboard to stay relevant as business evolves
**Our Product:** Monthly template updates + priority support (Rp 75K/month)
**Unlike:** One-time purchase (becomes outdated in 6 months)
**We Offer:** Peace of mind, always-current metrics, expert help when stuck

---

## Core Messaging Themes

### Theme 1: Obsolescence Anxiety

**Problem:** "Your template is great today. What about 6 months from now?"

**Pain Points:**
- POS systems update their CSV format → Template breaks
- New metrics get discovered → You miss out
- Bugs found → You're stuck with broken version
- Industry benchmarks change → Your targets become irrelevant

**Our Solution:**
- Monthly updates keep template working (POS format changes)
- New metrics added based on user feedback
- Bug fixes delivered automatically
- Benchmarks refreshed quarterly

**Messaging:**
> "Don't let a Rp 2M investment become worthless in 6 months. Rp 75K/month keeps it relevant forever."

---

### Theme 2: Support = Time Savings

**Problem:** "I'm stuck. Do I hire consultant (Rp 1M) or struggle alone?"

**Pain Points:**
- Template won't open → Hours wasted troubleshooting
- Data won't refresh → Revenue insights delayed
- Don't know how to interpret metrics → Dashboard unused
- CSV format changed → Manual re-mapping hell

**Our Solution:**
- <24h email support (weekdays)
- WhatsApp for urgent issues (<4h response)
- Screen-share troubleshooting (complex issues)
- Monthly tips email (how to use metrics better)

**Messaging:**
> "Rp 75K buys you 2-3 hours of expert help per month. One stuck issue costs you more in lost time."

---

### Theme 3: Early Access Insider

**Problem:** "I want to stay ahead of competitors"

**Benefits:**
- Beta access to E1 Lite Dashboard (Week 6)
- New template features before public release
- Feature voting rights (build what YOU need)
- Monthly webinars with other F&B operators

**Messaging:**
> "Be in the inner circle. Get new features first, influence what we build next."

## Messaging by Customer Tier

### Tier 1 (DIY) Buyers - Budget-Conscious

**Their Mindset:** "I bought DIY to save money. Why pay monthly?"

**Objection:** "I can't afford Rp 75K/month"

**Counter-Messaging:**
- **ROI Angle:** "You saved Rp 3-5M buying Tier 1. Rp 75K = 2.5% of that savings per month"
- **Time Value:** "One hour fixing broken CSV = Rp 100K opportunity cost. Support saves 2-3 hours/month"
- **Comparison:** "Consultant charges Rp 500K per issue. We're Rp 75K for unlimited questions"

**Headline for Tier 1:**
> "You Saved Money on Setup. Don't Lose It on Broken Updates."

---

### Tier 2 (Assisted) Buyers - Time-Conscious

**Their Mindset:** "I paid for setup help. Ongoing support makes sense."

**Sweet Spot:** Highest attach rate (40-50% expected)

**Messaging:**
- **Consistency:** "You valued our expertise during setup. Keep that support going for Rp 75K/month"
- **Investment Protection:** "You paid Rp 5M for setup. Rp 75K keeps that investment current"
- **Upgrade Path:** "Need more? Tier 3 Fully Managed is Rp 300K/month with consulting calls"

**Headline for Tier 2:**
> "Extend Your Setup Support. Stay Current Forever."

---

### Tier 3 (Managed) Buyers - Strategic Partners

**Their Mindset:** "I pay Rp 300-500K/month for Tier 3. Is R1 redundant?"

**Reality:** Tier 3 already includes updates + support

**Messaging:**
- **Clarification:** "Template Support is already included in Tier 3 Fully Managed"
- **No Additional Cost:** "You don't need R1 - you already have it!"
- **Upsell (if applicable):** "Want more? Add R2 Accounting Module (Rp 50-100K) or upgrade to Full SaaS"

**Note:** Do NOT offer R1 to Tier 3 buyers (cannibalization)

## Objection Handling Library

### Objection 1: "I don't need updates"

**Response:**
> "I thought that too until my POS (Moka) changed their CSV format last month. 8 customers couldn't refresh their dashboards.
>
> Updates aren't just 'nice to have' - they're survival when systems change under you."

**Follow-Up:**
> "Even if POS stays the same, we add 2-3 new metrics every month based on user feedback. Miss those = miss insights competitors have."

---

### Objection 2: "Rp 75K is too expensive"

**Response:**
> "Let's do the math:
> - Template cost: Rp 2-3M one-time
> - Support: Rp 75K/month
> - Year 1 total: Rp 2.9M
>
> Compare to:
> - BI consultant: Rp 50M year 1 (build) + Rp 5M/month (maintain) = Rp 110M
> - Excel (free): Your time = 10 hours/week × Rp 20K/hour = Rp 800K/month
>
> We're 97% cheaper than consultant, 90% cheaper than DIY Excel."

---

### Objection 3: "I'll just re-buy when I need updates"

**Response:**
> "That actually costs MORE:
> - R1 subscription: Rp 75K/month × 12 = Rp 900K/year
> - Major template update: Rp 500K each (we release 3-4/year)
> - Plus: No support between updates
>
> Subscription saves Rp 1.1M+ per year vs. buying updates individually."

---

### Objection 4: "I don't use the template enough yet"

**Response:**
> "That's exactly WHY you need support!
>
> Most non-users are stuck on:
> 1. CSV import (10-min fix via email)
> 2. Don't know which metrics matter (15-min call)
> 3. Too busy (monthly tips keep you engaged)
>
> Subscribe now = we'll unblock you. Otherwise that Rp 2M template sits unused."

**Offer:** "First month free - try support, see if we get you unstuck"

---

### Objection 5: "What if I cancel?"

**Response:**
> "Super easy:
> - Cancel link in every email
> - No retention calls, no hassle
> - Keep template forever (you own it)
> - Re-subscribe anytime (same Rp 75K price)
>
> No lock-in. Month-to-month. You're in control."

## Brand Voice & Tone

**Voice:** Pragmatic partner, not salesy vendor

**Characteristics:**
- **Honest:** "This isn't for everyone. If you never use the template, don't subscribe."
- **Helpful:** "Let me help you decide: [pros/cons]"
- **No Pressure:** "Your template works without subscription. But here's why most people subscribe..."
- **ROI-Focused:** Always show math (Rp 75K vs alternatives)

**Avoid:**
- "Limited time only!" (fake urgency)
- "You NEED this" (pushy)
- "Everyone's doing it" (peer pressure)

**Use:**
- "Here's the math..."
- "80% of buyers subscribe because..."
- "Try it free, cancel anytime"
