## NOTES

**Additional Staff Metrics (165-191):**
These 27 metrics represent the expanded drill-through capabilities mentioned in section 2.2 "Individual Staff Metrics" which states "8 detailed metrics per staff member" in the original notes, but when fully expanded for comprehensive staff performance tracking, includes:
- Extended sales tracking (MTD/YTD breakdowns)
- Shift-level performance
- Qualitative scores
- Development tracking
- Behavioral metrics

**Metric Numbering Logic:**
- Core metrics numbered sequentially by section
- Related sub-metrics grouped together
- Achievement milestones (152-157) and tier badges (158-164) placed after core staff metrics
- Patronage funnel (148-151) placed at start of customer section for logical flow
- Extended staff metrics (165-191) complete the comprehensive staff analytics

**Data Source Requirements:**
All 191 metrics derive from 7 core data tables:
- Sales_Data
- Staff_Roster
- StaffHours
- Feedback
- Bookings
- Targets
- Date (dimension)
