# SaaS Platform Enterprise (C4)

**Product ID:** C4
**Sprint:** 6+
**Pricing:** Rp 8M+/month
**Target:** Large Restaurant Groups (15-100+ locations)

## Product Overview
The Enterprise tier of the PWBI F&B Growth SaaS platform is designed for large-scale operations requiring custom integrations, advanced security, and dedicated support.

## Key Features
- **Custom ERP Integrations:** SAP, Oracle, NetSuite connectors
- **Advanced Security:** SSO, Audit Logs, Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
- **Dedicated Infrastructure:** Isolated database instances
- **White-Labeling:** Custom branding and domain
- **SLA:** 99.9% uptime guarantee
