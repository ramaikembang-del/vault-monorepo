# 10 - Menu Engineering

**Product Code:** E4
**Sprint:** Sprint 3+ (Post-validation)
**Status:** Planning
**Owner:** Product

---

## Overview

Menu Engineering is a future product designed to help F&B businesses optimize their menu offerings through data-driven analysis of item profitability, popularity, and contribution margins. Helps identify stars, plow-horses, puzzles, and dogs in the menu mix.

**Target Market:** Phase 3-4 operators looking to optimize menu profitability and customer satisfaction
