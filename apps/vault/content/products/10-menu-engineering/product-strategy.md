# Menu Engineering Product Strategy
**Micro-SaaS for Menu Optimization**

**Product Code:** A4b (Pillar 1 - Acquisition)
**Target Segment:** Phase 1-2, restaurants wanting to optimize menu
**Pricing:** Rp 30K/month

---

## Purpose

Help restaurants identify their Stars, Plowhorses, Puzzles, and Dogs using menu engineering matrix. Simple tool that proves immediate value.

## Key Features

- **Menu item profitability calculator:** Revenue × margin analysis
- **BCG-style matrix:** Stars, Plowhorses, Puzzles, Dogs visualization
- **"What-if" pricing simulator:** See impact of price changes
- **Recommendations:** "Remove item X, promote item Y"
- **Menu photo upload:** Visual menu board

## Market Sizing

- **TAM:** 100,000 restaurants
- **SAM:** 30,000 restaurants (interested in menu optimization)
- **SOM:** 500-1,000 subscribers by Year 2

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Basic | Rp 30K/month | 50 menu items, matrix, basic recs |
| Pro | Rp 75K/month | Unlimited items, pricing simulator, trends |

## Pain Points Solved

- **"Which menu items should I remove?"** → Data-driven answer
- **"Am I pricing correctly?"** → Margin analysis + competitor comparison
- **"Which items to promote?"** → High-margin popular items highlighted

## Revenue Projections

- Year 1: Rp 10M (400 subscribers × Rp 30K avg)
- Year 2: Rp 30M (1,000 subscribers)
- Year 3: Rp 60M (2,000 subscribers + Pro tier)

## Upgrade Path

→ Menu data + revenue data → Full analytics valuable
→ Using 2-3 Micro-SaaS → Bundle discount → Full SaaS

## Data Synergy

Menu engineering data feeds into:
- Revenue analytics (which items drive revenue)
- Food Cost % optimization
- Seasonal menu trends
