# A4b Menu Engineer - Pricing & Launch

**Pricing:** Rp 500K/year (Rp 50K/month)
**Trial:** 14 days free
**Launch:** Q1 2025

---

## Pricing Strategy

**Value-Based:**
- Customer saves/earns: Rp 10-20M/year (margin improvement)
- Our price: Rp 500K
- ROI: 20-40x

**Competitive:**
- Manual (Excel): Free but ineffective
- Menu consultants: Rp 10M+ one-time
- **A4b: Ongoing intelligence for fraction of cost**

## Launch Plan

**Beta (January 2025):** 30 restaurants, gather feedback

**Scale (March+ 2025):** Paid ads, SEO, partnerships
