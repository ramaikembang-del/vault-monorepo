# A4d Supplier Manager - User Guide

**Setup:** 15 min | **Daily:** 10 min

---

## Quick Start

1. **Add Suppliers**
 - Name, contact (email, phone, WhatsApp)
 - Products they supply
 - Payment terms (NET 30, etc.)
 - Delivery days/times

2. **Import Price List**
 - Upload Excel OR enter manually
 - Update monthly

3. **Connect to A4a** (if using inventory optimizer)
 - Auto-generate POs from reorder alerts

---

## Creating Purchase Orders

**From A4a Alert:**
- A4a says "Reorder coffee beans"
- Click "Create PO" → Pre-filled with recommended supplier & quantity
- Review & send

**Manual PO:**
- Select supplier
- Add items & quantities
- Generate PDF
- Send via email/WhatsApp

---

## Tracking Deliveries

- Mark PO as "Sent" → Status: Pending Delivery
- When received → Mark "Delivered"
- Rate delivery (on-time? quality?)
- Match to invoice (3-way matching)

---

## Supplier Performance Dashboard

**Metrics Tracked:**
- On-time delivery %
- Average lead time
- Price trends (increasing/stable)
- Quality scores (your ratings)

**Action:** Review quarterly → switch poor performers

---

## Tips

 Update price lists monthly (track inflation)
 Rate every delivery (builds performance history)
 Set up favorite suppliers per item (faster POs)
 Use WhatsApp for fastest communication
