## Features

**Q: Can I compare supplier prices?**
A: Yes, side-by-side comparison for same item across multiple suppliers.

**Q: How are suppliers rated?**
A: You rate each delivery (1-5 stars). System tracks on-time %, price stability, quality.

**Q: Can I send POs via WhatsApp?**
A: Yes, WhatsApp Business API integration.

---

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Supplier not receiving POs**
- Check email/WhatsApp contact correct
- Verify PO marked as "Sent"
- Alternative: Download PDF, send manually

**Problem: Price comparison not showing**
- Ensure multiple suppliers added for same item
- Update price lists current
