# C1 Full SaaS - Competitive Positioning

**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Market Position

**Segment:** Enterprise F&B Analytics & Operations Platform
**Position:** Premium value (enterprise features, mid-market price)

---

## vs. Legacy ERP (SAP, Oracle NetSuite)

**We Win:**
- 10x faster implementation
- 5x lower cost
- F&B-specific (not generic)
- Modern UX (mobile-first)
- Indonesia-specific

**They Win:**
- Established brand
- Global scale
- Deep financial features
- IT department preference

**Our Message:** "Built for F&B, not adapted from manufacturing ERP"

---

## vs. F&B Competitors (Kata.ai)

**We Win:**
- Better value (same features, 30% lower cost)
- Faster implementation
- Better customer support
- Indonesia-first (local integrations)

**They Win:**
- Brand recognition
- AI/ML capabilities (forecasting)
- Enterprise references

**Our Message:** "Same features, half the cost, 2x the support"

---

## vs. Point Solutions (POS + Accounting + Analytics)

**We Win:**
- Unified platform (single source of truth)
- No integration headaches
- Lower total cost of ownership
- Consistent UX

**They Win:**
- Best-of-breed features
- Lower switching costs
- Familiar to teams

**Our Message:** "Stop duct-taping 5 systems. One platform, infinite possibilities."

---

## Win/Loss Analysis

**Top Reasons We Win:**
1. Value (price vs features)
2. F&B expertise
3. Indonesia-specific
4. Fast implementation

**Top Reasons We Lose:**
1. Brand unknown (vs. Oracle, SAP)
2. Lacking AI forecasting (vs. Kata.ai)
3. IT mandates specific vendor (Microsoft, Oracle)

---

## Battle Cards

**vs. Kata.ai:**
- Price: 30% lower
- Implementation: 50% faster
- Support: Dedicated CS manager (vs. ticketing)

**vs. Oracle/SAP:**
- Price: 80% lower
- Implementation: 90% faster
- F&B-specific: Built for F&B, not generic

**vs. Point Solutions:**
- Integrated: No integration hassles
- TCO: Lower long-term cost
- Insights: Cross-functional analytics
