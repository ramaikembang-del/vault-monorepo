# Go-to-Market Plan: SaaS Basic (C1)

**Product:** SaaS Platform Basic (Rp 300K-500K/mo)
**Launch Phase:** Phase 2 (Extension of Lite Dashboard)
**Target:** 1,000 Active Users by End of Year 1

---

## 1. Market Strategy

### Target Audience (The "Mikro")
- **Profile:** 1 location, <5 staff, Rp 30M-100M monthly revenue.
- **Pain Points:** Untracked cash, manual inventory, exhausted owner (doing everything).
- **Trigger Event:** Theft incident, tax audit fear, or opening 2nd branch.

### Value Proposition
**"Exit the Chaos. Run your Warung from your Phone."**
- **Simplicity:** No IT guy needed. Setup in 15 minutes.
- **Verification:** Stop employee theft with audit logs.
- **Clarity:** See exactly how much money you made today (Net Profit, not just Revenue).

---

## 2. Marketing Channels

### A. Digital (Low Cost, High Volume)
- **Facebook/Instagram Ads:**
 - *Creative:* "Owner Capar?" (Tired Owner?) video ads.
 - *Targeting:* Small Business Owners, Admin Pages, F&B Interest.
 - *Cost:* Rp 5M/month budget.
- **SEO/Content:**
 - Blog: "Cara Hitung HPP Kopi Susu" (How to calculate Coffee COGS).
 - Templates: Free Excel COGS template that up-sells to SaaS.

### B. Community & Partnerships
- **POS Resellers:** Partner with Android POS hardware sellers (bundling).
- **GoFood/GrabFood Communities:** Webinars on "Optimizing Profit".
- **Referral Program:** "Get 1 Month Free" for every friend referred.

### C. Direct Sales (Inbound)
- **Lite App Conversion:** Upsell users from the Free Lite Dashboard (E1).
 - *Trigger:* When they hit <100 transactions/mo limit or try to click "Inventory".

---

## 3. Launch Timeline

### Month 1: Beta (Soft Launch)
- **Goal:** 50 Users, Bug Fixing.
- **Action:** Invite top 100 "Lite Dashboard" users to try beta.
- **Incentive:** 50% Lifetime Discount.
- **Metric:** Bug reports, Initial setup time <20 mins.

### Month 2: Public Launch
- **Goal:** 200 Users.
- **Action:**
 - Launch landing page.
 - Turn on Facebook Ads (Rp 2M/week).
 - Email blast to all newsletter subscribers.

### Month 3-6: Growth
- **Goal:** 500+ Users.
- **Action:**
 - Activate Referral Program.
 - Launch "Academy" (Video tutorials).
 - First Webinar with Influencer Owner.

## 5. Success Metrics (KPIs)

1. **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** < Rp 200,000.
2. **Conversion Rate:** > 5% from Website Visit to Trial.
3. **Trial-to-Paid:** > 25% (Simplicity is key).
4. **Churn:** < 5% per month (High risk in this segment, critical metric).

---

## 6. Risks & Mitigation

- **Risk:** High churn (businesses failing).
 - *Mitigation:* Focus on education (help them survive).
- **Risk:** "Too expensive" objection.
 - *Mitigation:* Highlight "Theft Prevention" > Cost of App.
- **Risk:** Support overload.
 - *Mitigation:* Strict "Self-Service First" policy, excellent video guides.
