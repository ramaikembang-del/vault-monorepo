# SaaS Customer Onboarding Playbook
**First 90 Days Success Framework**

**Playbook Owner:** Customer Success Team  
**Last Updated:** January 8, 2026  
**Status:** PLANNED - Launches with Product 05 (SaaS Basic) in Sprint 3+

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Goal:** Get new SaaS customers from signup to "aha moment" within 7 days, and to active daily usage within 30 days.

---

## Executive Summary

### Onboarding Philosophy

**"Time to Value" is the #1 churn predictor.**
- Customers who reach aha moment in <7 days have 85% retention
- Customers who take >30 days have 30% retention

**Our Promise:** "Dashboard insights within 24 hours of signup"

---

### Onboarding Journey (30-60-90 Days)

| Phase | Timeline | Goal | Success Metric |
|-------|----------|------|----------------|
| **Phase 1: Setup** | Days 1-7 | Connect data, see first insights | 80%+ complete setup |
| **Phase 2: Activation** | Days 8-30 | Daily usage habit formed | 50%+ login 3x/week |
| **Phase 3: Expansion** | Days 31-90 | Explore advanced features | 30%+ use 3+ tabs |

---

## Part 1: Pre-Onboarding (Sales Handoff)

### Sales ‚Üí CS Handoff Checklist

**What Sales Must Provide:**
- [ ] Customer business profile (outlets, revenue, POS system)
- [ ] Primary use case ("I need to track gross profit daily")
- [ ] Technical contact (who will do setup?)
- [ ] Payment confirmed (subscription active)

**CS Receives:**
- Email notification within 1 hour of signup
- CRM entry with notes from sales call
- Customer flagged in Supabase (tier, plan, expected MRR)

---

## Part 2: Days 1-7 (Setup Phase)

### Day 1: Welcome Email (Automated)

**Subject:** "Selamat datang! Setup dashboard Anda dalam 3 langkah mudah"

**Content:**
1. **Video:** 2-minute setup walkthrough (Loom/YouTube)
2. **Setup Guide:** PDF with screenshots
3. **CTA:** "Upload data sekarang" (link to CSV upload page)

**Goal:** 40% of users complete setup within 24 hours of this email

---

### Day 2: CSV Upload Assistance

**Trigger:** User hasn't uploaded data within 24 hours

**Action:** Personal email from CS Manager

**Template:**
```
Hi [Name],

Saya [CS Name] dari tim Customer Success Clario. 

Saya lihat Anda belum upload data - ada kendala?

Berikut 3 cara mudah upload data:
1. Export dari POS: [link video tutorial]
2. Manual CSV template: [download template]
3. Jadwalkan call 15 menit dengan saya: [calendly link]

Salam,
[CS Name]
```

**Goal:** Reduce setup abandonment from 60% ‚Üí 30%

---

### Day 3-5: Setup Completion Support

**For users who uploaded data:**
- **Automated:** "Dashboard ready! Lihat insights pertama Anda" email
- **Include:** Screenshot of their actual dashboard (personalized)
- **CTA:** "Explore dashboard" button

**For users stuck:**
- **Day 3:** SMS reminder ("Dashboard Anda menunggu - butuh bantuan?")
- **Day 5:** Call from CS (if MRR >Rp 500K) OR email (if <Rp 500K)

---

### Day 7: Aha Moment Check-In

**Goal:** Confirm customer sees value

**Email:**
```
Subject: Sudah lihat insight menarik?

Hi [Name],

Sudah 1 minggu sejak Anda join Clario!

Pertanyaan: Apa 1 insight paling berguna yang Anda temukan?
(Contoh: "Profit saya turun 12% di weekday - ternyata kurang staff")

Reply email ini atau klik untuk share: [typeform survey]

Terima kasih,
[CS Team]
```

**Why This Matters:** Customers who articulate value stay 3x longer

## Part 3: Days 8-30 (Activation Phase)

### Week 2: Usage Habit Formation

**Goal:** Get customer logging in 3x/week minimum

**Tactics:**

**1. Email Digest (Weekly)**
- Subject: "Laporan mingguan: Revenue naik/turun X%"
- Content: Key metrics summary (revenue, profit, top items)
- CTA: "Lihat detail di dashboard"

**2. In-App Tooltips**
- Day 8: "Explore tab Customer Insights untuk tahu siapa pelanggan setia Anda"
- Day 12: "Coba filter by outlet untuk compare performance"
- Day 15: "Export report ini ke PDF untuk meeting dengan tim"

**3. Milestone Celebrations**
- "üéâ Anda sudah login 10x! Top 20% users."
- "üèÜ Dashboard Anda tracking Rp 50M+ revenue - impressive!"

### Week 3-4: Feature Discovery

**Goal:** Move beyond basic dashboards (Revenue tab only) to advanced features

**Feature Adoption Path:**

| User Journey | Feature to Introduce | Trigger | How |
|--------------|---------------------|---------|-----|
| "I check revenue daily" | **Profit Margin tab** | After 10 logins | Email: "Revenue tinggi tapi profit rendah? Cek Profit Margin tab" |
| "I have 3 outlets" | **Outlet comparison** | Multi-location detected | Tooltip: "Compare outlet performance di sini" |
| "I ran a promo" | **Campaign tracking** | Revenue spike detected | Email: "Promo berhasil! Track ROI dengan Campaign tab" |

## Part 4: Days 31-90 (Expansion Phase)

### Month 2: Power User Development

**Goal:** Turn active users into power users (use 5+ features, invite team members)

**Tactics:**

**1. Team Collaboration Push**
- Email: "Invite tim Anda (gratis tambah 2 user)"
- Show value: "Dashboard lebih bermanfaat kalau seluruh tim pakai"
- Offer: Free onboarding for team members

**2. Advanced Features Training**
- Webinar (monthly): "10 fitur tersembunyi di Clario" (Bahasa Indonesia)
- Office hours: Weekly 1-hour Zoom Q&A session
- Help center: Video tutorials for each advanced feature

**3. Custom Metrics Request**
- Proactive: "Butuh metric khusus? Request gratis untuk SaaS Pro users"
- Form: Submit custom metric request (we review, build if demand >5 requests)

---

### Month 3: Retention Lock-In

**Goal:** Build habits + switching costs so customer renews

**Tactics:**

**1. Historical Data Value**
- Email: "Anda punya 90 days data sekarang - compare Q1 vs Q4 last year"
- Show visualizations only possible with 90+ days history

**2. Integration Deepening**
- Offer: "Connect Accurate accounting software untuk P&L otomatis"
- Benefit: Less manual work ‚Üí more dependency on Clario

**3. Quarterly Business Review (QBR)**
- **For Pro/Enterprise customers only**
- 45-minute call with CS Manager
- Review: Usage stats, business trends, feature requests
- Deliver: Custom insights report PDF (C-suite ready)

## Part 7: Customer Segmentation

### Tier-Based Onboarding

| Tier | MRR | Onboarding Style | CS Touch |
|------|-----|------------------|----------|
| **Basic** (Rp 300-500K) | Low | Self-serve (automated emails) | Email only, no calls |
| **Pro** (Rp 900K-1.2M) | Medium | Hybrid (emails + 1 call) | 1 setup call, 1 Month 1 call |
| **Enterprise** (Rp 2M+) | High | White-glove (dedicated CSM) | Weekly calls, custom training |

---

### Persona-Based Messaging

**Persona: Amanda (Phase 2 SME, 1-3 outlets)**
- Pain: "Too busy to check dashboard daily"
- Onboarding emphasis: Weekly digest emails, mobile app (when available)
- Success metric: 1x/week login OK (not 3x/week)

**Persona: Budi 2.0 (Phase 2, tech-savvy)**
- Pain: "Wants to DIY, doesn't need hand-holding"
- Onboarding emphasis: Advanced features early, API docs
- Success metric: Uses 5+ features within Month 1

**Persona: Corporate Manager (Phase 3 Enterprise)**
- Pain: "Needs to show ROI to CFO"
- Onboarding emphasis: QBR with ROI report, C-suite presentation deck
- Success metric: Invites 3+ team members, integration with ERP

## Part 8: Churn Prevention (Red Flags)

### Early Warning Signals

| Signal | Threshold | Risk Level | Action |
|--------|-----------|------------|--------|
| **No data upload (Day 3)** | 0 uploads | HIGH | Call if Pro+, Email if Basic |
| **Week 1 ghost** | 0 logins | HIGH | SMS + Email |
| **Declining usage** | 50% drop MoM | MEDIUM | CS check-in email |
| **No Month 2 login** | 0 in 30 days | CRITICAL | Cancellation imminent, urgent call |
| **Feature abandonment** | Used 5 features, now only 1 | MEDIUM | Re-engagement campaign |

---

### Intervention Playbook

**Red Flag: No login in 14 days (Active user went dark)**

**Day 14:** Email: "Miss us? Here's what you're missing..." (show insights snapshot)  
**Day 21:** Email from founder ("Personal note: How can we help?")  
**Day 25:** Call (if Pro+): "Considering cancellation? Let's chat"  
**Day 28:** Last chance email: "Special offer: 50% off next month if you stay"

**If still churns:** Exit interview ("Why did you leave? What could we do better?")

**Must-Have:**

## Part 10: Continuous Improvement

### Monthly Retrospective

**Review:**
- This month's cohort: Setup %, Month 1 retention, Month 3 retention
- Compare vs last month: What improved? What regressed?
- Customer feedback themes: Top 3 complaints, top 3 praises

**Iterate:**
- Update email copy (A/B test subject lines)
- Simplify setup flow (remove friction points)
- Add new tooltip for confusing feature

**Goal:** Improve Month 1 ‚Üí Month 3 retention by 2-5% per quarter
