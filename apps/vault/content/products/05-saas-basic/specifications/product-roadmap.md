# C1 Full SaaS - Product Roadmap

**Vision:** The F&B Operating System for Indonesia

---

## Q1 2025

**Theme:** Performance & Reliability

**Features:**
- Database performance optimization (50% faster queries)
- Mobile app polish (iOS + Android parity)
- Offline mode (mobile)
- Excel export (all reports)

**Target:** 10 enterprise customers

---

## Q2 2025

**Theme:** Intelligence & Automation

**Features:**
- AI-powered forecasting (sales, inventory)
- Automated anomaly detection
- Smart recommendations (pricing, menu, staffing)
- Workflow automation (approvals, alerts)

**Target:** 20 enterprise customers

---

## Q3-Q4 2025

**Theme:** Platform & Ecosystem

**Features:**
- Marketplace (3rd-party integrations)
- Advanced permissions (granular RBAC)
- White-labeling (partners)
- Multi-currency support (international expansion)

**Target:** 30+ enterprise customers

---

## 2026+

**Vision:** AI-First F&B Platform
- Predictive analytics (churn, demand, etc.)
- Natural language queries ("Show me underperforming locations")
- Autonomous operations (auto-ordering, auto-scheduling)
