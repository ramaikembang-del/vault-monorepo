## 1. Financial Survival

> *"Can I pay my bills and stay open?"*

### KPIs (Always Visible)
| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| Net Revenue | Money coming in |
| Net Profit | Money left after everything |
| Profit Margin % | Sustainability indicator |
| Prime Cost % | COGS + Labor ÷ Revenue (target: <65%) |
| Cash Position | Can I pay this week's bills? |

### Views
| View | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Simple P&L | Revenue - COGS - Labor - OpEx = Profit |
| Cost Breakdown | Where is money going? (donut chart) |
| Revenue Trend | Am I trending up or down? (line chart) |
| Actual vs Target | Am I on track? (bar comparison) |

### Survival Questions Answered
- "Am I making money or losing money?"
- "Where is my money going?"
- "Am I on track for this month's target?"
- "Can I afford next week's payroll?"

## 5. Early Warning System

> *"What problems should I see before it's too late?"*

### Built-in Alerts (Simple Rules)
| Alert Type | Trigger | Message |
|------------|---------|---------|
| Critical | Prime Cost > 70% | "Costs too high - check suppliers/labor" |
| Critical | Negative cash flow 2 weeks | "Cash running low" |
| Warning | Revenue down 15% WoW | "Sales declining - investigate" |
| Warning | Top item sales drop 20% | "Your best seller is slipping" |
| Positive | Profit margin > target | "You're doing great" |

### Survival Health Score
Single number (0-100) combining:
- Financial health (40%)
- Sales trend (25%)
- Cost control (25%)
- Menu balance (10%)

```
Score 80-100: Smooth Sailing
Score 60-79: Watch Carefully
Score 40-59: Action Needed
Score 0-39: Survival Mode
```
