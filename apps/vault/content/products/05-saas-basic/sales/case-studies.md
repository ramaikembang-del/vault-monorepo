# Case Studies: SaaS Basic (C1)

**Target Audience:** Single-Outlet Micro-Enterprises (Mikro → Kecil)
**Product:** SaaS Platform Basic (Rp 300K/month)

---

## 1. Kopi Kenangan Abadi (Independent Coffee Shop)
**Location:** Jakarta Selatan
**Type:** Single Outlet (GrabFood/GoFood heavy)
**Owner Profile:** "The Hustler" (Manual operations, overwhelmed)

### The Challenge
- **Inventory Chaos:** Owner manually counted beans/milk every night. 15% discrepancy between POS sales and stock usage.
- **Theft Suspicions:** High staff turnover, no audit trail.
- **Manual Reporting:** Spent 2 hours/night copying receipts to Excel.

### The Solution: SaaS Basic
- **Implementation:** 1-day setup. Imported menu items from generic template.
- **Feature Usage:**
 - Real-time stock deduction from POS.
 - "End of Shift" report auto-emailed to owner.
 - Staff role restrictions (Owner vs Cashier).

### The Results (3 Months Later)
- **Waste Reduced:** 15% → 3% (Saved Rp 2.5M/month).
- **Time Saved:** 60 hours/month (Admin time reduced to 15 mins/day).
- **Revenue:** +12% due to zero stock-outs on bestsellers (Kopi Susu Gula Aren).

> "I finally have my evenings back. The Rp 300K subscription paid for itself in the first week just by stopping milk wastage." — *Budi, Owner*

## 2. Warung Makan Sejahtera (Traditional Warteg Upgrade)
**Location:** Surabaya
**Type:** Dine-in & Takeaway
**Owner Profile:** "The Traditionalist" (Skeptical of tech)

### The Challenge
- **Pricing Guesswork:** Didn't know actual food costs (COGS). Prices hadn't changed in 3 years despite rising ingredient costs.
- **Cash Leaks:** No receipt printer; cash drawer often short.

### The Solution: SaaS Basic
- **Transition:** Moved from calculator -> Tablet POS (Lite) -> SaaS Basic Dashboard.
- **Key Insight:** Dashboard showed "Ayam Goreng" margin was negative due to oil price hike.

### The Results
- **Profitability:** Adjusted menu prices by 10%, immediate 8% net profit increase.
- **Accountability:** Daily cash reconciliation matches system sales 99.9% of the time.
- **Growth:** Now planning to open a second branch using the profits.

---

## 3. Mama Cookies (Home Bakery)
**Location:** Bandung (Home-based)
**Type:** Seasonal/Pre-order
**Owner Profile:** "The Artisan" (Passion for product, hates numbers)

### The Challenge
- **Seasonal Spikes:** Lebaran orders overwhelmed manual tracking.
- **Lost Orders:** Orders via WhatsApp, DM, and Tokopedia got mixed up.

### The Solution: SaaS Basic
- **Order Management:** centralized all orders into the SaaS dashboard manually.
- **Production Planning:** Used the basic inventory report to generate a shopping list.

### The Results
- **Zero Missed Orders:** 100% fulfillment rate during Lebaran rush (vs 90% previous year).
- **Better Buying:** Bulk bought flour at correct quantities, saving Rp 1M.
