# C1 Full SaaS - Pricing & Packaging

**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Pricing Model

**Base Platform:** Rp 100M/year
- Up to 10 locations
- 50 users included
- All modules included
- Standard support

**Add-Ons:**
- Additional location: +Rp 8M/year
- Additional user (power user): +Rp 3M/year
- API access: +Rp 20M/year
- Premium support: +Rp 30M/year

---

## Pricing Examples

**Scenario 1: Mid-Sized Chain (15 locations)**
- Base: Rp 100M
- +5 locations: Rp 40M
- +25 power users: Rp 75M
- **Total: Rp 215M/year**

**Scenario 2: Large Chain (50 locations)**
- Base: Rp 100M
- +40 locations: Rp 320M
- +150 power users: Rp 450M
- API access: Rp 20M
- **Total: Rp 890M/year**
- **Discounted: Rp 750M/year (15% volume discount)**
