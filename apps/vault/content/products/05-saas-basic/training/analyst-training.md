# SaaS Analyst Training Guide
**Product 05: SaaS Basic - Data Analyst Training**

**Audience:** Business analysts, finance staff  
**Duration:** 2 hours (self-paced)  
**Status:** PLANNED - Launches with SaaS Basic

---

## Training Objectives

By the end of this training, analysts will be able to:
1. Navigate the SaaS dashboard
2. Generate standard reports (daily P&L, weekly summary)
3. Export data for further analysis
4. Troubleshoot common data issues

---

## Module 1: Dashboard Overview (20 min)

### Lesson 1.1: Logging In
- Navigate to app.clario.id
- Enter credentials
- Enable 2FA (optional but recommended)

### Lesson 1.2: Dashboard Layout
- **Left sidebar:** Navigation (Revenue, Profit, Customers, Settings)
- **Top bar:** Date range selector, export button
- **Main area:** Charts and tables

**Exercise:** Log in and explore each tab (5 min)

---

## Module 2: Core Reports (40 min)

### Lesson 2.1: Revenue Dashboard
- **What it shows:** Daily/weekly/monthly revenue trends
- **Key metrics:** Total revenue, avg transaction, peak days
- **How to use:** Identify high/low revenue days

**Exercise:** Find your highest revenue day last month

### Lesson 2.2: Profit Dashboard
- **What it shows:** Gross profit, net profit, margins
- **Key metrics:** Profit %, cost breakdown
- **How to use:** Identify cost optimization opportunities

**Exercise:** Calculate your monthly profit margin

### Lesson 2.3: Customer Insights
- **What it shows:** Customer frequency, loyalty, lifetime value
- **Key metrics:** Repeat rate, avg spend per customer
- **How to use:** Identify VIP customers vs one-timers

**Exercise:** Export list of top 10 customers

---

## Module 3: Data Export & Analysis (30 min)

### Lesson 3.1: CSV Export
- Click "Export" button
- Select date range
- Download CSV file
- Open in Excel

**Exercise:** Export last month's transactions, open in Excel

### Lesson 3.2: Excel Analysis
- Pivot tables for custom views
- Charts for visualization
- Formulas for calculations

**Exercise:** Create pivot table of revenue by day of week

---

## Module 4: Troubleshooting (20 min)

### Common Issues

**Issue 1: Data not updating**
- Solution: Check POS integration status (Settings → Integrations)

**Issue 2: Wrong numbers**
- Solution: Verify date range filter (top bar)

**Issue 3: Missing transactions**
- Solution: Check if POS sync failed (Settings → Sync Log)

**Exercise:** Simulate data issue, practice troubleshooting

---

## Module 5: Advanced Features (10 min)

### Filters & Segments
- Filter by outlet (for multi-location)
- Filter by product category
- Compare periods (this month vs last month)

### Scheduled Reports
- Set up weekly email reports
- Customize recipients
- Choose metrics to include

---

## Assessment (10 min)

**Quiz:** 10 multiple choice questions
- Pass threshold: 7/10 correct
- Retake allowed

**Certificate:** Upon passing, receive "Clario Analyst Certified" badge
