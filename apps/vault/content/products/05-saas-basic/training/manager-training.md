# Manager Training Guide
**Product 05: SaaS Basic - Manager Training**

**Audience:** Restaurant owners, F&B managers  
**Duration:** 1.5 hours (self-paced)  
**Status:** PLANNED - Launches with SaaS Basic

---

## Training Objectives

Managers will learn to:
1. Use dashboard for business decisions
2. Interpret key metrics (not just view)
3. Share insights with team
4. Set up alerts and goals

---

## Module 1: Business Dashboard Basics (15 min)

**Lesson:** Navigate the manager view
- **Dashboard:** Executive summary (revenue, profit, trends)
- **Reports:** Pre-built weekly/monthly summaries
- **Alerts:** Automated notifications (revenue drops, cost spikes)

**Exercise:** View last month's performance summary

---

## Module 2: Making Data-Driven Decisions (30 min)

### Scenario 1: Revenue is Down
**Data to Check:**
- Which days/hours dropped?
- Which menu items selling less?
- Any seasonal patterns?

**Action:** Adjust staffing, run promotions on slow days

### Scenario 2: Profit Margins Shrinking
**Data to Check:**
- Cost of goods sold (COGS) trend
- Labor cost % of revenue
- Waste/spoilage data

**Action:** Renegotiate supplier prices, optimize labor schedule

### Scenario 3: Customer Frequency Declining
**Data to Check:**
- Repeat customer rate
- Average days between visits
- Customer feedback scores

**Action:** Launch loyalty program, improve service quality

---

## Module 3: Team Collaboration (20 min)

**Sharing Insights:**
- Export weekly report (PDF)
- Present at team meetings (use charts)
- Set team goals ("Increase profit margin to 65%")

**Delegating:**
- Grant staff access (view-only or edit)
- Assign specific metrics to team members
- Review together in daily standups

---

## Module 4: Setting Up Alerts (15 min)

**Automated Alerts:**
- Revenue drops >20% week-over-week
- COGS exceeds 40% (profit margin risk)
- Customer complaints spike

**Delivery:** Email or WhatsApp notification

**Exercise:** Set up 1 custom alert

---

## Module 5: Advanced Features (10 min)

**Forecasting:**
- Predict next month revenue (AI-powered)
- Plan inventory based on demand forecast

**Benchmarking:**
- Compare your metrics vs industry average
- "Your profit margin: 62% vs industry 58%"

**Custom Reports:**
- Build your own charts (drag-and-drop)
- Schedule monthly reports to email
