# C1 Full SaaS - Support Playbook

**Support Model:** White-glove enterprise support

---

## Support Tiers

**Standard (Included):**
- Email support: support@pwbi.co.id
- Response SLA: < 24 hours
- Business hours: Mon-Fri 9 AM-6 PM WIB

**Premium (+Rp 30M/year):**
- Dedicated support engineer
- Response SLA: < 4 hours (P1), < 2 hours (P0)
- Phone support
- After-hours support (P0/P1)

---

## Escalation Path

**L1 Support → L2 Support → Engineering → Product → CTO**

**P0 (Critical):** Immediate escalation to Engineering + CTO notified
**P1 (High):** L2 within 1 hour, Engineering within 4 hours
**P2/P3:** Standard workflow

---

## Common Enterprise Support Requests

1. **Custom Reports** - Template provided or built by support
2. **Integration Issues** - Dedicated engineer assigned
3. **Performance Optimization** - Engineering review
4. **Training Requests** - Scheduled sessions
5. **Feature Requests** - Logged, prioritized by Product

---

## Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)

**Attendees:** Customer C-suite + PWBI leadership
**Agenda:**
- Usage review
- Value delivered (ROI)
- Roadmap preview
- Feedback session
