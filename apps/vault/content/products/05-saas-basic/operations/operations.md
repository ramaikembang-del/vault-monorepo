## Success Metrics Overview

### Activation (Week 1)
- Account setup complete
- First dashboard view
- At least 3 metrics explored

### Engagement (Monthly)
- 10+ logins per month
- 5+ different reports viewed
- At least 1 insight acted upon

### Value Realization
- Revenue impact: Customer identifies 1+ growth opportunity
- Cost savings: Customer identifies 1+ cost reduction area
- Operational efficiency: Time saved vs manual reporting
