# C1 Full SaaS - User Guide (Analyst)

**Audience:** Financial analysts, data analysts
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Your Role

As an analyst, you:
- Create reports & dashboards
- Analyze trends & patterns
- Support decision-making with data
- Prepare executive presentations

---

## Key Features

### 1. Custom Reports
- Drag-and-drop report builder
- 50+ pre-built templates
- Export to Excel, PDF, PowerPoint
- Schedule automated delivery

### 2. Data Exploration
- Ad-hoc queries (SQL editor)
- Pivot tables
- Advanced filtering
- Data visualization (charts, graphs)

### 3. Financial Analysis
- Variance analysis (budget vs. actual)
- Trend analysis (YoY, MoM)
- What-if scenarios
- Forecasting models

---

## Common Analysis Tasks

**Task: Monthly P&L Review**
1. **Reports → P&L by Location**
2. Select date range
3. Compare to budget & prior year
4. Identify variances > 5%
5. Drill into details
6. Document findings
7. Export to PowerPoint

**Task: Menu Profitability Analysis**
1. **Analytics → Product Performance**
2. Filter: Last 30 days
3. Sort by **Gross Margin %**
4. Identify loss-making products
5. Recommend pricing changes or discontinuation

**Task: Labor Cost Optimization**
1. **Analytics → Labor Analysis**
2. Compare **Labor % of Revenue** by location
3. Identify outliers
4. Analyze staffing patterns
5. Recommend schedule optimizations

**SQL Editor:**
- Write custom queries

**API Access:**

## Tips for Analysts

1. **Use Templates First** - Faster than building from scratch
2. **Schedule Reports** - Automate recurring analysis
3. **Collaborate** - Share dashboards with team
4. **Validate Data** - Always sanity-check results
