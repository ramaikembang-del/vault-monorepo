# C1 Full SaaS - User Guide (Owner/CEO)

**Audience:** Business owners, C-suite executives
**Last Updated:** December 2025

## Getting Started

**Your Dashboard:** Executive Summary View

Upon login, you see:
- **Company Performance:** Revenue, profit, costs (real-time)
- **Location Comparison:** Best & worst performing locations
- **Alerts:** Critical issues requiring attention
- **Trends:** Week-over-week, month-over-month changes

---

## Key Use Cases

### 1. Monitor Overall Business Health

**Daily (5 minutes):**
- Check yesterday's sales vs. target
- Review alerts (low inventory, high waste, etc.)
- Approve pending items (expenses, POs)

**Weekly (30 minutes):**
- Review P&L by location
- Identify over/underperforming locations
- Make strategic decisions

**Monthly (2 hours):**
- Deep-dive financial review
- Budget vs. actual analysis
- Plan next month

### 2. Location Performance Comparison

**Question:** "Which locations are profitable?"

**Steps:**
1. Navigate to **Locations Dashboard**
2. Sort by **Gross Margin %**
3. Drill into underperformers
4. Identify root cause (COGS, labor, rent?)
