# C1 Full SaaS - FAQ

**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## General

**Q: What is C1 Full SaaS?**
A: Complete F&B operations platform for multi-location businesses. Replaces POS analytics, inventory, finance, and HR systems.

**Q: Who is it for?**
A: Enterprise F&B businesses (10+ locations, Rp 5B+ revenue).

**Q: How is it different from R2 Suite?**
A: C1 is all-in-one platform. R2 is modular (pick specific modules). C1 is for larger enterprises.

---

## Pricing

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Starts at Rp 100M/year (10 locations, 50 users). Custom pricing for larger deployments.

**Q: What's included?**
A: All modules, implementation, training, support, unlimited data.

**Q: Are there contract minimums?**
A: 12-month minimum. Annual prepay gets 10-15% discount.

---

## Implementation

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
A: 4-8 weeks for pilot, 3-6 months for full rollout (depends on # of locations).

**Q: Do we need to migrate data?**
A: Yes, we help migrate historical data (typically 12-24 months).

**Q: Can we integrate with our existing POS?**
A: Yes, we integrate with Moka, Olsera, Pawoon, Qasir, and others.

---

## Technical

**Q: Is it cloud-based?**
A: Yes, 100% cloud (AWS). No on-premise servers needed.

**Q: Is our data secure?**
A: Yes. ISO 27001 compliant, encrypted, backed up daily.

**Q: What if the internet goes down?**
A: Mobile app has offline mode. Web requires internet.

**Q: Can we access via mobile?**
A: Yes, iOS and Android apps available.

---

## Support

**Q: What support is included?**
A: Email support (< 24h response), business hours. Premium support available (< 4h response, phone, after-hours).

**Q: Do you provide training?**
A: Yes, comprehensive training during implementation + ongoing webinars.

**Q: Can we customize it?**
A: Yes, custom reports, workflows, integrations available (additional cost).
