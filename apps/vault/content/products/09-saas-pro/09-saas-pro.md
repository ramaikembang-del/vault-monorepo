# SaaS Platform Pro (C2)

**Product ID:** C2
**Sprint:** 4
**Pricing:** Rp 900K-1.2M/month
**Target:** Multi-outlet (2-5 locations)

## Product Overview
The Pro tier extends the SaaS platform with multi-outlet capabilities, advanced inventory management, and deeper analytics.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Outlet Support:** Consolidated dashboard
- **Inventory Management:** Recipe costing and stock tracking
- **User Management:** Multiple roles (Manager, Staff)
- **Data Retention:** 3 years history
