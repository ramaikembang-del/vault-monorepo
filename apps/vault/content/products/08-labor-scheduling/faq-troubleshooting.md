# A4c Labor Scheduler - FAQ

---

## General

**Q: What is A4c?**
A: AI-powered staff scheduling tool that reduces labor costs 15-20% by optimizing schedules based on predicted demand.

**Q: How much?**
A: Rp 500K/year (up to 30 staff). 14-day free trial.

**Q: Who is it for?**
A: Restaurants, cafés with 5-30 staff, multiple shifts, variable demand.

---

## Features

**Q: How does AI scheduling work?**
A: Predicts demand (based on sales history) → calculates required staff → assigns best-fit team → minimizes labor cost.

**Q: Can staff view schedules on phones?**
A: Yes, mobile app (iOS + Android). View schedules, request time-off, swap shifts.

**Q: Does it handle labor law compliance?**
A: Yes, Indonesian labor law (max 40 hours regular, 14 OT, rest periods, holiday pay).

**Q: Can staff clock in/out?**
A: Yes, GPS-verified clock in/out (prevents fraud).

---

## Manager Questions

**Q: How long to create a schedule?**
A: Auto-schedule: 5 minutes. Manual: 30-60 minutes (before A4c: 3-5 hours).

**Q: Can I override AI recommendations?**
A: Yes, drag-drop to adjust. AI suggests, you decide.

**Q: What if someone calls in sick?**
A: Send notification to available staff → first to accept gets shift.

---

## Staff Questions

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Mobile app → Request Time-Off → select dates → manager approves.

**Q: Can I swap shifts with colleagues?**
A: Yes, offer shift via app → colleague accepts → manager approves.

**Q: Will I get enough hours?**
A: AI distributes hours fairly. Set availability to maximize hours.

---

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: Schedule seems understaffed**
- Check: Forecast accurate? Staff availability up-to-date?
- Fix: Adjust required staff per shift, update forecasts

**Problem: Overtime happening too much**
- Solution: Set stricter OT limits, AI will distribute hours differently
