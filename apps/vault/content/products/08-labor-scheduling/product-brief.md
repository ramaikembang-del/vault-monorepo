# A4c Labor Scheduler - Product Brief

**Product:** A4c - Labor Scheduler
**Type:** Micro-SaaS
**Target:** SMB F&B with 5-30 staff
**Pricing:** Rp 500K/year
**Launch:** Q1 2025

---

## Problem

Manual scheduling wastes 5+ hours/week and causes costly over/understaffing. 80% of SMB F&B create schedules manually (Excel/paper), resulting in:

**Pain Points:**
- **Time-consuming:** 5-10 hours/week creating/adjusting schedules
- **Overstaffing:** Labor costs 5-10% too high (fear of being short)
- **Understaffing:** Rush hours understaffed → poor service, lost sales
- **Staff conflicts:** Overlapping shifts, missed breaks, overtime violations
- **Communication chaos:** WhatsApp groups, last-minute changes

**Financial Impact:** Rp 10-15M/year excess labor costs

---

## Solution

AI-powered staff scheduler that optimizes schedules based on predicted demand, reducing labor costs 15-20% while ensuring adequate coverage.

**How It Works:**
1. **Forecast Demand** - AI predicts busy/slow times (based on sales history)
2. **Staff Availability** - Staff input their availability via mobile app
3. **Auto-Schedule** - AI creates optimal schedule (right people, right times)
4. **Publish & Notify** - Staff get schedules via WhatsApp/push notifications
5. **Manage Changes** - Shift swaps, time-off requests handled in-app

---

## Key Features

### 1. AI-Powered Scheduling
- Predicts demand by hour/day (ML forecasting)
- Matches required staff levels to predicted demand
- Considers staff skills, preferences, availability
- Optimizes for minimum labor cost while meeting service levels
- Respects labor laws (max hours, overtime, breaks)

**Example:**
```
Monday Lunch Rush Forecast: 80 customers/hour
Required: 3 servers + 2 kitchen + 1 cashier
AI assigns: Best-performing team for lunch
Labor cost: Optimized to 28% of revenue (target < 30%)
```

### 2. Staff Availability Management
- Staff mark availability via mobile app
- Recurring patterns (e.g., always off Sundays)
- Time-off requests (approved/rejected by manager)
- Preferred shifts noted

### 3. Mobile App for Staff
- View schedules (current week + next 2 weeks)
- Request time off
- Swap shifts (manager approval)
- Clock in/out (GPS verified)
- View payroll hours

### 4. Labor Cost Tracking
- Real-time: Labor % of revenue
- Projected vs actual hours
- Overtime alerts
- Budget adherence

### 5. Compliance
- Indonesian labor law compliance:
 - Max 40 hours/week regular, 14 hours OT
 - Minimum rest periods (12 hours between shifts)
 - Holiday pay calculations
- BPJS integration (export for payroll)
