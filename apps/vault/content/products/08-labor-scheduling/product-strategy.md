# Staff Scheduling Product Strategy
**Micro-SaaS for F&B Staff Management**

**Product Code:** A4a (Pillar 1 - Acquisition)
**Target Segment:** Phase 1 Mikro, 5-15 staff operations
**Pricing:** Rp 50K/month

---

## Purpose

Low-cost point solution for restaurants struggling with manual staff scheduling. Land customers with a specific pain point, then expand to full platform.

## Key Features

- **Weekly schedule builder:** Drag-and-drop shift assignment
- **Staff availability tracking:** Who can work when
- **Conflict detection:** Overlapping shifts, overtime alerts
- **WhatsApp notifications:** Schedule changes sent to staff
- **Simple payroll export:** Hours worked → Excel for payroll

## Market Sizing

- **TAM:** 50,000+ F&B businesses with 5+ staff
- **SAM:** 20,000 restaurants (manual scheduling pain)
- **SOM:** 500-1,000 subscribers by Year 2

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | Rp 50K/month | 10 staff, basic scheduling |
| Pro | Rp 100K/month | 25 staff, payroll export, WhatsApp |

## Pain Points Solved

- **"I spend 2 hours every week making the schedule"** → 15 minutes
- **"Staff don't know their shifts"** → Auto WhatsApp notifications
- **"I can't track overtime"** → Automatic overtime alerts

## Revenue Projections

- Year 1: Rp 15M (300 subscribers × Rp 50K avg)
- Year 2: Rp 50M (1,000 subscribers)
- Year 3: Rp 100M (2,000 subscribers + Pro tier)

## Upgrade Path

→ Using 2-3 Micro-SaaS tools → Bundle saves money → Full SaaS Basic
→ Growing to 20+ staff → Need analytics → Full SaaS Pro

## Data Synergy

Staff scheduling data feeds into:
- Staff Performance analytics (attendance, punctuality)
- Labor Cost % calculations
- Benchmark: Staff-to-revenue ratio
