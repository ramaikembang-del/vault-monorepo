## Product Overview

**Product 06: Custom Dashboard Development Service**

**What It Is:** White-glove, bespoke dashboard development for enterprise customers who outgrow templates

**Target Segment:**
- 10+ outlet F&B chains
- Corporate F&B (Starbucks franchisees, restaurant groups)
- Revenue >Rp 100M/month

**Pricing:** Rp 25-50M per project (one-time)

---

## Strategic Rationale

### Why Build This?

**Market Gap:**
- Template (Product 01) serves 1-5 outlets
- Full SaaS (Product 05) serves 5-20 outlets
- **Gap:** 20-100+ outlet enterprises need custom solution

**Revenue Opportunity:**
- 5 enterprise projects/year Ã— Rp 35M avg = **Rp 175M/year**
- High margin (60-70%, lower than template due to custom dev labor)

**Strategic Value:**
- **Upsell path:** Template customers who grow big
- **Case studies:** Enterprise logos for sales collateral
- **Platform learnings:** Custom requests inform SaaS roadmap

---

## Product Scope

### Included in Base Price (Rp 25M)
- Discovery workshop (2 days, on-site)
- Custom data model (multi-source integration)
- 5 custom dashboards (tailored to business KPIs)
- 2 rounds of revisions
- Training for 3 users
- 30-day post-launch support

### Add-Ons (Extra Cost)
- API integrations (+Rp 5M per integration)
- White-label branding (+Rp 3M)
- Ongoing maintenance (Rp 2M/month)
- Additional users training (+Rp 500K per user)

---

## Delivery Process (6-8 Weeks)

### Week 1-2: Discovery
- Stakeholder interviews
- Data audit (what systems, what data)
- Requirements doc (what dashboards needed)

### Week 3-5: Development
- Build data pipelines
- Create custom dashboards
- Internal QA testing

### Week 6: User Acceptance Testing (UAT)
- Client reviews dashboards
- Collect feedback
- Fix bugs

### Week 7-8: Training & Launch
- On-site training (full day)
- Handoff to client
- Monitor for issues (30-day support)

---

## Competitive Positioning

**vs Freelancers:**
- Faster (6 weeks vs 3-6 months)
- F&B expertise (prebuilt metrics)
- Ongoing support included

**vs Enterprise BI (Tableau, Qlik):**
- Cheaper (Rp 35M vs Rp 200M+ implementations)
- F&B-specific (not generic consulting)
