# Integration Marketplace - Platform Strategy

**Vision:** The app store for F&B businesses

## Marketplace Components

**1. Pre-Built Integrations**
- POS systems (20+)
- Accounting software (10+)
- Payment gateways (15+)
- Delivery platforms (GoFood, GrabFood, etc.)
- HR/Payroll systems

**2. Developer Platform**
- Public APIs
- SDK in multiple languages
- Developer docs & sandbox
- Partner certification program

**3. Revenue Model**
- Free integrations (core POS, accounting)
- Paid integrations: Revenue share (20% to PWBI, 80% to developer)
- Premium connectors: Subscription-based

## Market Opportunity
- 1,000+ potential integration partners
- Platform fees: Rp 500M-1B additional ARR

**Timeline:** 2026-2027
