## Product Overview

**R2 - Accounting Module for Mikro F&B**
Simple P&L automation for Indonesian Mikro F&B businesses (1-3 outlets).

**Core Value:** "P&L in 5 Minutes, Not 5 Hours"

---

## Product Structure

R2 is a modular accounting suite with **6 sub-products:**

| Module | Name | Focus | Sprint | Status |
|--------|------|-------|--------|--------|
| **R2a** | P&L Analytics | Profit & Loss tracking | Sprint 2 | Validating |
| **R2b** | Cash Flow Tracker | Cash flow management | Sprint 2-3 |  Planned |
| **R2c** | COGS Calculator | Cost of goods tracking | Sprint 3 |  Planned |
| **R2d** | Tax Compliance | Tax reporting | Q3 2025 | Future |
| **R2e** | Invoicing | Billing & invoicing | Q2 2025 | Future |
| **R2f** | Expense Tracking | Expense categorization | Sprint 3 |  Planned |

**Current Priority:** R2a P&L Analytics (validates demand for entire R2 suite)

## Pricing

| Tier | Price | For | Features |
|------|-------|-----|----------|
| **Basic** | Rp 50K/month | 1 outlet | Manual expense entry, Monthly P&L, SAK EMKM compliant |
| **Plus** | Rp 75K/month | 2-3 outlets | Multi-location P&L, Consolidated dashboard |
| **Pro** | Rp 100K/month | 3+ outlets / B2B | All Plus + Invoicing (R2e module) |

**Bundled Pricing:**
- With A1 (Power BI Template): +Rp 25-75K/month
- With E1 (Web Dashboard): +Rp 50K/month OR free (E1 Premium tier)
