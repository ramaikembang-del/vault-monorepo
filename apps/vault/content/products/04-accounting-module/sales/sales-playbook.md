# Sales Playbook - R2 Accounting Module
**Product:** R2 - Analytics Suite / Accounting Module
**Target:** 20+ paying customers by Sprint 2 (Week 6)
**Pricing:** Rp 50-100K/month standalone, bundled discounts available

---

## Two Sales Strategies

### Strategy 1: Standalone Sales (Cold Market)
**Target:** Restaurants NOT using A1/E1
**Channel:** Instagram ads, POS partnerships, direct outreach
**Positioning:** Affordable accounting for Mikro F&B

### Strategy 2: Bundled Sales (Warm Market)
**Target:** Existing A1/E1 customers
**Channel:** Upsell, cross-sell, retention
**Positioning:** Complete analytics + accounting solution

---

## Standalone Sales Playbook

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Phase 1-2 Mikro F&B:**
- 1-3 outlets (warung, café, small restaurant)
- Monthly revenue: Rp 50-200M
- Currently using: Excel OR no accounting
- Pain: Spends 5+ hours/month on manual P&L
- Budget: Can afford Rp 50-100K/month
- Decision maker: Owner (not delegated)

**Negative signals (don't pursue):**
- Already using Jurnal/Accurate happily (switching cost too high)
- Enterprise (5+ outlets) - need full accounting software
- Pre-revenue (can't afford Rp 50K/month)

---

### Sales Script (Standalone)

**Opening (WhatsApp/Instagram DM):**
```
Hi [Name]! Lihat restoran/café kamu di [location].
Mau tanya - sekarang pakai apa untuk cere tracking untung-rugi bulanan?

(Translation: I saw your restaurant/café in [location].
Quick question - what do you currently use to track monthly profit-loss?)
```

**Discovery Questions:**
1. "Berapa jam habis per bulan untuk itung untung-rugi?" (How many hours/month for P&L?)
2. "Pakai Excel manual atau ada hire akuntan?" (Excel or hired accountant?)
3. "Kalau bisa hemat 5 jam/bulan, worth it gak bayar Rp 50K?" (Worth Rp 50K to save 5 hours?)

**Pitch (if pain confirmed):**
```
Kami punya solusi: automated P&L for F&B, cuma Rp 50K/bulan.

Yang kamu dapat:
 Auto tracking revenue (connect ke POS)
 Input expense in 30 sec (via WhatsApp or app)
 Monthly P&L report (SAK EMKM compliant)
 Export PDF for bank loan application

Hemat waktu, save 5 jam/bulan. Minat coba 1 bulan free trial?
```

**Objection Handling:** See [[biz/departments/growth/sales/templates/objection-handling-guide|objection-handling.md]]

**Close:**
```
Oke, saya kirim link daftar ya. Isi data 5 menit, langsung bisa pakai hari ini.
First month FREE, kalau suka lanjut Rp 50K/bulan. Deal?

(I'll send signup link. 5 min setup, use today.
First month FREE, then Rp 50K/month if you like it. Deal?)
```

## Sales Funnel Stages

### Stage 1: Awareness (Cold → Warm)
**Channels:**
- Instagram ads: "Akuntansi untuk Mikro F&B"
- F&B Facebook groups (organic posts + testimonials)
- POS provider partnerships (Pawoon/Moka referrals)

**Content:**
- Pain post: "Habis 5 jam/bulan untuk Excel accounting? Ada solusi Rp 50K"
- Testimonial: "[Customer] hemat 10 jam/bulan"
- Explainer video: "P&L in 5 minutes demo"

**Goal:** 100 leads/month

---

### Stage 2: Interest (Warm → Hot)
**Touchpoint:** Landing page, lead magnet

**Lead magnet:** "Template Excel Laporan Keuangan F&B (FREE)"
- Download → Email capture
- Follow-up email: "Tired of Excel? Try automated (Rp 50K/month)"

**Goal:** 40% landing page → email sign-up conversion

---

### Stage 3: Decision (Hot → Customer)
**Offer:** 1-month FREE trial

**Onboarding sequence (WhatsApp):**
- Day 0: Welcome message + setup link
- Day 1: "Input expense pertama kamu hari ini?"
- Day 7: Check-in call (10 min) "Ada kesulitan?"
- Day 21: "Udah coba generate P&L? Mau bantuan?"
- Day 28: "Trial ends in 3 days - lanjut Rp 50K/month?"

**Conversion Goal:** 50% trial → paid

---

### Stage 4: Retention (Customer → Advocate)
**Touchpoint:** Monthly check-ins, WhatsApp support

**Retention tactics:**
- Month 2: "Pakai 2 bulan - gimana hasilnya?"
- Month 3: "Mau refer teman? Kalian berdua dapat 1 month free"
- Month 6: "Sudah 6 bulan - ready untuk R2c COGS module upgrade?"

**Upsell triggers:**
- Opens 2nd outlet → Offer Plus tier (Rp 75K)
- Asks about invoicing → Offer R2e module
- Happy + active → Ask for testimonial (exchange for 1 month free)

## Sales Team Structure (Sprint 2)

**For 20-customer target, need minimal team:**

**Option 1: Founder-led sales (Week 1-4)**
- Founder does all sales (validates messaging)
- Hire VA for lead gen (Week 3+)
- Cost: Rp 3M (VA salary) + Rp 2M (ads) = Rp 5M

**Option 2: Sales VA (Week 5+)**
- Hire 1 commission-based sales VA
- Pay: Rp 2M base + Rp 100K per customer (20% first month revenue)
- Cost: Rp 2M base + Rp 2M commission (20 customers) = Rp 4M

**Recommended:** Option 1 for Sprint 2 (founder validates playbook first)

## Competitive Win Strategy

**When competing with Jurnal/Accurate:**
- "We're F&B-specific, they're generic accounting"
- "We're Rp 50K, they're Rp 200-500K + setup complexity"
- "We integrate with your analytics (A1/E1), they don't"

**When competing with Excel:**
- "Excel = 5 hours/month, we = 30 minutes"
- "Excel prone to errors, we auto-calculate"
- "Excel not SAK EMKM compliant, we are"

**When competing with Full Accountant:**
- "Accountant = Rp 3-5M/month, we = Rp 50K"
- "You still own your data (not outsourced)"
- "Real-time (not wait for month-end accountant report)"
