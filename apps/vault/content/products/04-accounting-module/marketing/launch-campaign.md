# Launch Campaign - R2 Accounting Module
**Product:** R2 - Analytics Suite / Accounting Module
**Sprint:** Sprint 2 (Week 3-6)
**Goal:** 20+ paying customers by Week 6
**Budget:** Rp 5M

---

## Campaign Overview

**Campaign Name:** "Akuntansi untuk Owner, Bukan Akuntan"
(Translation: Accounting for Owners, Not Accountants)

**Core Message:** Simple P&L automation for Mikro F&B owners who don't have time for Excel or money for accountants.

**Target:** Phase 1-2 Mikro F&B (Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya)
- 1-3 outlets
- Revenue Rp 50-200M/month
- Currently using Excel OR no accounting

**Campaign Duration:** 4 weeks (Sprint 2, Week 3-6)

---

## Week-by-Week Campaign Plan

### Week 1: Awareness (Soft Launch)

**Goal:** Generate 100+ landing page visits, 50+ email signups

**Activities:**

**Monday (Day 1): Soft Launch**
- Publish landing page (one-pager.md content)
- Announce in F&B Facebook groups (organic post)
 - Post: "Bosen Excel accounting? Ada solusi Rp 50K/month - check link"
 - Groups: Owner F&B Indonesia, Komunitas Restaurant, Café Community Jakarta
- Target: 500+ views, 50+ clicks

**Tuesday-Wednesday: Content Marketing**
- Instagram post 1: "5 Jam untuk Excel? Ada Cara Lebih Baik"
 - Carousel: Before (Excel hell) vs After (R2 automation)
 - CTA: Link in bio → Landing page
- Instagram post 2: "Kenapa Bank Tolak Excel P&L Kamu?"
 - Explainer: SAK EMKM compliance requirement
 - CTA: Download free SAK EMKM guide → Email capture

**Thursday-Friday: WhatsApp Outreach**
- Direct message to 25 warm leads (from E1/A1 customer list)
 - Message: "Hi [Name], we launched accounting add-on - interested?"
 - Target: 10+ responses, 5+ signups

**Saturday-Sunday: Community Engagement**
- Answer questions in F&B groups (build authority)
- Share customer pain point testimonials
 - "I used to spend 10 hours/month on Excel..."

**Week 1 Metrics:**
- Landing page visits: 100+
- Email signups: 50+
- Trial signups: 5-10 (early adopters)

---

### Week 2: Interest (Lead Nurturing)

**Goal:** Convert 50 email signups → 20+ trial signups

**Activities:**

**Monday: Email Campaign (Drip 1)**
- Subject: "Template Excel Gratis - Laporan Keuangan F&B" (Lead magnet)
- Content: Download Excel template + pitch R2 as easier alternative
- CTA: Try R2 free for 1 month

**Tuesday-Wednesday: Instagram Ads Launch**
- Budget: Rp 1M (Week 2 only)
- Targeting: Jakarta/Bandung males 25-45, interests: F&B business, accounting
- Creative: "Hemat 5 Jam/Bulan - R2 Accounting Rp 50K"
- CTA: Try free → Landing page
- Target: 500+ clicks, 50+ trial signups

**Thursday: Email Campaign (Drip 2)**
- Subject: "Bedanya R2 vs Jurnal/Accurate (Comparison Table)"
- Content: Feature comparison + price comparison
- CTA: Start free trial (limited to first 20 customers Rp 25K/month early-bird)

**Friday-Saturday: Webinar/Live Demo**
- Instagram Live: "Setup R2 in 15 Minutes - Live Demo"
- Show: Connect POS + Enter expense + Generate P&L
- Q&A session (30 min)
- CTA: Sign up now, get early-bird Rp 25K/month

**Sunday: Retargeting**
- Retarget landing page visitors who didn't sign up
- Ad: "Last chance: Early-bird Rp 25K/month ends Monday"
- Target: 20+ conversions

**Week 2 Metrics:**
- Trial signups: 20+ (cumulative: 25-30 total)
- Webinar attendees: 30+
- Early-bird conversions: 10+ @ Rp 25K/month

---

### Week 3: Desire (Social Proof)

**Goal:** Convert trials → paying customers (target: 10+ paying)

**Activities:**

**Monday-Tuesday: Case Study Launch**
- Publish Case Study 1: "Amanda - Hemat 10 Jam/Bulan"
 - Instagram post + blog
 - WhatsApp status broadcast
- Target: 500+ views, 5+ "I want this too" responses

**Wednesday: Email Campaign (Drip 3)**
- Subject: "How [Customer Name] Got KUR Rp 100M Approved Using R2 P&L"
- Content: Bank loan success story + SAK EMKM compliance benefit
- CTA: Get bank-ready P&L today (start trial)

**Thursday-Friday: Influencer Partnership**
- Partner with F&B micro-influencer (5K-10K followers)
- Content: "Review R2 - Akuntansi Gampang untuk Café Owner"
- Budget: Rp 500K (1 post + WhatsApp story)
- Target: 1K+ views, 20+ signups

**Saturday: Trial Follow-Up (Week 1 cohort)**
- WhatsApp message to Week 1 trial users (Day 21 of trial)
 - "Trial ends in 9 days - upgrade to Rp 25K early-bird?"
- Offer: Extend Rp 25K rate if commit now (create urgency)
- Target: 50% conversion (5+ paying from Week 1 cohort)

**Sunday: POS Partnership Announcement**
- Announce partnership with Pawoon/Moka (if secured)
- Joint marketing: "Pawoon POS users get Rp 10K discount"
- Target: 50+ Pawoon users sign up

**Week 3 Metrics:**
- Paying customers: 10+ (Rp 25-50K/month)
- Case study views: 500+
- POS partnership leads: 50+

---

### Week 4: Action (Conversion Push)

**Goal:** Hit 20+ paying customers, close out early-bird promo

**Activities:**

**Monday: Early-Bird Deadline Announcement**
- Instagram post: "Last 5 Days - Rp 25K/Month Promo Ends Friday"
- Email blast: "Don't miss early-bird - price increases to Rp 50K Saturday"
- Target: 50+ last-minute signups

**Tuesday-Wednesday: Instagram Ads (Final Push)**
- Budget: Rp 1M (aggressive final push)
- Creative: "Final 48 Hours - Rp 25K/Month" (countdown timer)
- Retarget: All landing page visitors, webinar attendees
- Target: 100+ clicks, 20+ conversions

**Thursday: Customer Testimonial Showcase**
- Instagram carousel: 5 customer testimonials
 - "Hemat waktu", "Bank approve loan", "Super simple"
- CTA: Join 15+ restoran already using R2

**Friday: Early-Bird Deadline (LAST DAY)**
- 3× Instagram stories throughout day: "Last Hours!"
- WhatsApp broadcast to all leads: "Price increases tomorrow"
- Live countdown on Instagram (final 2 hours)
- Target: 10+ conversions (panic buyers)

**Saturday-Sunday: Post-Promo Transition**
- Announce promo closed, now Rp 50K/month regular price
- Continue selling at Rp 50K (test price elasticity)
- Email to non-converters: "You missed Rp 25K, but Rp 50K still 75% cheaper than Jurnal"

**Week 4 Metrics:**
- Paying customers: 20+ TOTAL (Gate 2A target met!)
- Regular price (Rp 50K) conversions: 5+ (validates sustainable pricing)
- Email list: 200+ (for future campaigns)

## Success Metrics (Campaign KPIs)

### Primary Metrics (Gate 2A Criteria)
- 20+ paying customers by Week 6
- Rp 1-2M MRR (20 × Rp 50-100K avg)
- <20% Month 1 churn

### Secondary Metrics (Campaign Health)
- Landing page conversion: 10%+ (visit → email signup)
- Email conversion: 20%+ (email → trial)
- Trial conversion: 50%+ (trial → paying)
- Instagram ad ROAS: 2:1 (Rp 2M revenue / Rp 2M ad spend)
- CAC: <Rp 250K (Rp 5M budget / 20 customers)

---

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk 1: Low ad conversions (Instagram ads don't work)**
- Mitigation: Shift budget to influencer partnerships (Week 3)
- Backup: Aggressive WhatsApp outreach to warm leads

**Risk 2: Trial users don't convert to paid**
- Mitigation: Personal WhatsApp follow-up (high-touch)
- Offer: Extend trial to 2 months if budget concern

**Risk 3: Only 10-15 customers by Week 6 (miss 20 target)**
- Mitigation: Extend early-bird promo 1 extra week
- Adjust Gate 2A criteria: 15+ customers = conditional pass

---

## Post-Campaign Actions (Week 6+)

**If Gate 2A PASSED (20+ customers):**
- Greenlight R2b-f development (expand accounting modules)
- Scale marketing (double budget Month 2)
- Hire part-time customer success VA

**If Gate 2A FAILED (<20 customers):**
- Analyze: Was it pricing? Messaging? Market readiness?
- Pivot: Test Rp 40K/month OR bundled-only strategy
- Re-validate Week 7-8 with adjusted approach
