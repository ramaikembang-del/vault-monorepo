## How It Works (3 Simple Steps)

### Langkah 1: Connect POS (5 Menit)
Upload 1 CSV file dari POS kamu (Pawoon, Moka, manual).
Revenue otomatis ter-import.

### Langkah 2: Input Expense (30 Detik/Entry)
WhatsApp kami: "Spent Rp 500K on ingredients today"
→ Auto-logged ke kategori COGS.

Atau pakai app: 10 kategori expense (COGS, Labor, Rent, Utilities, dll).
Tap-tap-done in 30 seconds.

### Langkah 3: Generate P&L (1-Click)
End of month: Click "Generate P&L"
→ Instant report (SAK EMKM compliant)
→ Export PDF for bank/investor.

**Total time: 30 minutes/month (vs 5 hours Excel)**
