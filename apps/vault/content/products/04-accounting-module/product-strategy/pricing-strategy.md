# Product 04 - Accounting Module Pricing Strategy
**Product Family:** R2 Accounting Tools  
**Pricing Model:** Standalone + Bundle + SaaS Integration  
**Last Updated:** January 9, 2026

---

## Overview

**Philosophy:** Affordable micro-SaaS pricing for Mikro F&B businesses, with bundle discounts and SaaS upgrade path.

**Pricing Tiers:**
1. **Standalone** - Individual tool purchase
2. **Pillar 2 Bundle** - All R2 tools together (discounted)
3. **SaaS Integration** - Included in SaaS Basic/Pro

---

## Standalone Pricing

| Product | Monthly Price | One-Time Price | Target Customer |
|---------|--------------|----------------|-----------------|
| **R2a: P&L Analytics** | Rp 50K/mo | Rp 500K (lifetime) | Mikro needing basic accounting |
| **R2b: Cash Flow Monitor** | Rp 50K/mo | Rp 400K (lifetime) | Cash-heavy businesses |
| **R2c: COGS Calculator** | Rp 75K/mo | Rp 750K (lifetime) | Restaurants managing food cost |

**Why Two Options:**
- **Monthly:** Lower barrier, recurring revenue
- **One-Time:** Indonesian preference (avoid subscriptions), higher upfront revenue

---

## Bundle Pricing

### Pillar 2 Bundle: "Accounting Essentials"

**Includes:**
- R2a: P&L Analytics
- R2b: Cash Flow Monitor
- R2c: COGS Calculator

**Pricing:**
- **Monthly:** Rp 100K/mo (save Rp 75K = 43% discount)
- **One-Time:** Rp 1.5M (save Rp 150K = 9% discount)

**Target:** Multi-outlet or growing businesses needing full financial visibility

---

## SaaS Integration Pricing

**Included in SaaS Basic (C1):**
- All R2 tools built-in
- Cloud sync (multi-device)
- No separate purchase needed

**SaaS Basic Pricing:** Rp 300-500K/mo

**Migration Path:**
- R2 standalone users → Upgrade to SaaS Basic
- Get 3 months 50% off SaaS when upgrading
- Keep standalone as offline backup

## Competitive Positioning

**vs. Accountants:**
- Traditional: Rp 500K-2M/month (bookkeeper salary)
- R2 Bundle: Rp 100K/month (5-20x cheaper)
- **Value Prop:** "DIY accounting at 10% the cost"

**vs. Accounting Software (Accurate, Jurnal):**
- Competitors: Rp 200-500K/month (complex, enterprise-focused)
- R2 Tools: Rp 50-100K/month (simple, F&B-specific)
- **Value Prop:** "Built for warung, not corporations"

**vs. Excel:**
- Excel: Free (but requires skills)
- R2 Tools: Rp 50-100K/month (pre-built templates, automatic calculations)
- **Value Prop:** "Accounting without Excel skills"

---

## Pricing Experiments & A/B Tests

**Test 1: Monthly vs Annual Prepay**
- **Hypothesis:** Annual prepay at 2 months free (17% discount) will lock in customers
- **Metric:** % choosing annual vs monthly
- **Target:** 30%+ annual adoption

**Test 2: Bundle Discount Threshold**
- **Current:** 43% discount for bundle
- **Test:** 50% discount (Rp 175K → Rp 87.5K/mo)
- **Metric:** Bundle attach rate
- **Target:** 40%+ of R2 users buy bundle

**Test 3: SaaS Migration Incentive**
- **Current:** 50% off SaaS for 3 months
- **Test:** Free 6 months upgrade
- **Metric:** R2 → SaaS conversion rate
- **Target:** 20%+ migrate within 12 months

## Discounts & Promotions

**Launch Discount (Sprint 2):**
- 50% off first month for early adopters
- "Introductory pricing: Rp 25K/month!"
- Target: Get first 20-50 customers

**Referral Program:**
- Refer a friend → Both get 1 month free
- Target: Viral growth via word-of-mouth

**Multi-Outlet Discount:**
- 2-3 outlets: 10% off
- 4-5 outlets: 20% off
- 6+ outlets: Custom pricing (or suggest SaaS Pro)

## Payment Options

**Accepted Methods:**
1. **Bank Transfer** (primary in Indonesia)
