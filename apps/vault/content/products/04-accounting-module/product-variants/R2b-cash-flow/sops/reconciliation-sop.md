# R2b Cash Flow - Reconciliation SOP

**Owner:** Customer Success
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Purpose
Help customers reconcile cash flow data for accuracy.

## Reconciliation Process

### Monthly Reconciliation (Every month-end)

**Step 1: Compare Balances**
- Dashboard cash balance vs actual bank balance
- Identify discrepancies

**Step 2: Identify Missing Transactions**
- Check for unsynced transactions
- Manual entry if needed
- Categorize correctly

**Step 3: Adjust for Timing**
- Outstanding checks
- Deposits in transit
- Bank fees/interest

**Step 4: Validate**
- Final reconciled balance = bank statement
- Review with customer
- Mark as reconciled

---

## Common Discrepancies
| Issue | Cause | Fix |
|-------|-------|-----|
| Missing transactions | Sync failure | Manual entry |
| Wrong categorization | Auto-rule incorrect | Recategorize + update rule |
| Duplicate entries | Double import | Delete duplicate |

---

## Quality Checks
- [ ] Monthly reconciliation completed
- [ ] Discrepancies < 1%
- [ ] Customer signed off on reconciliation
