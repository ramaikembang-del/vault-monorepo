# Market Positioning - Cash Flow Forecasting

**Product:** R2b - Cash Flow Forecasting
**Product ID:** R2b
**Pillar:** P2 - Retention (Accounting Add-On)
**Target:** Phase 2.5-3 Scaling F&B (Multi-outlet, B2B, Rapid Hiring)

---

## Value Proposition

**For:** Indonesian F&B businesses scaling beyond single outlet
**Who:** Are profitable but run out of cash (payment timing mismatch)
**Our Product:** R2b is cash runway visibility + payment reminder tool
**Unlike:** Full accounting (too complex) OR Excel cash sheets (manual, error-prone)
**We Offer:** Know "How many months until we run out of cash?" in 10 seconds

---

## Target Segments & Messaging

### Segment 1: The "Profitable But Broke" Paradox (Multi-Outlet)

**Who:** Dimas - 3 dark kitchen locations, profitable on paper but always scrambling for cash

**Pain Points:**
- "P&L shows Rp 10M profit this month, but I can't pay tomorrow's supplier bill Rp 8M. Where's the cash?"
- "Location A has Rp 15M sitting, Location B overdrafted. I'm juggling constantly."
- "Ran out of cash last month, had to borrow from family. Embarrassing."

**Job to Be Done:** Predict cash shortfalls before they happen, avoid scrambling

**R2b Message:**
> **"Profitable != Cash Rich. Know Your Runway."**
> "Your P&L says Rp 10M profit. But rent (Rp 15M) due in 3 days, GoBiz payment (Rp 8M) comes in 7 days. R2b shows: You'll be short Rp 5M on Day 3. Transfer from Location A now OR delay rent 5 days. Never scramble again."

**Competitive De-Positioning:**
- **vs P&L Only (R2a):** "R2a shows profit. R2b shows survival. You need both."
- **vs Excel:** "Excel cash sheet takes 2 hours to update. One formula error = wrong forecast = cash crisis. R2b updates automatically."

---

### Segment 2: The "Why Am I Always Short?" (B2B Catering)

**Who:** Rika - B2B catering, large orders billed net-30 (customer pays 30 days later)

**Pain Points:**
- "Did Rp 50M catering order for wedding, won't get paid until next month. Meanwhile, supplier bills due this week."
- "I'm profitable but always cash-poor. Don't understand why."
- "Had to take expensive short-term loan just to bridge 2 weeks. Interest killing me."

**Job to Be Done:** Understand payment timing gaps, plan bridge financing

**R2b Message:**
> **"Net-30 Kills Cash. See the Gap."**
> "You invoiced Rp 50M catering (Feb 1), customer pays March 1 (net-30). But supplier bills Rp 30M due Feb 10. R2b shows 20-day cash gap. Plan: Get short-term loan OR negotiate supplier payment delay to Feb 20. Bridge the gap intelligently."

**Competitive De-Positioning:**
- **vs Accountant:** "Accountant tells you after the fact ('You were short last month'). R2b warns you 30 days ahead."

---

### Segment 3: The "Rapid Hire Cash Burn" (Scaling Business)

**Who:** Amanda - Hired 15 staff in 3 months to handle growth

**Pain Points:**
- "Payroll jumped from Rp 10M  ->  Rp 25M/month. Didn't realize how fast cash burns."
- "Runway dropped from 6 months  ->  2 months. Panicking."
- "Need to know: Can I afford 5 more hires next month or will I run out of cash?"

**Job to Be Done:** Model hiring impact on cash runway before committing

**R2b Message:**
> **"Hiring Burns Cash Fast. Model It."**
> "Current runway: 2.8 months. Hire 5 more staff (Rp 10M/month payroll increase)  ->  Runway drops to 1.6 months (Red zone). R2b scenario: Cut 20% non-staff costs first  ->  Runway stays at 2.5 months. Hire safely."

**Competitive De-Positioning:**
- **vs Gut Feel:** "Guessing cash impact = business Russian roulette. R2b shows exact runway with/without new hires."

## Competitive Landscape & De-Positioning

### Direct Competitors

**Full Accounting Software (Jurnal, Accurate):**
- Price: Rp 500K-1M/month
- Features: Complete accounting, cash flow is one small module
- Target: SME 10+ outlets

**Our De-Positioning:**
> "Jurnal is overkill if you just need cash visibility. You're paying Rp 500K for 20 features when you only use 2 (cash forecast + runway). R2b gives you those 2 for Rp 100K. Save Rp 400K/month."

---

**Excel / Google Sheets Cash Templates:**
- Price: Free
- Effort: 2-3 hours setup, 1 hour/week maintenance
- Risk: Formula errors, manual updates

**Our De-Positioning:**
> "Excel is 'free' but costs you 4+ hours/month (Rp 200K+ opportunity cost at your wage). Plus one formula error = wrong forecast = cash crisis. R2b costs Rp 100K but gives you 4 hours back + prevents \Rp 5M+ crisis. Worth it?"

---

**Fractional CFO / Accountant:**
- Price: Rp 3-5M/month (fractional), Rp 10M+ (full-time)
- Features: Human judgment, strategic advice
- Availability: Limited hours

**Our De-Positioning:**
> "CFO is great when you're Phase 4+ enterprise. For Phase 2-3, R2b gives you 80% of cash visibility for 2% of cost (Rp 100K vs Rp 5M). Use savings to hire CFO for strategic decisions only."

## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Practical survival guide, not dry finance tool

**Do's:**
- Use survival language ("runway", "cash burn", "bridge the gap")
- Show real Rupiah amounts (Rp 5M shortfall, not "21% variance")
- Acknowledge cultural realities (supplier relationships, debt shame)
- Celebrate "crisis avoided" wins

**Don'ts:**
- Don't use complex finance jargon ("working capital ratio," "days sales outstanding")
- Don't shame users for cash problems ("You should have known")
- Don't oversell ("Revolutionary!"  ->  keep it practical)
