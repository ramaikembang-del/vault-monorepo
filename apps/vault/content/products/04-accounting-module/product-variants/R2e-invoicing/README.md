# Product R2e: Invoicing Module

> [!PLAN]
> **Status:** Future Product (Not Yet Developed)

> **Product ID:** R2e (Retention Pillar - Accounting Suite)

---

## Overview

Professional invoicing module for F&B businesses serving B2B clients (catering, wholesale, corporate accounts).

---

## Product Concept

### Target Audience
- Phase 3-4 businesses with B2B revenue
- Catering companies
- Wholesale suppliers
- Corporate meal programs

### Problem Solved
- Manual invoice creation in Word/Excel
- No invoice tracking (paid/unpaid)
- No automated payment reminders
- Unprofessional invoice design

### Solution
- Professional invoice templates
- Automated numbering & tracking
- Payment status monitoring
- WhatsApp payment reminders
- Multi-currency support (IDR, USD)

---

## Planned Features

### Core Features
- Invoice creation (itemized, tax-inclusive)
- Professional PDF generation
- Payment tracking (paid, pending, overdue)
- Automated reminders (WhatsApp, email)
- Recurring invoices (monthly contracts)

### Advanced Features
- Online payment links (Xendit integration)
- Multi-currency invoicing
- Credit notes & refunds
- Aging report (30/60/90 days)

---

## Pricing (Planned)

**Subscription:** Rp 150-200K/month
**Target ARPU:** Rp 175K
**Market Size:** ~3,000 Phase 3-4 B2B businesses in Year 3

## When to Build
