## Overview

Tax compliance module for Indonesian F&B businesses. Automates tax calculations, filing prep, and compliance reporting for VAT (PPN) and income tax (PPh).

---

## Product Concept

### Target Audience
- Phase 3-4 businesses (Kecil to Menengah)
- >Rp 500M annual revenue (VAT registration threshold)
- Monthly tax filing obligations

### Problem Solved
- Manual tax calculations prone to errors
- Missed filing deadlines (penalties)
- Difficulty tracking VAT receivable/payable
- Accountant fees high (Rp 3-5M/month)

### Solution
- Automated VAT calculation from POS data
- Tax calendar with deadline reminders
- Pre-filled tax forms (SPT Masa PPN, PPh 21/23)
- Export to e-Filing format

---

## Planned Features

### Core Features
- VAT (PPN) calculation (11% standard rate)
- Input/output VAT tracking
- Monthly PPN report generation
- Income tax withholding (PPh 21, 23)
- Tax calendar & reminders

### Advanced Features (Phase 4+)
- Direct e-Filing integration (DJP Online)
- Multi-entity tax consolidation
- Tax audit trail reporting
- Accountant collaboration portal
