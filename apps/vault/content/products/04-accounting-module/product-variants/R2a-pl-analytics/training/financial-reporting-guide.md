# R2a - Financial Reporting Guide

**Understanding and using your P&L**

---

## Reading Your P&L

**Sample P&L:**
```
Revenue: Rp 100,000,000 (100%)
COGS: Rp 30,000,000 (30%)
Gross Profit: Rp 70,000,000 (70%)

Operating Expenses:
 Labor: Rp 25,000,000 (25%)
 Rent: Rp 15,000,000 (15%)
 Utilities: Rp 5,000,000 (5%)
 Other: Rp 10,000,000 (10%)
Total Expenses: Rp 55,000,000 (55%)

Net Profit: Rp 15,000,000 (15%)
```

---

## Key Ratios

**Food Cost %:** COGS / Revenue
**Target:** 25-35%

**Labor %:** Labor / Revenue
**Target:** 25-35%

**Prime Cost:** (COGS + Labor) / Revenue
**Target:** <60%

**Profit Margin:** Net Profit / Revenue
**Target:** >10%

---

## Month-over-Month Analysis

Compare this month vs last month:
- Revenue trend
- Cost changes
- Margin improvement/decline

**Action:** Investigate large variances (>10%)

---

## Exporting Reports

**For Accountant:**
- Export as PDF
- Include notes on unusual items
- Attach supporting invoices
