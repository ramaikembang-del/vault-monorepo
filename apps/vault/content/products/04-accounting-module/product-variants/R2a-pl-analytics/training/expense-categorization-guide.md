# R2a - Expense Categorization Guide

**Properly categorize expenses for accurate P&L**

---

## Standard Categories for F&B

### Cost of Goods Sold (COGS)
- Food ingredients
- Beverage ingredients
- Packaging (takeaway containers, bags)

**Target:** 25-35% of revenue

### Labor
- Salaries (kitchen, front-of-house, managers)
- Wages (hourly staff)
- Overtime
- Staff meals
- Payroll taxes/BPJS

**Target:** 25-35% of revenue

### Occupancy
- Rent/lease
- Property tax
- Insurance (building)
- Security

**Target:** 5-10% of revenue

### Utilities
- Electricity
- Water
- Gas
- Internet/phone

**Target:** 3-5% of revenue

### Marketing
- Social media ads
- Promotions/discounts
- Events
- Influencer collaborations

**Target:** 2-5% of revenue

### Repairs & Maintenance
- Equipment repairs
- Facility maintenance
- Kitchen equipment servicing

**Target:** 1-2% of revenue

### Supplies
- Cleaning supplies
- Uniforms
- Smallwares

**Target:** 1-2% of revenue

### Other Operating Expenses
- Licenses & permits
- Bank fees
- Professional services (accountant)
- Delivery/logistics commissions
- Waste disposal

---

 **Mixing COGS with supplies** - Ingredients = COGS, Cleaning supplies = Supplies
