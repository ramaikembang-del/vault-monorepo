# R2a Simple P&L - Quick Start Guide

**Duration:** 10 minutes
**Goal:** See your first Profit & Loss statement

---

## Step 1: Connect Revenue (2 min)

**Option A:** Auto from POS (if integrated)
**Option B:** Manual entry

---

## Step 2: Set Up Expense Categories (3 min)

1. Settings → Expense Categories
2. Add your categories:
 - Rent
 - Utilities (electric, water, gas)
 - Salaries & wages
 - Food/beverage costs (COGS)
 - Marketing
 - Repairs
 - Delivery
 - Miscellaneous

**Tip:** Start with 5-7 main categories, add more later

---

## Step 3: Enter This Month's Expenses (5 min)

P&L → Enter Expenses

For each category, enter amount spent this month.

**Example:**
- Rent: Rp 15,000,000
- Salaries: Rp 25,000,000
- COGS: Rp 30,000,000
- Utilities: Rp 5,000,000

**Tip:** Do this on 1st of month for previous month

---

## Step 4: View P&L

**Your P&L shows:**
```
Revenue: Rp 100,000,000
COGS: Rp 30,000,000 (30%)
Gross Profit: Rp 70,000,000

Expenses: Rp 45,000,000
Net Profit: Rp 25,000,000 (25% margin)
```

**Health check:**
- Is profit positive?
- Food cost <35%?
- Profit margin >10%?
