## Product Overview

**Simple P&L (Profit & Loss) Tracker for Indonesian Mikro F&B Businesses**

**For:** Phase 1-2 Mikro F&B owners (1-3 outlets)
**Who:** Need basic accounting but can't afford full accountant (Rp 3-5M/month)
**Our Product:** Rp 50-100K/month automated P&L generation from POS + manual expense tracking
**Unlike:** Full accounting software (too complex) OR Excel (manual hell)
**We Offer:** SAK EMKM-compliant P&L in 5 minutes/month, not 5 hours

---

## What's Included

### Automated Revenue Tracking
- Import POS sales data (CSV or API)
- Categorize revenue streams (Dine-in, Takeaway, Delivery)
- Monthly revenue summary auto-generated

### Manual Expense Tracking
- **NOT full accounting** - simplified expense categories
- 10 expense types: COGS, Labor, Rent, Utilities, Marketing, Supplies, Maintenance, Licenses, Other
- Quick-entry: "Spent Rp 500K on ingredients today"  ->  logged to COGS

### Monthly P&L Report
- **SAK EMKM Compliant** (Indonesian Mikro accounting standard)
- Revenue - COGS - Operating Expenses = Net Profit
- Month-over-month comparison
- Export to PDF (for bank loan applications)

### What's NOT Included
- [x] Full double-entry accounting (use proper accounting software)
- [x] Tax filing (refer to accountant)
- [x] Inventory tracking (see R2c COGS module for this)
- [x] Invoicing (see R2f Invoice module)
