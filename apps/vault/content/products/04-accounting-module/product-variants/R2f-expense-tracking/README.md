## Overview

Comprehensive expense tracking for F&B businesses. Captures all non-COGS expenses (marketing, utilities, salaries) for profitability analysis.

---

## Product Concept

### Target Audience
- Phase 2-4 businesses
- Businesses with multiple expense categories
- Owners wanting full P&L visibility

### Problem Solved
- Expenses tracked in scattered receipts/notes
- No categorization of spending
- Can't calculate true net profit
- Budget overruns undetected

### Solution
- Mobile receipt capture (photo OCR)
- Expense categorization (marketing, utilities, etc.)
- Budget tracking & alerts
- Real-time P&L with full OpEx

---

## Planned Features

### Core Features
- Receipt photo upload & OCR
- Expense categorization (Chart of Accounts)
- Monthly budget setting
- Budget vs. actual tracking
- Variance alerts (>10% over budget)

### Advanced Features
- Vendor linkage (auto-categorize recurring expenses)
- Multi-user approval workflow
- Credit card integration (bank feed)
- Expense analytics (trends, top categories)
