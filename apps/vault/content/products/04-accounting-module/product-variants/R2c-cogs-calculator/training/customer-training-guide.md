# R2c: COGS Calculator - Training Guide

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Quick Start

### What is COGS?
Cost of Goods Sold = How much you spend on ingredients for each dish

### Why It Matters
- Know which dishes are profitable
- Price menu correctly
- Control food costs

### Setup (30 min first time)
1. Add ingredients (chicken, rice, oil, etc.)
2. Enter cost per unit
3. Create recipes (which ingredients, how much)
4. R2c calculates dish cost automatically!

---

## Adding Ingredients

**Format:**
- Name: Chicken Breast
- Unit: kg
- Cost per unit: Rp 50,000/kg
- Supplier: (optional)

**Tips:**
- Use consistent units (grams, mL, pieces)
- Update prices monthly
- Start with top 20 ingredients (80% of menu)

---

## Creating Recipes

**Example: Nasi Goreng**

| Ingredient | Quantity | Cost |
|------------|----------|------|
| Rice | 200g | Rp 2,000 |
| Chicken | 100g | Rp 5,000 |
| Eggs | 2 pcs | Rp 3,000 |
| Oil | 30mL | Rp 600 |
| Seasoning | - | Rp 1,000 |
| **Total COGS** | | **Rp 11,600** |

**Sell price:** Rp 35,000
**Food cost %:** 33% (11,600/35,000) Good!

---

## Target Food Cost %

**Guidelines:**
- Fine dining: 28-32%
- Casual dining: 30-35%
- Fast casual: 32-38%
- QSR/Fast food: 30-35%

**Above target?** Raise price or reduce portion/change recipe

---

## Tracking Actual COGS

**How:**
1. R2c links recipes to POS sales
2. Sold 50 Nasi Goreng today
3. Each costs Rp 11,600
4. Total daily COGS = Rp 580,000

**Compare:**
- Theoretical COGS (from recipes): Rp 580K
- Actual spending on ingredients: Rp 650K
- Difference = Rp 70K â†’ Wastage or theft!

---

## Wastage Tracking

**Enter wastage:**
1. Daily count spoiled/wasted items
2. Enter in R2c
3. See wastage cost

**Reduce wastage:**
- Better inventory management
- Smaller portions
- Use soon-to-expire ingredients first

---

## Menu Engineering

**Classify dishes:**

**Stars** (High volume + High margin): Promote!
**Plow Horses** (High volume + Low margin): Raise price
**Puzzles** (Low volume + High margin): Market better
**Dogs** (Low volume + Low margin): Remove from menu

R2c shows this automatically!

---

## Price Changes

**When ingredient prices change:**
1. Update ingredient cost
2. R2c recalculates all recipes
3. See new food cost %
4. Adjust menu prices if needed

**Note:** Historical data keeps old costs (accurate for that period)

---

## Advanced: Seasonal Menu

**For seasonal/promo items:**
1. Create temporary ingredients/recipes
2. Track separately
3. Deactivate when season ends
