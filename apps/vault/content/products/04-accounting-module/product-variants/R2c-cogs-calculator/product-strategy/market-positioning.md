**For:** Indonesian bakeries, catering businesses, and food manufacturers (Phase 2)

## Target Segments & Messaging

### Segment 1: The "Guessing Game Player" (Bakery Owners)

**Who:** Budi 2.0 - Bakery with 20+ bread/cake SKUs, prices based on "feel" or competitor matching

**Pain Points:**
- "I think my Chocolate Cake costs Rp 20K to make, but I'm not sure"

- "Competing bakery charges Rp 45K for similar cake. Should I match or go lower?"

**Job to Be Done:** Price with confidence based on actual costs, not guesswork

> **"Stop Guessing. Start Knowing."**

**Competitive De-Positioning:**
- **vs Excel:** "Manual recipe calculations take 2 hours/week. One formula error = Rp 500K loss on big catering order. R2c auto-calculates, never makes mistakes."
- **vs Full Inventory Software:** "Rp 500K/month for features you don't need (multi-warehouse, barcode scanning). R2c gives you recipe costing for Rp 100K. Pay for what matters."

## Indonesian Cultural Context

### SAK EMKM Compliance for Mikro

**What:** Indonesian accounting standard for micro businesses (Standar Akuntansi Keuangan Entitas Mikro, Kecil, dan Menengah)

**Why It Matters:** Banks require SAK EMKM-compliant financials for KUR loans (Kredit Usaha Rakyat)

**R2c Advantage:**
> "R2c COGS reports follow SAK EMKM format. When you apply for Rp 50M KUR loan to expand, your financials are ready. No scrambling to reconstruct costs from memory."

---

### "Naik Kelas" (Social Climbing) Motivation

**Cultural Context:** Indonesian entrepreneurs aspire to move from informal  ->  formal business status

**Phase 1 (Informal):** Warung, no accounting, cash-only
**Phase 2 (Semi-Formal):** Registered business, uses POS, wants proper accounting  -> **R2c sweet spot**
**Phase 3+ (Formal):** Multiple outlets, hires accountant, full ERP

**R2c Message:**
> "You've upgraded from notebook to POS system (Naik Kelas). Next step: Upgrade from guessing costs to knowing exactly (Naik Kelas lagi). R2c helps you run like a professional business."

---

### WhatsApp-First Support Culture

**Cultural Reality:** Indonesians prefer WhatsApp over email for business communication

**R2c Differentiation:**
- Premium tier includes WhatsApp support (<24h response)
- Quick ingredient logging via WhatsApp bot (future feature)
- Community WhatsApp group for recipe sharing among R2c users

**Message:**
> "Email support is slow and formal (bikin ribet). We use WhatsApp because it's fast and friendly. Send 'Tepung naik harga jadi Rp 16K/kg'  ->  We help you update recipes immediately."

## Brand Voice Guidelines

**Tone:** Helpful consultant, not salesy software

**Do's:**
- Use simple Indonesian terms (HPP = Harga Pokok Penjualan, not "Cost of Goods Sold")
- Show real examples (Rp amounts, actual menu items)
- Acknowledge pain ("We know recipe costing is tedious")
- Celebrate wins ("You just saved Rp 500K by fixing that price!")

**Don'ts:**
- Don't be overly technical ("weighted average cost"  ->  "harga rata-rata")
- Don't oversell ("Revolutionary!"  ->  "Helpful tool")
- Don't shame current methods ("Excel is stupid"  ->  "Excel works but takes time")
