## D1 Data Methodology

**Sources:** 10K+ locations, Rp 50T GMV, 100M+ transactions/month
**Anonymization:** Strip PII → Aggregate (min 10 locs) → Add noise (±5%) → Legal review
**Quality:** Outlier detection, cross-validation, customer opt-in required
**Limitations:** Sample bias (tech-savvy), not comprehensive, point-in-time
