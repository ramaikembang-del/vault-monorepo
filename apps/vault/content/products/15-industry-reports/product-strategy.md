# Industry Reports Product Strategy
**F&B Market Intelligence Publications**

**Product Code:** D4 (Pillar 4 - Defense)
**Target Segment:** Investors, consultants, industry analysts, large chains
**Pricing:** Free summary, Rp 500K-1M per deep-dive report

---

## Purpose

Monetize aggregated platform data as industry intelligence. Positions company as "Indonesia F&B data authority" while creating competitive moat.

## Key Products

| Report Type | Price | Content |
|-------------|-------|---------|
| Quarterly Summary | Free | Market trends, headline stats |
| Deep-Dive Report | Rp 500K-1M | City analysis, segment breakdowns |
| Custom Research | Rp 2-5M | Tailored analysis for specific clients |
| Annual Almanac | Rp 2M | Comprehensive yearly review |

## Market Sizing

- **TAM:** Investors, banks, consultants, franchises, suppliers
- **SAM:** 500+ potential report buyers (companies/individuals)
- **SOM:** 100-200 report sales by Year 3

## Report Topics (Examples)

- **"State of Jakarta F&B Q1 2025"** — Revenue trends, openings/closures
- **"SME Food Cost Benchmarks"** — What restaurants pay for ingredients
- **"Staff Turnover in Indonesian F&B"** — Labor market analysis
- **"Consumer Spending Patterns"** — Basket size, frequency, preferences

## Strategic Value

**Moat Creation:**
- Only source of this data in Indonesia
- Requires 100+ customers to generate meaningful insights
- Competitors need years to replicate

**Brand Building:**
- Press picks up our data → Free marketing
- Establishes thought leadership
- Creates premium perception

## Revenue Projections

- Year 1: Rp 10M (20 reports sold, building credibility)
- Year 2: Rp 100M (200 reports, custom research starts)
- Year 3: Rp 300M (scale with data quality)

## Dependencies

- Requires customer base (100+ contributors for valid data)
- Requires data quality controls
- Requires research/writing capability

---

**Status:** Backlog (Year 2+)
**Dependencies:** Full SaaS customer base
**Build Estimate:** Ongoing content production
