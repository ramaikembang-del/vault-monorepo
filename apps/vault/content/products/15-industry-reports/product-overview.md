## Data Methodology

### Data Collection

**Sources:**
- Sales data from 10,000+ F&B locations (PWBI customers)
- Represents Rp 50T+ annual GMV
- 70% cafÃ©s/restaurants, 20% bakeries, 10% other

**Coverage:**
- Jakarta: 60%
- Surabaya: 15%
- Bandung: 10%
- Bali: 10%
- Others: 5%

### Anonymization

**Process:**
1. Strip PII (names, specific locations)
2. Aggregate (minimum 10 locations per data point)
3. Noise addition (differential privacy)
4. Review (legal & ethics check)

**Example:**
- [x] "Kopi Kenangan Senopati sold 1,000 cups"
- [x] "Premium coffee shops in South Jakarta: 950-1,050 cups/day average"

### Quality Assurance

**Validation:**
- Outlier detection (remove anomalies)
- Cross-reference (does it match industry knowledge?)
- Customer opt-in (customers must consent to data usage)

**Limitations disclosed:**
- Sample bias (PWBI customers skew tech-savvy)
- Not comprehensive (10K out of 250K F&B in Indonesia)
- Point-in-time (monthly snapshots)
