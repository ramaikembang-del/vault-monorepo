# 15 - Industry Reports

**Product Code:** B2 (Business Pillar - Data Products)
**Sprint:** Sprint 4+ (Post-Scale)
**Status:** Planning
**Owner:** Product / Research

---

## Overview

Industry Reports is a subscription-based data product that provides aggregated industry benchmarks, market trends, and competitive intelligence for F&B businesses in Indonesia. Leverages anonymized data from the PWBI platform to generate valuable market insights.

**Target Market:**
- F&B operators wanting to benchmark against industry
- Investors and VCs analyzing F&B market
- Consultants advising F&B clients
- Media and research firms

## Report Types

### Market Benchmarking Reports
- Revenue benchmarks by segment
- Average transaction values
- Customer frequency patterns
- Staff productivity metrics

### Trend Analysis Reports
- Seasonal patterns in F&B
- Menu item popularity trends
- Payment method adoption
- Technology adoption curves

### Competitive Intelligence
- Market share by segment
- Pricing analysis
- Service model trends
- Growth rates by maturity phase
