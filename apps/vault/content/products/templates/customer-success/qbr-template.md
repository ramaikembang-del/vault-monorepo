## QBR Success Indicators

**Good QBR:**
- Customer actively engaged
- Shared specific wins/results
- Clear goals for next quarter
- Renewal risk: Low

**Concerning QBR:**
- Low engagement
- Couldn't articulate value
- Many complaints, few wins
- Renewal risk: High → Take action!
