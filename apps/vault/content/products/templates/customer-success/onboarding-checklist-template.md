# Onboarding Checklist Template

**Customer:** ________________
**Start Date:** ________________
**CSM:** ________________
**Product:** ________________

---

## Day 1: Welcome

- [ ] Welcome email sent (within 1 hour of signup)
- [ ] Account credentials sent
- [ ] Schedule kickoff call (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Add to CRM with health score tracking

---

## Days 2-7: Setup Week

### Kickoff Call (30 min)
- [ ] Introductions (CSM + customer team)
- [ ] Confirm goals and success criteria
- [ ] Walk through setup process
- [ ] Schedule next check-in

### Technical Setup
- [ ] POS integration connected OR CSV upload process established
- [ ] User accounts created for team members
- [ ] Permissions configured
- [ ] Data syncing successfully

### Training
- [ ] Product walkthrough completed
- [ ] Sent training resources (videos, docs)
- [ ] Customer can navigate basic features

**Goal:** "Aha moment" by Day 7 (customer sees value)

---

## Days 8-14: Adoption

- [ ] Daily usage confirmed (check login activity)
- [ ] Customer using 3+ key features
- [ ] Address any questions/blockers
- [ ] Send tips email ("Did you know...")
- [ ] Schedule Week 2 check-in call

---

## Days 15-30: Value Realization

### Week 3
- [ ] Customer achieving initial goals
- [ ] Using product without support prompts
- [ ] Invite to webinar/office hours (if available)

### Week 4
- [ ] 30-day success review call
- [ ] Review progress against goals
- [ ] Identify next use cases
- [ ] Ask for feedback/NPS
- [ ] Transition to standard cadence (monthly check-ins)

---

## Success Criteria (30 Days)

**Customer successfully onboarded when:**
- Logging in weekly minimum
- Using 3+ reports regularly
- Team trained and using product
- Achieving stated goals (or progressing)
- Health score: Green

---

## Red Flags (Escalate Immediately)

- No logins after Day 7
- POS integration failing
- Customer dissatisfied in calls
- Not responding to outreach

**Action:** Escalate to CS Manager
