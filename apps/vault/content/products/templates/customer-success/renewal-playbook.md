# Renewal Playbook

**Goal:** Achieve >90% renewal rate

---

## Timeline

**90 days before renewal:** Check health score
**60 days before:** Outreach begins
**30 days before:** Decision call
**7 days before:** Contract sent
**Renewal date:** Renewed or churned

---

## 60 Days Before Renewal

**Health Check:**
- Review health score
- Analyze usage last 90 days
- Review support tickets
- Check payment history

**If GREEN:** Standard renewal process
**If YELLOW/RED:** Save plan (see Churn Prevention Playbook)

---

## Standard Renewal Process (GREEN customers)

**Day -60: Initial Outreach**

Email template:
```
Subject: [Customer Name] Renewal - Let's Chat

Hi [Name],

Hope you're doing well! I noticed your PWBI subscription renews on [date].

Before we get into renewal details, I'd love to schedule a quick call to:
1. Review how PWBI has helped you this year
2. Discuss your goals for next year
3. Explore how we can support you better

15 minutes this week?
[Calendar link]
```

**Day -45: Value Review Call**

Discuss:
- Wins and ROI delivered
- Usage and adoption
- Goals for next year
- Any concerns or requests
- Pricing for renewal

**Day -30: Proposal**

Send renewal proposal:
- Current plan recap
- Renewal terms (same price or adjusted)
- Add-ons/upsells if applicable
- Contract for signature

**Day -14: Follow-up**

If not signed:
- Call to discuss questions
- Address concerns
- Set deadline

**Day -7: Final Push**

If still not signed:
- Escalate to manager if needed
- Offer incentives (early renewal discount, etc.)
- Make it easy to sign

**Renewal Date:**
- Contract signed → Celebrate!
- Not signed → Move to churn prevention

---

## Upsell Opportunities

**During renewal, explore:**
- Additional users
- Higher tier
- New products (R2 modules if using E1)
- Multi-year contract (discount incentive)

**Timing:** Bring up after confirming renewal, not before

---

## Pricing Discussions

**Price increase:**
- Communicate 90 days in advance min
- Show value delivered
- Phase in gradually (5-10% max)

**Discount requests:**
- Multi-year: 15-20% off
- Expansion: Bundle pricing
- Prepay annual: 17% off

---

## Success Metrics

**Target:** >90% renewal rate
**Track:** Revenue retention (including upsells)
