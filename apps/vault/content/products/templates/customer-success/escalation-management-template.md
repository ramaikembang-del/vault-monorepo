# Escalation Management Template

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## When to Escalate

**Escalate immediately if:**
- Customer threatens to churn
- Legal/compliance issue
- Data breach or security incident
- Executive-level complaint
- Payment dispute >Rp 10M
- Product bug affecting multiple customers

---

## Escalation Levels

### Level 1: CSM → CS Manager
**For:** Yellow health scores, moderate issues
**Response time:** 4 hours
**Who:** Your direct manager

### Level 2: CS Manager → VP CS
**For:** Red health scores, high-value accounts
**Response time:** 2 hours
**Who:** VP Customer Success

### Level 3: VP CS → Executive Team
**For:** Strategic accounts, legal issues, PR risk
**Response time:** 1 hour
**Who:** CEO/CFO/CTO as appropriate

---

## Escalation Process

### 1. Document the Issue

**Use this template:**
```
**Customer:** [Name]
**ARR:** Rp [X]
**Health Score:** [Color/Score]
**Issue Summary:** [1-2 sentences]
**Impact:** [Business impact, urgency]
**Root Cause:** [If known]
**Attempted Resolution:** [What you've tried]
**Customer Ask:** [What they want]
**Recommendation:** [Your proposed solution]
**Timeline:** [When does this need resolution?]
```

### 2. Notify Escalation Chain

**Slack:** Post in #escalations channel
**Email:** CC manager + escalation level
**CRM:** Update account with escalation flag

### 3. Hand-off Call

**Brief the person taking over:**
- Full context
- Customer personality/communication style
- What NOT to say
- Desired outcome

### 4. Follow Through

**Don't disappear:**
- Stay involved
- Follow up on commitments
- Close the loop with customer

---

## Escalation Response Templates

### To Customer (Escalating Internally)

```
Hi [Name],

I understand this is important and I want to make sure we handle it properly.

I'm bringing in [Manager Name/Title] who has more authority to [resolve/decide/commit].

They'll reach out within [timeframe]. In the meantime, I'm here for any questions.
```

### To Manager (Internal Escalation)

```
Subject: ESCALATION - [Customer Name] - [Issue]

Customer: [Name]
ARR: Rp [X]
Issue: [Summary]
Impact: [Churn risk/Revenue impact]
Details: [See template above]
Needed by: [Date/Time]

I've tried: [Your attempts]
I recommend: [Solution]

Can you help?
```

---

## De-escalation

**Once resolved:**
1. Confirm resolution with customer
2. Document outcome in CRM
3. Thank everyone involved
4. Post-mortem: What can we improve?
5. Update playbooks if needed

---

## Escalation Metrics

**Track:**
- Escalations per month
- Resolution time
- Resolution rate (% resolved vs churned)
- Root causes (to prevent future)

**Goal:** Decreasing escalations over time
