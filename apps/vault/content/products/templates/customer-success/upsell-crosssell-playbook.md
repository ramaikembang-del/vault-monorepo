# Upsell/Cross-sell Playbook

**Goal:** 10% of customers expand each quarter

---

## Identifying Opportunities

**Good upsell candidates:**
- Green health score
- High product usage
- Growing business (revenue up, new locations)
- Positive sentiment
- Using product to capacity (hitting limits)

**Red flags (wait):**
- Yellow/red health
- Low usage
- Recent complaints
- Payment issues

---

## Upsell Scenarios

### 1. More Users
**Trigger:** All seats being used
**Pitch:** "I noticed all 5 users are active. Need more seats for your team?"

### 2. Tier Upgrade
**Trigger:** Hitting free/basic tier limits
**Pitch:** "You've maxed out your 3 reports. Want access to 15+ advanced reports?"

### 3. Add Products
**Trigger:** Using E1, asking about accounting features
**Pitch:** "Since you're tracking sales well, want to add R2a P&L to see full profitability?"

### 4. Multi-location
**Trigger:** Customer opens new outlet
**Pitch:** "Congrats on the new location! Want to add it to PWBI for cross-location comparison?"

---

## Approach

**Don't cold pitch.** Wait for natural trigger.

**Steps:**
1. Identify opportunity
2. Prepare value prop (specific to them)
3. Bring up in regular check-in call (not separate "sales call")
4. Show in product if possible
5. Send proposal
6. Close

**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

---

## Pricing

**Expansion pricing:**
- Additional users: Rp 50K/user/month
- Tier upgrade: Price difference
- Add products: Bundle discount (10% off when buying 2+)

---

## Success Metrics

**Target:** 10% MRR expansion per quarter
**Track:** Upsell conversion rate, average expansion size
