# Health Score Framework

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Health Score Components

Track 4 dimensions: Usage, Engagement, Sentiment, Commercial

**Score each 0-25 points, total 100**

---

## 1. Usage Score (0-25 points)

### Login Activity (10 points)
- 10: Daily logins
- 7: 3-4×/week
- 4: Weekly
- 2: 2-3×/month
- 0: <2×/month

### Feature Adoption (10 points)
- 10: Using 5+ reports regularly
- 7: Using 3-4 reports
- 4: Using 1-2 reports
- 0: Minimal usage

### User Seats Utilized (5 points)
- 5: >80% of seats active
- 3: 50-80% active
- 1: 20-50% active
- 0: <20% active

**Usage Score:** ___/25

---

## 2. Engagement Score (0-25 points)

### Responsiveness (10 points)
- 10: Responds to emails within 24hr
- 7: Within 48hr
- 4: Within week
- 0: Rarely responds

### Participation (10 points)
- 10: Joins QBRs, office hours, webinars
- 7: Joins most meetings
- 4: Joins occasionally
- 0: Rarely participates

### Proactiveness (5 points)
- 5: Asks questions, requests features, shares feedback
- 2: Passive, only responds when asked
- 0: Disengaged

**Engagement Score:** ___/25

---

## 3. Sentiment Score (0-25 points)

### NPS/CSAT (15 points)
- 15: Promoter (NPS 9-10)
- 10: Passive (NPS 7-8)
- 5: Detractor (NPS 0-6)
- 0: Refused to give score

### Support Ticket Tone (10 points)
- 10: Positive,friendly, grateful
- 7: Neutral, factual
- 4: Frustrated but polite
- 0: Angry, threatening to churn

**Sentiment Score:** ___/25

---

## 4. Commercial Score (0-25 points)

### Payment Status (15 points)
- 15: Always on time
- 10: Occasionally late (<7 days)
- 5: Frequently late (7-30 days)
- 0: >30 days overdue

### Growth Potential (10 points)
- 10: Expanding (more users, products, locations)
- 7: Stable, potential for growth
- 4: Stable, unlikely to grow
- 0: Downsizing or churning

**Commercial Score:** ___/25

---

## Overall Health Score

**Total Score:** ___/100

### Health Tiers

 **GREEN (80-100):** Healthy
- Low churn risk
- High renewal likelihood
- Upsell opportunities
- Action: Maintain, ask for referrals

 **YELLOW (50-79):** At Risk
- Medium churn risk
- Needs attention
- Action: Increase engagement, address concerns

 **RED (<50):** Critical
- High churn risk
- Immediate intervention needed
- Action: Escalate to manager, save plan

---

## Tracking Frequency

**Update health scores:**
- Manual check: Weekly
- Automated (if available): Daily
- Deep review: Monthly in 1-on-1s with manager

---

## Health Score Actions

### GREEN Customers
- Monthly check-ins (maintain relationship)
- Quarterly QBRs
- Ask for case studies, testimonials, referrals
- Identify upsell opportunities
- Invite to beta programs

### YELLOW Customers
- Bi-weekly check-ins
- Investigate: What changed?
- Address concerns proactively
- Offer additional training/resources
- Set specific success metrics

### RED Customers
- **Immediate action required**
- Weekly check-ins minimum
- Escalate to CS Manager
- Create save plan
- Consider executive sponsor involvement
- Address root cause urgently

---

## Red Flag Indicators (Auto-RED)

Regardless of score, mark RED if:
- Mentioned "cancel" or "churn"
- Payment >60 days overdue
- No logins in 30 days
- Formal complaint or legal threat
- Key stakeholder left company

**Commercial:** 25/25

- Growth: Adding 2nd location (10)

**Total: 83/100 → GREEN**
