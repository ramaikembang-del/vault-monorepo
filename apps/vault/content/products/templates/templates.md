## How to Use Templates

1. **Copy the template** to your working directory
2. **Rename** to match your specific use case
3. **Fill in** all sections marked with `[TODO]` or `[PLACEHOLDER]`
4. **Remove** any sections not relevant to your use case
5. **Save** and share with stakeholders
