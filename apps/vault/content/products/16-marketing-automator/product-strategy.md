# Waste Tracking Product Strategy
**Micro-SaaS for Food Waste Reduction**

**Product Code:** A4e (Pillar 1 - Acquisition)
**Target Segment:** Phase 1-2, restaurants with high food costs
**Pricing:** Rp 25K/month

---

## Purpose

Help restaurants track and reduce food waste. Simple logging tool that reveals hidden costs and drives margin improvement.

## Key Features

- **Daily waste log:** Quick entry for discarded items
- **Waste category tracking:** Spoilage, overproduction, mistakes, theft
- **Cost calculator:** Waste Ã— ingredient cost = money lost
- **Trend analysis:** Which days/items have most waste
- **Reduction goals:** Set targets, track progress

## Market Sizing

- **TAM:** 100,000 restaurants
- **SAM:** 15,000 restaurants (high food cost %, aware of waste problem)
- **SOM:** 300-500 subscribers by Year 2

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Basic | Rp 25K/month | Daily logging, basic reports |
| Pro | Rp 50K/month | Trend analysis, goals, ingredient-level tracking |

## Pain Points Solved

- **"I don't know how much food we throw away"**  ->  Visibility
- **"My food cost is too high"**  ->  Identify waste drivers
- **"Staff are careless with ingredients"**  ->  Accountability through logging

## Revenue Projections

- Year 1: Rp 5M (200 subscribers Ã— Rp 25K avg)
- Year 2: Rp 15M (600 subscribers)
- Year 3: Rp 30M (1,200 subscribers + Pro tier)

## Upgrade Path

 ->  Waste data + revenue data  ->  Cost optimization analytics
 ->  Want inventory management  ->  Full SaaS Advanced (inventory module)

## Data Synergy

Waste data feeds into:
- Food Cost % calculations (actual vs theoretical)
- Inventory analytics
- Profitability by menu item
