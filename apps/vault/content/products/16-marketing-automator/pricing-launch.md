# A4e Marketing Automator - Pricing & Launch Plan

**Product:** A4e Marketing Automator
**Pricing Model:** Base subscription + pay-per-message
**Launch:** Q1 2025

## Pricing Structure

### Base Platform Fee

**Annual Subscription:** Rp 500,000/year
**Monthly Equivalent:** Rp 42,000/month

**What's Included:**
- Unlimited email campaigns
- Customer database (up to 50,000 contacts)
- All campaign templates (Birthday, Win-Back, Loyalty, etc.)
- Social media scheduler (Instagram + Facebook)
- RFM segmentation engine
- Analytics & reporting dashboards
- A/B testing
- Customer support (email + chat)

### Messaging Credits (Pay-as-you-go)

**Email:** Rp 0 (unlimited, included in base)

**SMS:** Rp 100 per message
- Delivery rate: 95%+
- Open rate: 40-60%
- Best for: Urgent notifications, order confirmations

**WhatsApp:** Rp 50 per message
- Delivery rate: 98%+
- Open rate: 70-90%
- Best for: Promotions, customer engagement (preferred in Indonesia)

### Prepaid Messaging Packages (Discounted)

**WhatsApp Credits:**
- 1,000 messages: Rp 40,000 (Rp 40/msg, save 20%)
- 5,000 messages: Rp 175,000 (Rp 35/msg, save 30%)
- 10,000 messages: Rp 300,000 (Rp 30/msg, save 40%)
- 25,000 messages: Rp 625,000 (Rp 25/msg, save 50%)

**SMS Credits:**
- 1,000 messages: Rp 85,000 (Rp 85/msg, save 15%)
- 5,000 messages: Rp 400,000 (Rp 80/msg, save 20%)

**Credits never expire** (rollover monthly)

## ROI Calculation

### Value Delivered

**Repeat Rate Increase:**
- Baseline: 25% (1 in 4 customers return)
- With A4e: 35% (1 in 3 customers return)
- Improvement: +10 percentage points (40% relative increase)

**Revenue Impact (Small Café Example):**
- Monthly customers: 500
- Average spend per visit: Rp 100K
- Additional repeat customers: 50/month (10% of 500)
- **New monthly revenue: Rp 5M (50 × Rp 100K)**
- **Annual: Rp 60M**

**Time Saved:**
- Manual marketing: 3-5 hours/week
- Value: Rp 150K/hour (manager time)
- **Annual savings: Rp 9M** (3 hrs × Rp 150K × 52 weeks)

**Total Annual Value:**
- Revenue: Rp 60M
- Time: Rp 9M
- **Total: Rp 69M/year**

**Cost:** Rp 800K/year
**ROI:** 86x (conservative estimate)

**Optimistic Scenario:** Repeat rate increases to 45%
**Value:** Rp 100M+/year
**ROI:** 125x

### Break-Even Analysis

**Monthly cost:** Rp 67K (small café)
**Need to generate:** Rp 67K in additional revenue
**Requires:** 1 additional customer/month (at Rp 100K spend)

**Conclusion:** If campaigns bring back even 1 customer per month, you break even. Typical result: 10-50 customers/month.

## Launch Plan

### Phase 1: Beta (January 2025)

**Target:** 100 beta customers

**Offer:**
- 50% discount (Rp 250K/year)
- Unlimited WhatsApp credits (first 3 months)
- Premium support
- Direct feedback channel to product team

**Criteria:**
- F&B business with 500+ customer database
- Active social media presence
- Willing to provide feedback
- Testimonial agreement

**Goals:**
- Validate product-market fit
- Collect case studies
- Identify bugs/issues
- Refine features

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (February-March 2025)

**Target:** 500 customers

**Offer:**
- Early adopter price: Rp 400K/year (20% discount, lifetime)
- Standard messaging rates
- Priority onboarding

**Marketing:**
- Beta customer testimonials
- Webinars ("How to 3x Your Repeat Rate")
- Partnership with POS vendors (Moka, Olsera)
- Content marketing (blog, YouTube)

**Goals:**
- Reach Rp 200M ARR
- Prove scalability
- Train support team

### Phase 3: General Availability (April 2025+)

**Target:** 2,000 customers (Year 1)

**Pricing:** Standard (Rp 500K/year)

**Marketing Channels:**
- POS vendor partnerships (bundled offering)
- Direct sales (outbound to F&B businesses)
- Content marketing (SEO, YouTube)
- Referral program (refer 3 businesses, get 1 year free)
- Industry events (F&B conferences)

**Goals:**
- Rp 1B ARR (Year 1)
- 80% customer satisfaction
- <5% churn rate
- 10+ case studies

### Phase 4: Scale (2026+)

**Target:** 10,000 customers

**Revenue Target:** Rp 5B+ ARR

## Frequently Asked Pricing Questions

**Q: Can I switch from annual to monthly?**
A: After annual expires, yes. Can't switch mid-year.

**Q: What if I run out of messaging credits?**
A: Auto-top-up (if enabled) or campaigns pause until you add credits.

**Q: Do unused credits expire?**
A: No, rollover indefinitely.

**Q: Can I get a discount?**
A: Early adopters (Q1 2025): 20% off lifetime. Multi-location: Volume discounts. No negotiation on single-location pricing.

**Q: Is there a setup fee?**
A: No. Onboarding & training included.

**Q: Hidden fees?**
A: No. Only base + messaging. No transaction fees, no per-user fees, no surprise charges.
