**Sprint:** Sprint 4+ (Post-Scale)
**Status:** Planning
**Owner:** Product

## Overview

Marketing Automator is a future product designed to help F&B businesses automate customer marketing campaigns based on behavioral data and analytics insights. Enables targeted promotions, loyalty programs, and customer re-engagement campaigns.

**Target Market:** Phase 4-5 operators with established customer databases looking to increase customer lifetime value through targeted marketing

## Key Features (Planned)

### Automated Campaigns
- Re-engagement campaigns for lapsed customers
- Birthday/anniversary promotions
- VIP customer rewards
- New customer onboarding sequences

### Behavioral Triggers
- Purchase frequency drop-off alerts
- Average spend decrease notifications
- Milestone celebrations (10th visit, etc.)
- Seasonal promotions

### Channel Support
- WhatsApp Business API integration
- Email marketing
- SMS campaigns
- In-app notifications (if applicable)
