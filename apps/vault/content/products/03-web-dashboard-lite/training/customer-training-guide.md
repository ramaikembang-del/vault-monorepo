## Quick Start (15 minutes)

### Step 1: Sign Up
1. Go to app.pwbi.id/signup
2. Enter email, create password
3. Confirm email
4. Log in

### Step 2: Connect POS
**Supported POS:** Pawoon, Moka, Qasir, Olsera

1. Dashboard → Settings → Integrations
2. Select your POS
3. Click "Connect"
4. Log in to your POS (authorize PWBI)
5. Select outlets to sync
6. First sync starts (5-10 min)

**No POS?** Use Manual Entry or CSV Upload

### Step 3: Explore Dashboard
**Home:** Overview widgets (today's sales, top items)
**Reports:** 15+ pre-built reports
**Settings:** Team, outlets, billing

**First Win:** Check today's sales in real-time!

## Troubleshooting

**POS not syncing:**
- Check POS is online
- Reconnect in Settings → Integrations
- Verify API credentials valid
- Last resort: Disconnect & reconnect

**Numbers look wrong:**
- Verify date range
- Check outlet filter
- Compare to same metric in POS
- Contact support with screenshots

**Can't log in:**
- Reset password
- Check email for verification
- Clear browser cache
- Try different browser
