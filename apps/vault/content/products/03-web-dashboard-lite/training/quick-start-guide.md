# E1 Dashboard Lite - Quick Start Guide

**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Goal:** See your first real-time dashboard

---

## Step 1: Sign Up (2 min)

1. Go to app.pwbi.id/signup
2. Enter email, create password
3. Click confirmation link in email
4. Log in

---

## Step 2: Connect Your POS (5 min)

**Supported:** Pawoon, Moka, Qasir, Olsera

1. Dashboard → Settings → Integrations
2. Click your POS system
3. Click "Connect"
4. Login to your POS (authorize PWBI)
5. Select outlets to sync
6. First sync starts automatically (5-10 min)

**No POS?** Use Manual Entry or CSV Upload option

---

## Step 3: Explore Dashboard (5 min)

**Home Page:**
- Today's sales (real-time!)
- Top 5 selling items
- Payment breakdown
- Quick stats

**Reports Menu:**
- Sales trends
- Item performance
- Financial summary

**Try This:**
- Change date range (top-right)
- Filter by outlet (if multiple)
- Click on an item to see details

---

## First Win!

Check **Home → Today's Sales** - See your revenue update in real-time!

## Common Setup Issues

**POS won't connect:**
- Check POS is online
- Verify you're admin in POS
- Try disconnect/reconnect

**No data showing:**
- Wait for first sync to complete (can take 10 min)
- Check date filter
- Verify POS has transactions in selected period
