# E1: Dashboard KPIs
**Purpose:** Health indicators for E1 product success

---

## North Star Metric

**Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**
- Ultimate success measure
- Target Month 6: Rp 50M
- Formula: # Premium × Rp 250K + # Pro × Rp 350K

---

## Core KPIs (Weekly Review)

### 1. Activation Rate
**Target:** 60-70%
**Formula:** Onboarding completed / Signups
**Green:** >65% | Yellow: 50-65% | Red: <50%

### 2. Engagement Rate (WAU)
**Target:** 50%
**Formula:** Weekly active / Total activated
**Green:** >50% | Yellow: 30-50% | Red: <30%

### 3. Free→Paid Conversion
**Target:** 8-10% (cumulative Month 2-6)
**Formula:** Paying customers / All-time signups
**Green:** >8% | Yellow: 5-8% | Red: <5%

### 4. MRR Growth
**Target:** +20% MoM
**Formula:** (MRR this month - last month) / last month
**Green:** >20% | Yellow: 10-20% | Red: <10%

### 5. Churn Rate (Paid)
**Target:** <10%/month
**Formula:** Cancellations / Active subscriptions
**Green:** <10% | Yellow: 10-15% | Red: >15%

---

## Supporting Metrics

### 6. Net Revenue Retention (NRR)
**Target:** 100%+ (negative churn via upsells)
**Formula:** (MRR + Upgrades - Downgrades - Churn) / Starting MRR

### 7. Trial→Paid Conversion
**Target:** 60-70%
**Formula:** Trial starts / Paid conversions

### 8. Health Score Average
**Target:** 65+ (all users)
**Measure:** Avg health score (0-100)
