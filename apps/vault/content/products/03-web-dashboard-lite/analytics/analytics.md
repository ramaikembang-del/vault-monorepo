## Metrics Philosophy

**Focus on leading indicators:**
- Activation rate ? Predicts engagement
- Engagement ? Predicts conversion
- Health scores ? Predicts churn

**Not vanity metrics:**
- Signups alone don't matter (need activation)
- Opens don't matter (need data input + value realization)
