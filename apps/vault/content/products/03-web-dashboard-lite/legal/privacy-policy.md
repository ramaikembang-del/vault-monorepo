## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Our Commitment to Privacy

Power BI FnB Growth ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use E1 Web Dashboard Lite ("Service", "E1", "Application").

**Key Principles:**
- **Transparency:** We clearly explain what data we collect and why
- **Minimal Collection:** We only collect data necessary to provide the Service
- **User Control:** You control your data and can request access, correction, or deletion
- **Security:** We use industry-standard encryption and security measures
- **No Selling:** We NEVER sell your personal or business data to third parties

### 1.2 Legal Basis

This Privacy Policy complies with:
- **Indonesia PDP Law 2022** (UU PDP No. 27 Tahun 2022) - Primary legal framework
- **GDPR** (EU General Data Protection Regulation) - International best practices
- **Indonesia Ministry of Communication Regulation No. 20/2016** - Electronic system security

**Your Rights:** Under Indonesia PDP Law 2022, you have the right to:
- Know what personal data we collect
- Access and correct your personal data
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Withdraw consent for data processing
- Lodge complaints with Indonesia's PDP Authority (Lembaga Pelindungan Data Pribadi)

### 1.3 Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:
- E1 Web Dashboard Lite desktop application (Electron)
- E1 Web Dashboard (browser version)
- E1 cloud sync services (Supabase)
- E1 website and marketing materials
- Customer support interactions (WhatsApp, email)

**Not Covered:**
- Third-party POS systems you connect to E1 (governed by their own privacy policies)
- Third-party payment processors (Midtrans - see Section 5)

**What We Collect:**

- Phone number (required for WhatsApp support - Starter/Pro only)

**Why We Collect:**

**Legal Basis:** Contractual necessity (required to provide the Service)

**Retention:**
- Active accounts: Indefinitely while account is active
- Cancelled accounts: 90 days after cancellation (for re-activation), then permanently deleted

### 2.2 Business Data (Transaction Analytics)

**What We Collect:**
- POS transaction data (date, time, amount, payment method, product name)
- Sales metrics (daily revenue, transaction count, average transaction value)
- Product performance data (top-selling items, low-performing items)
- Customer traffic patterns (peak hours, slow periods)

**What We DO NOT Collect:**
- Customer names or personal information from your POS system
- Customer phone numbers or email addresses
- Customer payment card details (we never see or store card numbers)
- Employee personal information

**Why We Collect:**
- Provide analytics dashboards and business intelligence
- Calculate metrics (e.g., revenue growth, transaction trends)
- Generate reports and export data

**Legal Basis:** Contractual necessity (core function of the Service)

**Storage Location:**
- **Free Tier:** Local SQLite database on your device only (never leaves your computer)
- **Starter/Pro:** Optional cloud sync via Supabase (Indonesia Jakarta region, encrypted)

**Retention:**
- **Free:** 7 days (auto-delete)
- **Starter:** 30 days active + 90 days post-cancellation
- **Pro:** 90 days active + 90 days post-cancellation

### 2.3 Usage and Analytics Data

**What We Collect:**
- Application usage patterns (e.g., "User opened Revenue Dashboard 5 times this week")
- Feature usage (e.g., "User exported data to Excel 3 times")
- Error logs and crash reports (technical diagnostics)
- Device information (OS version, screen resolution, app version)
- Performance metrics (page load times, dashboard render speed)

**What We DO NOT Collect:**
- Screen recordings or screenshots
- Keystroke logging
- Personal conversations or messages
- Data from other applications on your device

**Why We Collect:**
- Improve product performance and user experience
- Identify and fix bugs
- Understand which features are most valuable
- Plan future product development

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (product improvement)

**Data Handling:**

- Example: "30% of users use the Revenue Dashboard daily" (not "User X opened Revenue Dashboard")

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

**Free Tier:**
- **Account data:** Supabase (Indonesia Jakarta region)
- **Transaction data:** Local SQLite database on your device (never uploaded to cloud)
- **Benefit:** Full control, data never leaves your device

**Starter/Pro Tiers:**
- **Account data:** Supabase (Indonesia Jakarta region)
- **Transaction data:**
 - Local SQLite (always available offline)
 - Optional cloud sync via Supabase (Indonesia Jakarta region, encrypted)
- **Benefit:** Cloud backup, multi-device access

**Indonesia PDP Law Compliance:**
- All cloud data stored in Indonesia (Supabase Jakarta region)
- No cross-border data transfer without consent
- Compliant with data localization requirements

### 4.2 Security Measures

**Encryption:**
- **In Transit:** TLS 1.3 (all data encrypted during transmission)
- **At Rest:** AES-256 encryption (cloud-stored data encrypted on servers)
- **Local Data:** OS-level encryption (if you enable device encryption, e.g., BitLocker, FileVault)

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC) for Pro tier multi-user accounts
- Two-factor authentication (2FA) available for all users (optional, recommended)
- Password requirements: Minimum 8 characters, mix of letters/numbers/symbols

**Infrastructure Security:**
- Supabase: SOC 2 Type II certified
- Regular security audits (quarterly penetration testing)
- Automated vulnerability scanning
- DDoS protection via Cloudflare

**Employee Access:**
- Strict "need-to-know" basis (only engineers troubleshooting support tickets)
- All access logged and audited
- Background checks for employees with data access

### 4.3 Data Backup and Disaster Recovery

**Cloud-Synced Data (Starter/Pro):**
- Daily automated backups (retained for 30 days)
- Geo-redundant storage (replicated to 3 availability zones in Indonesia)
- Recovery Point Objective (RPO): 24 hours (max 1 day of data loss)
- Recovery Time Objective (RTO): 4 hours (Service restored within 4 hours of disaster)

**Local Data (Free Tier):**
- No cloud backup (user responsible for device backups)
- Recommendation: Use Windows Backup or macOS Time Machine

### 4.4 Data Breach Protocol

**If a Data Breach Occurs:**
1. **Detection:** Within 24 hours of becoming aware
2. **Containment:** Immediate action to stop breach and secure systems
3. **Notification:**
 - Affected users notified within 72 hours (via email)
 - Indonesia PDP Authority notified within 72 hours (as required by law)
4. **Remediation:** Free credit monitoring or identity theft protection (if personal data compromised)

**What We'll Tell You:**
- What data was compromised
- What we're doing to fix it
- What you should do (e.g., change password)

**Your Rights:** Request detailed incident report via privacy@powerbifnb.com

## 6. Your Privacy Rights (Indonesia PDP Law 2022)

### 6.1 Right to Access

**What You Can Request:**
- Copy of all personal data we hold about you
- How we use your data
- Who we share your data with

**How to Request:**
- Email: privacy@powerbifnb.com with subject "Data Access Request"
- We respond within 14 days with CSV or PDF export

**Cost:** Free (first request per year), Rp 50K for additional requests (administrative cost)

### 6.2 Right to Correction

**What You Can Do:**
- Correct inaccurate account information (Settings → Account → Edit Profile)
- Request correction via privacy@powerbifnb.com if settings not available

**Response Time:** Within 7 days

### 6.3 Right to Deletion ("Right to Be Forgotten")

**What Gets Deleted:**
- Account information (name, email, phone)
- Transaction data (all sales analytics)
- Usage analytics
- Support history

**What We Retain (Legal Requirements):**
- Payment records (7 years - Indonesia tax law)
- Fraud prevention logs (2 years - security)

**How to Request:**
- Settings → Account → Delete Account (immediate self-service)
- Email: privacy@powerbifnb.com with "Delete Account" request

**Timeline:**
- Account deactivated immediately
- Data deleted within 30 days
- Confirmation email sent when deletion complete

**Cannot Be Undone:** Deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed

### 6.4 Right to Data Portability

**What You Can Export:**
- Transaction data (CSV format)
- Account settings (JSON format)
- Usage history (PDF report)

**How to Export:**
- Starter/Pro: Settings → Export → Download Data (immediate)
- Free: Email privacy@powerbifnb.com (manual export within 14 days)

**Format:** Machine-readable CSV (compatible with Excel, Google Sheets, competitor dashboards)

### 6.5 Right to Withdraw Consent

**What You Can Withdraw:**
- Marketing email consent (Settings → Privacy → Email Preferences)
- Usage analytics consent (Settings → Privacy → Usage Analytics)
- Cloud sync consent (Settings → Privacy → Data Sync → Disable)

**Effect:**
- Immediate (applied within 24 hours)
- Does not affect data collected before withdrawal

**Cannot Withdraw:**
- Data processing necessary for the Service (e.g., account creation, billing)

### 6.6 Right to Lodge Complaint

**If You're Not Satisfied:**
- Contact us: privacy@powerbifnb.com (we'll respond within 7 days)
- Lodge complaint with Indonesia PDP Authority:

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### 7.1 Desktop Application (Electron)

**Cookies Used:**
- Session cookies (authentication, keeps you logged in)
- Preference cookies (e.g., dashboard layout, dark mode)

**Third-Party Cookies:** None (desktop app does not use third-party cookies)

**Your Control:**
- Clear cookies via Settings → Privacy → Clear Session Data
- Cookies required for app to function (cannot disable authentication cookies)

### 7.2 Web Dashboard (Browser Version)

**Cookies Used:**
- **Essential:** Session, authentication (cannot disable)
- **Analytics:** Google Analytics (can disable via Settings → Privacy)
- **Preferences:** Dashboard layout, theme

**Third-Party Cookies:**
- Google Analytics (if enabled)
- Cloudflare (DDoS protection, essential)

**Your Control:**
- Browser settings: Block third-party cookies
- Settings → Privacy → Usage Analytics (disable Google Analytics)

### 7.3 Cookie Lifespan

| Cookie Type | Lifespan | Purpose |
|-------------|----------|---------|
| Session | Until logout or 7 days | Keep you logged in |
| Preferences | 1 year | Remember settings (theme, layout) |
| Analytics | 2 years | Track usage patterns (opt-out available) |

**Browser-Level Control:**
- Chrome: Settings → Privacy → Cookies
- Firefox: Settings → Privacy → Cookies and Site Data
- Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
