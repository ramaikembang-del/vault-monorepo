# E1: Pricing Justification (Premium & Pro Tiers)

## Why We Charge (When Free Exists)

**Philosophy:** Gratis selamanya untuk dasar, berbayar untuk professional use.

---

## Tier Breakdown

| Feature | Free (Rp 0) | Premium (Rp 250K/mo) | Pro (Rp 350K/mo) |
|---------|-------------|----------------------|------------------|
| **Metrics** | 6 basic | 12 custom | Unlimited |
| **Data history** | 30 days | 1 year | Unlimited |
| **Users** | 1 | 3 | 5 |
| **Export** | Screenshot only | PDF auto-export | PDF + CSV + API |
| **Outlets** | 1 | 1 (aggregated view) | 2-5 (per-outlet dashboard) |
| **Support** | Email (48h) | WhatsApp (4h SLA) | Priority (1h SLA) + dedicated CSM |

---

## Premium (Rp 250K/month) - Value Justification

### What You Get

**6 additional metrics:**
- Custom calculated metrics (profit margin, waste %, etc.)
- Customer segmentation (new vs repeat, high-value)
- Advanced staff analytics (productivity per shift, sales per hour)
- Inventory turnover
- Food cost %
- Forecasting (next week revenue prediction)

**Extended data history:**
- 1 year vs 30 days
- Year-over-year comparison ("Omzet Ramadan 2025 vs 2023")
- Seasonal trend analysis

**Multi-user access:**
- Owner + manager + admin can login
- Role-based permissions (manager gak bisa lihat profit margin, misalnya)
- Audit log (siapa ubah data kapan)

**PDF auto-export:**
- Weekly/monthly reports auto-generated
- Send via WhatsApp/email to investor/owner
- Professional format (bukan screenshot)

### ROI Breakdown

**Time saved:**
- Manual Excel: 10 jam/bulan → Rp 500K (@ Rp 50K/jam)
- Auto PDF reports: 2 jam/bulan → Rp 100K
- **Total:** Rp 600K/bulan saved

**Better decisions:**
- Identify menu yang rugi (tapi kelihatan laris) → Potensial hemat Rp 1-2M/bulan
- Optimize staff scheduling → Hemat Rp 500K-1M/bulan (less over-staffing)
- **Total:** Rp 1.5-3M potential savings/month

**Cost:** Rp 250K/month
**Value:** Rp 2-4M/month (time + savings)
**ROI:** 8-16x

---

## Pro (Rp 350K/month) - Value Justification

### What You Get (vs Premium)

**Multi-outlet dashboard:**
- 2-5 outlets, each with separate dashboard
- Consolidated view (total across all outlets)
- Per-outlet metrics (which outlet most profitable?)
- Cross-outlet comparison

**Unlimited custom metrics:**
- Build ANY metric you want (metric builder)
- Example: "(Total Sales - COGS - Rent) / Staff Count" = Profit per staff
- Save templates for recurring use

**API access:**
- Pull data into your own systems
- Automate reporting (send to accountant)
- Integrate with accounting software (Accurate, Jurnal)

**Dedicated CSM:**
- 1 person assigned to your account
- Monthly strategy call (30 min)
- Priority feature requests

### ROI Breakdown (Multi-Outlet)

**For 2-3 outlet operators:**

**Time saved (per outlet):**
- 10 jam/bulan × 3 outlets = 30 jam = Rp 1.5M

**Without Pro:**
- Need to manually consolidate data from 3 outlets
- Excel hell: 5+ hours/week just merging data
- **Cost:** Rp 1M/month (staff time)

**With Pro:**
- Auto-consolidated
- 1 dashboard shows all 3 outlets
- **Savings:** Rp 1M/month

**Cost:** Rp 350K/month
**Savings:** Rp 1M/month (min)
**ROI:** 3x (conservative)

## Common Pricing Objections (Answered)

### "Terlalu mahal untuk ukuran saya"

**Response:**
"Boleh tahu omzet per bulan Mas/Mba berapa?

Kalau <Rp 50M/bulan → Pakai Free aja sudah cukup (6 metrik dasar)
Kalau Rp 50-200M/bulan → Premium (Rp 250K) = 0.1-0.5% omzet (affordable)
Kalau >Rp 200M/bulan → Pro (Rp 350K) = <0.2% omzet (very affordable)

Rule of thumb: Kalau software cost <1% omzet + save >5 jam/bulan, it's worth it."

---

### "Kenapa gak gratis aja semua?"

**Response:**
"Kami pengen sustainable:
- Gratis selamanya untuk basic (6 metrik) → Ini promise
- Berbayar untuk premium features → Biar kami bisa hire developer, improve product, kasih support bagus

Kalau semua gratis, kami gak bisa maintain jangka panjang.

Tapi jaminan: 6 metrik dasar TIDAK AKAN PERNAH jadi berbayar."

---

### "Kompetitor lebih murah"

**Response:**
"Boleh tahu kompetitor mana?

Biasanya kalau lebih murah:
- Bukan F&B-specific (generic analytics)
- No support (self-serve only)
- Limited features (basic dashboards)

Kami premium karena:
 F&B-specific (menu analysis, staff, food cost)
 Indonesian market (offline-first, WhatsApp support, Bahasa)
 Dedicated CSM (Pro tier)

Tapi kalau budget ketat, pakai Free tier dulu. Nanti upgrade kalau udah ngerasa value."

## Freemium Conversion Psychology

### Why Users Upgrade (Real Reasons)

**Top 3 triggers:**
1. **Hit limit** (need 7th metric, need >30 day history)
2. **Team need** (manager minta akses dashboard)
3. **Reporting need** (investor minta monthly report PDF)

**Not:** "Want to support the company" (altruistic)

**Implication:** Feature gating must be strategic—gate features they'll NEED after engagement, not want upfront.

## Pricing Strategy (Why These Numbers)

**Rp 250K/month (Premium):**
- ~0.1-0.5% of typical customer revenue (Rp 50-200M/month)
- Comparable to Accurate lower tier (Rp 300K)
- Below "needs approval" threshold for most SMEs (<Rp 500K purchases)

**Rp 350K/month (Pro):**
- Multi-outlet value (Rp 350K < hiring 1 admin to consolidate data @ Rp 4M/month)
- API access premium (~Rp 100K value)
- Still accessible for 2-3 outlet operators

**Annual discounts:**
- 2 months free (16% off) → Incentive for commitment
- Reduces churn (paid upfront = sunk cost)

---

## When to Offer Discounts

### Never Discount (Maintain Value):
- New signups (exception: early access pricing for beta)
- Users who haven't used Free tier yet
- Users asking "Why so expensive?" without trying

### Okay to Discount:
- **Churn prevention:** User canceling after 3+ months → Offer 20% renewal discount
- **Upgrade incentive:** Free user engaged for 2+ months, offer 1 month Premium free trial
- **Multi-outlet deal:** 3+ outlets → Custom pricing (Rp 300K/outlet vs Rp 350K flat)

### Special Offers:
- **Early access (beta):** 50% off for 6 months (Rp 125K/month instead of Rp 250K)
- **Annual commitment:** 2 months free
- **Referral:** Refer 3 paying customers → 1 month free
