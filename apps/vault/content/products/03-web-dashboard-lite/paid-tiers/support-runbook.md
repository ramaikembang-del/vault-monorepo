# Support Runbook - E1 Paid Tiers

**Product:** E1 - Web Dashboard (Starter & Pro)
**Tiers:** Starter (Rp 200K), Pro (Rp 300K)
**Support Channels:** Email (Starter/Pro), WhatsApp (Pro only)
**Owner:** Customer Success Lead

---

## Support Philosophy

**Core Principle:** "Help Users Extract Maximum Value"

Starter/Pro users are paying customers - must feel supported and successful. Focus on showing ROI, not just fixing issues.

**Why This Works:** Users who see value in first 30 days have 60% lower churn. Proactive support = retention.

**6. "Can I Export Data to Excel?"**

- Bank Transfer (BCA, Mandiri, BRI)
- E-Wallet (GoPay, OVO, DANA)
- Credit Card (Visa, Mastercard)

**10. "Is There a Discount for Annual Payment?"**
- Yes! 17% off if pay annually
- Starter: Rp 2M/year (vs Rp 2.4M monthly = save Rp 400K)
- Pro: Rp 3M/year (vs Rp 3.6M monthly = save Rp 600K)
