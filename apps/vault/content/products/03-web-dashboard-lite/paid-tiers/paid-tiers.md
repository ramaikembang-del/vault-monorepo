## Tier Structure

**E1 has 3 tiers:**
1. **Lite (Free)** - 5 core metrics, limited history - Already built
2. **Starter (Rp 200K/month)** - 15 metrics, 30-day history, basic analytics
3. **Pro (Rp 300K/month)** - Unlimited metrics, 90-day history, custom reports

This document covers **Starter** and **Pro** paid tiers only.

---

## E1 Starter Tier (Rp 200K/month)

**Target:** Phase 2 businesses ready to invest in data-driven decisions

**Included Metrics (15 total):**

**Revenue Metrics (5):**
1. Total Revenue
2. Revenue by Channel (Dine-in/Takeaway/Delivery)
3. Average Transaction Value
4. Revenue Growth % (MoM)
5. Top 10 Best-Selling Items

**Customer Metrics (4):**
6. Total Customers
7. New vs Returning Customers
8. Customer Frequency (avg visits/month)
9. Customer Lifetime Value (basic)

**Operational Metrics (4):**
10. Staff Hours & Labor Cost %
11. Peak Hours Analysis
12. Table Turnover Rate
13. Delivery vs Dine-in Split %

**Financial Metrics (2):**
14. Gross Margin % (Revenue - COGS estimate)
15. Month-over-Month Comparison

**Data Retention:** 30 days (vs 7 days free tier)

**Support:** Email support (<24h)

---

## E1 Pro Tier (Rp 300K/month)

**Target:** Phase 2-3 businesses scaling to multiple outlets or serious about optimization

**All Starter features PLUS:**

**Unlimited Metrics:** Access to all 40+ pre-built metrics

**Extended History:** 90-day data retention

**Custom Reports:**
- Build your own metrics (e.g., "Profit per Staff Hour")
- Schedule automated reports (weekly email)
- Export to Excel/PDF with branding

**Advanced Analytics:**
- Cohort analysis (customer retention by signup month)
- Predictive trends (forecast next month revenue)
- A/B testing dashboard (test menu changes)

**Multi-User Access:** Add up to 3 team members (manager, accountant, partner)

**Priority Support:** WhatsApp + Email (<4h response)

## Upgrade Paths

**Free → Starter:**
- Unlock 10 more metrics (5 → 15)
- 4× longer history (7 days → 30 days)
- Month-over-month trending

**Starter → Pro:**
- Unlock all 40+ metrics (15 → unlimited)
- 3× longer history (30 days → 90 days)
- Custom reports + multi-user access
- Priority support

**Bundle with R2a:**
- E1 Starter + R2a (P&L): Rp 250K total (save Rp 50K)
- E1 Pro + R2a + R2c: Rp 450K "Complete CFO Stack" (save Rp 150K)

---

## Pricing Rationale

**Why Rp 200K for Starter?**
- Competitor analytics tools: Rp 300-500K/month (we're 33-60% cheaper)
- Value: 15 metrics = Rp 13.3K per metric (affordable)
- ROI: One insight (e.g., "Stop delivery promo, it's unprofitable") = Rp 500K saved

**Why Rp 300K for Pro?**
- Full-featured analytics platform
- Still 40-60% cheaper than competitors (Tableau, Power BI SaaS)
- Custom reports alone worth Rp 200K (freelance analyst charges Rp 2M/month)
