## Upgrade Conversion Optimization

**Free → Starter Conversion Tactics:**

1. **14-Day Trial:** First 2 weeks free (no credit card required)
2. **First Month 50% Off:** Rp 100K first month, then Rp 200K/month
3. **Annual Plan Discount:** Rp 2M/year (save Rp 400K vs monthly)

**Starter → Pro Conversion Tactics:**

1. **Incremental Upgrade:** "Try Pro features for Rp 50K/month extra (first month only)"
2. **Bundle Incentive:** "Add R2c for only +Rp 100K/month more (vs Rp 100K standalone)"
3. **Free Custom Report Setup:** "Upgrade to Pro, we'll build your first 3 custom reports free"
