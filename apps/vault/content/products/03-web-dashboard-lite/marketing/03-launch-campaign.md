# E1: Sprint 1 Launch Campaign
**Product:** Web Dashboard Lite (Freemium)
**Goal:** 100 free signups in Month 1
**Budget:** Rp 2-5M (optional)
**Timeline:** 4 weeks (Week 1-4 post-product readiness)

---

## Campaign Overview

**Objective:** Drive awareness and free signups for E1 (Web Dashboard Lite) freemium product.

**Target Audience:** Phase 1 Mikro resto owners
- 10-30 staff
- Rp 80-250M annual revenue
- Currently using Excel or basic POS reports
- Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Yogyakarta focus

**Success Metrics:**
- **Week 1:** 30 signups
- **Week 2:** 60 total signups
- **Week 3:** 80 total signups
- **Week 4:** 100+ total signups
- **Activation:** 60% complete onboarding within 24h
- **Engagement:** 50% open app 3+ times in Week 1

---

## Week 1: Pre-Launch Buzz (Awareness)

### Goal
Create anticipation and awareness before official launch.

### Activities

**Day 1-2: Asset Creation**
- Finalize landing page (see: `01-one-pager.md`)
- Create demo video (2min walkthrough)
- Design social graphics (5 templates: problem, solution, features, testimonial, CTA)

**Day 3-4: Soft Launch to Inner Circle**
- WhatsApp broadcast to personal network (50-100 people)
- Message: "Halo! Saya baru buat dashboard gratis untuk resto. Mau coba beta? Free selamanya + dapat premium 3 bulan gratis."
- Goal: 10-15 early signups from warm leads

**Day 5-7: Facebook Group Seeding**
- Post in 5-10 F&B owner groups
- Copy: "Halo! Saya founder [Company], baru launch dashboard gratis buat track omzet & menu. Ada yang mau jadi beta tester? Link: [waitlist]"
- Post timing: 9-11 AM or 8-10 PM (peak engagement)
- Goal: 20-25 total signups by end of Week 1

## Channel Strategy

### Primary Channels (Owned)

**Facebook Groups:**
- Free to post
- High engagement from target audience
- Post frequency: 2-3x/week rotating groups
- No spamming (provide value, not just promotion)

**WhatsApp:**
- Personal touch
- High open rate (>80%)
- Use for 1-on-1 outreach + broadcast (max 256 contacts)

**Instagram:**
- Visual platform
- Good for behind-the-scenes + testimonials
- Stories > Feed posts (higher engagement)

### Secondary Channels (Experiments)

**LinkedIn:**
- Target modern F&B owners (Amanda 2.0 persona)
- Post professional case studies
- B2B tone

**TikTok (if capacity):**
- Short demos (15-30sec)
- "Resto owner hacks" content
- Viral potential but requires different content format

### Paid Channels (Optional)

**Facebook Ads:**
- Budget: Rp 1-2M for Week 4 test
- Targeting: F&B business owners, Jakarta/Bandung, 25-45 age
- Creative: Landing page + demo video
- Goal: <Rp 100K cost per signup

## Success Metrics & Tracking

### Acquisition Metrics
- Waitlist signups (target: 100)
- Landing page conversion rate (target: 5-10%)
- Source breakdown (Facebook vs WhatsApp vs Instagram)

### Activation Metrics
- % complete onboarding (target: 60%)
- Time to first dashboard view (target: <24h)
- % input first data point (target: 50%)

### Engagement Metrics
- % open app 3+ times in Week 1 (target: 50%)
- Average session duration (target: 2+ min)
- Features used distribution (which metrics viewed most?)

### Conversion Metrics (Post-Launch)
- Free → Paid conversion % (target: 5-10% in Month 2)
- Referral rate (target: 10% users refer 1+ friend)

**Dashboard:** Google Analytics + App analytics (see: `analytics/product-metrics.md`)

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Signup Rate (<50 by Week 2)

**Mitigation:**
- Increase Facebook group posting frequency (5 → 10 groups)
- Offer higher incentive (Premium 6 months vs 3 months)
- Run paid ads earlier (Week 2 vs Week 4)

### Risk 2: High Signup, Low Activation (<40%)

**Mitigation:**
- Improve onboarding flow (reduce steps)
- Add video tutorial in-app
- Proactive WhatsApp outreach to inactive users

### Risk 3: High Churn (Users stop using after Week 1)

**Mitigation:**
- Daily engagement nudges (WhatsApp reminders)
- Feature discovery emails
- 1-on-1 check-ins for high-value users

---

## Post-Launch (Month 2+)

**Week 5: Optimization**
- A/B test landing page variations
- Optimize activation flow based on data
- Double down on best-performing channels

**Week 6-8: Scale**
- If >100 signups + >50% engagement → Continue organic growth
- If metrics strong → Increase paid ads budget (Rp 5-10M/month)
- Launch referral program V2 (cash rewards, not just premium)

**Month 3: Conversion Focus**
- Shift from acquisition to conversion (Free → Paid)
- See: `sales/sales-playbook.md` for conversion strategy
