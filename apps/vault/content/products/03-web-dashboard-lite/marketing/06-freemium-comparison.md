## Tier Recommendations

### Who Should Use Free Tier?

**Best For:**
- **Testing E1** before committing to paid tier (14-day trial)
- **Very small businesses** (1-5 transactions/day, <Rp 1M/month revenue)
- **Budget-conscious owners** who can't afford Rp 200K/month yet
- **Seasonal businesses** (only need data during peak season)

**When to Upgrade:**
- You need more than 7 days of history (e.g., "What was last month's revenue?")
- You want cloud backup (computer failure would lose all data)
- You need WhatsApp support (not just community forum)
- You want to export data to Excel (for accountant, tax reporting)

**Free Tier Limitations:**
- [x] Only 7 days of data (can't track monthly trends)
- [x] No cloud backup (data lost if computer fails)
- [x] No export (can't share with accountant)
- [x] No support (community forum only)

**Free Tier Users (Typical Profile):**
- Solo owner, no employees
- Revenue: Rp 500K - 2M/month
- Transactions: 5-20/day
- Just starting out, testing E1

**Best For:**
- **Most F&B SMEs** (restaurants, cafes, bakeries, food trucks)
- **Solo owners or small teams** (1-3 people)

**Use Cases:**
- "I want to see this month's sales compared to last month"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I switch tiers anytime?**
A: Yes, upgrade/downgrade anytime. Upgrade is immediate (prorated), downgrade is effective next renewal.

**Q: What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
A: Data older than new tier's retention period is deleted. Example: Pro (90 days)  ->  Starter (30 days) = data >30 days deleted.

**Q: Is there a contract or commitment?**
A: No, all tiers are month-to-month. Cancel anytime, no penalty.

**Q: Can I try Starter/Pro before paying?**
A: Yes, 14-day free trial (no credit card required). Auto-downgrade to Free after trial if no payment.

**Q: Which tier is most popular?**
A: Starter (70% of paid users). Most F&B SMEs need 30-day history but don't need Pro's advanced features.

**Q: How do I upgrade?**
A: Settings  ->  Account  ->  Subscription  ->  "Upgrade to Starter/Pro". Or email: sales@powerbifnb.com
