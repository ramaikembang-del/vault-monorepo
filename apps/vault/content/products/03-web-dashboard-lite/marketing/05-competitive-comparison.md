**Moka Analytics (Bundled with Moka POS)**

**Strengths:**
- Seamless integration with Moka POS (no manual import)

**Weaknesses:**

- **No Free Tier:** Must pay Rp 350K/month (Moka subscription)

- Pain: Spend 10+ hours/month on manual reports, no growth insights

### 10.1 Why Customers Choose E1(Win Reasons)

**Top 3 Win Reasons (Based on Sales Data):**

**Win Patterns:**
- Multi-outlet franchises (different POS per outlet)
- Rural F&B (unreliable internet)
- Cost-conscious SMEs (price sensitive)

### 10.2 Why Customers Choose Competitors (Loss Reasons)

**Top 3 Loss Reasons:**
1. **Real-Time Sync (40% of losses):** "E1 requires daily CSV import, Moka auto-syncs"
2. **Already Committed to POS Ecosystem (35% of losses):** "We're 100% on Moka, no reason to switch"
3. **Feature Parity (25% of losses):** "Moka has all features I need, no added value from E1"

**Loss Patterns:**
- Single-outlet businesses (already happy with bundled POS analytics)
- Tech-savvy users (don't mind manual CSV import, prefer real-time)

**How to Reduce Losses:**
- **Roadmap:** Add auto-sync via POS APIs (Q3 2025) to reduce "real-time sync" losses
- **Marketing:** Target multi-outlet businesses (where E1 wins 70%+)
- **Messaging:** Emphasize "future-proofing" (what if you switch POS in 2 years?)
