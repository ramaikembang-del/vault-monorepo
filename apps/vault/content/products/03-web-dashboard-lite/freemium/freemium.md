E1 Freemium Model: Free Forever ? Premium/Pro Conversion

## Strategy

**Free Tier Purpose:**
- Acquisition (low barrier to entry)
- Validation (test engagement)
- Community building

**Paid Tiers Purpose:**
- Monetization (Premium/Pro)
- Advanced features
- Professional use cases

## Metrics

- Free user retention (Month 3): **50% target**
- Free?Paid conversion: **8-10% cumulative (Month 2-6)**
- Trial?Paid conversion: **60-70% target**

## Freemium Economics

**Costs:**
- Infrastructure: Rp 50K/user/year (free tier)
- Support: Rp 100K/user/year (avg)
- Total: Rp 150K/user/year

**Revenue (from 100 free users):**
- 8% convert to Premium (Rp 250K/mo) = 8 Rp 2.5M LTV = Rp 20M
- ROI: Rp 20M revenue / Rp 15M cost (100 users) = 1.3x

**Breakeven:** Need 6% conversion rate minimum

**Reality:** 8-10% conversion = profitable freemium
