# E1: Conversion Funnel (Free → Paid)

## Funnel Overview

\\\
100 Free Signups
 ↓ (Activation)
70 Complete Onboarding (70%)
 ↓ (Engagement)
50 Active Week 1 (50%)
 ↓ (Value Realization)
30 Engaged Month 1 (30%)
 ↓ (Feature Gate or Value Trigger)
15 Premium Trial Signup (15%)
 ↓ (Trial → Paid)
8-10 Convert to Paying (50-70% trial conversion)

**Overall: 8-10% Free→Paid in Month 2**
\\\

---

## Stage 1: Activation (Day 0-1)

**Goal:** Install + complete onboarding

**Conversion rate:** 70% (free signup → activated)

**Optimizations:**
- Simple onboarding (2min max)
- Video tutorial in-app
- WhatsApp support if stuck

---

## Stage 2: Engagement (Day 2-7)

**Goal:** Open app 3+ times Week 1

**Conversion rate:** 50% (activated → engaged)

**Optimizations:**
- Daily reminders (WhatsApp 9 AM)
- Milestone celebrations ("3 day streak!")
- Feature discovery nudges

---

## Stage 3: Value Realization (Week 2-4)

**Goal:** User has "Aha!" moment (dashboard provides insight)

**Conversion rate:** 60% (engaged → value realized)

**Signals:**
- Positive sentiment WhatsApp
- Asked analysis question
- Mentioned data-driven decision

**Optimizations:**
- Proactive insights ("Omzet Anda naik 15%!")
- Case study sharing
- Personal check-in call (high-value users)

---

## Stage 4: Conversion Trigger (Week 3-8)

**Goal:** User hits feature gate OR realizes need for Premium

**Conversion rate:** 50% (value realized → trial signup)

**Triggers:**
- Hard gate: Tried to add 7th metric
- Soft nudge: Health score >75 for 2 weeks

**Optimizations:**
- Strategic feature gating
- Personalized upgrade offers
- Free 14-day trial (no credit card)

---

## Stage 5: Trial → Paid (14 days)

**Goal:** Convert trial to paying customer

**Conversion rate:** 60-70% (trial → paid)

**Optimizations:**
- Feature usage in trial (use 3+ premium features)
- Value reinforcement (show ROI during trial)
- Conversion push Day 11-13
- Easy payment (Midtrans, instant activation)
