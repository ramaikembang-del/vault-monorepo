# Hiring Approval Process

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Owner:** HR + Finance

---

## Overview

Process for approving new hires and compensation at PWBI FnB Growth.

---

## Headcount Planning

**Annual Process:**
- Headcount plan approved as part of annual budget
- Each department gets approved headcount slots
- Hiring within approved plan = streamlined approval

**Mid-Year Additions:**
- Requires CEO approval
- Must show business case (revenue impact or critical gap)

---

## Approval Thresholds

| Role Level | Approver | Timeline |
|------------|----------|----------|
| IC (Individual Contributor) | Hiring Manager + HR | 1 week |
| Senior IC | Department Head + HR | 2 weeks |
| Manager | Department Head + CEO | 3 weeks |
| Director+ | CEO + Board | 4-6 weeks |

---

## Hiring Request Process

### Step 1: Submit Headcount Request

**Hiring Manager** creates request with:
- Role title
- Department
- Reporting structure
- Justification (why needed, impact if not filled)
- Proposed compensation range
- Start date (target)

### Step 2: HR Review

**HR validates:**
- Role is in approved headcount plan
- Compensation aligns with bands
- Job description is clear

### Step 3: Approval

**Per threshold table above**

### Step 4: Recruitment

Once approved:
- HR posts job
- Hiring manager interviews
- Offer stage requires final compensation approval

---

## Compensation Approval

**Within Pre-Approved Bands:**
- No additional approval needed
- HR confirms band alignment

**Above Bands (Counter-Offers, Exceptional Candidates):**
- <10% above band: Department Head approval
- >10% above band: CEO approval
- Requires written justification

---

## Compensation Bands (2025)

**Engineering:**
- Junior: Rp 8-12M/month
- Mid: Rp 15-25M/month
- Senior: Rp 30-45M/month
- Lead/Principal: Rp 50-70M/month

**Product:**
- PM: Rp 20-30M/month
- Senior PM: Rp 35-50M/month
- CPO: Rp 60-80M/month

**Sales & CS:**
- SDR: Rp 8-12M/month (+ commission)
- AE: Rp 15-25M/month (+ commission)
- CSM: Rp 12-20M/month

**Operations & Finance:**
- Coordinator: Rp 8-12M/month
- Manager: Rp 15-25M/month
- Director: Rp 30-45M/month

---

## Offer Approval

**Final step before extending offer:**
- HR prepares offer letter
- Hiring manager reviews
- CEO approves (for all roles)
- HR extends offer

**Offer includes:**
- Base salary
- Benefits
- Equity (if applicable)
- Start date
- Reporting structure
