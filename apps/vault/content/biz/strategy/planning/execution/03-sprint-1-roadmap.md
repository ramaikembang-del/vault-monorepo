## Product Deep-Dive

### Product A1: Power BI Template (Tier 1)

**What It Is:**  
One-time Power BI dashboard template (3 tabs, 75+ metrics) for Phase 2 F&B operators who already use Excel

**Target Customer:**  
- Phase 2 (Warung Naik Kelas): Amanda, Budi 2.0
- 1-3 outlets, Rp 50-200M revenue/year
- Currently using Excel manually, familiar with basic POS reports

**Pricing:**  
- Tier 1 (Essentials): Rp 3.5-6M (30-50 metrics)
- Sprint 1 Focus: Tier 1 only (validate before expanding to Tier 2/3)

**Development:**  
- **Status:** 50% complete (core metrics framework done)
- **Remaining:** 1-2 weeks (visual polish, documentation, delivery SOP)
- **Capabilities Needed:** Power BI Desktop (have), Excel template creation (have)

**Go-to-Market:**  
- Week 1-2: Warm network outreach (15-20 conversations via WhatsApp)
- Sales playbook: [[products/01-power-bi-template/sales/strategy/01-sales-playbook|Sales Playbook]]
- Pre-sale validation: [[products/01-power-bi-template/sales/strategy/04-pre-sale-validation-playbook|Pre-Sale Playbook]]

**Success Metrics:**  
- 5+ Template sales in 4 weeks
- Rp 20-50M revenue (avg Rp 4.5M per sale)
- 30%+ leads convert to Support subscription

**Gate 1 Criteria (Week 2):**  
- 2+ sales closed
- 5+ qualified leads in pipeline

### Product R1: Template Support

**What It Is:**  
Monthly subscription service for Template buyers (updates, bug fixes, feature additions, Q&A support)

**Target Customer:**  
- A1 (Template) buyers
- Expect 30-50% of Template buyers to subscribe

**Pricing:**  
- Rp 75K/month (enterprise/complex buyers might pay Rp 150K)
- Bundled option: Template + 6 months Support = Rp 5M (saves Rp 450K)

**Development:**  
- **Timeline:** 1-2 days
- **What to Build:**
  - Landing page/subscription form
  - Notion workspace for support tickets
  - Monthly update process (documented SOP)
  - [[products/05-saas-basic/operations/support-playbook|Support Playbook]]

**Revenue Model:**  
- Conservative: 30% of 5 buyers √ó Rp 75K = Rp 112.5K MRR
- Optimistic: 50% of 10 buyers √ó Rp 100K = Rp 500K MRR

**Success Metrics:**  
- 30%+ of Template buyers subscribe
- \<48h response time on support tickets
- 90%+ renewal rate (Month 2)

**Strategic Value:**  
- **Proves subscription willingness** (critical data for SaaS investment)
- **Builds retention muscle** early (customer success processes)
- **Revenue stability** (offsets one-time Template lumpiness)

### Product E1: Web Dashboard Lite (Phase 1)

**What It Is:**  
Offline-first Electron app with basic P&L dashboard, freemium model designed for Phase 1 Mikro (warung/kaki lima)

**Target Customer:**  
- Phase 1 (Mikro Bertahan): Siti, Pak Agus
- 500K+ TAM (street food, small warungs)
- Currently using paper/WhatsApp, no systematic tracking

**Pricing:**  
- **Phase 1 (Sprint 1):** Free forever (validation focus)
- **Phase 2 limits (Sprint 2):** 30-day history, 1 location (upsell to paid Rp 50-75K/mo)

**Development:**  
- **Timeline:** 2 weeks (AI-coded via Cursor/Claude)
- **Tech Stack:** Electron, SQLite (offline), simple HTML/CSS UI
- **Features (P1):**
  - Manual daily revenue/expense entry
  - Simple P&L chart (7/30-day view)
  - Profit margin % calculator
  - CSV export

**Go-to-Market:**  
- Facebook groups (Pengusaha Kuliner Indonesia, PKL communities)
- Instagram Stories (warung/kaki lima influencers)
- **No paid ads** (organic only for Sprint 1)

**Success Metrics:**  
- **Acquisition:** 50+ downloads in 4 weeks
- **Activation:** 20%+ enter 7+ days of data
- **Engagement:** 10%+ use 3+ times/week
- **Qualitative:** 5+ user interviews (understand friction)

**Validation Questions:**  
1. Will Phase 1 Mikro download an analytics tool?
2. Will they input data manually (or abandon after Day 1)?
3. What features do they ask for most? (informs Phase 2)

**Gate 1 Criteria (Week 4):**  
- 50+ downloads
- 10+ active users (used 3+ days)
- 5+ user interviews completed

**Strategic Importance:**  
- **Unblocks Full SaaS:** Can't justify Sprint 3 SaaS investment without confirming Phase 1 actually engages
- **Pipeline Generation:** Free users = upgrade candidates for SaaS Basic (Pillar 3‚Üí5 cascade)
- **Data Generation:** Usage patterns inform SaaS feature roadmap

## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Template Sales Below Target

**Likelihood:** Medium (cold start, no brand)  
**Impact:** High (no Sprint 2 funding)

**Mitigation:**
- Pre-sales validation (Week 0): Confirm 3+ verbal commitments
- Warm network first (higher conversion)
- Offer early-access discount (Rp 3.5M vs Rp 4.5M)
- Flexible pricing (Rp 3.5-6M based on business size)

**Contingency:**  
If Week 2 shows \<2 sales, extend Sprint 1 by 2 weeks OR lower price to Rp 2.5M

---

### Risk 2: Lite Fails to Engage Phase 1

**Likelihood:** Medium (Phase 1 = low tech adoption)  
**Impact:** High (invalidates SaaS strategy)

**Mitigation:**
- Hand-hold first 10 beta users (WhatsApp support)
- Simplify UI to bare minimum (3 fields: revenue, expense, profit)
- Offline-first (no internet required)
- Indonesian UI (Bahasa, not English)

**Contingency:**  
If Week 4 shows \<10 active users, iterate to Phase 2 SME target instead (Amanda/Budi) with desktop app

---

### Risk 3: Support Conversion Below 30%

**Likelihood:** Low (natural upsell)  
**Impact:** Medium (less MRR but not critical)

**Mitigation:**
- Bundle incentive (Rp 5M = Template + 6mo Support)
- First month free trial (converts after delivery)
- Highlight value (free updates, priority support)

**Contingency:**  
If \<20% convert, OK - still validates subscription willingness, just at lower rate

---

### Risk 4: Time Overrun (Can't Build 3 Products)

**Likelihood:** Medium (solo execution risk)  
**Impact:** Medium (delays Sprint 2)

**Mitigation:**
- AI-code Lite (reduce build time from 4 weeks ‚Üí 2 weeks)
- Support is 1-2 days only (minimal feature set)
- Template already 50% done (only polish needed)

**Contingency:**  
If behind schedule, ship Lite as closed beta only (10 users, manual onboarding)
