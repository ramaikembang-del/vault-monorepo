## Sprint 2 Pivot Scenarios

**If Tier 2 Fails (<3 sales):**
- Action: Don't build Tier 3, focus on scaling Tier 1
- Rationale: Market not ready for higher tiers

**If Content Marketing Fails (CAC >Rp 2M):**
- Action: Stop paid ads, focus on organic/referrals
- Rationale: Not cost-effective channel yet

**If Freemium → Paid Fails (0 conversions):**
- Action: Keep Lite free longer, don't rush monetization
- Rationale: Phase 1 not ready to pay (as hypothesized)
