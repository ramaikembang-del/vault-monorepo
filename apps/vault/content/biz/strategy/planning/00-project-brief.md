## Part 1: Evolution - Vision to Reality

**Timeline:** December 2025
**Journey:** Single Dashboard Concept → Validation-First Approach → 3-Product Ecosystem

### Phase 1: Initial Vision (Early December 2025)

**Original Concept:** Single Power BI performance dashboard

**Target Market Assumptions:**
- Single customer segment: Established F&B operations aiming for Rp 12Bn annual revenue
- One comprehensive dashboard solution serving all needs
- Focus: Sales intelligence, staff gamification ("Wolf Pack"), customer loyalty loop

**Original Financial Model:**
- Target: Help customers reach Rp 12Bn annual revenue
- Solution: Power BI dashboard with ~150 metrics
- Pricing: Assumed single-tier offering
- Market: Tech-savvy restaurant operators

**Key Features (from original brief):**
- Revenue waterfall and hourly heatmap
- Staff leaderboard with gamification ("League Table")
- Customer patronage ladder (First-timer → VIP)
- Dark mode UI with mobile layout

**Strategic Limitation Identified:**
- Assumed one-size-fits-all solution
- No product-market fit validation
- No clear path from concept to revenue
- Generic financial projections without TAM/SAM analysis

---

### Phase 2: Product Validation & Discovery (Mid December 2025)

**Key Insight:** Market requires 3 distinct products, not one universal dashboard

**Discovery Process:**
1. **Market Segmentation Analysis:** Identified 5 maturity phases (Mikro → Menengah)
2. **Persona Development:** Created 19 detailed customer personas across phases
3. **Validation-First Methodology:** Adopted /validation-first-docs workflow to test assumptions before building

**Critical Findings:**

**Finding 1: Three Distinct TAMs Identified**
- **TAM 1 (A1 Template):** 30,000 tech-savvy restaurants (Phase 2-3)
 - Have POS systems + Power BI skills
 - Willing to pay Rp 2M+ for DIY template
 - Want customization, not locked platform

- **TAM 2 (E1 Lite Dashboard):** 150,000 Mikro F&B (Phase 1)
 - Mobile-first, non-technical operators
 - Need freemium entry point (trust-building)
 - Willing to pay Rp 200-300K/month after validation

- **TAM 3 (R1 Subscription):** 650 A1 buyers (derivative market)
 - Subset of A1 customers needing ongoing support
 - 30% conversion rate (template → subscription)
 - Pricing: Rp 75K/month recurring

**Finding 2: Cultural Context Critical**
- Indonesian market requires WhatsApp-first support (not email)
- "Tall Poppy Syndrome" → Private dashboards, not public leaderboards
- Offline-first builds trust (anti-cloud anxiety)
- Ewuh Pakewuh → "Data as Neutral Mediator" UX pattern

**Finding 3: Build Sequence Matters**
- Cannot build comprehensive platform first (high risk, no validation)
- Parallel A1+E1 build leverages AI-coding capability
- A1 generates revenue (Rp 300M Y1) to fund E1 infrastructure
- E1 validates engagement to justify Full SaaS investment

---

**Strategic Shift:**
- From: "Build one perfect dashboard" → To: "Validate market fit with minimal products first"
- From: "Assume customers want features" → To: "Test willingness to pay before building"
- From: "Generic Indonesian F&B market" → To: "Three precise segments with 19 personas"
- From: "Abstract Rp 12Bn goal" → To: "Concrete Rp 580.5M Y1 target with product breakdown"

### Strategic Lessons Learned

**1. Market Segmentation is Non-Negotiable**
- **Original Error:** Assumed single "restaurant" segment
- **Learning:** Different phases = different products (Mikro ≠ Kecil ≠ Menengah)
- **Impact:** 3-product ecosystem vs failed one-size-fits-all

**2. Validation Before Building Saves Years**
- **Original Error:** "Build dashboard, launch, hope customers come"
- **Learning:** Test willingness-to-pay BEFORE 6-month build
- **Impact:** E1 freemium validates engagement → de-risks Full SaaS investment

**3. Cultural Context Shapes Product**
- **Original Error:** Generic global dashboard UX
- **Learning:** Indonesian market = WhatsApp-first, mobile-first, trust-first
- **Impact:** Offline E1 + WhatsApp support = 10× better adoption

**4. Revenue Velocity Matters in Bootstrapped Context**
- **Original Error:** No cash flow plan (assume investors?)
- **Learning:** A1 template generates Rp 300M Y1 → funds E1 infrastructure
- **Impact:** Self-funded Sprint 1-3 vs dependent on external funding

**5. Ecosystem Synergies Amplify Value**
- **Original Error:** Standalone dashboard (no upgrade path)
- **Learning:** A1 → R1 upsell (+67% LTV), E1 → Full SaaS pipeline
- **Impact:** Customer LTV grows 67-100% through journey progression

## 1. Technical Data Architecture

* **Data Model:** Star Schema (Fact_Sales, Dim_Date, Dim_Staff, Dim_Customer, Dim_Product).
* **Target Integration:** Connect to a `Targets.xlsx` file containing monthly targets to hit the 12 Bn annual mark.
* **Refreshes:** Minimum 4x daily (or DirectQuery if POS allows).

---

## 2. Global Section: Sales & Growth Intelligence

**Focus:** Yield Management & Target Pacing.

* **Header KPIs:** * **Total Revenue:** Actual YTD vs. Target (12 Bn).
* **Required Run Rate:** `(Remaining Target) / (Days Left in Year)`. This is the most critical metric for the Assistant Manager.
* **Avg Check (per Pax):** Comparison of Reservation vs. Regular walk-ins.


* **Key Visuals:**
* **Revenue Waterfall:** Base Sales Reservations Upsells Discounts.
* **Hourly Heatmap:** Revenue density by hour/day to optimize the 40-person staff roster.



---

## 3. The "Wolf Pack" Section: Staff Gamification

**Focus:** Motivating the 20 direct reports through transparent competition.

* **The League Table (Matrix):**
* **Ranking:** Dynamic `RANKX` based on Total Sales.
* **Upsell Strike Rate:** % of receipts containing high-margin items (Desserts, Appetizers, Premium Alcohol).
* **Visual Flair:** Data bars for revenue; "" icons for high-performing upsellers.


* **The Spotlight (Drill-through):**
* Selecting a staff member opens a side panel with their **Product Mix** (Food/Bev/Alcohol split) and their specific **Gap-to-Bonus** progress bar.



---

## 4. The "Loyalty Loop" Section: Customer Insights

**Focus:** Retention and Feedback Loop.

* **Patronage Ladder (Funnel):**
* Classification of customers: *First-timer Returner (2nd-3rd visit) Regular (4-9) VIP (10+).*


* **Sentiment Score:** Average rating from Google/Internal Feedback.
* **Revenue at Risk:** A KPI card showing the potential lost revenue from VIPs who haven't visited in >45 days.

## 5. Implementation DAX Snippets (For Developer)

```dax
// 1. Daily Target to reach 12Bn
Required Daily =
DIVIDE(
 (12000000000 - TOTALYTD(SUM(Sales[Amount]), 'Date'[Date])),
 COUNTROWS(FILTER('Date', 'Date'[Date] >= TODAY() && 'Date'[Date] <= DATE(YEAR(TODAY()), 12, 31))),
 0
)

// 2. Upsell Strike Rate (Gamification)
Upsell Strike Rate =
DIVIDE(
 CALCULATE(DISTINCTCOUNT(Sales[OrderID]), Sales[Category] IN {"Dessert", "Add-on"}),
 DISTINCTCOUNT(Sales[OrderID]),
 0
)

```
