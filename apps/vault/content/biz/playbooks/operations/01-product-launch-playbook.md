**Owner:** Product & Marketing
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Launch Checklist (90-Day Timeline)

### T-90: Planning Phase
- [ ] Define product vision & positioning
- [ ] Create product brief & roadmap
- [ ] Identify target customers (beta list)
- [ ] Set success metrics

### T-60: Beta Phase
- [ ] Recruit 10-20 beta customers
- [ ] Beta onboarding & training
- [ ] Weekly feedback collection
- [ ] Iterate based on feedback

### T-30: Launch Prep
- [ ] Finalize product (feature freeze)
- [ ] Create marketing materials (website, demos, videos)
- [ ] Pricing & packaging finalized
- [ ] Sales/CS training completed
- [ ] Support docs & FAQs ready

### T-7: Pre-Launch
- [ ] Press release drafted
- [ ] Email campaigns scheduled
- [ ] Social media content ready
- [ ] Launch event/webinar planned

### Launch Day (T-0)
- [ ] Product live in production
- [ ] Announcement published (blog, email, social)
- [ ] Launch event/webinar
- [ ] Monitor metrics & support

### T+30: Post-Launch
- [ ] Analyze first 30 days (adoption, feedback)
- [ ] Iterate on product
- [ ] Case studies from early adopters

---

## Launch Tiers

**Tier 1 (Major Launch):** New product line (e.g., C1 Full SaaS)
- PR campaign
- Launch event (webinar/in-person)
- Paid advertising
- 90-day timeline

**Tier 2 (Standard Launch):** New module (e.g., R2f Customer Analytics)
- Blog post + email
- Social media campaign
- 30-day timeline

**Tier 3 (Minor Launch):** Feature addition
- In-app notification
- Release notes
- 1-week timeline
