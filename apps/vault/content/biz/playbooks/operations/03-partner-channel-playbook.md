# Partner Channel Playbook

**Owner:** Business Development
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Partner Types

**1. F&B Consultants/Accountants (Primary)**
- Serve 10-50 F&B clients each
- Natural fit (already advising on financials)
- Revenue model: 20% referral fee or 30% revenue share

**2. POS Resellers**
- Sell Moka, Olsera, Pawoon
- Bundle opportunity
- Revenue model: Commission on new sales

**3. F&B Communities/Associations**
- Reach thousands of F&B owners
- Co-marketing opportunity
- Revenue model: Member discounts, sponsorship

---

## Partner Recruitment

**Ideal Partner Profile:**
- 10+ F&B clients
- Trusted advisor (not just vendor)
- Tech-savvy
- Growth-minded

**Outreach:**
1. Identify potential partners (LinkedIn, directories)
2. Personalized email/call
3. Partner value prop:, "Earn Rp 50-100M/year reselling PWBI"
4. Onboarding (1 week training)

**Target:** Recruit 50 partners in 2025

---

## Partner Economics

**Option A: Referral Fee**
- 20% of first year ARR
- One-time payment
- Example: Refer Rp 50M deal → earn Rp 10M

**Option B: Revenue Share**
- 30% of ongoing ARR (lifetime)
- Recurring income
- Example: Rp 50M/year customer → earn Rp 15M/year

**Expectation:** Partners refer 5-10 customers/year → Rp 50-150M partner income

---

## Partner Enablement

**Training (Week 1):**
- Product overview
- Demo scripts
- Objection handling
- CRM/portal access

**Marketing Materials:**
- Co-branded decks
- Case studies
- ROI calculator
- Email templates

**Ongoing Support:**
- Dedicated partner manager
- Monthly partner webinars
- Deal support (pre-sales help)

---

## 2025 Partner Channel Targets

**Q1:** Recruit 10 partners → 50 referrals (Rp 250M ARR)
**Q2:** Recruit 15 partners → 100 referrals (Rp 500M ARR)
**Q3:** Recruit 15 partners → 150 referrals (Rp 750M ARR)
**Q4:** Recruit 10 partners → 150 referrals (Rp 750M ARR)

**Total:** 50 partners, 450 referrals, Rp 2.25B ARR
