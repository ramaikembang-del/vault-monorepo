# Reseller Program

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Owner:** Partnerships Team

---

## Overview

Reseller partnership program for PWBI FnB Growth, enabling partners to sell our products to their customer base.

**Status:** Planned for Sprint 4+ (after C1 SaaS launch)

---

## Program Goals

1. **Distribution:** Reach customers we can't reach directly
2. **Scale:** Grow revenue without proportional sales headcount
3. **Localization:** Partners understand local F&B markets better
4. **Credibility:** Partner endorsement builds trust

---

## Ideal Reseller Partners

**Target Partners:**
- **POS Vendors:** (Pawoon, Moka, Qasir) - already trusted by F&B owners
- **Accounting Software:** (Accurate, Jurnal) - complementary products
- **F&B Consultants:** Help restaurants optimize operations
- **Business Coaches:** Serving SME F&B businesses
- **Industry Associations:** Paguyuban, restaurant groups

**Selection Criteria:**
- Existing F&B customer base (>50 customers)
- Reputation in the market
- Sales capability
- Willingness to invest in training

---

## Commission Structure

### Standard Reseller

**Commission:** 20% of first-year revenue

**Example:**
- Reseller sells C1 Basic (Rp 300K/month, annual contract = Rp 3.6M)
- Commission: 20% × Rp 3.6M = **Rp 720K** paid to reseller
- Paid quarterly (Rp 180K per quarter)

**Terms:**
- Annual contracts only (for commission eligibility)
- Commission paid on collected revenue (not billed)
- Clawback if customer churns within 3 months

---

### Premier Reseller (Higher Volume)

**Requirements:** >10 customers per quarter

**Commission:** 30% of first-year revenue

**Benefits:**
- Higher commission
- Dedicated partner manager
- Co-branded marketing materials
- Priority support

## Reseller Onboarding

### Step 1: Application & Approval

**Reseller applies:**
- Company information
- Customer base description
- Sales capability
- References

**PWBI reviews:** 1-2 weeks
**Decision:** Approve / Reject

--- Step 2: Training (1 week)

**Product Training:**
- Deep dive on all PWBI products
- Demo best practices
- Common objections & responses
- Target customer profiles

**Sales Training:**
- Pricing & packaging
- Lead qualification
- Demo scripts
- Contract negotiation

**Systems Training:**
- Partner portal access
- Deal registration
- Commission tracking
- Support escalation

---

### Step 3: Agreement Signing

**Partner Agreement includes:**
- Commission structure
- Territory (if exclusive)
- Performance targets (optional)
- Co-marketing commitments
- Support responsibilities
- Termination clauses

**Term:** 12 months, auto-renew

---

### Step 4: Launch

- Announce partnership
- Provide marketing materials
- Assign partner manager
- Begin lead sharing

---

## Deal Registration

**Process:**
1. Reseller registers deal in partner portal
2. PWBI approves within 48 hours
3. Deal "locked" to reseller for 90 days
4. Reseller manages sale
5. PWBI provides technical support
6. Commission paid quarterly

**Deal Registration Required Fields:**
- Customer name & contact
- Estimated deal size
- Expected close date
- Current stage (prospecting, demo, proposal, negotiation)

---

## Partner Responsibilities

**Reseller responsible for:**
- Lead generation
- Initial customer contact
- Product demos
- Proposal & negotiation
- Contract signing
- Commission tracking

**PWBI responsible for:**
- Product development
- Technical infrastructure
- Customer support (post-sale)
- Billing & invoicing
- Commission payment

## Co-Marketing Support

**PWBI provides:**
- Co-branded slide decks
- Case studies
- Email templates
- Social media graphics
- Joint webinars (quarterly)
- Partner directory listing

**Reseller provides:**
- Customer testimonials
- Market feedback
- Co-marketing budget (50/50 split for joint campaigns)

## Partner Tiers

| Tier | Requirements | Benefits |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Authorized** | 0-5 customers/quarter | Standard commission (20%), product training |
| **Silver** | 6-10 customers/quarter | Higher commission (25%), dedicated partner manager |
| **Gold** | 11-20 customers/quarter | Highest commission (30%), co-marketing budget, priority support |
| **Platinum** | 20+ customers/quarter | Custom terms, strategic partnership, joint roadmap input |

## Performance Tracking

**Quarterly Business Review (QBR) with each partner:**
- Deals won/lost
- Pipeline review
- Commission paid
- Customer health (churn, NPS)
- Next quarter goals

**Metrics:**
- Total partner-sourced revenue
- Average deal size
- Conversion rate (demo → close)
- Partner satisfaction (NPS)

**Targets (Year 1):**
- 10 active reseller partners
- 30% of new revenue from partners
- <10% partner churn
