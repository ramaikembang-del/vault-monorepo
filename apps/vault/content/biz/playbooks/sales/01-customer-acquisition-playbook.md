## Funnel Stages & Conversion

```
ACQUISITION FUNNEL

Website Visitors (10,000/month)
 ↓ 3% conversion
Trial/Demo Sign-ups (300/month)
 ↓ 18% conversion (SMB self-serve) / 50% (enterprise)
Paid Customers (60/month)
 ↓ 85% retention (12 months)
Retained Customers (51/month)
```

**Optimization Priorities:**
1. Increase trial sign-ups: 3% → 5% (content, CTAs)
2. Improve trial→paid: 18% → 25% (onboarding,sales)

## Playbook by Segment

### SMB Acquisition (A1, E1)
**Channels:** SEO, content, referrals
**Offer:** 14-day free trial
**Decision Maker:** Owner (single decision)
**Sales Cycle:** < 2 weeks
**Motion:** Product-led growth (self-service)

**Tactics:**
- SEO blog posts (40/year)
- ROI calculator on website
- Email drip campaign (7 emails over 14 days)
- Self-service checkout

### Mid-Market (R2 Suite)
**Channels:** Referrals, events, outbound
**Offer:** Custom demo + pilot
**Decision Maker:** Owner + Manager
**Sales Cycle:** 1-3 months
**Motion:** Sales-assisted

**Tactics:**
- Outbound prospecting (LinkedIn, F&B databases)
- Case studies by vertical (coffee, QSR, etc.)
- Personalized demos
- Reference calls

### Enterprise (C1 Full SaaS)
**Channels:** Referrals, partnerships, events
**Offer:** POC + custom proposal
**Decision Maker:** C-suite + IT + Finance
**Sales Cycle:** 3-6 months
**Motion:** Enterprise sales

**Tactics:**
- Account-based marketing
- Executive briefings
- RFP responses
- Custom pricing & SOWs
