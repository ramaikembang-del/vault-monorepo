# Account Expansion Playbook

**Owner:** Account Management & Sales
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Expansion Strategy

**Goal:** 120% Net Revenue Retention
**Current:** 105% NRR

**Components:**
- Upsells (tier upgrades)
- Cross-sells (add modules)
- Multi-location expansion
- User seat expansion

---

## Expansion Readiness Signals

**Green Lights (Expand Now):**
- Health score > 75
- NPS > 8
- Using product 3+ times/week
- Documented ROI
- Growing business (new locations, etc.)

**Yellow Lights (Nurture First):**
- Health score 60-75
- NPS 7-8
- Moderate usage
- ROI unclear

**Red Lights (Retention Focus):**
- Health score < 60
- NPS < 7
- Low usage
- Considering churn

---

## Expansion Plays

### Play 1: Upsell to Higher Tier
**Target:** A1 customers → E1 Dashboard
**Trigger:** Outgrowing spreadsheet, wants real-time
**Value Prop:** "Real-time insights, not monthly refreshes"
**ARR Impact:** +Rp 10M

**Process:**
1. Identify: Customer manually refreshing A1 template daily
2. Reach out: "Spending too much time on this?"
3. Demo: Show E1 auto-refresh, real-time
4. Close: Special upgrade pricing

### Play 2: Cross-Sell Modules
**Target:** R2a P&L customers → Add R2c COGS
**Trigger:** Asking about waste, margins
**Value Prop:** "Find hidden waste costing Rp X/month"
**ARR Impact:** +Rp 15M/module

**Process:**
1. Identify: Customer mentions COGS concerns
2. Quick ROI calc: "You're likely wasting Rp XM"
3. Pilot: 30-day trial of R2c
4. Convert: Full implementation

### Play 3: Multi-Location Expansion
**Target:** Single-location → All locations
**Trigger:** Customer opens 2nd/3rd location
**Value Prop:** "See all locations in one dashboard"
**ARR Impact:** +Rp 10-30M (per location)

**Process:**
1. Monitor: Track customer business growth (LinkedIn, news)
2. Congratulate: "Heard you opened location #2!"
3. Offer: Multi-location pricing (discounted)
4. Onboard: New locations quickly

### Play 4: User Seat Expansion
**Target:** C1 customers (seat-based pricing)
**Trigger:** More team members need access
**Value Prop:** "Empower your entire team"
**ARR Impact:** +Rp 3M/seat/year
