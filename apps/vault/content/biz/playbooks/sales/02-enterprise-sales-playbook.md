# Enterprise Sales Playbook

**Owner:** Enterprise Sales Team
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Enterprise Definition

**Criteria:**
- 10+ locations OR
- Rp 5B+ annual revenue OR
- 100+ employees OR
- Deal size > Rp 100M ARR

---

## Sales Process (6-9 Months)

**Stage 1: Qualification (Weeks 1-2)**
- Research account
- Identify decision makers
- BANT qualification (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)

**Stage 2: Discovery (Weeks 3-4)**
- Executive meeting
- Deep-dive pain points
- Map stakeholders

**Stage 3: Solution Design (Weeks 5-8)**
- Custom demo
- ROI model
- Proposal & SOW

**Stage 4: POC/Pilot (Weeks 9-16)**
- 1-2 locations pilot
- Success metrics tracked
- Weekly check-ins

**Stage 5: Procurement (Weeks 17-24)**
- Legal review
- Security audit
- Contracting

**Stage 6: Close (Weeks 25-32)**
- Final negotiations
- Contract signing
- Kick-off

---

## Stakeholder Map

**Economic Buyer:** CEO/Owner (signs contract)
**Champion:** CFO/Finance Director (internal advocate)
**Users:** Managers, Analysts
**Influencers:** IT, Operations
**Blockers:** Procurement, Legal

**Strategy:** Multi-thread - engage all stakeholders

---

## Key Success Factors

1. **Executive Sponsorship** - C-level buy-in required
2. **Proof via POC** - De-risk with pilot
3. **Custom Pricing** - Flexible commercial terms
4. **White-Glove Service** - Dedicated resources
5. **References** - Similar-sized customers

---
