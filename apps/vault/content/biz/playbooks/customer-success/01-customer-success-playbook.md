# Customer Success Playbook

**Owner:** VP Customer Success
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Customer Success Mission

**Goal:** Ensure customers achieve their business objectives using PWBI products

**Metrics:**
- Customer retention: 85%+
- NPS: 65+
- Net Revenue Retention: 120%+

---

## CS Team Structure

**CS Manager (1:50 customers)**
- Account management
- Quarterly business reviews
- Expansion opportunities

**CS Specialist (1:100 customers - automated support)**
- Onboarding coordination
- Health monitoring
- Proactive outreach

**Support Engineer (1:200 customers)**
- Technical troubleshooting
- Product training
- Escalations

---

## CS Lifecycle

**Days 1-30:** Onboarding
- Kickoff call
- Training
- Weekly check-ins
- Activation goal: 80%+ users

**Days 31-90:** Adoption
- Feature adoption campaigns
- Success metrics tracking
- First business review

**Days 91-365:** Value Delivery
- Quarterly business reviews
- Expansion discussions
- Renewal prep (Month 10)

**Year 2+:** Advocacy
- Case study participation
- Referrals
- Advisory board

---

## Proactive CS Motions

**Weekly:** Review health scores, reach out to at-risk
**Monthly:** Product usage analysis, feature adoption campaigns
**Quarterly:** Business reviews with top customers
