# Customer Retention Playbook

**Owner:** Customer Success
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Retention Metrics

**12-Month Retention Rate:** 78% (target: 85%)
**Monthly Logo Churn:** 4.5% (target: < 3%)
**Monthly Revenue Churn:** 3.2% (target: < 2%)
**NPS:** 52 (target: > 60)

---

## Churn Prevention Framework

### Health Score (0-100)
- **Usage:** 40 points
- **Engagement:** 30 points
- **Business Outcomes:** 20 points
- **Sentiment:** 10 points

### Risk Tiers
- **Healthy (80-100):** Normal engagement
- **Watch (60-79):** Weekly check-in
- **At-Risk (40-59):** Save plan + executive call
- **Critical (<40):** All-hands, commercial offers

---

## Retention Tactics by Stage

### Month 0-3: Onboarding & Activation
**Goal:** Achieve first value < 14 days

**Tactics:**
- Kickoff call (Day 1-3)
- Product training (Week 1-2)
- Weekly check-ins
- In-app onboarding checklist
- Quick win identification

**Success Criteria:**
- 80%+ users active
- 3+ features used
- Measurable insight discovered

### Month 3-12: Deepening Value
**Goal:** Embed into workflow, expand usage

**Tactics:**
- Quarterly business reviews
- Feature adoption campaigns
- Success story sharing
- Upsell/cross-sell

**Success Criteria:**
- NPS > 7
- Weekly active usage
- Documented ROI

### Month 12+: Renewal & Advocacy
**Goal:** Automatic renewal, referrals

**Tactics:**
- Early renewal outreach (Month 10)
- Renewal incentives (multi-year deals)
- Case study participation
- Referral program

## Churn Reasons & Mitigation

| Reason | % of Churn | Preventable? | Mitigation |
|--------|------------|--------------|------------|
| Business closed | 35% | No |  -  |
| Not seeing ROI | 30% | Yes | Better onboarding, proactive CS |
| Switched to competitor | 20% | Yes | Competitive pricing, feature parity |
| Too expensive | 15% | Maybe | Flexible pricing, discounts |

**Focus:** Reduce preventable churn from 65%  ->  40%

---

## Proactive Outreach Triggers

**Auto-Alert CS Manager if:**
- Health score drops below 60
- Usage decreases > 50% (2 weeks)
- Support ticket with negative sentiment
- Missed 2+ scheduled calls
- No login in 14 days

**Response:** CS call within 24 hours
