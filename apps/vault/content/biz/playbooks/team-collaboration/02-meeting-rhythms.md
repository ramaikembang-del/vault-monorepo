# Meeting Rhythms

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Owner:** Operations Team

---

## Overview

Consistent meeting rhythms ensure alignment, rapid decision-making, and efficient communication across PWBI FnB Growth.

**Philosophy:** Meetings should enable action, not replace it.

---

## Daily Rhythms

### Daily Standup (Engineering)
**Time:** 9:00 AM (15 minutes)
**Attendees:** Engineering team
**Format:**
- What did you ship yesterday?
- What are you shipping today?
- Any blockers?

**Slack Alternative:** Post in #engineering if async

---

## Weekly Rhythms

### Monday: Week Planning
**Time:** 10:00 AM (30 minutes)
**Attendees:** Leadership team
**Agenda:**
1. Review last week's progress vs goals
2. Set this week's priorities (top 3)
3. Identify risks/blockers
4. Assign owners

**Output:** Week priorities doc shared in Slack

### Wednesday: Product Review
**Time:** 2:00 PM (60 minutes)
**Attendees:** Product + Engineering + Design
**Agenda:**
1. Demo completed features
2. Review user feedback
3. Discuss roadmap changes
4. Prioritize next sprint backlog

### Friday: Metrics Review
**Time:** 11:00 AM (30 minutes)
**Attendees:** Leadership team
**Agenda:**
1. Key metrics dashboard review
2. Identify trends (positive/negative)
3. Quick wins for next week
4. Celebrate wins

---

## Monthly Rhythms

### Month Start: OKR Review
**Time:** 1st Monday, 2:00 PM (90 minutes)
**Attendees:** All team
**Agenda:**
1. Review last month's OKR progress
2. Update current quarter OKRs
3. Identify course corrections
4. Communicate priorities

### Month End: Financial Review
**Time:** Last Friday, 3:00 PM (60 minutes)
**Attendees:** Leadership + Finance
**Agenda:**
1. P&L review (budget vs actual)
2. Cash flow projection
3. Key expense variances
4. Next month forecast

### Mid-Month: All-Hands
**Time:** 15th of month, 4:00 PM (45 minutes)
**Attendees:** Entire company
**Agenda:**
1. Company updates (wins, challenges)
2. Product demos
3. Team spotlights
4. Q&A with leadership

---

## Quarterly Rhythms

### Quarter Start: OKR Planning
**Time:** Week 1 of quarter (Half day)
**Attendees:** Leadership team
**Process:**
1. Review last quarter results
2. Set company-level OKRs
3. Cascade to department OKRs
4. Get team alignment

### Quarter End: Retrospective
**Time:** Last week of quarter (2 hours)
**Attendees:** All team
**Format:**
1. What went well?
2. What didn't go well?
3. What will we change?
4. Action items with owners

---

## Meeting Best Practices

**Before Meeting:**
- Send agenda 24 hours in advance
- Share pre-read materials
- State meeting goal/outcome

**During Meeting:**
- Start/end on time
- Take notes (rotate note-taker)
- Make decisions, assign actions
- Park off-topic items

**After Meeting:**
- Share notes within 2 hours
- Follow up on action items
- Cancel if no agenda/

purpose
