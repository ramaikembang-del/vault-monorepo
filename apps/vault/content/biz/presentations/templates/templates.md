## Usage Guidelines

**When Creating New Presentation:**

1. Start with appropriate template
2. Replace [PLACEHOLDER] content
3. Follow brand guidelines for colors/fonts
4. Use high-quality images only
5. Keep slides simple (1 key idea per slide)
6. Include sources/data citations

**File Naming Convention:**
- `PWBI-[PresentationType]-[Audience]-[Date].pptx`
- Example: `PWBI-ProductDemo-Template-2025-12.pptx`
