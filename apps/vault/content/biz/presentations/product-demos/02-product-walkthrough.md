# Product Walkthrough
## F&B 12Bn Growth Dashboard - User Training

**Duration:** 20 minutes
**Audience:** Product team, Assistant Manager (end user)
**Purpose:** Feature demonstration, user training

---

## Slide 1: Meet Your New Command Center

**You're the Assistant Manager:**
- Manage 20 staff members
- Responsible for daily operations
- Goal: Help close IDR 4Bn gap to reach 12Bn

**Your Problem Today:**
- You don't know if you're on track until month-end
- You can't identify which staff need coaching until it's too late
- You don't know which VIP customers haven't returned

**Your Dashboard Tomorrow:**
- 5-minute morning check shows yesterday's performance
- One-click drill-down shows exactly who needs coaching
- Instant view of at-risk customers

---

## Slide 2: Your Daily Workflow

**7:00 AM - Morning Check (5 minutes):**
1. Open dashboard on phone (mobile-ready)
2. Tab 1: Did we hit yesterday's target?
3. Tab 2: Which staff were late? Who underperformed?
4. Tab 3: How many VIPs haven't returned?

**During Shift:**
- Quick glance: Are we on pace for today's run rate?
- Staff visible on leaderboard (gamification)

**5:00 PM - End of Day Review (10 minutes):**
1. Tab 1: Final revenue vs target
2. Tab 2: Click underperformers → see 8-metric drill-through
3. Tab 3: NPS check - what are customers saying?

**Weekly Management Meeting (30 minutes):**
- Deep dive into trends
- Use drill-throughs for coaching data

### **Performance Score Breakdown (6 Components):**

| Component | What It Measures | Why It Matters |
|-----------|------------------|----------------|
| Sales Per Hour (25%) | Productivity | Fair across shifts (lunch vs dinner) |
| Upsell Count (20%) | Behavior | Pushing desserts/drinks |

| **Discipline Score (10%)** | **Reliability** | **Punctuality, accuracy, conflict resolution** |

### **Drill-Through (Click Any Staff Name):**

Shows 8 metrics for coaching:
1. Gap to Target - How close to their monthly goal?
2. Gap to Bonus - Will they hit bonus threshold?
3. Upsell Count - Are they pushing add-ons?
4. Service Speed - Average table time
5. Positive Mentions - Customer compliments
6. Late Count - Punctuality issues
7. Discipline Breakdown - Radar chart (5 components)
8. Rank Position - #X of 20

**Your Actions:**

**Morning:** Check late count → Talk to staff who were late
**End of Day:** Click bottom 3 performers → See drill-through → Prepare coaching talking points

## Slide 13: Why This Works for You

** Built for Your Workflow:**
- 5-minute morning check, not 30-minute analysis
- One screen per tab (no scrolling maze)
- Mobile-ready (check from anywhere)

** Action-Focused:**
- Red/green indicators (not just numbers)
- Drill-through for root cause (not guesswork)
- Alert banners for urgent issues

** Transparent for Staff:**
- They see their own performance
- Tier badges = recognition
- Clear path to bonus

## Slide 14: Training Plan

**Week 1 (You - AM):**
- 2-hour hands-on training
- Practice: Morning check, drill-through, mobile mode
- Shadow mode: Use alongside current methods

**Week 2 (Your 20 Staff):**
- 30-minute group walkthrough
- Show them: How to see their own drill-through card
- Explain: Performance Score components, Discipline Score

**Week 3 (Go-Live):**
- Daily use begins
- Quick feedback loop (what's working? What's confusing?)

**Ongoing:**
- Weekly check-ins (first month)
- Feature requests logged (future enhancements)

## Slide 15: Q&A

**Common Questions:**

**Q: What if I want a metric not on the dashboard?**
A: Tell product team - ~150 total metrics available in comprehensive catalog:

**Revenue (32):** Forecast to 12Bn, Acceleration Needed, Run Rate to Goal, Food/Beverage/Alcohol/Dessert %, Peak Hour Volume, Reservation Conversion, YoY Growth %, Best/Worst Day, Days Behind/Ahead, Quarter-to-Date, Monthly Performance, Avg Check Size, Transactions per Hour

**Staff (51):** Rank Position, Gap to Leader, Percentile, Peer Avg (Same Role), Shift Peer Comparison, Improvement Trend, Consistency Rating, Weakness/Strength Indicators, Training Priority, Achievement Milestones (First Sale, 100 Club, Upsell Master, Perfect Week, 5-Star Streak, Target Achiever), Team Performance Variance, Team Discipline Score

**Customer (36):** First Visit Conversion, Regular-to-VIP Promotion, Avg Visits Per Month, Peak Visit Day, Avg Customer Lifetime, CLV (Simple), Top Decile Revenue, VIP Retention, Churn Rate, Lost Revenue Risk, Promoter %, Detractor %, Food/Service/Staff/Atmosphere Scores, Feedback Response Rate, Complaint Ratio, Avg Check by Segment, Upsell Acceptance (VIP), Discount Dependency, Reservation Premium

**Operational (20):** Sales Per Man Hour, Revenue Per Cover, Avg Service Duration, Table Turnover Rate, Seat Utilization %, Reservation Fill Rate, Net Revenue Ratio, Avg Check by Shift, Lunch vs Dinner Revenue, Staff Coverage Ratio, Shift Profitability, Void Rate, Staff Absentee Rate

**Q: Can staff see other staff's scores?**
A: Leaderboard shows all (transparency). Drill-through shows individual detail.

**Q: How often does data refresh?**
A: Real-time goal. Initially: 4x/day or overnight.

**Q: Can I use this on my personal phone?**
A: Yes - mobile layout optimized for phone.
