# Presentation Guidelines
**Professional Deck Standards**

**Owner:** Operations + Marketing  
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

## Presentation Types

### 1. Sales Deck
**Purpose:** Win new customers  
**Audience:** Prospects (business owners)  
**Length:** 10-15 slides, 20 min presentation

### 2. Product Demo
**Purpose:** Show features  
**Audience:** Existing or prospective customers  
**Length:** 5-10 slides + live demo

### 3. Internal Update
**Purpose:** Team alignment  
**Audience:** Internal team  
**Length:** 5-8 slides, 15 min

### 4. Investor Pitch
**Purpose:** Raise funding (if needed)  
**Audience:** VCs, angels  
**Length:** 10 slides, 10 min

---

## Slide Structure

### Title Slide
- Company logo (top left)
- Presentation title (large, centered)
- Presenter name + date (bottom)
- Background: Brand color (subtle)

### Content Slide
- Slide number (bottom right)
- Title (top, 32px font)
- Body: 3-5 bullet points OR 1 visual
- White space (don't overcrowd)

### Closing Slide
- Call-to-action ("Request Demo")
- Contact info (email, website)
- Thank you message

---

## Design Principles

**Rule of  3s:**
- Max 3 bullet points per slide
- Max 3 words per bullet (if possible)
- Max 3 colors per deck

**Visual Hierarchy:**
- Title: 32px, bold
- Body: 18px, regular
- Captions: 14px, light

**Images:**
- High quality (no pixelated screenshots)
- Relevant (not stock photos of random people)
- Captioned (what am I looking at?)

---

## Dos & Don'ts

**✅ Do:**
- Use brand colors consistently
- Include customer logos (with permission)
- Show metrics (numbers build credibility)
- Tell a story (problem → solution → result)

**❌ Don't:**
- Read slides verbatim (boring!)
- Use Comic Sans or decorative fonts
- Cram 10 bullet points on one slide
- Use meaningless clip art

## Sample Sales Deck Structure

1. **Title:** "Clario - F&B Analytics for Indonesia"
2. **Problem:** "F&B owners can't see profit (manual tracking is messy)"
3. **Solution:** "Automated dashboard - see P&L in 24 hours"
4. **How It Works:** 3-step process (upload → analyze → decide)
5. **Key Features:** Top 3 benefits
6. **Customer Proof:** Testimonial + metrics ("Profit up 18%")
7. **Pricing:** Tiers + what's included
8. **Comparison:** Us vs competitors
9. **Next Steps:** "Start free trial" CTA
10. **Contact:** email, calendly link

## Delivery Tips

**Rehearse:**
- Practice 3x before real presentation
- Time yourself (stay within limit)
- Anticipate questions

**Engage Audience:**
- Ask questions ("How many of you track profit manually?")
- Pause for effect (don't rush)
- Make eye contact (not just reading slides)

**Handle Q&A:**
- Repeat question (so everyone hears)
- Answer concisely (don't ramble)
- Defer if unsure ("Good question, let me follow up")
