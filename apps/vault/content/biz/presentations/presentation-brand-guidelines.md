## Overview

This document extends the core brand guidelines specifically for **presentation contexts** (PowerPoint, Keynote, Google Slides, PDFs for partners/investors).

**When to Use This Guide:**
- Investor pitch decks
- Partner briefings
- Conference presentations
- Sales presentations
- Client onboarding decks

**Difference from Core Brand Guidelines:**
- **Core:** General brand usage (website, marketing, social media)
- **This:** Presentation-specific layout, slide templates, storytelling

## Presentation Templates

### 1. Investor Pitch Deck Template

**Slide Ratio:** 16:9 (widescreen)  
**Total Slides:** 10-15 slides (max 20 minutes)  
**File Location:** `biz/presentations/templates/01-investor-pitch-template.pptx`

**Slide Order:**
1. Cover: Company name + tagline
2. Problem: Market pain points (1-2 slides)
3. Solution: Product overview (2 slides)
4. Market: TAM/SAM/SOM (1 slide)
5. Traction: Key metrics (1 slide)
6. Business Model: Pricing & revenue (1 slide)
7. Team: Founders + key hires (1 slide)
8. Ask: Funding amount + use of funds (1 slide)
9. Appendix: Detailed metrics, tech stack, etc.

## Design Principles for Presentations

### 1. Clarity Over Creativity

**Do:**
- One message per slide
- Large fonts (minimum 24pt for body text)
- High contrast (dark text on light, or vice versa)

**Don't:**
- Walls of text (max 6 bullet points per slide)
- Too many colors (stick to brand palette)
- Fancy transitions (simple fade or cut only)

---

### 2. Visual Hierarchy

**Title:** 42-48pt, Bold, Brand Blue (#2563EB)  
**Subtitle:** 24-32pt, Regular, Gray (#64748B)  
**Body:** 24-28pt, Regular, Dark Gray (#1E293B)

**Spacing:**
- Minimum 1.5× line spacing
- Wide margins (10% from edges)

---

### 3. Data Visualization

**Charts:**
- Use brand colors (primary blue, secondary orange/green)
- Always label axes
- Include data source
- Keep it simple (max 5 data series)

**Tables:**
- Zebra striping (alternate row colors)
- Header row in brand blue
- Left-align text, right-align numbers

## Color Palette for Presentations

### Primary Colors
- **Brand Blue:** #2563EB (main headings, CTAs)
- **White:** #FFFFFF (backgrounds, clean space)
- **Dark Gray:** #1E293B (body text)

### Accent Colors
- **Success Green:** #10B981 (positive metrics, growth)
- **Warning Orange:** #F59E0B (highlights, callouts)
- **Error Red:** #EF4444 (problems, pain points - use sparingly)

### Chart Colors (Data Viz)
1. Brand Blue (#2563EB)
2. Orange (#F59E0B)
3. Green (#10B981)
4. Purple (#8B5CF6)
5. Pink (#EC4899)

**Accessibility:** All color combinations meet WCAG AA contrast standards (4.5:1 minimum)

## Typography

### Fonts

**Primary Font:** Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: Inter Bold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Numbers/Data: Inter Medium

**Fallback:** Arial or Helvetica (if Inter unavailable)

**Download:** https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter

### Font Sizes

| Element | Size | Weight |
|---------|------|--------|
| Slide Title | 42-48pt | Bold |
| Section Header | 32-36pt | Bold |
| Body Text | 24-28pt | Regular |
| Caption/Footnote | 16-18pt | Regular |
| Data/Numbers | 32-48pt | Medium/Bold |

**Rule:** Never go below 16pt (unreadable from 10+ feet away)

## Logo Usage in Presentations

### Logo Placement

**Cover Slide:**
- Top center or top left
- Size: 15-20% of slide width
- Minimum clearance: 5% on all sides

**Content Slides:**
- Top left corner (consistent placement)
- Size: 8-10% of slide width
- Or footer (small, 3-5% width)

### Logo Variations

**Use:**
- Full color logo on white background (primary)
- White logo on brand blue background (secondary)

**Don't Use:**
- Black & white logo (unless printing requirements)
- Stretched or squished logo
- Logo with drop shadow or effects

## Image Guidelines

### Photography

**Style:**
- Authentic Indonesian F&B context
- Real restaurant/warung settings
- Warm, inviting tones

**Sources:**
- Custom photography (best)
- Unsplash/Pexels (free stock)
- Avoid obviously western/generic stock photos

**Placement:**
- Full-bleed (edge-to-edge) for impact
- Or 50% width with text on left/right

---

### Icons & Illustrations

**Style:** Flat, minimalist, 2D  
**Color:** Brand blue or single accent color  
**Source:** Heroicons, Lucide, or custom

**Size:** Consistent across deck (recommend 64x64px minimum)

---

## Animation & Transitions

### Allowed

**Transitions:**
- Fade (300-500ms)
- Cut (instant slide change)
- Push (for sequential slides)

**Animations:**
- Fade in (for bullet points, one at a time)
- Wipe/Appear for revealing data

**Timing:** 300-500ms (fast enough to not annoy, slow enough to register)

---

### Not Allowed

**Avoid:**
- Spinning, bouncing, flying effects
- Sound effects (unless demo video)
- Auto-advancing slides (presenter controls pace)
- More than 2 animation types per slide

## Storytelling Framework

### Problem-Solution-Impact

**Structure:**
1. **Hook:** Start with a relatable problem
2. **Context:** Market/industry background
3. **Problem:** Customer pain points (emotional + rational)
4. **Solution:** How Clario solves it
5. **Impact:** Results, metrics, testimonials
6. **Call to Action:** What you want audience to do

---

### Data Storytelling

**Framework:**
1. **Big Number:** Start with the headline metric
2. **Context:** Why it matters
3. **Breakdown:** How we got there
4. **Proof:** Chart/graph showing trend

**Example:**
```
Slide Title: "150% Customer Growth in Q1"

1. Big number: 150% (centered, 72pt)
2. Context: "We onboarded 450 new F&B businesses"
3. Breakdown: "Mika: 60%, Kikil: 30%, Manengah: 10%"
4. Chart: Bar chart showing monthly growth
```

## Presentation Checklist

**Before You Present:**

**Content:**
- [ ] Slides tell a story (not just bullet points)
- [ ] Data is current (check dates)
- [ ] No typos (spell check + human review)
- [ ] Branded correctly (logos, colors, fonts)

**Design:**
- [ ] Consistent fonts throughout
- [ ] High contrast (readable from 10+ feet)
- [ ] Images are high-res (no pixelation)
- [ ] Animations are subtle (if any)

**Technical:**
- [ ] Tested on presentation laptop/projector
- [ ] Backup saved (PDF + PPTX)
- [ ] Video/demo links work
- [ ] Presenter notes added
