# Remote Work Policy

**Effective Date:** December 2025
**Scope:** All eligible employees (template for future team growth)
**Owner:** HR + Operations
**Current Team:** 2-person founding team (Month 1-9), 3-person team (Month 9+ with CSM hire)

> [!NOTE]
> **Template Status:** This policy is designed for the company at scale (5-15 person team). Current 2-person founding team operates more flexibly. Formal policies phase in as team grows.

---

## Our Philosophy

**Remote-first, not remote-only.** We believe great work happens anywhere, but we value occasional in-person connection.

**Default:** Work from anywhere in Indonesia
**Office:** Jakarta office available (hot-desking)
**Requirements:** Quarterly team gatherings (company-paid travel)

---

## Eligibility

### Who Can Work Remotely

**Fully Remote (100%):**
- Engineering, Product, Design
- Customer Success (after onboarding)
- Marketing, Finance

**Hybrid (2-3 days/week office):**
- Sales (client meetings benefit from office)
- Operations

**Office-Required:**
- Founding Team (for now, leading by example on collaboration)
- Roles requiring physical presence (rare)

**Probation:** First 3 months typically hybrid (learn culture, build relationships)

---

## Work Hours & Availability

### Core Hours

**Required overlap:** 10 AM - 3 PM WIB (Monday-Friday)
- Everyone online and available
- Meetings scheduled during core hours
- Quick Slack responses expected

**Outside core hours:** Flexible
- Start early/late based on preference
- Total 40 hours/week expected
- Log hours in system (honor-based)

### Async-First Communication

**Default to async:**
- Write it down (docs > meetings)
- Slack messages (not calls, unless urgent)
- Record Loom videos (explain complex topics)
- Use threads (organize conversations)

**Sync when:**
- Complex decision needing discussion
- Brainstorming
- Conflict resolution
- Team bonding

**No expectation of immediate response** outside core hours.

## Equipment & Setup

### Provided by Company

**Standard Package (all remote workers):**
- Laptop (MacBook Pro or equivalent Windows)
- Monitor (24" minimum, upon request)
- Keyboard + mouse
- Headphones with mic
- Laptop stand
- Webcam (if laptop camera insufficient)

**Home Office Stipend:** Rp 5M (one-time)
- Desk + chair
- Lighting
- Decor/personal items
- Internet router upgrade

### Internet Requirements

**Minimum:**
- 25 Mbps download
- 10 Mbps upload
- Low latency (< 50ms to Jakarta)

**Stipend:** Rp 500K/month internet allowance

**Backup:** Mobile hotspot recommended (company reimburses data if primary fails)

### Security Requirements

**Must:**
- Use company VPN (provided)
- Encrypt hard drive (FileVault/BitLocker)
- Lock screen when away (< 5 min auto-lock)
- Secure WiFi at home (WPA3, strong password)
- Physical security (lock laptop when not home)

**Don't:**
- Work from public WiFi (without VPN)
- Share logins with family
- Leave laptop unattended in public
- Take photos of screens with sensitive data

**Lost/stolen laptop:** Report immediately (24/7 hotline)

## Workspace Guidelines

### Home Office

**Ideal:**
- Dedicated space (separate from bedroom if possible)
- Good lighting (natural + desk lamp)
- Professional background for video calls
- Minimal noise/distractions

**Reality:** We understand home life happens
- Kids/pets interrupt? No problem, we're understanding
- Can't always control background
- Blur/virtual background ok

### Coworking Spaces

**Allowance:** Up to Rp 2M/month (if you prefer coworking to home)

**Popular options:**
- GoWork, WeWork, EV Hive
- Submit expense with receipt

### Working from Anywhere (within Indonesia)

**Allowed:**
- Bali, Bandung, parents' home, etc.
- Notify team (for timezone/availability)
- Maintain core hours

**Not allowed (without approval):**
- Working from abroad (tax/legal complications)
- Exception: 2-week "workcation" allowed 1x/year

## Communication

### Daily Standups (Async)

**Post by 10 AM in #standup channel:**
- Yesterday: What I accomplished
- Today: What I plan to do
- Blockers: Any help needed

**Purpose:** Visibility, not surveillance. Trust-based.

### Meetings

**Calendar Expectations:**
- Keep calendar updated
- Accept/decline promptly
- Arrive on time
- Camera on (default, ok to turn off for personal reasons)

**Meeting Best Practices:**
- Agenda required (or decline)
- Start/end on time
- Take notes (rotate note-taker)
- Record important meetings (for those who can't attend)

**"No meeting" blocks:** Encouraged (focus time)

**Meeting-free Fridays:** After 2 PM, no meetings

### Response Times

**Slack:**
- Core hours: < 2 hours
- Outside core hours: Next business day

**Email:**
- Non-urgent: 24 hours
- Urgent: Mark as such, Slack if critical

**After-hours/weekends:**
- Ok to send messages (schedule send recommended)
- NO expectation of response until next business day

## Performance & Productivity

### How We Measure

**Output, not hours:**
- Did you deliver what you committed?
- Is your work high quality?
- Do you meet deadlines?

**Not:**
- When you're online
- How many hours logged
- How fast you respond to Slack

### Self-Management

**Expected:**
- Plan your day/week
- Proactively communicate blockers
- Ask for help when stuck
- Update project status

**Weekly check-ins:** 30 min with manager (discuss progress, blockers, support needed)

### Red Flags

**We'll address if:**
- Consistently missing deadlines
- Poor communication (teammates can't reach you)
- Low quality work
- Not participating in team interactions

**We won't:**
- Micromanage your schedule
- Track your keystrokes
- surveil your activity

## Team Connection

### Quarterly Gatherings

**Cadence:** 4x/year (Q1,Q2,Q3, Q4)

**Format:**
- 3-day offsite (Thu-Sat)
- Mix: Work + fun
- All-expenses paid (travel, hotel, meals)
- Attendance required (unless exceptional circumstance)

**Location:** Rotates (Jakarta, Bali, Bandung, etc.)

**Goals:**
- Team bonding
- Strategic planning
- Workshops/training
- Celebrate wins

### Virtual Social

**Monthly:**
- Virtual coffee roulette (random 1:1s)
- Team game night (optional)
- Show & tell (personal interests)

**Daily:**
- #random Slack channel (memes, life updates)
- Water cooler chat (non-work conversations encouraged)

## Mental Health & Boundaries

### Preventing Burnout

**Encouraged:**
- Take lunch break (away from computer)
- End work at reasonable hour
- Use all vacation days (mandatory minimum 10/year)
- Disconnect fully on weekends

**Discouraged:**
- Working sick (rest & recover)
- Responding to Slack after 8 PM
- Checking email on vacation
- "Always on" mentality

### "Office hours"

**Set yours:**
- Update Slack status ("Working 8AM-5PM")
- Block calendar outside those hours
- Team respects boundaries

### Support

**Mental health benefit:** Rp 10M/year
- Therapy/counseling
- Mental health apps (Calm, Headspace)
- Wellness programs

**Burnout signs? Talk to manager/HR.** We'll adjust workload.

## Challenges & Solutions

### Challenge: Isolation

**Solutions:**
- Join virtual coworking (Focusmate)
- Coworking space allowance
- Quarterly in-person gatherings
- Virtual social events

### Challenge: Overworking

**Solutions:**
- Clear start/end times
- Manager check-ins
- Auto-shutdown reminders (6 PM Slack notification)
- Mandatory time off

### Challenge: Communication Gaps

**Solutions:**
- Over-communicate
- Document everything
- Regular 1:1s
- Team retros (monthly)

- Quiet focus areas
