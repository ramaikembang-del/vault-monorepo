# Qualification Checklist - BANT Framework

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Use:** After discovery call to score leads

---

## BANT Scoring

Score each area 1-5 (5 = strong, 1 = weak)

### B - Budget

**Score: ___/5**

Questions asked:
- [ ] "Have you allocated budget for analytics tools?"
- [ ] "What are you currently spending on reports/data?"
- [ ] "What's your typical monthly revenue?" (size indicator)

**Scoring:**
- 5: Has confirmed budget, amount fits our pricing
- 4: Has budget, might need approval for exact amount
- 3: Can find budget if sees value
- 2: No budget allocated, would need to request
- 1: No budget, unlikely to get approved

---

### A - Authority

**Score: ___/5**

Questions asked:
- [ ] "Who else is involved in this decision?"
- [ ] "Do you need approval from anyone?"
- [ ] "Who signs off on new software purchases?"

**Scoring:**
- 5: They are the decision maker, no approval needed
- 4: They decide, just needs partner/co-owner sign-off (formality)
- 3: They influence, but boss/board decides
- 2: They're gathering info, someone else decides
- 1: No decision-making power

**Key Contact:** [Name and role of actual decision maker]

---

### N - Need

**Score: ___/5**

Questions asked:
- [ ] "What's your biggest challenge with current approach?"
- [ ] "On scale 1-10, how urgent is solving this?"
- [ ] "What happens if you don't solve this?"

**Scoring:**
- 5: Critical pain, actively looking for solution NOW
- 4: Significant pain, planning to solve in 1-3 months
- 3: Moderate pain, "nice to have"
- 2: Minor pain, low priority
- 1: No real pain, just exploring

**Their Top 3 Pain Points:**
1. _________________
2. _________________
3. _________________

---

### T - Timeline

**Score: ___/5**

Questions asked:
- [ ] "When would you like to have this in place?"
- [ ] "Is there a specific deadline or event?"
- [ ] "What's driving the timeline?"

**Scoring:**
- 5: Need solution within 2 weeks
- 4: Within 1 month
- 3: Within 1 quarter
- 2: Within 6 months
- 1: "Someday" / no timeline

**Target Go-Live Date:** __________

---

## Total BANT Score

**Total: ___/20**

**Lead Quality:**
- **18-20: HOT** - Prioritize! High chance to close within 2 weeks
- **14-17: WARM** - Good opportunity, likely to close in 1-3 months
- **10-13: COOL** - Nurture, may close in 3-6 months
- **<10: COLD** - Long-term nurture or disqualify

---

## Additional Qualification Factors

### Fit Score

- [ ] **Industry fit:** F&B business (restaurant, cafe, etc.)
- [ ] **Size fit:** Revenue >Rp 50M/month OR 2+ locations
- [ ] **Tech fit:** Has POS or willing to track manually
- [ ] **Geo fit:** Indonesia-based

**Red flags (disqualify):**
- [ ] Not F&B
- [ ] Too small (<Rp 20M/month, single location, no growth plans)
- [ ] Unrealistic expectations
- [ ] Bad payment history (if existing customer referral)

## Next Steps Based on Score

### HOT Leads (18-20)
1. Send proposal within 24 hours
2. Schedule decision call within 1 week
3. Aim to close within 2 weeks
4. Daily follow-ups OK

### WARM Leads (14-17)
1. Send proposal within 2-3 days
2. Schedule follow-up in 1 week
3. Weekly check-ins
4. Target close: 1-3 months

### COOL Leads (10-13)
1. Email resources (case studies, ROI calc)
2. Add to nurture sequence
3. Monthly check-ins
4. Re-qualify in 3 months

### COLD Leads (<10)
1. Politely disengage or
2. Long-term nurture (quarterly)
3. Focus energy on hotter leads
