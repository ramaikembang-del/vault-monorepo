# Contract Review Checklist

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Use:** Before sending contracts to customers

---

## Pre-Send Checklist

- [ ] **Correct customer information**
 - Company name (exact legal name)
 - Address
 - NPWP (if corporate)
 - Contact person name and email

- [ ] **Pricing accurate**
 - Product/tier matches proposal
 - Prices match quoted amount
 - Payment terms clear (monthly/annual)
 - PPN 11% calculated correctly

- [ ] **Dates correct**
 - Contract start date
 - Billing cycle start
 - Contract duration (if annual)

- [ ] **Product specifications**
 - Correct product(s) listed
 - Features match tier
 - User count accurate
 - Any custom terms noted

- [ ] **Payment details**
 - Payment method options listed
 - Bank details correct
 - Invoice schedule clear

- [ ] **Legal review** (if custom terms)
 - Standard contract → No review needed
 - Custom terms >Rp 50M → Legal team review required

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

 Wrong tier/price
 Misspelled company name
 Incorrect NPWP
 Missing PPN calculation
 Wrong start date
 Promised features not in contract

---

## Standard Contract Terms

**PWBI Standard Terms (for reference):**

- **Payment:** Net 30 for corporate, upfront for SMB
- **Cancellation:** 30 days notice
- **Data:** Customer owns their data
- **Support:** Email support included, SLA based on tier
- **Updates:** Free product updates included
- **Liability:** Limited to fees paid in last 12 months
- **Jurisdiction:** Indonesian law, Jakarta courts

**Custom terms require VP Sales approval.**

## Sending the Contract

**Email template:**
```
Subject: PWBI Contract - [Customer Name]

Hi [Name],

Excited to get you started with PWBI!

Attached is your contract for [Product] at Rp [X]/month.

Please review and if everything looks good:
1. Sign and return (digital signature or scan)
2. We'll invoice you for first month
3. Setup begins within 24 hours of payment

Questions? I'm here!

Best,
[Your Name]
```

**Attachments:**
- Contract PDF
- Invoice (if upfront payment)
- Getting Started Guide

---

## Post-Signature

- [ ] Save signed contract to Google Drive (contracts folder)
- [ ] Update CRM (deal status: "Closed-Won")
- [ ] Notify finance team (for invoicing)
- [ ] Notify CS team (for onboarding)
- [ ] Send welcome email with next steps

---

**Approval needed:** Contracts >Rp 100M require CFO signature
