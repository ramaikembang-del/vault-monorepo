## Tone of Voice

**Bahasa Indonesia (Customer-Facing):**
- Informal but respectful ("Anda" not "kamu")
- Simple language (no jargon)
- Action-oriented ("Lihat dashboard" not "Dashboard tersedia untuk dilihat")

**English (Internal/B2B):**
- Professional but friendly
- Data-driven (cite numbers)
- Clear and concise

## Brand Don'ts

❌ **Don't:** Use corporate buzzwords ("synergy", "paradigm shift")  
❌ **Don't:** Over-promise ("10x your revenue guaranteed!")  
❌ **Don't:** Use stock photos of Western restaurants  
❌ **Don't:** Be condescending ("Even YOU can use analytics!")
