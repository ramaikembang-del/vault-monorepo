# Sprint 1 Launch Strategy

**Timeline:** Weeks 1-4
**Focus:** Fast validation with minimal spend

---

## Product Launch Sequence

### Week 1-2: Power BI Template Launch
**Target:** 5+ sales (Gate 1A)

**Channels:**
1. **WhatsApp Direct Outreach** (Primary)
 - Target: Bandung research contacts (10+ warm leads)
 - Message: "Solusi dashboard profesional Rp 2-3M (hemat Rp 20M+ vs custom)"
 - CTA: Free consultation + demo

2. **Instagram Content Series**
 - Before/After dashboard transformations
 - Customer pain points → Template solutions
 - Pricing transparency (Rp 2-3M vs alternatives)

3. **LinkedIn Thought Leadership**
 - "Kenapa F&B Mikro-Kecil Perlu Analytics?"
 - Case studies from research phase

**Conversion Path:**
WhatsApp inquiry → Demo video → Purchase → Onboarding call

---

### Week 2-3: Template Support Launch
**Target:** 30% conversion from template buyers (Gate 1C)

**Strategy:**
- Offer during template onboarding call
- "Rp 75K/month = always up-to-date + priority support"
- Bundle discount: First month free with template purchase

**Value Messaging:**
- Template updates as we add features
- WhatsApp support (reply <4 hours)
- Monthly tips and best practices

---

### Week 1-4: Web Dashboard Lite Soft Launch
**Target:** 50+ users, 30%+ engagement (Gate 1B)

**Channels:**
1. **Existing Network** (Week 1-2)
 - Research participants
 - Personal connections
 - F&B owner groups

2. **Social Proof Campaign** (Week 3-4)
 - Early user testimonials
 - Usage screenshots
 - "Free forever for basic features"

**Messaging:**
- "Analytics tanpa ribet - Excel masuk, insight keluar"
- Offline-first (no internet needed)
- Free to start, upgrade when ready

---

## Budget Allocation

**Total Sprint 1 Marketing Budget:** Rp 2-5M (bootstrap mode)

| Channel | Budget | Expected ROI |
|---------|--------|--------------|
| WhatsApp/Direct (Template) | Rp 0 | Rp 10-30M revenue |
| Instagram Ads (Lite awareness) | Rp 1-2M | 50-100 signups |
| Content Creation (graphics, video) | Rp 1-2M | Reusable assets |
| Event/Workshop (optional) | Rp 1M | 10-20 qualified leads |

---

## Success Metrics

### Gate 1A (Week 2): Template Sales
- **Target:** 5+ sales
- **Metric:** Rp 10-15M revenue
- **Decision:** If met → Continue marketing push

### Gate 1B (Week 4): Lite Validation
- **Target:** 50+ users, 30%+ engagement
- **Metrics:**
 - 50+ downloads/signups
 - 30%+ open app 3+ times/week
 - NPS >40
- **Decision:** If met → Proceed to Sprint 2 paid tier

### Gate 1C (Week 4): Support Conversion
- **Target:** 30%+ of template buyers
- **Metric:** 3+ support subscriptions (if 10 templates sold)
- **Decision:** If met → Keep offering, else pivot to annual only

## Key Messaging by Product

### Power BI Template
**Headline:** "Dashboard Profesional Rp 2-3M. Hemat Rp 20M+ vs Bikin Custom."

**Pain Points:**
- Bingung mau analytics dari mana
- Ga sanggup bayar programmer Rp 20-50M
- Takut ribet setup sendiri

**Solution:**
- Template siap pakai (2-4 jam setup)
- Harga terjangkau (one-time Rp 2-3M)
- 3 tier: DIY / Assisted / Managed

---

### Template Support
**Headline:** "Rp 75K/bulan = Template Selalu Update + Support Priority"

**Value Props:**
- Always get latest features
- WhatsApp support <4 hours
- Monthly tips dari praktisi

---

### Web Dashboard Lite
**Headline:** "Excel Masuk. Insight Keluar. Offline & Gratis."

**USPs:**
- No internet needed (offline-first)
- Free forever (basic features)
- Simple: Upload Excel → Auto insights

**Upgrade Trigger:**
- When they love it → "Unlock 8 more features for Rp 200K/mo"

## Next Steps After Sprint 1

If gates pass:
- **Sprint 2 Marketing:** Paid acquisition (Meta ads, Google ads)
- **Lite Paid Tier Launch:** Conversion campaign for free users
- **Accounting Module Pre-launch:** Waitlist building

If gates fail:
- Analyze why (pricing? messaging? product-market fit?)
- Iterate messaging
- Consider pivot
