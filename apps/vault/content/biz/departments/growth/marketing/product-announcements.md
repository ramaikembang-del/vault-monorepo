New product or major version (Template, SaaS, etc.)

### 2. Feature Release
Significant new feature (Tier 2, new integration)

### 3. Minor Update
Small improvements, bug fixes

## Distribution Channels

**Email (Primary):**
- Customer list (existing customers)
- Newsletter subscribers
- Prospects (leads database)

**Social Media:**
- Linkedln (professional/B2B tone)
- Facebook (groups + company page)
- Instagram (visual, short copy)

**Blog:**
- Detailed announcement post
- SEO optimized
- Link from email/social

**In-App:**
- Banner notification (for existing users)
- Modal popup (for major launches)

---

## Timing Strategy

**Pre-Launch (T-7 days):**
- Teaser campaign ("Something big coming...")
- Early access list (for VIP customers)

**Launch Day (T-0):**
- 9am: Email announcement sent
- 10am: Social media posts
- 11am: Blog post published
- 12pm: In-app notification enabled

**Post-Launch (T+1 to T+7):**
- Follow-up email (to non-openers)
- Case study / testimonial (early adopters)
- FAQ update (common questions)
