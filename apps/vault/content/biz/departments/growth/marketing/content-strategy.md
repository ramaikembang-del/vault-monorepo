# Content Strategy
**Educational Content & Thought Leadership**

**Owner:** Marketing  
**Status:** PLANNED - Launches Sprint 2+

---

## Content Mission

**Educate F&B operators on data-driven decision making**

NOT: "Buy our product"  
YES: "Learn how data helps your business (oh and we have a tool for that)"

---

## Content Themes

### Theme 1: F&B Analytics 101
- "5 metrics setiap pemilik warung harus tahu"
- "Cara hitung gross profit margin"
- "Bedanya revenue vs profit (dan kenapa penting)"

**Format:** Blog posts, infographics, videos

### Theme 2: Success Stories
- Customer case studies ("Bagaimana Warung Pak Budi naikkan profit 18%")
- Before/after metrics (revenue, profit, efficiency)

**Format:** Video interviews, written case studies

### Theme 3: Industry Insights
- "Tren F&B Indonesia 2026"
- "Rata-rata profit margin warung kopi"
- Seasonal guides ("Persiapan Ramadan: stok dan pricing")

**Format:**Industry reports, webinars

---

## Content Calendar

**Frequency:**
- Blog: 2x/month (Bahasa Indonesia)
- Social: 3x/week (Instagram, Facebook)
- Newsletter: 1x/month (for customers)
- Webinar: 1x/quarter

---

## Distribution Channels

### Owned Media
- Company blog (Notion or WordPress)
- Email newsletter (for customers)
- YouTube channel (product tutorials)

### Earned Media
- Guest posts (F&B industry blogs)
- Podcast interviews (entrepreneur shows)
- PR (media coverage for milestones)

### Social Media
- Facebook (groups: Pengusaha Kuliner)
- Instagram (visual case studies)
- LinkedIn (B2B thought leadership)

---

## Success Metrics

**Engagement:**
- Blog: 500+ monthly views by Month 6
- Social: 1,000+ followers by Year 1
- Newsletter: 20%+ open rate

**Business Impact:**
- 10% of leads from content marketing
- 5+ inbound demo requests/month (attributed to content)
