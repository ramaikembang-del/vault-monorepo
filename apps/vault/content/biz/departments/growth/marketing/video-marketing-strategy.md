# Video Marketing Strategy
**Bahasa Indonesia Video Content for F&B**

**Owner:** Marketing Team  
**Status:** PLANNED - Year 1+

---

## Why Video?

**F&B Owners Prefer:**
- Visual learning (watch vs read)
- Bahasa Indonesia (not English subtitles)
- Short format (<10 min, mobile-friendly)

**Video Outperforms:**
- 10x engagement vs blog posts
- 5x shares on social media
- Better SEO (YouTube = 2nd largest search engine)

---

## Video Types

### 1. Educational Series (Primary)

**"F&B Analytics 101"**
- 5-minute explainer videos
- Topics: "Cara Hitung Gross Profit", "5 Metrics Penting untuk Warung"
- Publish: 2x/month

**Example:**
- Title: "Cara Tahu Warung Anda Untung atau Rugi (3 Langkah Mudah)"
- Length: 7 minutes
- Format: Screen recording + voice-over
- CTA: "Download template Excel gratis"

---

### 2. Customer Success Stories

**Case Study Videos**
- Customer testimonial + dashboard walkthrough
- "Bagaimana Pak Budi Naikkan Profit 18% dengan Data"
- Publish: 1x/quarter

**Format:**
- Intro: Problem (manual tracking, can't see patterns)
- Solution: Using Clario dashboard
- Results: Specific metrics (revenue up X%, saved Y hours)
- Call-to-action: "Request demo"

---

### 3. Product Tutorials

**How-To Videos**
- Dashboard setup guide
- Feature walkthroughs
- Troubleshooting common issues

**Publish:** As needed (when product updates)

---

### 4. Live Webinars (Interactive)

**Monthly Webinar Series**
- Topic: Tactical F&B tips (pricing, menu engineering)
- Format: 45-min presentation + 15-min Q&A
- Platform: Zoom webinar
- Recording: Posted to YouTube after

**Goal:** 50+ attendees, 10% convert to demo

---

## Distribution Channels

**YouTube (Primary):**
- Clario official channel
- SEO-optimized titles/descriptions (Indonesian keywords)
- Playlist organization (by topic)

**Facebook:**
- Post to F&B groups (Pengusaha Kuliner Indonesia)
- Native video upload (better reach than YouTube link)

**Instagram:**
- Reels (<90 seconds, highlights from long videos)
- Stories (behind-the-scenes, quick tips)

**Email:**
- Embed in newsletter
- Send dedicated video announcements

---

## Production Process

### DIY Production (Year 1 - Bootstrap)

**Equipment:**
- Smartphone camera (good enough for talking head)
- Screen recording software (Loom for demos)
- Basic editing (CapCut or DaVinci Resolve free)

**Team:**
- Founder = host (authentic, knows product)
- Marketing Lead = editor

**Cost:** ~Rp 0 (use existing tools)

---

### Professional Production (Year 2+ - Scale)

**Hire:**
- Videographer (freelance, Rp 2-5M per video)
- Editor (part-time, Rp 5M/month)
- Motion graphics (for intros/outros)

**Studio:** Simple home setup (lighting, backdrop) ~Rp 10M investment

## SEO Optimization

**YouTube SEO:**
- Title: Keyword-rich ("Cara Hitung Profit Warung Makan")
- Description: First 150 characters critical, include link
- Tags: Indonesian F&B keywords
- Thumbnail: Contrasting colors, text overlay (Bahasa)
- Closed captions: Auto-generate + edit for accuracy

**Target Keywords:**
- "dashboard restoran"
- "analisa keuangan warung"
- "cara hitung profit f&b"
- "aplikasi kasir restoran"
