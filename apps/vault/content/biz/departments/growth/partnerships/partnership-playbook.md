## Partnership Types

### 1. POS Partnerships (Strategic)
**Partners:** Moka, Pawoon, Qasir, Olsera  
**Value Exchange:**
- To them: Enhanced analytics for their customers
- To us: Distribution (access to 50K+ F&B businesses)

**Models:**
- Reseller (they sell our product, rev share)
- Integration (API connection, co-marketing)
- White-label (we build, they brand)

---

###  2. Accounting Partnerships
**Partners:** Accurate, Jurnal, Zahir  
**Value Exchange:**
- To them: F&B-specific features
- To us: Automatic P&L data (easier for customers)

**Model:** API integration + co-marketing

---

### 3. Channel Partnerships (Sales)
**Partners:** F&B consultants, business advisors  
**Value Exchange:**
- To them: Commission (10-20% of sales)
- To us: Qualified leads from trusted advisors

**Model:** Affiliate/referral program

---

## Partnership Process

### Stage 1: Identify (Month 1)
- Research potential partners
- Evaluate strategic fit (mutual value?)
- Prioritize top 3-5

### Stage 2: Outreach (Month 2)
- Warm intro (via mutual contact if possible)
- Cold email if needed
- Pitch deck prepared

### Stage 3: Negotiation (Month 3-4)
- Terms: Rev share, exclusivity, support
- Legal: Contract review
- Pilot: Test with 10-20 customers first

### Stage 4: Launch (Month 5)
- Co-marketing announcement
- Integration goes live
- Track metrics (referrals, revenue)

### Stage 5: Optimize (Ongoing)
- Monthly review meetings
- Adjust terms if not working
- Expand or terminate

---

## Partnership Metrics

**Success KPIs:**
- # of partner referrals per month
- Partner-sourced revenue as % of total
- Customer retention (partner vs direct)

**Year 2 Targets:**
- 2-3 active partnerships
- 20% of revenue from partners

---

## Sample Partnership (Moka)

**Opportunity:** Moka has 30K F&B merchants, no advanced analytics

**Proposal:**
- We build "Moka Analytics powered by Clario"
- They upsell to customers (Rp 200K/month add-on)
- Revenue split: 70% Moka, 30% us
- We provide technical support

**Benefits:**
- Moka: New revenue stream
- Us: 30K distribution overnight
- Customers: Seamless integration

**Risk:** Dependence on single partner (mitigate with multiple partnerships)
