# Customer Success Onboarding Playbook
**CS Team Member First 30 Days**

**Owner:** CS Manager  
**Status:** PLANNED - When hiring CS #2

---

## Role Overview

**Customer Success Specialist**
- Support customers (tickets, chat, calls)
- Drive activation (onboarding new users)
- Prevent churn (monitor usage, intervene early)
- Expand accounts (identify upsell opportunities)

**Success Criteria:** NPS >50, ticket resolution <24h, 0 escalations to founder

---

## Week 1: Product Mastery

### Day 1: Orientation
- Company overview (mission, values, products)
- Team introductions (meet everyone, 15 min each)
- Tools setup (email, Slack, CRM, dashboard access)
- Security training ([[biz/departments/it/training/security-basics|Security Basics]])

### Day 2-3: Product Deep Dive
- **Hands-On:** Use all products yourself
  - Install Template (Tier 1/2/3)
  - Access SaaS dashboard (all tiers)
  - Test onboarding flow end-to-end
- **Learn:** Features, pricing, use cases
- **Quiz:** 20 product questions (must score 90%+)

### Day 4-5: Customer Empathy
- Read: 20 recent support tickets (understand common issues)
- Listen: 5 recorded customer calls (learn tone, language)
- Shadow: Join 3 live customer calls (observe CS Manager)

**Milestone:** Can demo product confidently (mock demo with team)

---

## Week 2: Support Operations

### Day 6-7: Ticket Triage
- **Learn:** Ticket prioritization (P0-P3)
- **Practice:** Classify 10 sample tickets
- **Tools:** CRM ticketing system (Intercom or Zendesk)

### Day 8-10: Handling Tickets (Under Supervision)
- **Respond:** Draft responses to low-priority tickets (P3)
- **Review:** CS Manager reviews all drafts before sending
- **Iterate:** Learn from feedback

**Milestone:** Handle 5 independent tickets (approved by manager)

---

## Week 3: Customer Interactions

### Day 11-13: Live Support (Co-Pilot Mode)
- **Chat Support:** Handle live chats (manager monitors)
- **Email Support:** Respond independently (manager spot-checks)
- **Phone Calls:** Shadow manager on complex calls

### Day 14-15: Proactive Outreach
- **Learn:** Churn prevention playbook
- **Practice:** Draft outreach emails (to low-usage customers)
- **Execute:** Send 5 proactive emails (manager approves first)

**Milestone:** 90% customer satisfaction rating (from first interactions)

---

## Week 4: Advanced Skills

### Day 16-18: Product Expertise
- **Learn:** Advanced features (integrations, custom metrics)
- **Troubleshoot:** Practice debugging (common setup issues)
- **Escalation:** When to escalate vs handle (decision tree)

### Day 19-20: Upsell & Expansion
- **Learn:** Expansion strategy ([[biz/departments/growth/customer-success/expansion-strategy|Expansion Playbook]])
- **Practice:** Identify upsell opportunities (from CRM data)
- **Role-Play:** Practice upsell conversation (with manager)

**Milestone:** Pass CS certification (internal assessment)

---

## Day 30: Performance Review

**30-Day Check-In** (1 hour with CS Manager)

**Review:**
- Product knowledge: Excellent / Good / Needs Improvement
- Ticket quality: Response time, resolution rate, customer satisfaction
- Proactiveness: Churn prevention, upsell identification
- Team fit: Collaboration, communication, culture alignment

**Set Goals for Next 60 Days:**
- Handle 20 tickets/week independently
- Achieve NPS >8 from customers
- Close first upsell (Tier 1 â†’ Support)

**Decision:** Continue / Extend probation / Part ways
