# Customer Success Playbook
**Core CS Operations & Best Practices**

**Owner:** Customer Success Team  
**Last Updated:** January 8, 2026  
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

## Executive Summary

**CS Mission:** Ensure customers achieve their desired outcomes using our products, leading to retention, expansion, and advocacy.

**Key Metrics:**
- NPS >50 (customer satisfaction)
- Retention rate >85% (annual)  
- Expansion revenue >20% of total ARR

---

## Part 1: CS Team Structure

### Roles & Responsibilities

**CS Manager (1 person, Year 1)**
- Owns all customer relationships
- Handles onboarding, support, renewals
- Escalation point for all issues

**CS Specialist (Hire Year 2, when >100 customers)**
- Tier 1 support (tickets, chat)
- Onboarding coordination
- Reports to CS Manager

**Account Executive (Hire Year 2, Enterprise segment)**
- Manages Enterprise accounts (>Rp 2M/month)
- QBRs, upsells, contract negotiations

---

## Part 2: Customer Lifecycle

### Stage 1: Onboarding (Days 1-30)
- **Goal:** Activate customer (first value achieved)
- **Key Activity:** Setup assistance, first login celebration
- **Success Metric:** 80% active users by Day 30
- **Playbook:** [[products/05-saas-basic/customer-success/onboarding-playbook|Onboarding Playbook]]

### Stage 2: Adoption (Months 2-6)
- **Goal:** Build usage habit (3x/week login)
- **Key Activity:** Feature education, weekly digests
- **Success Metric:** 50% power users (5+ features used)

### Stage 3: Retention (Months 7-12)
- **Goal:** Renew subscription
- **Key Activity:** QBR (Enterprise), renewal outreach
- **Success Metric:** >85% renewal rate

### Stage 4: Expansion (Year 2+)
- **Goal:** Upsell to higher tier or add users
- **Key Activity:** Proactive upgrade suggestions
- **Success Metric:** 20% of revenue from expansion

---

## Part 3: Support Operations

### Ticket Prioritization

| Priority | Response SLA | Examples |
|----------|-------------|----------|
| **P0 Critical** | 1 hour | System down, data loss |
| **P1 High** | 4 hours | Core feature broken |
| **P2 Medium** | 24 hours | Minor bug |
| **P3 Low** | 48 hours | How-to questions |

### Escalation Path
1. **Tier 1:** CS Specialist (email/chat)
2. **Tier 2:** CS Manager (complex issues)
3. **Tier 3:** Product Team (bugs/features)
4. **Tier 4:** Founder (Enterprise escalations)

---

## Part 4: Churn Prevention

### Red Flags
- No login for 14+ days
- 50%+ usage drop month-over-month
- Multiple support tickets (frustration)
- Payment failures

### Intervention Tactics
- **Email:** "Miss us?" re-engagement
- **Call:** Personal check-in (Pro+ only)
- **Offer:** Discount (last resort, 20-30% off)

**Churn Prevention Playbook:** [[biz/departments/growth/customer-success/churn-prevention-playbook|Churn Prevention]]

---

## Part 5: Expansion & Upsells

### Triggers for Upsell
- Customer hits usage limits (data cap, user seats)
- Business growth detected (revenue up 50%+)
- Feature request matches higher tier

### Upsell Process
1. Identify opportunity (usage data + conversations)
2. Build business case (ROI calculation)
3. Present offer (email + call for Pro+)
4. Close or defer (don't be pushy)

**Target:** 20% of ARR from expansions by Year 2
