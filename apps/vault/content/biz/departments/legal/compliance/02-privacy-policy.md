### 1.3 Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Personal Data** | Any information relating to an identified or identifiable person (KTP number, email, phone, etc.) |
| **Sensitive Data** | Financial data, health data, biometric data (requires explicit consent) |
| **Processing** | Any operation performed on data: collection, storage, analysis, deletion |
| **Controller** | Entity that determines purposes and means of processing (Customer for their end-customer data, Provider for Customer data) |
| **Processor** | Entity that processes data on behalf of Controller (Provider processes data for Customer) |

## 2. What Data We Collect & Why

### 2.1 Customer Account Data

**What We Collect:**
- Business information: Company name, address, NPWP (tax ID), industry classification
- Contact person: Name, email, phone number, job title
- Payment information: Bank account details (for refunds), billing address
- Login credentials: Email, hashed password, MFA tokens

**Why We Collect:**
- Provide Service (account creation, authentication)
- Process payments and invoicing
- Customer support and communications
- Legal compliance (tax reporting, contracts)

**Legal Basis:**
- Contractual necessity (to fulfill SaaS agreement)
- Legal obligation (tax compliance)

**Retention:** Duration of customer relationship + 7 years (Indonesian tax law requirement)

---

### 2.2 Business Operations Data

**What We Collect:**
- POS transaction data: Sales amounts, product names, quantities, timestamps
- Inventory data: Stock levels, ingredient costs, supplier information
- Staff data: Employee names, schedules, performance metrics (if using staff scheduling module)
- Financial data: Revenue, expenses, profit/loss calculations

**Why We Collect:**
- Provide core analytics functionality
- Generate reports and insights
- Calculate benchmarks (if Customer opts-in)

**Legal Basis:**
- Contractual necessity (core Service functionality)
- Legitimate interest (product improvement)

**Retention:** Active subscription + 90 days (then deleted unless Customer exports)

---

### 2.3 Usage & Analytics Data

**What We Collect:**
- Product usage: Features accessed, reports generated, time spent
- Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type, OS version
- Performance data: Page load times, error logs, API response times

**Why We Collect:**
- Improve product functionality
- Detect and fix bugs
- Optimize performance
- Security monitoring (detect unauthorized access)

**Legal Basis:**
- Legitimate interest (product improvement, security)

**Retention:** 24 months

---

### 2.4 Marketing & Communications Data

**What We Collect:**
- Email interactions: Open rates, click-through rates
- Webinar attendance: Registration info, participation data
- Survey responses: Product feedback, satisfaction scores

**Why We Collect:**
- Send product updates and feature announcements
- Conduct user research
- Improve customer experience

**Legal Basis:**
- Consent (opt-in for marketing emails)
- Legitimate interest (transactional emails like invoices)

**Retention:** Until consent withdrawn or 3 years of inactivity

---

### 2.5 Sensitive Data (Requires Explicit Consent)

**We DO collect (with explicit opt-in):**
- Financial performance data (for benchmarking)
- Employee performance metrics (if using HR modules)

**We DO NOT collect:**
- Biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition)
- Health data
- Religious or political affiliations
- KTP numbers or passport numbers (except for identity verification if required by regulation)

**If Sensitive Data Required:**
- Separate explicit consent obtained
- Additional security measures applied
- Limited to essential business purposes only

## 3. How We Use Your Data

### 3.1 Primary Purposes

**Service Delivery:**

**Business Operations:**

**What We Do:**

- Create industry benchmarks (e.g., "Average revenue per sqm in Jakarta coffee shops")

- Compare performance against industry peers
- Identify improvement opportunities
- Access to market intelligence

**Data Protection:**
- Individual customer data NEVER shared

### 3.3 What We Do NOT Do

**We will NEVER:**
- Sell Customer data to third parties
- Share individual customer data with competitors
- Use data for purposes beyond those stated in this policy (without explicit consent)
- Transfer data outside Indonesia without adequate safeguards (GDPR-equivalent protection)

## 5. Data Storage, Security & Retention

### 5.1 Where We Store Data

**Primary Storage:** AWS Singapore (ap-southeast-1 region)
**Backup Storage:** AWS Singapore (same region, multi-AZ redundancy)
**No International Transfers:** Data does NOT leave Indonesia/Singapore without explicit consent

**If Customer Requires Indonesia-Only Storage:**
- Custom deployment to AWS Jakarta (higher cost, available on request)

---

### 5.2 Security Measures

**Technical Safeguards:**
- **Encryption at rest:** AES-256 encryption for all stored data
- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.3 for all network communications
- **Access controls:** Role-based access control (RBAC), multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- **Network security:** Firewalls, intrusion detection systems (IDS)
- **Regular backups:** Daily incremental, weekly full backups (90-day retention)

**Organizational Safeguards:**
- **Employee training:** Annual data protection training for all staff
- **Background checks:** All employees with data access vetted
- **Access logging:** Audit trails of all data access (who, when, what)
- **Incident response:** Data breach response plan (72-hour notification)

**Third-Party Audits:**
- AWS SOC 2 Type II certified infrastructure
- Annual penetration testing (planned Year 2)
- GDPR compliance certification (if processing EU data)

---

### 5.3 Data Retention & Deletion

| Data Type | Retention Period | Deletion Method |
|-----------|------------------|----------------|
| **Customer account data** | Contract duration + 7 years | Secure deletion (3-pass overwrite) |
| **Business operations data** | Contract duration + 90 days | Secure deletion |
| **Usage analytics** | 24 months | Automatic purge |
| **Backup tapes** | 90 days | Overwrite rotation |
| **Financial records (tax)** | 7 years (Indonesian law) | Archive, then secure deletion |

**Customer-Initiated Deletion:**
- Customer can request data deletion anytime
- 90-day grace period to allow data export
- After 90 days: Irreversible deletion (cannot be recovered)

## 6. Your Rights (Customer & End-Users)

### 6.1 Access Rights

**Right to Access:**
- Request copy of all personal data we hold
- Receive data in machine-readable format (CSV, JSON)
- No fee for first request per year

**How to Request:** Email privacy@pwbifnbgrowth.com
**Response Time:** 30 days

---

### 6.2 Correction & Update Rights

**Right to Rectify:**
- Update incorrect or incomplete data
- Self-service via platform settings
- Or email support for assistance

---

### 6.3 Deletion Rights ("Right to be Forgotten")

**Right to Erasure:**
- Request deletion of personal data
- Applies when data no longer needed or consent withdrawn

**Exceptions (Cannot Delete):**
- Data required for legal compliance (tax records)
- Data needed to fulfill active contract
- Data subject to ongoing legal dispute

---

### 6.4 Portability Rights

**Right to Data Portability:**
- Export data in standard format (CSV, Excel, JSON)
- Transfer data to another service provider
- Available via platform export function or API (Advanced/Enterprise tiers)

---

### 6.5 Objection & Restriction Rights

**Right to Object:**
- Object to processing for direct marketing (unsubscribe from emails)
- Object to automated decision-making (if any)

**Right to Restrict:**
- Temporarily restrict processing while investigating accuracy dispute
- Restrict processing if Customer contests lawfulness

---

### 6.6 Consent Withdrawal

**Right to Withdraw Consent:**
- Withdraw consent for optional processing (benchmarking, marketing emails)
- Does not affect lawfulness of prior processing
- May affect Service functionality (e.g., losing access to benchmarks)

**How to Withdraw:** Email privacy@pwbifnbgrowth.com or use in-platform toggle

## 10. Data Breach Notification

### 10.1 Our Obligations

**IF data breach occurs:**
1. **Assess severity:** Determine impact on Customer data
2. **Contain breach:** Immediate action to stop unauthorized access
3. **Notify authorities:** Report to Indonesian data protection authority (Kementerian Kominfo) within 3 days
4. **Notify Customer:** Inform affected Customers within 72 hours
5. **Remediate:** Implement fixes to prevent recurrence

**Notification Includes:**
- Nature of breach (what data affected)
- Likely consequences
- Measures taken to address breach
- Recommendations for Customer action

---

### 10.2 Customer Obligations

**If Customer causes breach (e.g., weak password):**
- Customer responsible for notifying their own end-users
- Provider assists with breach assessment and containment
