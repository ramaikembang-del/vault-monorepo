## 5. Intellectual Property

### 5.1 Work Product Ownership
**ALL work product created under this Agreement belongs to Client**, including but not limited to:
- Code, dashboards, designs
- Documentation, reports
- Concepts, ideas developed during the project

**"Work for Hire":** To the extent permitted by Indonesian law, work is considered "made for hire" and Client owns all IP rights.

### 5.2 Assignment of Rights
If work is not automatically owned by Client, Contractor hereby **assigns all rights, title, and interest** to Client.

### 5.3 Contractor Retains
- **Contractor may retain** portfolio samples (non-confidential screenshots) with Client approval
- **Contractor may not** use Client's confidential data or proprietary methods

## 9. Termination

### 9.1 By Client
Client may terminate:
- **For Cause:** Immediately if Contractor breaches agreement, fails to deliver, or delivers substandard work
- **For Convenience:** With 7 days' notice; Client pays for work completed to date

### 9.2 By Contractor
Contractor may terminate:
- With 14 days' notice if Client fails to pay or materially breaches agreement
- Contractor must deliver all work in progress

### 9.3 Upon Termination
- All work product transfers to Client (even if incomplete)
- Client pays for work completed and accepted
- Confidentiality obligations survive
