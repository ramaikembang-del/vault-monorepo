## Vesting Overview

**What is Vesting?**  
Earning equity over time (not all at once)

**Why Vest?**
- Align incentives (earn equity by staying/performing)
- Protect company (if someone leaves early, company retains equity)
- Standard practice (investors expect it)

**Who Gets Equity?**
- Founders (sweat equity)
- Early employees (equity compensation)
- Advisors (payment for advice)

---

## Standard Vesting Terms

### 4-Year Vesting with 1-Year Cliff

**Total Grant:** [X]% equity

**Vesting Schedule:**
- **Cliff:** 0% vests in Year 0 (Month 1-12)
- **After 1 Year:** 25% vests (cliff vest)
- **Monthly Vesting:** Remaining 75% vests monthly over next 36 months
- **Fully Vested:** After 4 years = 100% owned

**Example:**
- Grant: 10% equity
- After 12 months: 2.5% vested
- After 24 months: 5% vested
- After 36 months: 7.5% vested
- After 48 months: 10% fully vested

---

## Acceleration Clauses

**Single-Trigger Acceleration:**  
If company sold, unvested equity vests immediately

**Double-Trigger Acceleration:**  
If company sold AND you're fired without cause, unvested equity vests

**Typical:** Double-trigger (prevents windfall from acquisition alone)

## 3. Termination Provisions

**Voluntary Resignation:**  
Unvested equity forfeited. Vested equity retained.

**Termination with Cause:**  
Unvested equity forfeited. Vested equity forfeited (gross misconduct).

**Termination without Cause:**  
Unvested equity forfeited. Vested equity retained.

**Company Sale (Double-Trigger):**  
If company sold AND employee terminated without cause within 12 months, remaining equity accelerates (100% vests).

---

## 4. Repurchase Rights

**Right of First Refusal:**  
If employee wants to sell vested equity, company has first option to buy at fair market value.

**Repurchase on Exit:**  
Company may repurchase vested equity at FMV if employee leaves.

## Founder Vesting (Recommended)

**Why Founders Should Vest:**
- Protects co-founders (if one leaves early, others get their equity)
- Investor requirement (VCs won't invest without founder vesting)
- Fairness (earn equity by building, not just starting)

**Typical Founder Vesting:**
- 4-year vest, 1-year cliff
- OR: Reverse vesting (start with 100%, company buys back unvested if you leave)

**Founder Credit:**
- Credit for time already worked (e.g., "6 months credit" = 12.5% vested on Day 1)

## Employee Equity Guidelines

**Early Employees (1-5):**  
0.5-2% equity each

**Later Employees (6-20):**  
0.1-0.5% equity each

**Advisors:**  
0.25-1% equity (for 2-year advisory period)

**Adjustments:**
- Senior roles get more
- Critical hires get more
- Later hires get less (company is less risky)
