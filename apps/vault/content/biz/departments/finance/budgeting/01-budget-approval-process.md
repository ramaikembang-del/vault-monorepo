# Budget Approval Process

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Owner:** Finance Team

---

## Overview

Spending approval thresholds and workflows for PWBI FnB Growth.

**Principle:** Spend wisely, approve quickly.

---

## Approval Thresholds

| Amount (IDR) | Approver | Response SLA |
|--------------|----------|--------------|
| < Rp 5M | Department Head | 24 hours |
| Rp 5-25M | CFO | 48 hours |
| Rp 25-100M | CEO | 1 week |
| > Rp 100M | CEO + Board | 2-4 weeks |

---

## Within-Budget Spending

**If expense is in approved annual budget:**
- [x] Auto-approved (up to budget line item)
- Just notify finance for tracking

**Example:**
- Budget: Rp 10M for Google Ads (annual)
- Actual spend: Rp 8M so far
- Remaining: Rp 2M
- **Action:** Spend up to Rp 2M without additional approval

---

## Over-Budget or Unplanned Spending

**Process:**
1. Submit budget request form
2. Include justification (why needed, why now, what's the ROI)
3. Propose budget reallocation (cut elsewhere) OR accept higher burn
4. Get approval per threshold table
5. Finance updates budget

---

## Recurring vs One-Time

**Recurring Expenses (Subscriptions, Salaries):**
- Use annualized amount for threshold
- Example: Rp 500K/month subscription = Rp 6M annual  ->  CFO approval

**One-Time Expenses (Equipment, Events):**
- Use actual amount for threshold
- Example: Rp 3M conference sponsorship  ->  Department head approval

---

## Emergency Spending

**For P0 incidents or critical opportunities:**
- Spend first, get approval after
- Document decision + rationale
- Present at next finance review
- Maximum: Rp 10M (above this, must get CEO approval even if emergency)
