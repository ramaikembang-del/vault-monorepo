# Budgeting Playbook  
**Financial Planning & Budget Management**

**Owner:** Finance Team  
**Status:** PLANNED - Year 1+

---

## Purpose

Create and manage company budgets to:
- Plan spending aligned with revenue
- Track actual vs planned expenses
- Make data-driven financial decisions

---

## Budget Categories

### 1. Personnel (Largest expense, 40-60%)
- Salaries & wages
- Contractor fees
- Benefits (health insurance, Year 2+)
- Payroll taxes

### 2. Operations (20-30%)
- Office/co-working space
- Software subscriptions (Notion, Slack, Supabase)
- Hosting & infrastructure
- Legal & accounting fees

### 3. Marketing & Sales (10-20%)
- Paid ads (Facebook, Google - Sprint 2+)
- Content creation
- Events & sponsorships

### 4. Product Development (5-10%)
- Tools & licenses
- External dev contractors (if needed)
- Research & prototyping

---

## Budget Timeline

**Monthly:** Review budget vs actuals, adjust as needed  
**Quarterly:** Update forecasts based on performance  
**Annually:** Create next-year budget (Nov-Dec)

---

---

## Budget Review Process

**Step 1:** Collect actuals (accounting software)  
**Step 2:** Compare to budget (Excel or Notion table)  
**Step 3:** Identify variances (>20% over/under)  
**Step 4:** Adjust next month's plan if needed

**Tools:** Excel template, Accurate accounting
