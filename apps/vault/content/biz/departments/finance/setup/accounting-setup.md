**Recommended:** Accurate (Indonesian GAAP compliant)  
**Alternative:** Jurnal, Zahir

## Chart of Accounts (COA)

### Assets
- 1100 - Cash (Bank BCA)
- 1200 - Accounts Receivable
- 1300 - Prepaid Expenses

### Liabilities
- 2100 - Accounts Payable
- 2200 - Taxes Payable (PPh, PPN)

### Equity
- 3100 - Owner's Equity
- 3200 - Retained Earnings

### Revenue
- 4100 - Product Sales (Template, SaaS)
- 4200 - Support Revenue

### Expenses
- 5100 - Salary & Wages
- 5200 - Marketing
- 5300 - Software & Tools
- 5400 - Professional Fees (legal, accounting)

---

## Monthly Bookkeeping Checklist

- [ ] Record all revenue (invoices)
- [ ] Record all expenses (receipts)
- [ ] Reconcile bank accounts
- [ ] Generate P&L report
- [ ] Pay taxes (if due)

**Due Date:** 7th of each month (for previous month)

---

## Tax Compliance (Indonesia)

**PPh 23 (Withholding Tax):** 2% on service income  
**PPN (VAT):** 11% (if revenue >Rp 4.8B/year)  
**PPh 21 (Payroll Tax):** Progressive (5-30%)

**Deadlines:**
- Monthly tax filing: 20th of next month
- Annual tax return: March 31

---

## Invoicing & Payments

**Invoice Template:** Notion or Excel  
**Payment Terms:** Net 30 (standard), Net 7 (for small customers)  
**Accepted Methods:** Bank transfer
