# Company OKRs (Objectives & Key Results)

**Purpose:** Track company-wide and team-level goals using the OKR framework.

---

## OKR Structure

**Objectives:** Qualitative, aspirational goals
**Key Results:** Quantitative, measurable outcomes (3-5 per objective)

**Cadence:** Quarterly reviews with annual planning

---

## Phase 1 Focus (Weeks 1-4)

Rather than formal OKRs, Phase 1 uses **Validation Gates:**

### Gate 1A (Week 2): Template Validation
- **Target:** 5+ template sales
- **Result:** Rp 10M+ revenue
- **Decision:** Continue marketing if met

### Gate 1B (Week 4): Lite Validation
- **Target:** 50+ users, 30%+ engagement
- **Decision:** Proceed to Sprint 2 if met

### Gate 1C (Week 4): Support Validation
- **Target:** 30%+ conversion from template buyers
- **Decision:** Keep offering if met

See [[biz/strategy/planning/execution/03-sprint-1-roadmap|Sprint 1 Master Plan]] for details.
