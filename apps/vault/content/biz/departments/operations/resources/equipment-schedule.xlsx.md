## Overview

This Excel workbook will track all company equipment (computers, monitors, phones) and maintenance schedules. Relevant when team grows beyond founding team.

---

## Intended Workbook Structure

### Sheet 1: Equipment Inventory

**Columns:**
- Asset ID (EQ-001, EQ-002, etc.)
- Category (Laptop, Monitor, Phone, Server)
- Brand/Model
- Serial Number
- Purchase Date
- Purchase Price
- Warranty Expiration
- Assigned To (Employee/Contractor name)
- Status (In Use, Storage, Retired)
- Location
- Notes

---

### Sheet 2: Maintenance Schedule

**Columns:**
- Asset ID
- Equipment Name
- Last Maintenance Date
- Next Maintenance Due
- Maintenance Type (Cleaning, Upgrade, Repair)
- Cost (if applicable)
- Performed By
