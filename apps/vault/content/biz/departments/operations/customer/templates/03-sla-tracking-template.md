**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Support SLAs by Tier

| Tier | First Response | Resolution Target |
|------|---------------|------------------|
| Free | 48 hours | Best effort |
| Paid | 24 hours | 3 business days |
| Enterprise | 4 hours | 1 business day |

**Business hours:** 9 AM - 6 PM WIB, Monday-Friday

---

## Priority Levels

**P0 - Critical:**
- Product completely down
- Data breach/security issue
- Multiple customers affected
- **SLA:** 1 hour response, 4 hour resolution

**P1 - High:**
- Core feature broken
- Single customer blocked
- Revenue impact
- **SLA:** 4 hour response, 24 hour resolution

**P2 - Medium:**
- Feature degraded but usable
- Workaround available
- **SLA:** 24 hour response, 3 day resolution

**P3 - Low:**
- Minor issue
- Feature request
- Question
- **SLA:** 48 hour response, best effort resolution

## SLA Breach Protocol

**If approaching SLA breach:**
1. Update customer with status
2. Escalate to manager
3. Provide ETA
4. Log reason for delay

**After SLA breach:**
1. Apologize to customer
2. Explain what happened
3. Provide resolution ETA
4. Consider compensation (discount, credit)

---

## Metrics

**Track monthly:**
- SLA compliance rate (target: >95%)
- Average response time
- Average resolution time
- Breach reasons (to improve)
