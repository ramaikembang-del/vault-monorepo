# Escalation Email Template

## To Engineering

**Subject:** ESCALATION - [Customer Name] - [Short Issue Description]

**Priority:** P0 (Critical) / P1 (High) / P2 (Medium)

**Customer:** [Name]
**ARR:** Rp [X]
**Tier:** [Product/Plan]
**Reported:** [Date/Time]

**Issue:**
[Brief description - 2-3 sentences max]

**Impact:**
- Affecting [X] customers OR [Revenue impact] OR [Churn risk]
- [Business impact explanation]

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. [Step 1]
2. [Step 2]
3. [Expected vs Actual result]

**Customer Environment:**
- Browser: [Chrome/Firefox/etc + version]
- POS: [System name]
- Data volume: [X transactions/day]

**Attempted Resolution:**
- [What support tried]
- [Results]

**Customer Expectation:**
[What they need/when]

**Workaround:**
[If available, what we told customer to do temporarily]

**Attachments:**
- Screenshots: [Attach]
- Logs: [Attach if available]

**Need by:** [Date/Time]
