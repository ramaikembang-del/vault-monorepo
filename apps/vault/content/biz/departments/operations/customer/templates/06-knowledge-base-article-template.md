## Writing Guidelines

**Title:**
- Start with action verb ("How to...", "Setting up...", "Troubleshooting...")
- Include keywords customers search for
- Keep under 60 characters

**Content:**
- Write for non-technical users
- One step per screenshot
- Use numbered lists for procedures
- Use bullet lists for reference info
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences)

**Screenshots:**
- Annotate (arrows, boxes, highlights)
- Crop to relevant area only
- PNG format, max 500KB

**SEO:**
- Include keywords naturally
- Use headings (H2, H3)
- Link to related articles
