# Content Creation Workflow
**Producing High-Quality Marketing Content**

**Owner:** Marketing Team  
**Status:** PLANNED - Sprint 2+

---

## Content Types

### 1. Blog Posts
**Frequency:** 2x/month  
**Length:** 800-1,200 words  
**Languages:** Bahasa Indonesia (primary)

### 2. Social Media
**Frequency:** 3x/week  
**Platforms:** Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn  
**Formats:** Carousel posts, short tips, customer quotes

### 3. Videos
**Frequency:** 1x/month  
**Length:** 5-10 minutes  
**Topics:** Educational (F&B tips) + Product demos

### 4. Case Studies
**Frequency:** 1x/quarter  
**Format:** Written (PDF) + video testimonial

---

## Content Creation Process

### Step 1: Ideation (Week 1)

**Sources:**
- Customer questions (support tickets)
- Keyword research (what F&B owners search)
- Industry trends (what's happening in F&B Indonesia)
- Product updates (new features to announce)

**Brainstorm Session:** Team meeting (1 hour, monthly)
- Generate 10-15 ideas
- Prioritize top 5 for next month

**Output:** Content calendar (Notion board)

---

### Step 2: Outline & Research (Week 2)

**Writer creates:**
- Title (SEO-optimized, click-worthy)
- Outline (H2/H3 structure)
- Key points to cover
- Sources/references (data, examples)

**Review:** Marketing Lead approves outline (before writing)

---

### Step 3: First Draft (Week 2-3)

**Writer produces:**
-Full draft (800-1,200 words)
- Internal links (to other blog posts, product pages)
- External links (credible sources, data)
- Call-to-action (CTA: "Try dashboard" or "Download template")

**Timeline:** 4-6 hours per blog post

---

### Step 4: Review & Edit (Week 3)

**Editor checks:**
- Grammar/spelling (Grammarly)
- Clarity (is it easy to understand?)
- SEO (keywords, meta description)
- Tone (friendly, helpful, not salesy)

**Revisions:** Writer addresses feedback (1-2 rounds max)

---

### Step 5: Design & Media (Week 3)

**Designer adds:**
- Featured image (Canva template)
- In-post graphics (charts, infographics)
- Screenshots (if product-related)

**Photographer/Videographer** (if needed):
- Customer photos (for case studies)
- Product demo videos

---

### Step 6: Publish & Promote (Week 4)

**Publish:**
- Upload to blog (WordPress/Notion)
- Schedule publish date/time
- Set SEO meta (title, description, image)

**Promote:**
- Share on social media (same day)
- Email newsletter (weekly digest)
- Repurpose (Twitter thread, LinkedIn carousel)

## Quality Standards

**Before Publishing:**
- [ ] Passes Grammarly (90%+ score)
- [ ] Mobile-friendly (readable on phone)
- [ ] CTA included (clear next step)
- [ ] SEO optimized (keyword in title, H2s)
- [ ] Fact-checked (no false claims)

**Post-Publishing:**
- [ ] Posted to social (within 24h)
- [ ] Internal team notified (Slack announcement)
- [ ] Metrics tracked (views, conversions)

---

## Success Metrics

**Traffic:**
- Blog views: 1,000+/month (Year 1 target)
- Time on page: >2 min (engagement)
- bounce rate: <60% (quality content)

**Conversion:**
- Blog → Lead: 2-5% (20-50 leads from 1,000 views)
- Lead → Demo: 10-20% (2-10 demos from 50 leads)
