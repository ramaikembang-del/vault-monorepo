# Performance Review Framework
**Employee Evaluation & Feedback**

**Owner:** HR + Operations  
**Status:** PLANNED - Year 1+

---

## Review Cadence

**Quarterly Check-Ins:** Informal (30 min)  
**Annual Reviews:** Formal (1 hour + written)

**Purpose:**
- Assess performance vs expectations
- Provide feedback (strengths + areas to improve)
- Set goals for next period
- Discuss compensation/promotion

---

## Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Job Performance (40%)
**Questions:**
- Meeting deliverables/deadlines?
- Quality of work (bugs, customer satisfaction)?
- Productivity (output vs capacity)?

**Rating:** 1-5 scale
- 5 = Exceeds expectations significantly
- 4 = Exceeds expectations
- 3 = Meets expectations
- 2 = Below expectations
- 1 = Far below expectations

---

### 2. Collaboration (30%)
**Questions:**
- Helps teammates succeed?
- Communicates clearly?
- Resolves conflicts constructively?

**Evidence:** Peer feedback (360 reviews)

---

### 3. Growth & Learning (20%)
**Questions:**
- Improving skills (courses, self-study)?
- Takes on new challenges?
- Applies feedback?

**Evidence:** Certifications, projects, observable change

---

### 4. Cultural Fit (10%)
**Questions:**
- Lives company values (customer obsession, transparency, etc.)?
- Positive attitude?
- Contributes to team culture?

---

## Review Process

### Step 1: Self-Assessment (Week 1)
- Employee fills out self-review form
- Reflects on achievements, challenges, goals

### Step 2: Manager Review (Week 2)
- Manager completes evaluation
- Gathers peer feedback (2-3 colleagues)
- Prepares talking points

### Step 3: Review Meeting (Week 3)
- 1-hour discussion
- Manager shares evaluation
- Employee responds, discusses
- Collaboratively set Q+1 goals

### Step 4: Documentation (Week 4)
- Finalize review document
- Both sign (acknowledgment, not agreement)
- File in HR records

---

## Sample Goals

**SMART Format:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound

**Examples:**
- "Ship 3 features by Q2" (Engineering)
- "Achieve 90% customer satisfaction" (CS)
- "Generate 20 qualified leads/month" (Sales)
- "Reduce deployment time from 2h → 30min" (DevOps)

---

## Performance Improvement Plan (PIP)

**When:** If rating <3 (below expectations)

**Process:**
1. Define specific issues (be concrete)
2. Set improvement goals (30/60/90 days)
3. Provide support (training, mentorship)
4. Check-in weekly
5. Final decision at 90 days (improve or part ways)

**Not:** Punishment, but opportunity to improve

---

## Compensation Link

**Performance → Compensation:**
- 5 (Exceptional): 10-15% raise + bonus
- 4 (Exceeds): 5-10% raise
- 3 (Meets): 3-5% raise (inflation adjustment)
- 2 (Below): No raise, PIP
- 1 (Far below): PIP or immediate termination

**Promotion:** Consistently 4-5 ratings + demonstrate next-level skills
