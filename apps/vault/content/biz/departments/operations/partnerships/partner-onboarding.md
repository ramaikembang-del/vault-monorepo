# Partner Onboarding Process
**Channel Partner & Reseller Setup**

**Owner:** Partnerships Team  
**Status:** PLANNED - Year 2

---

## Partner Onboarding Timeline

### Pre-Application (Before Day 0)

**Prospect Research:**
- Partner finds us (website, referral)
- Reviews partner program (benefits, requirements)
- Self-assesses fit ("Do I serve F&B businesses?")

**Marketing Materials:**
- Partner program landing page
- One-pager (benefits, commission structure)
- FAQs

---

### Step 1: Application (Day 1-3)

**Partner Applies:**
- Fill out form (company, target market, why partner?)
- Provide: Business registration, client list (if applicable)
- Sign NDA (to see pricing, materials)

**We Review:**
- Qualify or reject (within 3 business days)
- Criteria: Relevant customer base, professional, no conflicts

**Outcome:** Invitation to onboarding OR rejection email

---

### Step 2: Onboarding Kickoff (Week 1)

**Welcome Call** (60 min)
- Introductions (our team + partner team)
- Program overview (commission, support, expectations)
- Set expectations (timeline, first deal timeline)
- Assign partner manager (dedicated contact)

**Deliverables:**
- Welcome packet (PDF: program guide, sales materials)
- Partner portal access (login credentials)

---

### Step 3: Product Training (Week 1-2)

**Self-Paced Learning** (3 hours)
- Watch product demo videos (Loom)
- Read product docs (features, pricing, use cases)
- Complete quiz (10 questions, must score 80%+)

**Live Training** (2 hours, webinar)
- Product deep-dive (demo walkthrough)
- Sales objection handling
- Q&A session

**Certification:**
- Pass quiz → "Clario Certified Partner" badge
- Unlocks partner portal full access

---

### Step 4: Sales Enablement (Week 2-3)

**Materials Provided:**
- Sales deck (customizable PowerPoint)
- One-pager (product overview)
- Pricing sheet (with partner discount)
- Email templates (prospecting, follow-up)
- ROI calculator (Excel tool for demos)

**Practice Session:**
- Partner records demo (video)
- We provide feedback
- Iterate until ready

---

### Step 5: First Deal Support (Week 4-8)

**Shadowing:**
- We join partner's first 2 demos (silent observer or co-presenter)
- Provide real-time coaching

**Co-Closing:**
- We help close first deal (split credit)
- Partner earns first commission (motivation!)

**Milestone:** First deal within 60 days (if not, check-in: blockers?)

---

### Step 6: Ongoing Support (Month 2+)

**Monthly Check-Ins:**
- Partner performance review (deals, pipeline)
- Address blockers (pricing questions, competitive intel)
- Share best practices (what's working for other partners)

**Quarterly Business Review (QBR):**
- Deeper dive (strategy, goals, co-marketing)
- Tier progression (Bronze → Silver → Gold)

---

## Partner Portal Features

**Access:**
- Sales materials (download decks, one-pagers)
- Training videos (product updates, advanced features)
- Deal registration (claim leads, track commissions)
- Commission dashboard (earnings, payment status)

**Communication:**
- Slack channel (#partners - shared with all partners)
- Direct message to partner manager
- Monthly newsletter (product updates, tips)

---

## Partner Support Levels

**Bronze (All Partners):**
- Email support (24h response)
- Access to partner portal
- Monthly newsletter

**Silver (5+ Deals Closed):**
- Dedicated partner manager
- Co-marketing support (we promote you)
- Quarterly QBRs

**Gold (20+ Deals Closed):**
- Priority support (2h response)
- White-label option
- Product roadmap input

---

## Partner Success Metrics

**Onboarding Phase (First 90 Days):**
- Certification completion: 100%
- First deal closed: >50% of partners
- Time to first deal: <60 days

**Ongoing:**
- Active partners (1+ deal in last 90 days): >70%
- Avg deals per partner/month: 2-3
- Partner NPS: >8

---

## Offboarding (If Needed)

**Reasons to Terminate:**
- Inactivity (0 deals in 6 months)
- Policy violations (below MAP pricing, misrepresenting product)
- Customer complaints (poor partner experience)

**Process:**
- Warning (first offense, 30-day cure period)
- Termination notice (if not fixed, 30-day notice)
- Commission payout (honor existing deals)
- Portal access revoked

**Exit Interview:** Learn why they didn't succeed (improve program)
