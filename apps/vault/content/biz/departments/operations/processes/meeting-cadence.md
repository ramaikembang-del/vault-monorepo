**Effective Meeting Rhythms**

**Owner:** Operations Team

**Status:** ACTIVE

## Meeting Philosophy

**Default to async** (Slack, email, Notion). Meet only when necessary.

**When to Meet:**
- ✅ Brainstorming (need real-time collaboration)
- ✅ Conflict resolution (sensitive discussions)
- ✅ Complex decisions (need debate)
- ❌ Status updates (use async)
- ❌ Information sharing (write it down)

---

## Standard Meeting Rhythms

### Daily Standup (15 min, Mon-Fri)
- **Who:** Entire team
- **Format:** 3 questions per person:
  1. What did you do yesterday?
  2. What will you do today?
  3. Any blockers?
- **Rule:** No problem-solving (take offline)

### Weekly Planning (60 min, Monday)
- **Who:** Founder + Product Lead
- **Agenda:**
  - Review last week metrics
  - Set this week priorities (top 3)
  - Assign owners

### Monthly Review (90 min, Last Friday)
- **Who:** All team members
- **Agenda:**
  - Celebrate wins
  - Review monthly goals (hit or miss?)
  - Retrospective (what to improve?)

### Quarterly Planning (Half-day, Last week of quarter)
- **Who:** Leadership team
- **Agenda:**
  - Review Q performance vs targets
  - Plan Q+1 roadmap
  - Budget adjustments

---

## Meeting Best Practices

### Before Meeting
- [ ] Create agenda (shared doc)
- [ ] Invite only essential people
- [ ] Share pre-read 24h advance(if needed)

### During Meeting
- [ ] Start on time (don't wait for latecomers)
- [ ] Assign note-taker
- [ ] End with action items (who, what, when)

### After Meeting
- [ ] Share notes within 2 hours
- [ ] Track action items in project management tool

---

## No-Meeting Days

**Wednesday = No-Meeting Day** (deep work time)

Exceptions:
- Customer calls (revenue-generating)
- Urgent escalations only
