# Enterprise Sales Toolkit
**B2B Sales Resources for Enterprise Segment**

**Owner:** Sales Team  
**Status:** PLANNED - Launches Year 2

---

## Toolkit Components

### 1. Sales Collateral

**One-Pager:** Product overview (Bahasa + English)  
**Case Studies:** 3-5 success stories with metrics  
**ROI Calculator:** Excel tool (input costs → output savings)  
**Pricing Sheet:** Enterprise tiers with feature matrix

### 2. Presentation Decks

**Discovery Deck:** For first meetings (problem-focused)  
**Demo Deck:** Feature walkthrough (solution-focused)  
**Executive Deck:** C-suite presentation (ROI-focused)

### 3. Proposal Templates

**Standard Proposal:** Word/PDF template  
**SOW Template:** Scope of work for custom projects  
**Contract Template:** Legal-approved boilerplate

### 4. Objection Handling Scripts

**Common Objections:**
- "Too expensive" → Show TCO vs competitors
- "Not ready yet" → Offer pilot program
- "Need board approval" → Provide exec summary

---

## Sales Process

**Stage 1:** Qualification (BANT framework)  
**Stage 2:** Discovery call (needs assessment)  
**Stage 3:** Demo presentation  
**Stage 4:** Proposal delivery  
**Stage 5:** Negotiation & close  
**Stage 6:** Handoff to CS
