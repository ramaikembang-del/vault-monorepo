# Channel Sales Strategy
**Partner & Reseller Program**

**Owner:** Sales + Partnerships  
**Status:** PLANNED - Year 2

---

## Why Channel Sales?

**Direct Sales Limits:**
- Founder can only do ~10 demos/month
- Slow customer acquisition
- No geographic reach

**Channel Sales Benefits:**
- Partners sell on our behalf (scale without hiring)
- Local presence (partners know their markets)
- Credibility (trusted advisors recommend us)

**Tradeoff:** Lower margin (share revenue with partners)

---

## Partner Types

### 1. Resellers
**Who:** F&B consultants, business advisors  
**Model:** Buy from us at discount, resell at markup  
**Economics:** We sell at 70% (Rp 3.1M for Tier 1), they sell at 100% (Rp 4.5M), they pocket 30%

**Target:** 5-10 resellers (Year 2)

---

### 2. Affiliates
**Who:** Influencers, bloggers, community  leaders  
**Model:** Refer customers, earn commission  
**Economics:** 10-15% commission per sale (Rp 450-675K per Tier 1 sale)

**Target:** 20-30 affiliates (Year 2)

---

### 3. Strategic Partners (POS/Accounting)
**Who:** Moka, Pawoon, Accurate, Jurnal  
**Model:** Co-sell, revenue share, or integration partnership  
**Economics:** Variable (negotiated per partner)

**Target:** 2-3 strategic partners (Year 2-3)

---

## Partner Program Structure

### Tier 1: Bronze (Entry)
**Requirements:**
- 1+ deal closed
- Completed product training

**Benefits:**
- 20% commission or 30% reseller discount
- Marketing materials access
- Monthly partner newsletter

---

### Tier 2: Silver (Growth)
**Requirements:**
- 5+ deals closed (cumulative)
- 90+ day active

**Benefits:**
- 25% commission or 35% discount
- Co-marketing support (we promote you)
- Quarterly business reviews

---

### Tier 3: Gold (Elite)
**Requirements:**
- 20+ deals closed
- Dedicated partner manager

**Benefits:**
- 30% commission or 40% discount
- White-label option (your brand)
- Product roadmap input

---

## Partner Onboarding

**Step 1: Application**
- Fill out form (company, target market, why partner?)
- We review (qualify or reject)

**Step 2: Training (2 hours)**
- Product overview
- Demo practice
- Sales objection handling

**Step 3: Certification**
- Pass quiz (10 questions, 80% pass)
- Receive partner portal access

**Step 4: First Deal Support**
- We shadow first 2 demos
- Provide feedback
- Close together

---

## Partner Support

**Resources:**
- Partner portal (sales materials, training videos)
- Dedicated Slack channel (#partners)
- Monthly webinar (product updates, Q&A)

**Incentives:**
- SPIFFs (bonuses for specific products/promotions)
- Leaderboard (top performers get recognition)
- Annual partner summit (networking, training)

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk 1: Partners compete with us (sell cheaper)**
- Mitigation: MAP (Minimum Advertised Price) policy

**Risk 2: Partners provide poor customer experience**
- Mitigation: Require certification, monitor customer NPS by partner

**Risk 3: Over-dependence on one partner**
- Mitigation: No partner >20% of total revenue
