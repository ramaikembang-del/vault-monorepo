**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

## General Pricing

**Q: How much does PWBI cost?**
A: Pricing varies by product:
- **A1 Templates:** Rp 2-7M (one-time)
- **R1 Support:** Rp 75-150K/month
- **E1 Lite:** Rp 0-300K/month
- **R2 Accounting:** Rp 200-400K/month each module
- **C1 SaaS:** Rp 300K-3M/month (coming Q2 2025)

**Q: Is there a free trial?**
A:
- E1 Dashboard Lite: Free tier (forever)
- A1 Templates: 7-day money-back guarantee
- R2 Accounting: 14-day free trial (coming soon)

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: Credit card (Visa/Mastercard via Stripe), bank transfer (BCA, Mandiri, BRI), e-wallets (GoPay, OVO, Dana via Xendit).

## Billing & Subscriptions

**Q: How does billing work?**
A:
- **Monthly:** Charged at start of each month
- **Annual:** Charged upfront, save 15-20%
- **One-time (Templates):** Pay once, use forever

**Q: When will I be charged?**
A:
- **First charge:** Immediately upon signup
- **Recurring:** Same day each month (e.g., if signup on 15th, charge on 15th every month)
- **Invoice sent:** 3 days before charge

**Q: Can I change my plan?**
A: Yes! Upgrade anytime (prorated credit), downgrade at next renewal.

**Q: What happens if payment fails?**
A:
1. We retry in 3 days
2. Email reminder sent
3. Service suspended after 7 days
4. Account deleted after 30 days

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A:
- **Templates (A1):** 7-day money-back guarantee
- **Subscriptions:** Prorated refund if canceled mid-month
- **Service outage:** Full refund if >48 hours downtime

**Q: Do you offer discounts?**
A: Yes!
- **Annual plans:** 15-20% discount vs monthly
- **Multi-product:** 10% discount when buying 2+ products
- **Referral:** Rp 50K credit per successful referral
- **Early adopters:** Check current promotions

## Enterprise Pricing

**Q: Do you offer enterprise pricing?**
A: Yes, for C1+ customers (10+ outlets). Contact sales for custom quote.

**Q: Can we negotiate pricing?**
A: For annual contracts >Rp 50M, yes. Contact sales@pwbi.id.

**Q: Do you offer volume discounts?**
A: Yes, for multi-location businesses. 5-10 outlets: 15% off, 11-20: 25% off, 21+: custom pricing.

## Taxes & Invoices

**Q: Is pricing including tax?**
A: No, prices are excluding PPN (11% VAT). Tax added at checkout.

**Q: Will I receive an invoice?**
A: Yes, invoices sent via email within 24 hours of payment. Includes:
- Company details & NPWP
- Itemized charges
- Tax breakdown (PPN 11%)
- Payment method

**Q: Can I get a tax invoice (Faktur Pajak)?**
A: Yes, for corporate customers with NPWP. Provide NPWP at signup or request via support@pwbi.id.

---

## Cancellations

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes, cancel anytime. No penalties, no questions asked.

**Q: What happens when I cancel?**
A:
- Access continues until end of billing period
- No refund for unused days (except service outages)
- Data retained for 30 days (can reactivate)
- Data permanently deleted after 90 days

**Q: Can I pause my subscription?**
A: Not currently, but you can cancel and reactivate later. Your data is safe for 30 days.
