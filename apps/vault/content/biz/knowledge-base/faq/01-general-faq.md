## About PWBI

**Q: What is PWBI FnB Growth?**
A: PWBI FnB Growth provides analytics and financial management tools specifically for F&B businesses in Indonesia. We help restaurant owners make data-driven decisions through Power BI templates, dashboards, and SaaS solutions.

**Q: Who is PWBI for?**
A: F&B business owners, managers, and finance teams managing restaurants, cafes, cloud kitchens, and catering businesses in Indonesia (from single outlets to multi-location chains).

**Q: What makes PWBI different from other analytics tools?**
A:
- **F&B-specific:** Built for Indonesian restaurant operations
- **Local context:** Understanding of Indonesian F&B challenges, regulations, accounting
- **POS integrations:** Direct sync with popular Indonesian POS systems
- **Affordable:** Pricing designed for Indonesian SME F&B businesses

**Q: What products do you offer?**

- **A1:** Power BI Templates (one-time purchase, Rp 2-7M)

A: Yes! Many customers use Templates (A1) + Support (R1) together, or E1 + R2 accounting modules.

## Getting Started

**Q: How do I get started?**
A:
1. Visit pwbi.id
2. Choose your product (see product comparison)
3. Sign up or purchase
4. Follow setup guide (included with purchase)
5. Connect your POS system (if applicable)
6. Start analyzing!

**Q: Do I need technical skills?**
A: No. Our products are designed for non-technical F&B owners. If you can use Excel, you can use PWBI.

**Q: How long does setup take?**
A:
- Templates (A1): 30-60 minutes (connect data, customize)
- Dashboard Lite (E1): 15-30 minutes (sign up, connect POS)
- Accounting modules (R2): 1-2 hours (initial setup, configure)

**Q: Do you offer training?**
A: Yes. All products include setup tutorials. Paid support includes personalized training sessions via Zoom or WhatsApp.

## Data & Integration

**Q: What data do I need?**
A: Transaction data from your POS system (sales, items, payments, customers) or manual entry if no POS.

**Q: Which POS systems do you integrate with?**
A: Currently: Pawoon, Moka, Qasir, Olsera. More Indonesian POS systems coming soon.

**Q: Can I use PWBI without a POS?**
A: Yes! You can:
- Manually upload transaction data via CSV
- Enter data directly in dashboard (for smaller volumes)
- Use Excel templates then import

**Q: How often does data sync?**
A:
- POS integrations: Real-time or hourly (depends on POS)
- Manual uploads: Anytime you upload
- Recommended: Daily uploads minimum

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes. We use:
- Bank-level encryption (AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit)
- Secure Azure cloud hosting in Southeast Asia
- Compliant with Indonesian data protection laws (UU PDP)
- Regular security audits
- See [[biz/knowledge-base/faq/05-security-faq|Security FAQ]] for details
