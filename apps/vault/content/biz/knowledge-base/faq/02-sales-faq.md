## Choosing the Right Product

**Q: Which PWBI product should I buy?**
A: Depends on your needs:

**Just exploring analytics?**
→ Start with **E1 Dashboard Lite** (free tier)

**Want Power BI expertise but don't have time to build?**
→ **A1 Templates** (Rp 2-7M one-time) + **R1 Support** (Rp 75-150K/month)

**Need accounting features (P&L, cash flow, invoicing)?**
→ **R2 Accounting Modules** (Rp 200-400K/month each)

**Multi-location chain (5+ outlets)?**
→ Wait for **C1 SaaS** (coming Q2 2025) or contact sales for early access

**Q: Can I start small and upgrade later?**
A: Yes! Common path:
1. Try E1 free tier
2. Upgrade to E1 paid (more features)
3. Add R2 accounting modules
4. Migrate to C1 when ready for multi-location

**Q: Do products work together?**
A: Yes. Many customers use:
- A1 Templates + R1 Support
- E1 Dashboard + R2 Accounting
- All products can access same data source

## Comparison Questions

**Q: How does PWBI compare to Excel?**
A:
- **Excel:** Manual, error-prone, no POS integration, no real-time
- **PWBI:** Automated, accurate, POS-integrated, real-time insights

**Q: How does PWBI compare to [competitor]?**
A: See [[biz/strategy/market-analysis/05-competitive-intelligence|Competitive Analysis]] or request comparison doc from sales@pwbi.id.

**Q: Why not just use POS reports?**
A: POS reports show "what happened". PWBI shows:
- **Why it happened** (trend analysis)
- **What to do next** (recommendations)
- **Cross-location comparison** (multi-outlet)
- **Financial integration** (P&L, cash flow)

## Implementation

**Q: How long does implementation take?**
A:
- **Templates (A1):** 1-2 days (setup + training)
- **Dashboard (E1):** 1-4 hours (connect POS, configure)
- **Accounting (R2):** 1 week (setup chart of accounts, train team)
- **Full SaaS (C1):** 2-4 weeks (multi-location, custom setup)

**Q: Do you help with implementation?**
A:
- **Self-service:** All products include setup guides
- **Premium support (R1, paid tiers):** 1-on-1 onboarding call
- **Enterprise (C1+):** Dedicated implementation manager

**Q: Will this disrupt my operations?**
A: No. PWBI reads data from your POS (read-only). Your POS continues working normally.
