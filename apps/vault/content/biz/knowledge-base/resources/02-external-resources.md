# External Resources for Indonesian F&B

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Industry Associations

**Paguyuban Pengusaha Restoran dan Kafe Indonesia (PERKAKI)**
- National restaurant association
- Website: perkaki.com
- Benefits: Networking, advocacy, industry updates

**Asosiasi Pengusaha Kafe dan Restoran Indonesia (APKRINDO)**
- Another major F&B association
- Focus: Policy advocacy, training

**Local Associations:**
- Check your city for local F&B groups (Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya have active communities)

---

## Government Resources

**Kementerian Pariwisata dan Ekonomi Kreatif**
- Tourism & creative economy ministry
- SME support programs
- Website: kemenparekraf.go.id

**BPOM (Drug & Food Control Agency)**
- Food safety regulations
- Certification requirements
- Website: pom.go.id

**Direktorat Jenderal Pajak**
- Tax authority
- Tax regulations, filing
- Website: pajak.go.id

## Legal & Compliance

**Business Licenses:**
- OSS System (Online Single Submission): oss.go.id
- NIB, SIUP, TDP applications

**Food Safety:**
- Halal certification: MUI (mui.or.id)
- BPOM registration for packaged foods

**Labor Law:**
- UU Ketenagakerjaan (Labor Law)
- BPJS Ketenagakerjaan, BPJS Kesehatan (mandatory insurance)
