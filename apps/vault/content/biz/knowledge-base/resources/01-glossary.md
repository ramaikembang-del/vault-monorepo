# Glossary - F&B & Technical Terms

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## F&B Financial Terms

**ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)**
- Total revenue / Number of customers
- Measures how much each customer spends on average

**COGS (Cost of Goods Sold)**
- Direct cost of food/beverage ingredients
- Does NOT include labor, rent, utilities

**Gross Profit**
- Revenue - COGS
- Profit before overhead expenses

**Net Profit**
- Revenue - All Expenses (COGS + overhead)
- "The bottom line"

**Prime Cost**
- COGS + Labor
- Target: <60% of revenue for healthy F&B business

**Food Cost %**
- (COGS / Revenue) × 100
- Target: 25-35% depending on F&B type

**Labor Cost %**
- (Labor / Revenue) × 100
- Target: 25-35%

**Break-Even Point**
- Revenue needed to cover all costs (profit = 0)
- Calculation: Fixed Costs / Gross Profit Margin %

## F&B Operations Terms

**Covers**
- Number of customers served (restaurant term)
- "We did 150 covers today" = served 150 customers

**Par Level**
- Minimum inventory level to maintain
- "We're below par on coffee beans" = need to restock

**86'd / Habis**
- Item sold out, removed from menu (temporarily)
- "86 the salmon" = we're out of salmon

**Comps**
- Complimentary items (free to customer)
- Track comps separately from sales

**Void**
- Canceled transaction (before payment)
- Should be minimal (<1% of transactions)

**Refund**
- Returned transaction (after payment)

## Indonesian Business Terms

**NPWP (Nomor Pokok Wajib Pajak)**
- Tax identification number
- Required for business operations. See [[biz/departments/legal/sops/licensing-permits|Licensing SOP]].

**PPN (Pajak Pertambahan Nilai)**
- Value-Added Tax (VAT) = 11% in Indonesia
- Must be collected and remitted to government

**PPh (Pajak Penghasilan)**
- Income tax
- 0.5-1% of gross revenue for SMEs (PP 23)

**NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha)**
- Business identification number
- Obtained via OSS system

**SIUP (Surat Izin Usaha Perdagangan)**
- Business trading license

**PDPN / UU PDP**
- Personal Data Protection Law (No. 27/2022)
- Indonesian version of GDPR. See [[biz/departments/legal/compliance/02-privacy-policy|Privacy Policy]].

## Technical / Software Terms

**API (Application Programming Interface)**
- Way for software to talk to each other
- PWBI uses POS APIs to get your data. See [[products/shared-technical/integrations/integrations|POS Connectors]].

**OAuth**
- Secure authorization method
- "Sign in with Google" uses OAuth

**Webhook**
- Automatic notification when something happens
- "New sale → send data to PWBI"

**Sync / Synchronization**
- Updating data between systems (POS → PWBI)

**Dashboard**
- Visual display of key metrics
- Charts, graphs, numbers at a glance

**Widget**
- Individual component on dashboard
- Sales chart, top items list, etc.

**Filter **
- Narrow down data (e.g., show only "Beverages" or "Last 7 days")

**Export**
- Download data to Excel/PDF

**CSV (Comma-Separated Values)**
- Simple file format for data (Excel can open)

## Power BI Terms (for A1 Template Users)

**Power BI Desktop**
- Free Microsoft software to build reports
- What [[products/01-power-bi-template/01-power-bi-template|A1 templates]] use

**Data Model**
- How tables are related to each other
- Foundation of Power BI reports

**DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
- Formula language in Power BI
- Like Excel formulas, but for Power BI

**Measure**
- Calculated field (e.g., Total Sales = SUM of all transactions)

**DirectQuery vs Import**
- **Import:** Faster, data copied into Power BI
- **DirectQuery:** Slower, queries database live

**Refresh**
- Update Power BI data from source
- Scheduled refresh: Automatic daily updates

## Analytics Terms

**KPI (Key Performance Indicator)**
- Critical metric to track
- E.g., daily sales, profit margin, customer count

**Cohort**
- Group of customers with shared characteristic
- "January cohort" = customers acquired in January

**Churn**
- Customer who stopped coming/buying
- Churn rate = % of customers lost per month

**NPS (Net Promoter Score)**
- Customer satisfaction metric (-100 to +100)
- "Would you recommend us?" (0-10 scale)

**MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)**
- Predictable revenue each month (for subscriptions)

**ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)**
- MRR × 12

---

## Payment Terms

**MDR (Merchant Discount Rate)**
- Fee charged by payment processor (e.g., 2.9% for credit cards)
- Reduces your net revenue

**Settlement**
- When payment processor sends money to your bank
- Usually T+1 to T+3 days (transaction date + 1-3 days)

**Chargeback**
- Customer disputes credit card charge
- Avoid by providing good service + clear refund policy
