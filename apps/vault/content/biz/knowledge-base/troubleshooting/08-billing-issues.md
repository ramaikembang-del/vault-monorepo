# Billing Issues

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Payment Failures

### Credit Card Declined

**Common Reasons:**
1. **Insufficient funds:** Check bank balance
2. **Expired card:** Update card info
3. **Card blocked:** Bank flagged international transaction
4. **Wrong CVV/expiry:** Double-check details

**Solutions:**
1. **Update payment method:** Settings → Billing → "Update Card". See [[biz/knowledge-base/faq/03-pricing-faq|Pricing FAQ]].
2. **Call bank:** Ask to whitelist "PWBI" or "Stripe"
3. **Try different card**
4. **Use bank transfer instead:** Settings → Billing → "Bank Transfer"

---

### Auto-Renewal Failed

**What Happens:**
1. We try to charge your card
2. If fails, retry in 3 days
3. Email sent after each failure
4. Service suspended after 7 days
5. Account deleted after 30 days

**To Fix:**
1. Update payment method immediately
2. Click "Retry Payment" in email
3. Service reactivates instantly upon successful payment

---

### Can't Update Payment Method

**Solutions:**
1. **Clear browser cache:** Old card info might be cached
2. **Try incognito mode**
3. **Use different browser**
4. **Contact support:** We can send secure payment link

## Invoices & Receipts

### Didn't Receive Invoice

**Check:**
1. **Spam folder:** Add billing@pwbi.id to contacts
2. **Correct email:** Settings → Account → Verify email
3. **Invoice date:** Sent within 24 hours of payment

**Not there?**
- Download from Settings → Billing → "Invoice History"
- Or email billing@pwbi.id

---

### Need Invoice with Company Details

**Corporate Invoices (with NPWP):**
1. Settings → Account → "Company Information"
2. Enter: Company name, address, NPWP
3. Save
4. Future invoices will include these details

**Already paid?** Email billing@pwbi.id to request reissued invoice with company details.

---

### Need Tax Invoice (Faktur Pajak)

**Requirements:**
- Corporate account with valid NPWP
- Indonesian company

**Process:**
1. Provide NPWP at signup or update in Settings
2. Email billing@pwbi.id to request Faktur Pajak
3. We'll issue within 5 business days

## Refunds

### Requesting a Refund

**Refund Policy:**
- **Templates (A1):** 7-day money-back guarantee
- **Subscriptions:** Prorated refund if canceled mid-month
- **Service outage >48hr:** Full refund (See [[biz/departments/legal/compliance/03-terms-of-service|Terms of Service]])

**How to Request:**
1. Email billing@pwbi.id with:
 - Account email
 - Reason for refund
 - Invoice number (if known)
2. We respond within 2 business days
3. Refund processed within 7 business days

**Refund Method:** Same as original payment (credit card, bank transfer)

---

### Refund Still Not Received

**Timeline:**
- **Credit card:** 5-10 business days
- **Bank transfer:** 3-5 business days
- **E-wallet:** 1-3 business days

**If longer:** Email billing@pwbi.id with:
- Original payment date
- Amount
- Payment method

## Subscription Management

### Can't Cancel Subscription

**How to Cancel:**
1. Settings → Subscription → "Cancel Subscription"
2. Confirm cancellation
3. Access continues until end of billing period

**Button not working?**
- Try different browser
- Email billing@pwbi.id to cancel manually

---

### Accidentally Canceled

**Within 24 hours:**
- Email billing@pwbi.id, we can reverse

**After 24 hours:**
- Re-subscribe (same pricing if within 30 days)
- Your data is safe for 30 days

---

### Charged After Cancellation

**Why this happens:**
- Cancellation scheduled for next cycle (you still have access)
- Cancellation didn't process (rare)

**Solution:**
1. Check Settings → Subscription → Status
2. If shows "Active",cancellation didn't work → Try again
3. If shows "Canceled", email billing@pwbi.id with invoice for erroneous charge

**We'll refund:** 100% if charged in error

---

## Pricing & Plan Changes

### Want to Upgrade Plan

**Immediate Upgrade:**
1. Settings → Subscription → "Change Plan"
2. Select higher tier
3. Pay prorated difference
4. Upgraded instantly

**Example:** Mid-month upgrade from Rp 100K → Rp 300K plan
- Already paid: Rp 100K
- Days remaining: 15 (50% of month)
- Prorated charge: (Rp 300K - Rp 100K) × 50% = Rp 100K

---

### Want to Downgrade Plan

**Next-Cycle Downgrade:**
1. Settings → Subscription → "Change Plan"
2. Select lower tier
3. Change applies at next renewal
4. You keep current features until then

---

### Charged Wrong Amount

**Check:**
1. Settings → Billing → "Invoice History"
2. Verify: Plan tier + tax (PPN 11%)
3. Exchange rate (if USD → IDR)

**Still wrong?** Email billing@pwbi.id with:
- Invoice number
- Expected amount
- Charged amount
