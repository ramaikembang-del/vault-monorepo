# Authentication Issues

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Login Problems

### Can't Login - "Invalid email or password"

**Causes:**
- Typo in email/password
- Wrong account (multiple emails)
- Account not activated

**Solutions:**
1. Double-check email spelling
2. Try "Forgot Password" link
3. Check spam folder for activation email
4. Contact support if still stuck

---

### Forgot Password

**Steps:**
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email
3. Check email for reset link (valid 1 hour)
4. Click link, enter new password
5. Login with new password

**Not receiving email?**
- Check spam/junk folder
- Add noreply@pwbi.id to contacts
- Try different email if you have multiple accounts
- Wait 5 minutes, try again

---

### Account Locked

**Why accounts get locked:**
- 5 failed login attempts (automatic 15-min lockout)
- Suspected security issue
- Payment overdue >30 days

**Solutions:**
- **Auto-lock:** Wait 15 minutes, try again
- **Security lock:** Email security@pwbi.id (See [[biz/knowledge-base/faq/05-security-faq|Security FAQ]])
- **Payment lock:** Update payment method

---

## Session Issues

### Keep Getting Logged Out

**Causes:**
- Session timeout (4 hours inactivity)
- Browser clearing cookies
- Multiple devices/tabs

**Solutions:**
1. Check "Remember me" at login
2. Don't clear browser cookies (or whitelist pwbi.id)
3. Close other tabs/devices
4. Update to latest browser version

---

### "Session Expired" Error

**Solution:**
- Simply login again
- Normal security feature after 4 hours
