# Customer Feedback Synthesis

**Owner:** Product & Customer Success
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Review Frequency:** Monthly

## Executive Summary

Aggregated analysis of 500+ customer feedback items (Q3-Q4 2025) from NPS surveys, support tickets, feature requests, and interviews. Identifies patterns, prioritizes improvements, and tracks sentiment over time.

## Top Themes (Negative Feedback / Requests)

### 1. Mobile App Missing (Mentioned 87 times)
**Quotes:**
- "Need to check dashboard on my phone"
- "I'm never at my computer"
- "Competitors have mobile apps"

**Priority:** **HIGH** - Roadmap: E2 Mobile Dashboard Q2 2025

### 2. Performance Issues (Mentioned 65 times) **Quotes:**
- "Dashboard loads slowly with 1 year of data"
- "Times out when generating reports"
- "Laggy on older laptops"

**Priority:** **HIGH** - Ongoing optimization

### 3. Integration Limitations (Mentioned 54 times)
**Quotes:**
- "Doesn't work with my POS (Loyverse)"
- "Can't sync with QuickBooks"
- "Want automatic GoFood/GrabFood import"

**Priority:** **MEDIUM** - Add integrations incrementally

### 4. Pricing Too High (Mentioned 43 times)
**Quotes:**
- "Great product but too expensive for my small café"
- "Why is it in USD? That's 15,000 Rupiah!"
- "Need a cheaper tier"

**Priority:** **MEDIUM** - Launch A4 micro-SaaS (lower price points)

### 5. Feature Gaps (Mentioned 38 times)
**Top Requests:**
- Excel export (23 mentions) - **In roadmap**
- Multi-currency support (8 mentions)
- Custom reports (7 mentions)

**Priority:** **MEDIUM**

### 6. Onboarding Complexity (Mentioned 32 times)
**Quotes:**
- "Too many features, don't know where to start"
- "Wish there was a guided tour"
- "Training was good but too much at once"

**Priority:** **MEDIUM** - Improve onboarding UX

## Churn Feedback (Exit Interviews - 12 customers)

**Why They Left:**
1. Business closed (5) - Not preventable
2. Too expensive (3) - **Price-sensitive SMB**
3. Switched to competitor (2) - **Better mobile experience**
4. Not using it (2) - **Onboarding failure**

**Learnings:**
- Need cheaper tier for struggling SMBs
- Mobile app critical for retention
- Proactive engagement during onboarding
