# Jobs-To-Be-Done Framework

> **When customers hire F&B Ops Insight, what job are they hiring it to do?**

---

## JTBD Methodology

### The Core Question

**"When [situation], I want to [motivation], so I can [outcome]"**

---

## Mikro Segment JTBD

### Job #1: Survival Assurance

**When** I'm running my small café working 80+ hours/week
**I want to** know instantly if my business is healthy
**So I can** sleep at night knowing I'll survive the month

**Functional Aspects**:
- See profitability in real-time
- Get alerts before cash crisis
- Track key metrics without spreadsheets

**Emotional Aspects**:
- Feel in control (not drowning)
- Confidence I'll survive Year 1
- Pride in running a "real" business

**Social Aspects**:
- Appear professional to suppliers
- Show family I'm not failing

**Current Alternatives & Why They Fail**:
- Bank balance (lagging indicator, too late)
- Gut feel (unreliable, stressful)
- Spreadsheets (time-consuming, error-prone)

**Success Metric**: Health Score >60, no surprises

---

### Job #2: Menu Optimization

**When** I need to decide what to keep/remove from menu
**I want to** see which items are actually profitable
**So I can** maximize profit without guessing

**Functional**:
- Revenue vs profit by item
- Identify "dogs" (low sales, low margin)
- See category performance

**Emotional**:
- Confidence in menu decisions
- Relief from analysis paralysis

**Social**:
- Compete with bigger chains (data-driven menu)

**Current Alternatives**:
- Guess based on popularity
- Copy competitor menus
- Ask chef/staff opinion

**Success Metric**: Removed 2+ low-margin items, profit +5%

---

### Job #3: Labor Efficiency

**When** I'm scheduling staff for next week
**I want to** know exactly when I need people
**So I can** avoid overstaffing (wasting money) or understaffing (bad service)

**Functional**:
- Peak hours heatmap
- Sales per labor hour
- Staff schedule optimization

**Emotional**:
- Confidence in scheduling decisions
- Reduce stress of "did I schedule right?"

**Success Metric**: Labor cost <28% of revenue

## Mikro→Kecil Segment JTBD

### Job #4: Expansion Confidence

**When** I'm considering opening a 2nd/3rd outlet
**I want to** know if I can afford it and if it'll be profitable
**So I can** grow without risking everything

**Functional**:
- Cash flow projection
- ROI calculator for new outlet
- Multi-outlet comparison

**Emotional**:
- Confidence to take the leap
- Reduce expansion anxiety

**Social**:
- Impress investors/lenders with data

**Current Alternatives**:
- Gut feel + prayer
- Observe competitors
- Ask consultant (expensive)

**Success Metric**: Opened 2nd outlet, profitable within 6 months

---

### Job #5: Team Enablement

**When** I want my manager to make decisions without me
**I want to** give them access to business data
**So I can** delegate and not micromanage

**Functional**:
- Multi-user access
- Role-based dashboards
- Shared insights

**Emotional**:
- Trust team with data
- Feel like a CEO, not operator

**Social**:
- Build professional company culture

**Current Alternatives**:
- WhatsApp daily reports (manual, error-prone)
- Manager asks owner for every decision
- Excel shared via email (version hell)

**Success Metric**: Manager makes 3+ decisions/week without owner input

## Kecil Segment JTBD

### Job #6: Multi-Outlet Control

**When** I'm running 5-10 outlets across multiple cities
**I want to** see all locations in one dashboard
**So I can** identify problems and best practices quickly

**Functional**:
- Consolidated view (all outlets)
- Outlet comparison & ranking
- Automated alerts per location

**Emotional**:
- Feel in control of empire
- Confidence outlets run without me

**Social**:
- Compete with national chains
- Impress board/investors

**Current Alternatives**:
- Manual Excel consolidation (3+ hours/week)
- WhatsApp reports from managers
- Visit each outlet personally (time-consuming)

**Success Metric**: Identified underperforming outlet in <24 hours

---

### Job #7: Franchise Readiness

**When** I want to franchise my concept

**So I can** sell franchises or raise capital

**Functional**:
- Standard unit economics
- Replicable KPIs
- Franchisee dashboards

**Emotional**:
- Pride in building scalable business
- Confidence to pitch franchises

**Social**:

**Current Alternatives**:

## Universal JTBD (All Segments)

### Job #8: Time Saving

**When** I'm drowning in manual data entry and analysis
**I want to** automate this tedious work
**So I can** spend time on customers, not spreadsheets

**Quantifiable**:
- Save 2+ hours/week (Mikro)
- Save 5+ hours/week (Kecil)
- Save 20+ hours/week (Menengah)

**Alternatives**:
- Spend 2 hours Sundays in Excel (current reality)
- Hire analyst (expensive, Rp 5 Jt+/month)

**Success Metric**: <30 min/week on "business analysis"

---

### Job #9: Decision Confidence

**When** I need to make big decisions (hire, expand, pricing)
**I want** data to back up my gut
**So I can** make decisions with confidence, not fear

**Emotional Core**:
- Reduce decision paralysis
- Sleep better at night
- Feel like "real business owner"

**Alternatives**:
- Decide based on gut (50/50 success)
- Copy competitors
- Ask friends/family (opinions, not data)

**Success Metric**: Made 1+ major decision with data, outcome positive

---

### Job #10: Crisis Prevention

**When** problems are brewing (sales declining, costs rising)
**I want** early warning before it's too late
**So I can** act proactively, not react to crisis

**Functional**:
- Automated alerts
- Trend detection
- Thresholds & notifications

**Emotional**:
- Peace of mind
- Avoid panic mode

**Current Alternatives**:
- Realize problems too late (bank account empty)
- Constant manual checking (exhausting)

**Success Metric**: Caught 1+ problem ≥2 weeks before crisis

## Non-Consumption vs Competitive Hiring

### Why Most Don't Use BI Tools

**Non-consumption reasons**:
1. **Too expensive**: "Can't afford Rp 2M/year for enterprise BI"
2. **Too complex**: "Need data analyst to set up Tableau"
3. **Too time-consuming**: "Spreadsheets take 2 hours/week"
4. **Don't know better**: "Never thought about BI for small business"

**Our Solution**:
1. Free tier (remove cost barrier)
2. Simple as WhatsApp (remove complexity)
3. 30-second daily check (remove time barrier)
4. Education (show them what's possible)

## Key Takeaways

### Jobs Over Features

**Wrong**: "We have 50 features!"
**Right**: "We help you survive Year 1"

### Emotional > Functional

**Survival Assurance** job is 70% emotional:
- Sleep at night
- Feel in control
- Confidence not to fail

Only 30% functional (see KPIs)

### Job Evolution

```
Mikro: Survival jobs dominate
Mikro→Kecil: Expansion + delegation jobs emerge
Kecil: Efficiency + control jobs priority
Kecil→Menengah: Strategic jobs (franchise, exit)
```

**Product must evolve as jobs evolve.**
