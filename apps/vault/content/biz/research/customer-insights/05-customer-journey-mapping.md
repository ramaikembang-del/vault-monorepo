# Customer Journey Mapping

**Owner:** Product & Customer Success
**Last Updated:** December 2025
**Review Frequency:** Quarterly

---

## Executive Summary

Visual mapping of customer journeys across 5 stages: Awareness → Consideration → Purchase → Onboarding → Retention/Expansion. Identifies key touchpoints, pain points, and optimization opportunities.

---

## Journey Stage 1: Awareness (How They Find Us)

**Duration:** Days to Weeks

**Channels:**
1. **Organic Search (40%)** - "financial dashboard F&B", "Power BI template resto"
2. **Referrals (30%)** - Other F&B owners, consultants
3. **Social Media (20%)** - LinkedIn, Instagram F&B communities
4. **Events/Conferences (10%)** - F&B tech events, meetups

**Customer Questions:**
- "How do I track my café's profitability?"
- "Is there a cheaper alternative to [competitor]?"
- "What analytics do successful F&B businesses use?"

**Touchpoints:**
- Website landing page
- Blog articles (SEO)
- Case studies
- Free resources (templates, guides)

**Pain Points:**
- Too many options, confusing
- Don't know what they need
- Skeptical of sales pitches

**Optimization Opportunities:**
- Better SEO content
- Free tools/calculators to capture leads
- Clear product comparison guide

---

## Journey Stage 2: Consideration (Evaluation)

**Duration:** 1-4 Weeks

**Activities:**
- Reading case studies & reviews
- Comparing with competitors
- Requesting demo/trial
- Calculating ROI

**Touchpoints:**
- Product pages
- Pricing page
- Demo video
- Sales conversation (for enterprise)
- Free trial (for self-service)

**Customer Questions:**
- "Will this work with my POS?"
- "How long does implementation take?"
- "What if I'm not tech-savvy?"
- "Can I afford this?"

**Pain Points:**
- Unclear pricing (hidden fees?)
- Integration complexity concerns
- No transparent reviews
- Long sales cycles (enterprise)

**Decision Factors:**
| Factor | Weight | How We Win |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Price | 30% | Clear value proposition, ROI calculator |
| Ease of use | 25% | Product demos, trial, testimonials |
| Integration | 20% | Clear compatibility matrix, setup guide |
| Support | 15% | Responsive support, training included |
| Features | 10% | Right-sized (not overwhelming) |

**Optimization Opportunities:**
- Self-service trial for SMB
- ROI calculator on website
- Video testimonials
- Clear integration list

---

## Journey Stage 3: Purchase (Conversion)

**Duration:** 1 Day - 1 Week

**Customer Segments:**

**SMB (A1, E1):**
- Self-service checkout
- Credit card payment
- Instant access

**Mid-Market (R2):**
- Sales-assisted
- Contract signing
- Bank transfer / payment terms

**Enterprise (C1):**
- Procurement process
- Legal review
- Purchase order

**Touchpoints:**
- Checkout page
- Payment gateway
- Contract/MSA
- Welcome email

**Pain Points:**
- Payment friction (Indonesia: bank transfer preferred over CC)
- Long procurement (enterprise)
- Unclear next steps after purchase

**Conversion Rates:**
| Segment | Trial→Paid | Website→Trial |
|---------|------------|---------------|
| SMB | 15-20% | 2-3% |
| Mid-Market | 40-50% | N/A (sales-led) |
| Enterprise | 60-70% | N/A (sales-led) |

**Optimization Opportunities:**
- Local payment methods (QRIS, bank transfer)
- Transparent onboarding timeline
- Immediate post-purchase engagement

---

## Journey Stage 4: Onboarding (Time to Value)

**Duration:** 7-90 Days (varies by product)

**Milestones:**

**Week 1: Setup**
- Account created
- Data connected
- First dashboard viewed

**Week 2-4: Learning**
- Training completed
- Team using regularly
- First insights discovered

**Day 30-60: Adoption**
- 80% of users active
- Using 3+ features
- Integrated into workflow

**Day 60-90: Value Realization**
- Measurable business impact
- Customer can articulate ROI
- Renewal confidence

**Touchpoints:**
- Kickoff call
- Training sessions
- In-app guidance
- Success check-ins
- Knowledge base

**Pain Points:**
- Overwhelming features (don't know where to start)
- Technical integration issues
- Lack of time to learn
- No quick wins

**Success Metrics:**
| Metric | Target | Current |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 7-day activation | 80% | 72% |
| 30-day adoption | 70% | 65% |
| 90-day health score | > 75/100 | 68/100 |
| Time to first value | < 14 days | 18 days |

**Optimization Opportunities:**
- Better onboarding checklist
- Automated quick wins (template pre-populated data)
- Weekly engagement emails
- Proactive CS outreach

---

## Journey Stage 5: Retention & Expansion

**Duration:** Ongoing (Post-90 Days)

**Customer Lifecycle:**

**Months 3-6: Stabilization**
- Regular usage established
- Requests for enhancements
- Consider add-on modules

**Months 6-12: Expansion**
- Upsell to higher tier
- Add more modules
- Multi-location rollout

**Year 2+: Advocacy**
- Renewals automatic
- Provide references/case studies
- Refer other customers

**Expansion Paths:**
| Current Product | Next Step | ARR Increase |
|----------------|-----------|--------------|
| A1 Template | → E1 Dashboard | +Rp 10M |
| E1 Dashboard | → R2a P&L Module | +Rp 15M |
| R2 Single Module | → Full R2 Suite | +Rp 30M |
| R2 Suite | → C1 Full SaaS | +Rp 50M+ |

**Retention Metrics:**
| Metric | Target | Current |
|--------|--------|---------|
| 12-month retention | 85% | 78% |
| Logo churn (monthly) | < 3% | 4.5% |
| Revenue churn (monthly) | < 2% | 3.2% |
| NPS | > 50 | 52 |

**Churn Reasons (Top 3):**
1. Business closed (35%) - not preventable
2. Not seeing ROI (30%) - **addressable**
3. Switched to competitor (20%) - **addressable**
4. Too expensive (15%) - **addressable**

**Optimization Opportunities:**
- Quarterly business reviews
- Proactive value demonstration
- Competitive pricing adjustments
- Flexible pricing for struggling customers

---

## Critical Moments of Truth

**Moment 1: First Login (Hour 0)**
- Does dashboard load instantly?
- Is sample data helpful or confusing?
- Can they find what they need?

**Moment 2: Data Connection (Day 1-3)**
- Integration successful without help?
- Data accurate and up-to-date?
- Clear error messages if issues?

**Moment 3: First Insight (Week 1)**
- Did they discover something valuable?
- "Aha!" moment achieved?
- Can they share with team easily?

**Moment 4: Renewal Decision (Month 11)**
- Clear ROI demonstrated?
- Embedded in workflow?
- Alternatives considered?

## Journey Optimization Priorities

**Priority 1 (High Impact, Quick Win):**
- Improve 7-day activation from 72% → 85%
- Add ROI calculator to website
- Local payment methods

**Priority 2 (High Impact, Med Effort):**
- Reduce time-to-first-value from 18 → 10 days
- Automated onboarding workflow
- Pro active CS for at-risk customers

**Priority 3 (Med Impact, High Value):**
- Build expansion playbook
- Create use case library
- Customer advocacy program
