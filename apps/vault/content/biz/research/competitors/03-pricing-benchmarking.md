## Value-Based Pricing Analysis

**Customer ROI Examples:**

**A1 Template Customer (Solo Café):**
- Cost: Rp 2M one-time
- Benefit: Reduced waste Rp 10M/year + better pricing Rp 5M/year = Rp 15M/year
- ROI: 7.5x in Year 1

**R2 Suite Customer (5 locations):**
- Cost: Rp 50M/year
- Benefit: 3% margin improvement on Rp 2B revenue = Rp 60M/year
- ROI: 1.2x (pays for itself + Rp 10M profit)

**Pricing Headroom:** Significant room to increase prices if we improve value

---

## Pricing Strategy Recommendations

1. **Launch A4 Micro-SaaS at Rp 500K-1M/year** (capture budget-constrained SMB)
2. **Introduce usage-based pricing for E1** (scale with customer growth)
3. **Bundle discounts for R2** (already doing - 50M vs 90M)
4. **Annual prepay discount** (10-15% off, improves cash flow)
