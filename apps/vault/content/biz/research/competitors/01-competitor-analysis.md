## Executive Summary

## Competitive Landscape

### Direct Competitors (F&B-Specific Analytics)

**1. Kata.ai F&B Analytics**
- **Pricing:** Rp 15M - 50M/year
- **Strengths:** Strong AI, Indonesian market knowledge, enterprise focus
- **Weaknesses:** Expensive, complex implementation
- **Market Share:** ~15% (estimated)

**2. Moka Analytics (integrated with Moka POS)**
- **Pricing:** Included with POS (Rp 8M-20M/year)
- **Strengths:** Seamless POS integration, large installed base
- **Weaknesses:** Locked to Moka POS, basic analytics
- **Market Share:** ~25% (Moka has 40% POS market)

**3. BukuKas Pro ()**
- **Pricing:** Rp 2M - 10M/year
- **Strengths:** Simple, affordable, mobile-first
- **Weaknesses:** Very basic (mostly bookkeeping)
- **Market Share:** ~10%

### Indirect Competitors (General BI Tools)

**4. Tableau / Power BI (Microsoft)**
- **Pricing:** $15-70/user/month (~Rp 3M-10M/year per user)
- **Strengths:** Powerful, flexible, enterprise-grade
- **Weaknesses:** Generic (not F&B), expensive, complex
- **Market Share:** ~5% in F&B (mostly enterprise)

**5. Google Data Studio (Free)**
- **Pricing:** Free
- **Strengths:** Free, Google ecosystem
- **Weaknesses:** DIY, no F&B templates, technical knowledge required
- **Market Share:** ~20% (SMB tinkerers)

## Competitive Moats

**PWBI Advantages:**
1. **Best-in-class P&L/COGS for F&B** - Deepest F&B-specific insights
2. **Flexible pricing** - Template (Rp 2M) to Enterprise (Rp 50M+)
3. **No vendor lock-in** - Works with any POS
4. **Indonesian context** - Tax, BPJS, local business practices

**Competitor Advantages:**
- **Kata.ai:** AI/ML capabilities, brand recognition
- **Moka:** Built-in to POS (convenience)
- **BukuKas:** Ultra-simple, mobile-first
- **Power BI:** Enterprise IT preferred, unlimited customization

---

## Win/Loss Analysis (Last Quarter)

**Wins Against Competitors (15 deals):**
- vs Kata.ai (5): Lower price, faster implementation
- vs Moka (4): Not locked to POS, better analytics
- vs Power BI (3): F&B-specific, easier to use
- vs BukuKas (3): More sophisticated features

**Losses to Competitors (8 deals):**
- to Kata.ai (3): Needed AI forecasting, enterprise brand
- to Moka (2): Already using Moka POS (convenience)
- to Power BI (2): IT mandated Microsoft stack
- to BukuKas (1): Budget < Rp 2M

**Key Learnings:**
- Mobile app is critical (lost 4 deals due to lack of mobile)
- Need entry-level product < Rp 1M (launch A4 micro-SaaS)
- AI/ML features needed for enterprise deals
