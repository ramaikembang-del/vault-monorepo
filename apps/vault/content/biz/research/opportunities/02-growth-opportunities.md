## Executive Summary

Identified 7 high-potential growth opportunities for 2025-2026, ranked by impact and feasibility.

---

## Opportunity 1: Geographic Expansion (Jakarta → Major Cities)

**Current:** 80% customers in Jabodetabek
**Opportunity:** Expand to Surabaya, Bandung, Bali, Medan

**Market Size:**
- Surabaya: 15,000+ F&B businesses
- Bandung: 12,000+
- Bali: 8,000+
- Medan: 6,000+

**Strategy:**
- Partner with local F&B communities
- Hire regional sales reps
- Localized case studies

**Timeline:** Q2-Q4 2025
**Investment:** Rp 500M (hiring, marketing)
**Expected Return:** +30% ARR by end of 2025

---

## Opportunity 2: Channel Partnerships (Consultants/Accountants)

**Current:** 100% direct sales
**Opportunity:** Partner with 50-100 F&B consultants/accountants who serve 10-50 clients each

**Potential:** 500-5,000 customers via partners

**Partner Program:**
- 20% commission on referrals OR
- 30% revenue share on resold products
- Co-branded materials
- Partner portal

**Timeline:** Q1 2025
**Investment:** Rp 200M (portal, materials)
**Expected Return:** +15% ARR in Year 1

---

## Opportunity 3: Micro-SaaS Launch (A4 Suite)

**Current:** Entry product Rp 2M (limits addressable market)
**Opportunity:** Launch 5 micro-SaaS products at Rp 500K-1M/year

**Products:**
- A4a: Inventory Optimizer
- A4b: Menu Engineer
- A4c: Labor Scheduler
- A4d: Supplier Manager
- A4e: Marketing Automator

**Addressable Market:** 30,000+ SMB F&B businesses
**Timeline:** Q1-Q2 2025
**Investment:** Rp 1B (development)
**Expected Return:** +Rp 1.5B ARR by end of 2025

---

## Opportunity 4: Enterprise Push (C1 Full SaaS)

**Current:** 5 enterprise customers
**Opportunity:** 10-20 enterprise deals (Rp 100M+ ARR each)

**Target:** Major restaurant chains (Solaria, Chick-N-Roll, etc.)

**Strategy:**
- Dedicated enterprise sales team
- Custom implementations
- White-glove onboarding

**Timeline:** Ongoing 2025
**Investment:** Rp 800M (sales team, custom dev)
**Expected Return:** +Rp 2B ARR

---

## Opportunity 5: Vertical Expansion (Beyond F&B)

**Opportunity:** Retail, hospitality, services (same pain points)

**Potential Verticals:**
- Retail stores (apparel, electronics)
- Salons/spas
- Gyms/fitness centers

**Timeline:** 2026
**Decision:** After achieving product-market fit in F&B

---

## Opportunity 6: International Expansion (SEA)

**Opportunity:** Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Philippines

**Addressable Market:** 500,000+ F&B businesses (SEA)

**Challenges:**
- Localization (currency, language, regulations)
- Local competition
- Go-to-market challenges

**Timeline:** 2026-2027
**Investment:** Significant (TBD)

---

## Opportunity 7: Platform Play (Marketplace/Ecosystem)

**Vision:** Become the "operating system" for F&B businesses

**Components:**
- Analytics (current)
- Suppliers marketplace
- Staffing platform
- Marketing tools
- POS integration hub

**Timeline:** 2027+
**Decision:** After achieving scale (10,000+ customers)
