# Technology Adoption Patterns

**Owner:** Product & Research
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Current Tech Stack (F&B Businesses)

**POS Systems (60% adoption):**
- Moka (40%), Olsera (25%), Pawoon (15%), Others (20%)
- Trend: Moving from on-prem to cloud

**Accounting (50% adoption):**
- Accurate (30%), Jurnal (20%), QuickBooks (10%), Excel (40%)

**Analytics (15% adoption):**
- Spreadsheets (50%), Built-in POS reports (30%), Dedicated analytics (15%), Nothing (5%)

**Opportunity:** 85% don't have dedicated analytics!

---

## Adoption Drivers

1. **Pain Point Severity** - Critical pain → faster adoption
2. **Ease of Setup** - < 1 day setup → high adoption
3. **Proof of ROI** - Case studies matter
4. **Price** - Must fit budget (Rp 5-50M sweet spot)
5. **Peer Influence** - F&B community recommendations

---

## Adoption Barriers

1. **"I don't have time"** - Too busy running operations
2. **"Too expensive"** - Budget constraints (especially SMB)
3. **"Too complicated"** - Fear of technology
4. **"My current system works"** - Status quo bias
5. **"I'll do it later"** - Procrastination

**Our Solutions:**
- Quick setup (< 1 hour)
- Flexible pricing (Rp 2M - 100M+)
- Dead simple UX
- Clear ROI demonstration
- Create urgency (limited slots, early adopter pricing)

---

## Adoption Journey

**Phase 1: Awareness (Weeks 1-4)**
- Discover via search, referral, or event
- Read content, watch demos

**Phase 2: Consideration (Weeks 5-8)**
- Compare competitors
- Calculate ROI
- Request trial/demo

**Phase 3: Purchase (Week 9-10)**
- Sign contract
- Onboard

**Phase 4: Adoption (Weeks 11-20)**
- Daily usage
- See value
- Expand to team

**Total time to full adoption:** ~5 months
