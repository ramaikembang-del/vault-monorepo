## Executive Summary

Summary of 12 experiments run in Q3-Q4 2025 across product, pricing, and marketing. Documents learnings and next steps.

---

## Experiment 1: Free Trial vs. Demo-First (Marketing)

**Hypothesis:** Free trial will convert better than demo bookings
**Setup:**
- Control: Demo booking flow
- Variant: Self-service 14-day trial

**Results:**
- Demo booking: 5% trial-to-paid conversion
- Self-service trial: 18% trial-to-paid conversion
- **Winner: Self-service trial (+260%)**

**Learning:** SMB wants self-service, enterprise wants demos
**Action:** Segment by company size, offer both paths

---

## Experiment 2: Annual vs. Monthly Pricing (Pricing)

**Hypothesis:** Annual prepay with discount will improve cash flow
**Setup:**
- Control: Monthly billing
- Variant: Annual prepay with 15% discount

**Results:**
- 42% chose annual (vs 28% expected)
- Cash flow improved 3.2x
- **Winner: Annual option**

**Learning:** Customers value predictability, willing to commit
**Action:** Default to annual, offer monthly as "pay more" option

---

## Experiment 3: Onboarding Checklist (Product)

**Hypothesis:** In-app checklist will improve activation
**Setup:**
- Control: No checklist
- Variant: 5-item onboarding checklist

**Results:**
- 7-day activation: 72% → 81% (+9pp)
- Time to first value: 18 days → 12 days (-33%)
- **Winner: Onboarding checklist**

**Action:** Shipped to all customers, iterate on checklist items

---

## Experiment 4: Customer Testimonials on Pricing Page (Marketing)

**Hypothesis:** Social proof will increase conversions
**Setup:**
- Control: Pricing page without testimonials
- Variant: 3 customer testimonials on pricing page

**Results:**
- Conversion rate: 2.3% → 2.8% (+22%)
- **Winner: Testimonials**

**Action:** Added testimonials, testing video testimonials next

---

## Experiment 5: Proactive CS Outreach for Low-Usage (Retention)

**Hypothesis:** Proactive outreach will reduce churn
**Setup:**
- Control: Reactive support only
- Variant: CS reaches out to customers with <20% usage

**Results:**
- Churn rate: 4.5% → 3.2% (-29%)
- **Winner: Proactive outreach**

**Action:** Implemented as standard process, automated alerts

---

## Experiment 6: ROI Calculator on Website (Marketing)

**Hypothesis:** ROI calculator will increase qualified leads
**Setup:**
- Added interactive ROI calculator to homepage

**Results:**
- Lead quality score: +15%
- Trial sign-ups from calculator users: 3x higher conversion
- **Winner: ROI calculator**

**Action:** Shipped, promoting in ads

---

## Experiment 7: Mobile-Optimized Dashboard (Product)

**Hypothesis:** Mobile-responsive design will improve engagement
**Setup:**
- Control: Desktop-only
- Variant: Mobile-optimized views

**Results:**
- Mobile traffic: 35% of total (higher than expected 20%)
- Engagement on mobile: +40%
- **Learning:** Mobile app is critical**

**Action:** Prioritize E2 Mobile Dashboard for Q2 2025

---

## Experiment 8: Localized Payment Methods (QRIS, Bank Transfer) (Conversion)

**Hypothesis:** Local payment methods will reduce checkout drop-off
**Setup:**
- Control: Credit card only
- Variant: Add QRIS + bank transfer

**Results:**
- Checkout completion: 65% → 82% (+26%)
- Payment method split: QRIS 45%, Bank 30%, CC 25%
- **Winner: Local payment methods**

**Action:** Made default, considering e-wallet options

---

## Experiment 9: Feature Usage Tracking (Product Analytics)

**Hypothesis:** Understand which features drive retention
**Setup:** Instrumented all features with analytics

**Results:**
- P&L dashboard: Used by 95% (core value)
- COGS analysis: 78% (high value)
- Cash flow: 65% (important)
- Staff performance: 35% (nice-to-have)

**Learning:** Focus development on P&L and COGS
**Action:** Prioritize R2a and R2c improvements

---

## Experiment 10: Referral Program (Growth)

**Hypothesis:** Customers will refer peers for incentives
**Setup:** Offer Rp 2M credit for successful referrals

**Results:**
- 8% of customers made referrals (goal: 10%)
- Referred customers: 25% higher LTV
- **Partial win, needs iteration**

**Action:** Test higher incentives, make referral process easier

---

## Experiment 11: Content Marketing (SEO Blog) (Acquisition)

**Hypothesis:** High-quality blog content will drive organic traffic
**Setup:** Published 20 F&B business articles (Q4)

**Results:**
- Organic traffic: +120% (month 1 → month 3)
- Blog → trial conversion: 1.8%
- CAC for blog leads: Rp 500K (vs Rp 2M for ads)
- **Winner: Content marketing**

**Action:** Double down, publish 40 articles in 2025

---

## Experiment 12: Chatbot Support (Support)

**Hypothesis:** AI chatbot will reduce support tickets, improve response time
**Setup:** Deployed chatbot for common questions

**Results:**
- Ticket deflection: 30% (goal: 50%)
- Customer satisfaction: Same (no degradation)
- Cost savings: Rp 20M/year

**Learning:** Chatbot handles FAQs well, escalates complex issues
**Action:** Continue, improve knowledge base

---

## Summary of Learnings

**Product:**
- Onboarding checklists critical for activation
- Mobile is essential (not nice-to-have)
- Focus on P&L and COGS (highest usage)

**Marketing:**
- Self-service trials > demos (for SMB)
- Social proof (testimonials) matters
- Content marketing has best CAC

**Pricing:**
- Annual prepay is preferred
- Local payment methods critical in Indonesia

**Retention:**
- Proactive CS outreach reduces churn
- Usage tracking helps identify at-risk customers
