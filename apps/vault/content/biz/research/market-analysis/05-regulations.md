## UMKM Legal Framework

### Official Definition (UU No. 20/2008)

| Category | Annual Revenue | Assets | Employees |
|----------|---------------|--------|-----------|
| **Mikro** | <Rp 300 Jt | <Rp 50 Jt | 1-4 |
| **Kecil** | Rp 300 Jt - Rp 2.5 M | Rp 50 Jt - Rp 500 Jt | 5-19 |
| **Menengah** | Rp 2.5 M - Rp 50 M | Rp 500 Jt - Rp 10 M | 20-99 |

**Note**: F&B businesses typically don't meet asset threshold but dorevenue. Classification usually based on revenue.

## Registration Requirements

### NIB (Nomor Induk Berusaha)

**Required for**: All businesses (even Mikro)

**How to Get**: Online via OSS (Online Single Submission) system

**Cost**: FREE

**Timeline**: Same day

**Benefits**:
- Legal business identity
- Access to government programs
- Tax compliance easier

---

### NPWP (Tax ID)

**Required**: Yes, if revenue >Rp 60M/year

**How**: Online via DJP (tax office)

**Cost**: FREE

**Tax Rates for UMKM**:
- Revenue <Rp 4.8 M/year: 0.5% of gross revenue (PP 23/2018)
- Revenue >Rp 4.8 M: Progressive tax (up to 25%)

**Critical**: Simplified tax for UMKM is huge advantage

---

### Business Permits

**PIRT (Food Safety)**:
- Required: Home-based food production
- Cost: Rp 1-2M
- Validity: Lifetime
- Process: Health department inspection

**Izin Usaha (Business License)**:
- Now integrated into NIB
- No separate permit needed for most F&B

## Government Support Programs

### 1. KUR (Kredit Usaha Rakyat)

**What**: Subsidized loans for UMKM

**Terms**:
- Interest: 6% per year (subsidized from ~15%)
- Amount: Rp 10 Jt - Rp 500 Jt
- Collateral: Simplified (no heavy collateral)

**Banks**: BRI, BNI, Mandiri, BTN, etc.

**Eligibility**:
- NIB registered
- Business running >6 months
- Revenue <Rp 4.8 M/year

**How F&B Ops Insight Helps**:
- Our data proves cash flow → easier loan approval
- 6+ months of data → strong application

---

### 2. Ultra Mikro (Rp 1 Jt-10M loans)

**Terms**:
- Interest: 3% per year
- Amount: Rp 1 Jt - Rp 10 Jt
- No collateral

**Target**: Micro entrepreneurs

**Process**: Very simplified, community-based

---

### 3. Modal Ventura (Venture Capital Program)

**What**: Government co-investment fund

**For**: High-growth UMKM (Kecil→Menengah)

**How**: Match private VC funding

**Rare for F&B, more for tech/export businesses**

---

### 4. LPDB-KUMKM (UMKM Development Fund)

**What**: Grants + low-interest loans for UMKM

**Amount**: Rp 50 Jt - Rp 500 Jt

**Focus**: Capacity building, equipment

**Eligibility**: UMKM cooperatives or associations

## Tax Incentives

### PP 23/2018 (0.5% Gross Revenue Tax)

**Benefit**: Massively simplified tax

**Instead of**:
- Track all expenses
- Calculate net profit
- Pay 25% corporate tax

**You pay**: 0.5% of gross revenue, done.

**Example**:
```
Revenue: Rp 2 M/year
Tax: Rp 2 M × 0.5% = Rp 10 Jt

vs Normal Corporate Tax:
Profit (assuming 15%): Rp 300 Jt
Tax (25%): Rp 75 Jt

Savings: Rp 65 Jt!
```

**Limit**: Applies for first 7 years OR until revenue >Rp 4.8 M

---

### Export Tax Incentives

**Not applicable for most local F&B**, but exists for:
- Catering companies serving international events
- F&B exporting packaged goods

## Compliance Requirements

### Minimum Compliance (Mikro)

1. **NIB Registration**: FREE via OSS
2. **NPWP**: FREE via DJP (if revenue >Rp 60 Jt)
3. **Monthly Tax**: 0.5% of revenue
4. **Food Safety** (if applicable): PIRT

**Total Annual Cost**: <Rp 2 Jt

**Time**: <5 hours/year

---

### Medium Compliance (Kecil)

1. All of Mikro +
2. **Quarterly Tax Reporting**
3. **Annual Financial Statement**
4. **Labor Regulations** (if >5 employees):
 - BPJS Ketenagakerjaan (employment insurance)
 - BPJS Kesehatan (health insurance)

**Total Annual Cost**: Rp 5-10M (mostly BPJS)

**Time**: ~20 hours/year (hire bookkeeper)

---

### Full Compliance (Menengah)

1. All of Kecil +
2. **Audited Financial Statements**
3. **Full Corporate Tax** (if >Rp 4.8 M revenue)
4. **BPJS for all employees**

**Total Annual Cost**: Rp 20-50M

**Time**: Hire full-time finance team
