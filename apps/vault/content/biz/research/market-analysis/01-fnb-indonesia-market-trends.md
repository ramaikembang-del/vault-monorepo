# F&B Indonesia Market Trends

**Owner:** Strategy & Research
**Last Updated:** December 2025

---

## Market Size

**Indonesia F&B Market:**
- Total market: $60B USD (~Rp 900T) annually
- Growing 8-10% annually
- 250,000+ F&B businesses

**Addressable Market (Multi-location + tech-adopters):**
- ~20,000 businesses (8% of total)
- Average spend on analytics: Rp 10-50M/year
- **TAM:** Rp 200B - 1T annually

**Our Current Penetration:** < 0.1% (huge growth opportunity)

---

## Key Trends

### 1. Digital Transformation Acceleration
- COVID accelerated tech adoption 5-10 years
- 60% of F&B now use digital POS (up from 30% in 2019)
- Cloud-based solutions preferred over on-prem

### 2. Aggregator Dependency
- 70% of restaurants on GoFood/GrabFood
- Average commission: 25-30%
- Trend: Businesses want to reduce dependency, build direct channels

### 3. Data-Driven Decision Making
- Traditional: "Gut feel" management
- Modern: Want data, don't know how to start
- Opportunity: Easy-to-use analytics for non-technical owners

### 4. Multi-Location Expansion
- Successful single cafÃ©s expanding to 3-10 locations
- Franchising growing (Kopi Kenangan, Janji Jiwa models)
- Pain: Can't manage multiple locations without data

### 5. Premium Coffee & QSR Growth
- Specialty coffee boom (25% CAGR)
- Quick Service Restaurants expanding
- Higher margins = more budget for tools

## Technology Adoption Patterns

**Early Adopters (10%):**
- Tech-savvy, younger owners
- Already using POS, accounting software
- Will try new tools

**Early Majority (30%):**
- Need proof (case studies, referrals)
- Price-sensitive but willing to invest
- Our primary target

**Late Majority (40%):**
- Still manual/Excel
- Need extreme simplicity
- Future opportunity

**Laggards (20%):**
- Will never adopt
- Not addressable

**Our Strategy:** Target Early Majority, land-and-expand to Late Majority via partnerships

---

## Regulatory Environment

**Favoring Tech Adoption:**
- Government push for digitalization
- Tax reporting now electronic (e-Faktur)
- BPJS (social security) requires digital records

**Challenges:**
- Data privacy regulations evolving
- F&B licensing complex (per-city rules)
