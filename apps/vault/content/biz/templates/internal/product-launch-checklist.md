# Product Launch Checklist
**Pre-Launch Validation & Go-Live Steps**

**Owner:** Product + Operations  
**Status:** ACTIVE

---

## T-Minus 4 Weeks: Preparation

### Product Readiness
- [ ] Core features complete (MVP scope)
- [ ] QA testing passed (no P0/P1 bugs)
- [ ] Performance tested (load testing if applicable)
- [ ] Security review completed

### Documentation
- [ ] User guide written (help center articles)
- [ ] Product specs finalized
- [ ] Internal wiki updated (for CS team)
- [ ] FAQ documented (common questions)

### Legal & Compliance
- [ ] Terms of Service reviewed
- [ ] Privacy Policy updated (if new data collection)
- [ ] Pricing finalized (contracts ready)

---

## T-Minus 2 Weeks: Marketing & Sales Prep

### Marketing Assets
- [ ] Launch announcement drafted (blog post, email)
- [ ] Social media posts scheduled
- [ ] Case studies ready (if applicable)
- [ ] Product screenshots/videos (for website)

### Sales Enablement
- [ ] Sales deck updated
- [ ] Demo script prepared
- [ ] Pricing sheet finalized
- [ ] CS team trained (1-hour session)

---

## T-Minus 1 Week: Final Checks

### Technical
- [ ] Staging environment tested end-to-end
- [ ] Backup & rollback plan ready
- [ ] Monitoring/alerts configured
- [ ] Load balancing tested (if needed)

### Operations
- [ ] Support team briefed (what to expect)
- [ ] Escalation path defined (who handles issues)
- [ ] Launch day schedule (who's oncall?)

---

## Launch Day: Go-Live

### Morning (9am)
- [ ] Deploy to production (follow deployment process)
- [ ] Smoke test (basic functionality works?)
- [ ] Monitor error rates (Sentry dashboard)

### Mid-Day (12pm)
- [ ] Send launch announcement (email, blog)
- [ ] Post on social media
- [ ] Notify customers (in-app banner if applicable)

### Afternoon (3pm)
- [ ] Check metrics (signups, usage, errors)
- [ ] Address any issues (bug triage)
- [ ] Team check-in (how's it going?)

### Evening (6pm)
- [ ] Final status check
- [ ] Document any issues for tomorrow
- [ ] Celebrate! ðŸŽ‰ (launch is done)

---

## T-Plus 1 Day: Post-Launch

- [ ] Review launch metrics (vs targets)
- [ ] Collect early user feedback
- [ ] Fix critical bugs (if any)
- [ ] Update stakeholders (email summary)

---

## T-Plus 1 Week: Retrospective

**Review:**
- What went well?
- What went badly?
- What surprised us?
- What will we do differently next time?

**Document:** Add learnings to this checklist for next launch

---

## Launch Metrics Dashboard

**Track First 7 Days:**
- Signups / Downloads
- Activation rate (% who complete setup)
- Usage frequency (daily/weekly active)
- Support tickets (volume + sentiment)
- NPS (customer satisfaction)

**Success Criteria:** Define before launch (e.g., "100 signups, 80% activation")
