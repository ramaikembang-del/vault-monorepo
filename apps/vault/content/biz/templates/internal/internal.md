## How to Use Templates

**Step 1:** Find the template you need (see categories above)

**Step 2:** Make a copy (don't edit the original!)

**Step 3:** Fill in your specific details

**Step 4:** Save in appropriate location (not in templates folder)

---

## Template Standards

**Every Template Should Have:**
- [ ] Clear title ("Template: [Name]")
- [ ] Instructions (how to fill it out)
- [ ] Placeholders ([REPLACE THIS TEXT])
- [ ] Example (filled-out sample)

**Format:**
- Notion pages (for interactive documents)
- Google Docs (for collaborative editing)
- Markdown (for code/technical docs)

---

## Requesting New Templates

**If you need a template that doesn't exist:**
1. Check if similar one exists (can you adapt?)
2. If not, request via Slack #operations
3. Operations team will create within 1 week

**Priority:** Based on frequency of use (daily > weekly > monthly)

---

## Template Maintenance

**Review:** Quarterly (every 3 months)
- Update outdated templates
- Archive unused templates
- Add new frequently needed templates

**Ownership:** Operations team maintains library
