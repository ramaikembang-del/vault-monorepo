# Presentation Template Guide

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**File:** Use official PWBI PowerPoint template (.pptx file - request from training@pwbi.id)

---

## Slide Templates

### 1. Title Slide
**Elements:**
- Training name
- PWBI logo
- Date
- Presenter name and title
- Optional: Target audience

**Colors:** PWBI brand colors (blue #0066CC, green #00CC66)

---

### 2. Agenda Slide
**Format:**
- Numbered list of topics
- Estimated time per topic
- Break times

**Example:**
```
1. Introduction (15 min)
2. Product Overview (30 min)
3. Hands-on Practice (45 min)
 - Break (15 min)
4. Q&A (15 min)
```

---

### 3. Content Slides

**Text-Heavy Slides:**
- Max 6 bullet points
- Max 6 words per bullet
- Use sub-bullets sparingly

**Visual Slides:**
- Screenshots, diagrams, charts
- Minimal text (caption only)
- High-resolution images

**Demo Slides:**
- Step-by-step with screenshots
- Highlight areas of focus (arrows, boxes)
- Number steps clearly

---

### 4. Exercise/Activity Slides

**Format:**
```
 Exercise: [Name]

Objective: [What they'll practice]
Time: [Duration]
Instructions:
1. Step 1
2. Step 2
3. Step 3

[Optional: Link to materials]
```

---

### 5. Key Takeaways Slide

**Format:**
- 3-5 main points
- Simple, memorable phrases
- Visual icons

---

### 6. Q&A Slide

**Simple design:**
- "Questions?" heading
- Contact information
- Next steps

---

## Best Practices

**Design:**
- Consistent fonts (Arial or Calibri)
- High contrast (light background, dark text)
- Avoid red/green combinations (colorblind-friendly)

**Content:**
- One idea per slide
- More slides, less text per slide
- Use speaker notes for details

**Accessibility:**
- Alt text for images
- High-contrast colors
- Large fonts (min 24pt for body text)

**Timing:**
- Plan 2 minutes per slide
- Include buffer time
- Plan for questions

---

## Training-Specific Guidelines

### Customer Training
- Focus on "how-to" not "why"
- Lots of screenshots
- Simple language (assume no technical knowledge)

### Partner Training
- More depth on "why"
- Include sales/business context
- Technical details available on request

### Employee Training
- Company context and culture
- Processes and systems
- Internal tools and jargon OK
