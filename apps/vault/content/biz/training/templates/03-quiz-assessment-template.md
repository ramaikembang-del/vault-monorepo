## Question Type Guidelines

### Multiple Choice

**Format:**
```
What is the primary benefit of using PWBI for F&B businesses?

A) Cheaper than Excel
B) Real-time data-driven decision making
C) Replaces your accountant
D) Automatically increases sales

**Correct:** B
**Explanation:** PWBI provides real-time analytics to help owners make data-driven decisions, which is its core value proposition.
```

**Best Practices:**
- 4 options (A-D)
- One clearly correct answer
- Distract ors plausible but wrong
- Avoid "all of the above" or "none of the above"

---

### True/False

**Format:**
```
PWBI can automatically sync with any POS system.

A) True
B) False

**Correct:** B
**Explanation:** PWBI integrates with specific POS systems (Pawoon, Moka, Qasir, Olsera). Not all POS systems are supported.
```

**Use sparingly** (tends to be 50/50 guessing)

---

### Scenario-Based (Advanced)

**Format:**
```
A cafe owner notices their food cost % increased from 30% to 35% this month. What should they do first?

A) Raise menu prices immediately
B) Check for waste, theft, or supplier price increases
C) Fire the kitchen staff
D) Switch to cheaper ingredients

**Correct:** B
**Explanation:** First investigate root cause (waste, theft, supplier costs) before making changes. R2c COGS Calculator helps identify this.
```

**Best for:** Certification exams, advanced training

**Purpose:** Assess understanding of E1 features and usage
**Passing Score:** 80% (8/10 correct)
**Time Limit:** 15 minutes
**Question Count:** 10
**Question Types:** Multiple choice

---

## Instructions

1. Read each question carefully
2. Select the best answer
3. You have 15 minutes to complete
4. Passing score is 80% (8 out of 10 correct)
5. You may retake after 7 days if needed

---

## Questions

### Question 1
What is included in the Free tier of E1 Dashboard Lite?

A) Unlimited users and data
B) 1 user, 30 days of data history
C) 5 users, unlimited data history
D) Only demo data access

**Correct:** B
**Explanation:** Free tier includes 1 user and 30 days of data history. Paid tier has 5 users and unlimited history.
**Points:** 1

---

### Question 2
Which POS systems can E1 integrate with?

A) Only Pawoon
B) Any POS system
C) Pawoon, Moka, Qasir, Olsera
D) Only manual CSV upload

**Correct:** C
**Explanation:** E1 integrates with Pawoon, Moka, Qasir, and Olsera. More integrations coming soon.
**Points:** 1

---

### Question 3
How often does E1 sync data from your POS (Paid tier)?

A) Real-time (instant)
B) Every hour
C) Once per day
D) Manual sync only

**Correct:** B
**Explanation:** Paid tier auto-syncs hourly. Free tier requires manual daily sync.
**Points:** 1

---

### Question 4
A restaurant owner wants to see which menu items are most profitable. Which E1 report should they use?

A) Sales Summary
B) Item Performance
C) Customer Analytics
D) Payment Methods

**Correct:** B
**Explanation:** Item Performance report shows sales volume and profitability by menu item.
**Points:** 2 (application question)

What should you do if E1 shows sales data that doesn't match your POS?

## Using This Template

**For Knowledge Checks:**
- 5-10 questions
- 10-15 minutes
- Lower stakes (not certification)

**For Certification Exams:**
- 25-50 questions
- 60-90 minutes
- Mix of difficulty levels
- Scenario-based questions
- Higher passing score (80-85%)

**Question Writing Tips:**
- Test understanding, not memorization
- Use realistic scenarios
- Avoid trick questions
- Clear, concise wording
- One correct answer only
