## Days 8-30: First Month - Learn

**Goal:** Understand the business, products, and your role

### Product Knowledge
- [ ] Demo all products (A1, E1, R2a-f)
- [ ] Set up test accounts for each product
- [ ] Complete customer onboarding flow yourself
- [ ] Read competitive analysis docs
- [ ] Understand our target customers (personas)

### Customer Understanding
- [ ] Listen to 5 customer calls (sales or support)
- [ ] Read 10 customer feedback tickets
- [ ] Watch customer testimonial videos
- [ ] If possible: visit a customer's restaurant

### Team Integration
- [ ] 1-on-1 coffee chats with every team member
- [ ] Attend all team meetings (observe and learn)
- [ ] Shadow team members in their daily work
- [ ] Join team lunch/social events

### First Project
- [ ] Ship your first small project (defined with manager)
 - Engineering: Fix 1-2 bugs or small feature
 - Product: Write 1 PRD or user story
 - Sales: Shadow 3 demos, then lead 1 with backup
 - CS: Handle 5 support tickets with mentor
 - Finance/Ops: Complete 1 process improvement

### Month 1 Check-in
- [ ] 30-day review meeting with manager
- [ ] Discuss: What's going well? What's challenging?
- [ ] Set goals for Month 2

---

## Days 31-60: Second Month - Contribute

**Goal:** Own work independently, start making impact

### Autonomy
- [ ] Own a full project/feature end-to-end
- [ ] Participate actively in team meetings (not just listen)
- [ ] Make your first process improvement suggestion
- [ ] Start mentoring next new hire (if applicable)

### Deepen Expertise
- [ ] Become expert in 1-2 product areas
- [ ] Identify and document 1 gap in our processes/docs
- [ ] Complete advanced training for your role
- [ ] Present learnings to team (brown bag lunch)

### Customer Impact
- [ ] Directly interact with customers (demo, support, research)
- [ ] Contribute to product roadmap discussion
- [ ] Identify 1 customer pain point and propose solution

### Collaboration
- [ ] Collaborate cross-functionally (e.g., Eng + Product on feature)
- [ ] Help organize a team event or initiative
- [ ] Share knowledge (write doc, give training)

### Month 2 Check-in
- [ ] 60-day review meeting with manager
- [ ] Review progress on goals
- [ ] Identify areas for growth
- [ ] Set goals for Month 3

---

## Days 61-90: Third Month - Impact

**Goal:** Full contributor, measurable business impact

### Ownership
- [ ] Fully ramped in your role (no hand-holding needed)
- [ ] Driving projects independently
- [ ] Helping unblock others on the team
- [ ] Contributing to team strategy/planning

### Business Impact (Choose 1-2 relevant to your role)
- [ ] **Engineering:** Ship feature that drives engagement/revenue
- [ ] **Product:** Launch feature with measurable adoption (>50% of users)
- [ ] **Sales:** Close your first deal independently
- [ ] **CS:** Improve customer health score or reduce churn
- [ ] **Finance/Ops:** Implement process saving 10+ hours/month

### Leadership
- [ ] Mentor new hire through their first month
- [ ] Lead a project or initiative
- [ ] Contribute to hiring (interview candidates)
- [ ] Propose strategic improvement

### Company Contribution
- [ ] Participate in all-hands (present, not just attend)
- [ ] Write blog post or case study (internal or external)
- [ ] Improve documentation or training materials
- [ ] Help with company culture initiative

### 90-Day Review
- [ ] Formal 90-day performance review with manager
- [ ] Celebrate wins!
- [ ] Transition to standard performance management cycle
- [ ] Set quarterly OKRs

## Role-Specific Milestones

### Product Manager
- [ ] Month 1: Ship first user story
- [ ] Month 2: Own full feature end-to-end
- [ ] Month 3: Lead product review presentation

### Engineer
- [ ] Month 1: Fix 5+ bugs, ship 1 small feature
- [ ] Month 2: Own full feature (design  ->  deploy)
- [ ] Month 3: Mentor junior eng, contribute to architecture

### Sales
- [ ] Month 1: Complete 10 shadowed demos
- [ ] Month 2: Lead 5 demos with backup, close first deal
- [ ] Month 3: Hit 50% of quota independently

### Customer Success
- [ ] Month 1: Handle 20 tickets, 5 onboarding calls (shadowed)
- [ ] Month 2: Own 5 customer accounts, lead onboarding
- [ ] Month 3: Achieve >80% CSAT, drive 1 upsell

### Finance/Operations
- [ ] Month 1: Learn all financial processes
- [ ] Month 2: Own month-end close process
- [ ] Month 3: Identify and implement 1 automation

## Success Metrics

**You're succeeding if:**
- [x] Manager rates you "meeting or exceeding expectations" at 90 days
- [x] You feel confident in your role
- [x] Team members seek your input/help
- [x] You've made measurable business impact
- [x] You're excited to come to work!

**Struggling? That's normal!**
- Talk to your manager early and often
- Ask for help (everyone wants you to succeed)
- Adjust goals if needed (this is a guide, not rigid)
