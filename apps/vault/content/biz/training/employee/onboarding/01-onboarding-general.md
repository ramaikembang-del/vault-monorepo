## Our Values

**1. Customer Obsession**
- We exist to serve F&B owners
- Every decision starts with "does this help customers?"
- Talk to customers weekly (everyone, not just CS team!)

**2. Bias for Action**
- Move fast, iterate, learn
- Better done than perfect
- Two-way door decisions â†’ decide quickly

**3. Transparency**
- Share information openly
- Document decisions (in writing)
- Overcommunicate

**4. Ownership**
- You own outcomes, not just tasks
- If you see a problem, fix it (or flag it)
- No "that's not my job"

**5. Indonesian F&B Focus**
- We deeply understand local market
- Pricing in Rupiah, interfaces in Indonesian
- Compliance with local regulations (PDPN, tax)

## How We Work

**Communication:**
- **Slack:** Daily work, quick questions
- **Email:** External, formal, needs documentation
- **Meetings:** Decision-making, alignment (not status updates)

**Work Hours:**
- Core hours: 10 AM - 4 PM WIB (be available)
- Flexible otherwise
- Remote-friendly (but Jakarta office available)

**Meetings:**
- Monday: Week planning (whole team)
- Wednesday: Product review
- Friday: Metrics review
- Daily: Team standups (15 min)
