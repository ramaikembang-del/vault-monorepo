## Week 3-4: Sales Process

### Our Sales Process
1. **Lead qualification** (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
2. **Discovery call** (understand pain points)
3. **Demo** (show relevant solution)
4. **Proposal** (pricing, terms)
5. **Negotiation** (handle objections)
6. **Close** (contract signed!)

### Shadow & Practice
- [ ] Shadow 10 full sales cycles
- [ ] Practice discovery calls (role-play with manager)
- [ ] Practice demos (recorded, get feedback)
- [ ] Practice objection handling

**Common Objections:**
- "Too expensive" → ROI calculator, payment plans
- "We'll just use Excel" → Show limitations, time savings
- "Need to think about it" → Address real concern
- "Can you add feature X?" → Product roadmap, workarounds

## Sales Playbooks

### Discovery Call Script
```
1. Introduction (2 min)
 - Who we are, what we do

2. Learn about them (15 min)
 - How many outlets?
 - Current analytics process?
 - Biggest challenges?
 - What would success look like?

3. Qualification (5 min)
 - Budget for analytics tools?
 - Who makes buying decision?
 - Timeline to decide?

4. Next steps (3 min)
 - Schedule demo if qualified
 - Send resources if not ready
```

### Demo Flow (30 min)
```
1. Confirm their pain points (repeat back)
2. Show solution to THEIR specific problem
3. Walk through 2-3 key features
4. Show real F&B data (not dummy data)
5. Address questions
6. Discuss pricing
7. Next steps (proposal or follow-up)
```

**Demo Best Practices:**
- Less is more (don't show every feature)
- Make it about them, not us
- Use their business name in demo
- Handle objections in real-time
