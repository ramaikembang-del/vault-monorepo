# Finance & Operations Training

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Duration:** Week 1-4

---

## Week 1: Financial Systems

### Learn Our Financials
- [ ] Understand revenue model (subscriptions, one-time)
- [ ] Review P&L structure
- [ ] Learn invoicing process
- [ ] Understand payment methods (Stripe, bank transfer)
- [ ] Review chart of accounts

### Systems Access
- [ ] QuickBooks/Xero access
- [ ] Stripe dashboard
- [ ] Bank accounts (view-only)
- [ ] Expense management system

---

## Week 2: Month-End Close

### Shadow Finance Manager
- [ ] Observe full month-end close process
- [ ] Learn reconciliation procedures
- [ ] Understand accruals and deferrals
- [ ] Review variance analysis (budget vs actual)

### First Close (Month 3)
- [ ] Own month-end close independently
- [ ] Complete within 5 business days
- [ ] Produce P&L, balance sheet, cash flow

---

## Responsibilities

**Daily:**
- Process invoices
- Reconcile payments
- Answer billing questions
- Track expenses

**Weekly:**
- Cash flow forecast update
- AR aging report
- Approve expense reimbursements

**Monthly:**
- Month-end financial close
- P&L vs budget variance analysis
- Board reporting package
- Payroll processing (if applicable)

**Quarterly:**
- Tax filings (PPN, PPh)
- Financial forecast update
- Budget reforecasting

**Annually:**
- Budget planning
- Annual tax return-
- Financial audit (if applicable)

---

## Key Processes

**Invoice to Cash:**
1. Generate invoice (Stripe or manual)
2. Send to customer
3. Track payment
4. Reconcile to bank
5. Record in accounting system

**Expense Management:**
1. Employee submits expense
2. Manager approves
3. Finance validates
4. Payment processed
5. Record in accounting system

**Financial Reporting:**
1. Collect actuals from systems
2. Reconcile accounts
3. Categorize transactions
4. Produce reports (P&L, cash flow)
5. Analyze variances
6. Distribute to leadership

---

## Indonesian Tax Compliance

**Monthly:**
- PPN (VAT) filing - By 20th of following month
- PPh 21 (employee tax) if applicable

**Annual:**
- Corporate income tax - By March 31
- Financial statements

**Work with:** Tax consultant for compliance
