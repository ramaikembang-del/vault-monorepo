## Key Responsibilities

**Onboarding (First 30 Days with Customer):**
- Welcome email within 24 hours
- Setup call within 48 hours
- Weekly check-ins (4 total)
- Ensure "aha moment" by Day 7
- 30-day success review

**Ongoing Success:**
- Monthly check-in calls
- Quarterly Business Reviews (QBR)
- Monitor usage, flag low engagement
- Drive feature adoption
- Identify upsell opportunities

**Renewals:**
- Reach out 60 days before renewal
- Review value delivered
- Address any concerns
- Negotiate renewals/upsells

---

## Customer Health Score

**Track for each account:**
- Product usage (logins, features used)
- Engagement (responds to emails, joins calls)
- Sentiment (NPS, feedback tone)
- Payment (on-time, any issues)

**Health Levels:**
- Green (80-100): Healthy, likely to renew
- Yellow (50-79): At risk, needs attention
- Red (<50): Churn risk, escalate immediately

---

## Handling Difficult Situations

**Customer Wants to Cancel:**
1. Understand why (listen, don't interrupt)
2. Acknowledge their concern
3. Offer solution (discount, pause, feature they need)
4. If still canceling, make it graceful (easy offboarding)
5. Ask for feedback

**Customer is Angry:**
1. Empathize ("I understand your frustration")
2. Take ownership ("Let me fix this")
3. Provide timeline ("I'll have an update in 2 hours")
4. Follow through
5. Follow up after resolution

**Product Bug Affecting Customer:**
1. Acknowledge immediately
2. Escalate to engineering
3. Provide workaround if possible
4. Keep customer updated (even if no progress, update them)
5. Compensate if needed (discount, extended trial)

## Success Metrics

**Activity:**
- Onboarding calls: 10/month
- QBRs: 5/quarter
- Support tickets: 30/month (resolved)

**Outcomes:**
- Customer satisfaction (CSAT): >80%
- Net Promoter Score (NPS): >40
- Renewal rate: >90%
- Churn rate: <5%/month
- Upsell: 10% of accounts/quarter
