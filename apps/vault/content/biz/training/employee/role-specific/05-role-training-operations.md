# Operations Training

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Duration:** Week 1-4

---

## Overview

Operations at PWBI ensures everything runs smoothly - from office management to vendor relationships to process improvement.

---

## Week 1: Learn the Business

### Understand Operations
- [ ] Review all SOPs (standard operating procedures)
- [ ] Map out all business processes
- [ ] Identify key vendors and partners
- [ ] Learn escalation procedures

### Meet Stakeholders
- [ ] Meet all department heads
- [ ] Understand their operational needs
- [ ] Shadow operations manager

---

## Week 2-4: Take Ownership

### Process Management
- [ ] Own 1 process end-to-end (e.g., vendor management)
- [ ] Identify inefficiencies
- [ ] Propose improvements
- [ ] Implement changes

### Vendor Management
- [ ] Review all vendor contracts
- [ ] Negotiate 1 renewal
- [ ] Onboard new vendor
- [ ] Track vendor performance

---

## Key Responsibilities

**Office Operations:**
- Office supplies and equipment
- Facilities management (if physical office)
- Employee onboarding logistics (laptop, accounts)

**Vendor Management:**
- Negotiate contracts
- Track performance and SLAs
- Resolve issues
- Renew or replace vendors

**Process Improvement:**
- Document processes (SOPs)
- Identify bottlenecks
- Automate where possible
- Train team on new processes

**Incident Management:**
- Coordinate response to P0/P1 incidents
- Maintain on-call rotation
- Run post-mortems
- Track and resolve issues

---

## Success Metrics

**Efficiency:**
-Process cycle time reduction (20% in 90 days)
- Automation implemented (1+ process automated)

**Vendor Management:**
- Contract savings (5-10% on renewals)
- Vendor SLA compliance (>95%)

**Team Satisfaction:**
- Operations NPS from team (>50)
