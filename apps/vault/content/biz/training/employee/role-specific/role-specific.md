# Role-Specific Training

**Category:** Employee Training  ->  Role-Specific
**Audience:** New hires in specialized roles
**Owner:** HR Department

---

## Overview

This directory contains role-specific training materials tailored to different positions within the organization. Each role has customized onboarding content that builds on the core training foundation.

---

## Available Role Training

### Product & Development
- Product Manager training
- Developer onboarding
- QA/Testing processes

### Business Operations
- Finance role training
- Sales methodology
- Customer success playbook

### Support & Services
- Customer support workflows
- Technical support escalation
- Account management

---

## Using This Training

**New Hires:**
1. Complete core training first (general onboarding)
2. Review your specific role training module
3. Schedule follow-up with your manager

**Managers:**
- Assign role-specific modules during Week 2 of onboarding
- Track completion via onboarding checklist
- Customize content as roles evolve
