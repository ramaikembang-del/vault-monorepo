## Overview

A training video explaining FIFO (First-In-First-Out) inventory management for F&B businesses. Educational content for customers using our products (especially Products #10-13 and future inventory modules).

---

## Planned Video Content

### Target Audience
- Phase 1-2 Mikro business owners (warung, kafe kecil)
- Phase 3 Kecil business owners learning inventory management

### Duration
- 5-7 minutes

### Language
- Indonesian (primary)
- English subtitles (optional for future)

---

## Video Outline

### Part 1: Introduction (1 min)
- What is FIFO?
- Why it matters for F&B businesses
- Common problems without FIFO (spoilage, waste)

### Part 2: FIFO Basics (2 min)
- Simple explanation: "Use oldest stock first"
- Visual example: Bottles of milk with expiration dates
- Analogy: Queue at warung (first customer served first)

### Part 3: Implementing FIFO (2 min)
- Physical arrangement of stock (oldest in front)
- Labeling with dates (purchase date or expiration)
- Daily routine: Check dates, use oldest first

### Part 4: Using FIFO in Our Products (1 min)
- How Web Dashboard tracks FIFO automatically
- How Inventory Module (R2c) alerts you to expiring stock
- Setup tips in the app

### Part 5: Common Mistakes (1 min)
- Mistake 1: Not labeling dates
- Mistake 2: Putting new stock in front
- How to fix them
