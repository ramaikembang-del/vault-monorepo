# Reseller Training

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27
**Duration:** 2 days (16 hours)
**Format:** Online + certification exam

---

## Day 1: Product & Market Knowledge (8 hours)

### Module 1: PWBI Overview (2 hours)
- Company background and mission
- Target customers (Indonesian F&B SMEs)
- Product portfolio (A1, R1, E1, R2a-f, C1 pipeline)
- Competitive positioning

### Module 2: Product Deep Dive (4 hours)
**For each product (A1, E1, R2a-f):**
- Key features and benefits
- Use cases and customer stories
- Pricing and packaging
- Demo scripts

**Hands-on:** Set up test accounts, practice demos

### Module 3: F&B Industry Knowledge (2 hours)
- F&B business challenges in Indonesia
- Key metrics F&B owners track
- Common pain points we solve
- Indonesian regulatory context (PDPN, tax)

---

## Day 2: Sales & Operations (8 hours)

### Module 4: Sales Process (3 hours)
- Lead qualification (BANT framework)
- Discovery questions
- Demo best practices
- Objection handling
- Closing techniques

**Practice:** Role-play sales scenarios

### Module 5: Reseller Program Details (2 hours)
- Commission structure (10-15% recurring)
- Deal registration process
- Marketing support available
- Lead distribution rules
- Payment terms

**See:** [[biz/playbooks/operations/08-reseller-program|Reseller Program]]

### Module 6: Customer Onboarding (2 hours)
- Handoff from sales to CS
- Customer setup process
- Training resources for customers
- Support escalation procedures

### Module 7: Tools & Systems (1 hour)
- Partner portal access
- Deal registration system
- Marketing asset library
- Support ticket system

---

## Certification Exam

**Required to sell PWBI products.**

**Format:**
- 50 multiple choice questions
- 80% pass rate required
- 90 minutes

**Topics:**
- Product knowledge (40%)
- Sales methodology (30%)
- F&B industry (20%)
- Program rules (10%)

**Retake:** If fail, wait 7 days, retake (free)

**See:** [[biz/training/partner/05-partner-certification-program|Certification Details]]

## Success Metrics

**Good Reseller:**
- 2+ deals closed/month
- >20% close rate (qualified leads)
- <10% churn rate (customers you sold)
- >80% customer satisfaction score

**Top Tier Benefits** (Meet metrics for 3 months):
- Higher commission (15% vs 10%)
- Priority leads
- Co-marketing budget
- Early access to new products
