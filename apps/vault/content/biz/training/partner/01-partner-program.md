# Partner Ecosystem - Partner Program

**Program:** PWBI Partner Network
**Launch:** Q2 2025

## Partner Types

**1. Resellers (F&B Consultants, Accountants)**
- Resell PWBI products to clients
- Revenue share: 30% recurring
- Target: 50 partners (Year 1)

**2. Implementation Partners (System Integrators)**
- Deploy & customize PWBI for enterprise
- Project-based fees
- Target: 10 partners

**3. Technology Partners (ISVs)**
- Build integrations via marketplace
- Revenue share on paid integrations
- Target: 100 partners

## Partner Benefits
- Training & certification
- Co-marketing support
- Deal registration
- Partner portal

## Revenue Impact
- Partners drive 30% of new ARR (2026+)
- Rp 3B ARR via partners
