# Partner Certification Program

**Last Updated:** 2025-12-27

---

## Overview

All PWBI partners must be certified to ensure quality and customer success.

---

## Certification Types

### 1. Reseller Certification

**Who:** Resellers selling PWBI products
**Prerequisites:** Complete [[biz/training/partner/02-reseller-training|Reseller Training]]
**Exam:** 50 questions, 80% pass rate, 90 minutes
**Validity:** 1 year (annual recertification)

**Topics Covered:**
- Product knowledge (40%)
- Sales methodology (30%)
- F&B industry knowledge (20%)
- Reseller program rules (10%)

**Cost:** Free (included in reseller agreement)

---

### 2. Implementation Partner Certification

**Who:** Technical consultants implementing PWBI
**Prerequisites:** Complete [[biz/training/partner/03-implementation-partner-training|Implementation Partner Training]]
**Exam:** Technical exam + practical implementation
**Validity:** 2 years

**Requirements:**
- Technical exam: 80% pass rate
- Complete sample implementation project
- Demonstrate troubleshooting skills

**Cost:** Rp 5M (refunded after first 3 successful implementations)

---

### 3. Integration Partner Certification

**Who:** Developers building integrations with PWBI API
**Prerequisites:** Complete [[biz/training/partner/04-integration-partner-training|Integration Partner Training]]
**Certification:** Integration review + security audit
**Validity:** Ongoing (as long as integration maintained)

**Requirements:**
- Build working integration
- Pass security audit
- Meet performance benchmarks
- Provide documentation
- Test with pilot customers

**Cost:** Free

---

## Certification Process

### Step 1: Training
Complete required training program (online or in-person)

### Step 2: Exam/Project
- **Resellers:** Take online exam
- **Implementation:** Complete practical project
- **Integration:** Build and submit integration

### Step 3: Review
- Exam auto-graded (results immediate)
- Projects reviewed within 5 business days
- Integrations reviewed within 10 business days

### Step 4: Certification
- Pass → Certificate issued, added to partner directory
- Fail → Feedback provided, retake allowed (see below)

---

## Retake Policy

**Resellers:**
- Unlimited retakes
- Wait 7 days between attempts
- Study materials provided for failed topics

**Implementation Partners:**
- 2 retakes allowed (free)
- Additional retakes: Rp 1M each
- Must wait 30 days

**Integration Partners:**
- Fix issues and resubmit
- No retake limit
- Must address all review feedback

---

## Recertification

**Resellers:**
- Annual renewal
- Shortened exam (25 questions, covers new features)
- Must maintain performance metrics

**Implementation Partners:**
- Every 2 years
- Update training on new features
- Submit case study of recent implementation

**Integration Partners:**
- Ongoing (no formal recertification)
- Must keep integration updated with API changes

---

## Benefits of Certification

### All Certified Partners:
- Listed in partner directory (pwbi.id/partners)
- Access to partner portal
- Use of "PWBI Certified Partner" badge
- Partner Slack channel access

### Additional Benefits by Tier:

**Silver (Certified):**
- Standard commission/rev-share
- Marketing materials access
- Monthly partner newsletter

**Gold (Certified + High Performance):**
- Higher commission (15% vs 10% for resellers)
- Priority leads
- Co-marketing budget (Rp 10M/year)
- Quarterly training updates

**Platinum (Top 5% Partners):**
- Highest commission (20%)
- Dedicated partner success manager
- Early access to new products
- Annual partner summit (all-expenses paid)

---

## Performance Metrics for Tier Advancement

**Resellers (Gold/Platinum):**
- Monthly deals: 3+ (Gold), 5+ (Platinum)
- Customer retention: >90%
- Customer satisfaction: >85%
- Maintain for 3 consecutive months

**Implementation Partners:**
- Implementations/quarter: 5+ (Gold), 10+ (Platinum)
- Re-work rate: <5%
- Customer satisfaction: >85%
- On-time delivery: >90%

**Integration Partners:**
- Active users of integration: 50+ (Gold), 100+ (Platinum)
- Integration uptime: >99.5%
- Support response time: <24 hours

---

## Certification Revocation

**Grounds for revocation:**
- Violation of partner agreement
- Consistently poor customer feedback
- Fraudulent activity
- Data security breach

**Process:**
1. Warning issued
2. 30 days to remediate
3. If not resolved, certification revoked
4. Can reapply after 6 months
