## Core Training Modules

### Module 1: Quick Start (15 min)

**Video 1:** "Welcome to Clario" (3 min)
- What is Clario? (30 sec overview)
- Who is it for? (Phase 2-3 F&B owners)
- What will you learn? (course preview)

**Video 2:** "First Login" (5 min)
- Create account
- Navigate dashboard
- Understand layout (sidebar, top bar, main area)

**Video 3:** "Upload Your First Data" (5 min)
- Export from POS (or manual entry)
- Upload CSV
- See your first dashboard (celebrate!)

**Exercise:** Upload last month's data, take screenshot

---

### Module 2: Understanding Metrics (30 min)

**Video 4:** "Revenue Dashboard" (5 min)
- What metrics are shown
- How to filter (by date, location)
- Insights to look for (trends, peaks)

**Video 5:** "Profit Analysis" (5 min)
- Gross vs net profit
- Margin percentages
- Cost breakdown

**Video 6:** "Customer Insights" (5 min)
- Repeat customer rate
- Customer lifetime value
- Segmentation (VIP vs one-timers)

**Exercise:** Identify your highest revenue day last month

---

### Module 3: Taking Action (20 min)

**Video 7:** "Menu Optimization" (5 min)
- Which items are most profitable?
- Which to promote, which to sunset?

**Video 8:** "Cost Control" (5 min)
- Track COGS % (should be <35-40%)
- Identify cost spikes
- Supplier negotiation tips

**Video 9:** "Staffing Optimization" (5 min)
- Labor cost % (should be <25-30%)
- Schedule adjustments (based on busy hours)

**Exercise:** Make 1 business decision based on data

---

## Training Delivery

### Self-Paced (Default)
- Customer accesses training center (help.clario.id)
- Watches videos at own pace
- Completes exercises
- Takes quiz (optional certification)

### Guided Onboarding (Pro+)
- Scheduled 1-hour session with CS team
- Walkthrough + Q&A
- Customized to customer's business

### Group Training (Enterprise)
- On-site workshop (2-3 hours)
- Train 5-20 users at once
- Hands-on practice with their data

---

## Training Metrics

**Engagement:**
- % of customers who complete Module 1 (target: >80%)
- Avg time to complete all modules (target: <2 hours)
- Video completion rate (target: >70%)

**Impact:**
- Customers who train activate faster (30% vs 50% time-to-value)
- Trained users churn less (12% vs 18% churn rate)
- Higher NPS (+10 points)

## Certification Program

**"Clario Certified User"**

**Requirements:**
- Complete all 9 training videos
- Pass 20-question quiz (80% score)
- Submit 1 business insight from dashboard

**Benefits:**
- Digital badge (share on LinkedIn)
- Priority support (faster ticket response)
- Early access to new features

**Goal:** 30% of customers certified (Year 1)
